In 1271[\[1\]](#cite-note-year-1)[\[2\]](#cite-note-trenti-2) a [Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) vessel attacked the ship of the [Rotten Lot](Rotten_Lot.md), '[The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>)' in the [Eastern Luminant](Eastern_Luminant.md).

## Prelude

The Rotten Lot, aboard 'The Scoundrel's Bounty', were present in the seas of the Eastern Luminant on a trip to the territories of [Etrea](Etrea.md).[\[5\]](#cite-note-kelsius-5) During this period, their Captain, [Orlandeau](<Cap'n_Orlandeau.md>), was training [the Pathfinder](The_Pathfinder.md) in combat and survival.[\[3\]](#cite-note-orlandeau-3) Meanwhile, a Central Authority ship was sighted by [Serendi](Serendi.md) over the horizon, but she did not recognize its allegiance and thus didn't report its presence.[\[4\]](#cite-note-serendi-4)

## The attack

Eventually, the Authority vessel approached 'The Scoundrel's Bounty', announcing their presence by performing [The Gideshu March](The_Gideshu_March.md). Orlandeau recognized the tune and ordered his crew below deck, but it was too late. A single volley from the Central Authority ship effortlessly wiped out the crew[\[3\]](#cite-note-orlandeau-3) and capsized the vessel.[\[5\]](#cite-note-kelsius-5)

## Aftermath

Among the Rotten Lot, only the Pathfinder survived, and they were taken in by residents of Etrea, returning to full health a week after the attack.[\[2\]](#cite-note-trenti-2)

The crew's deaths saw them sent to [The Depths](The_Depths.md). Only Orlandeau was accounted for, and he navigated to a [Diver stronghold](Castle_Light.md) shortly after. He was later sent to [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](New_Kyrsa,_The_Sleeping_City.md) after offending [Klaris Llfiend, Mother of Lights](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md).[\[3\]](#cite-note-orlandeau-3)
