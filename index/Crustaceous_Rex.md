# Crustaceous Rex

“ Deceivingly swift, this blade was once used to pierce through the carapaces of the indomitable Crustaceous Rex by members of the ill-fated Authority 'Expeditionary Corps'. ”

― Inquisitor's Thorn Description

Template:Creature Infobox V3_Also known as **King Crab** or just simply **Crab.**_

## Description

Crustaceous Rex is a hostile Giant-type mob. Whilst appearing scary, it is one of the less threatening mobs in the Depths thanks to its low mobility and easily learned attack patterns. It has 6 glowing yellow eyes, with a white and purple exterior shell. The upper perimeter of its shell is dotted with large yellow stubs, and it has two large purple claws.

## Trivia

-   One of the only [monsters](Monsters.md) in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) that one can run away from without climbing.
    -   The Enforcer in Hell Mode, losing its pull ability, can also be ran away from.
-   Likely inspired by "Giant Crab" from Darksouls 3
    -   Is an almost exact copy of the foam spray, also having a slow effect when hit
-   The crab tends to glitch itself into objects, mainly by its claws, which makes it easier to deal with.
-   The crab's Crab is canonically named how it is. It wasn't a spelling mistake. It is not that the developer's intended to say Crab Crab, as all the other moves were referred to as a grab, but instead misspelled Grab, thus making it lore to cover up their mistake.[\[1\]](#cite-note-crabcrab-1)
