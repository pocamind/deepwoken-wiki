# Crustaceous Rex

Template:Creature Infobox V3

_Also known as Purple Crab, King Crab, Crabbo._

## Description

Crustaceous Rex is a hostile Giant-type mob. They're recommended killing for exp and loot. It has 6 glowing yellow eyes, with a white and purple exterior shell.

## Attacks

**  
Bubble Beam** - Shoots a barrage of bubbles at its foes.

**Crushing Claw** - Grabs someone and drops them doing a good amount of damage.

**Ground Smash**\- Jumps high up before slamming down. Cannot be parried.

**Hack & Slash** - Slashes twice, dealing a good amount of damage.

## Notes

-   One of, if not the only [Monster](Monsters.md) in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) that one can run away from without climbing.
