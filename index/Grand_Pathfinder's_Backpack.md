## Description

The Grand Pathfinder's Backpack is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Torso" category.

It's the greater version of the [Pathfinder's Backpack](<Pathfinder's_Backpack.md>), possessing 50 carry load instead of 35. This item is very handy for bank slots.
