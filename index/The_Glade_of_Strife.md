# The Glade of Strife

LocationL The Glade of Strife LocationR

Overcome the challenges ahead and you shall be greatly rewarded.

  

**The Glade of Strife** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the 2v2 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**The Glade of Strife** is a small grove that appears with stumps that look similar to [Lifevents](Lifevents.md).
