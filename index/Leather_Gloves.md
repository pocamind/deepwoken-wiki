## Trivia

-   The Leather Gloves are part of the _Leather Set_ containing the [Leather Pauldrons](Leather_Pauldrons.md) and the [Leather Boots](Leather_Boots.md).
    -   However, you can't wear the [Leather Pauldrons](Leather_Pauldrons.md) and the Leather Gloves as they both take up the _Arms_ equipment slot.
