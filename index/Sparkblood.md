# Sparkblood

## Info

File:Thundercall Trainer.png

Thundercall Trainer found on Summer Isle

A potion used to obtain [Thundercall](Thundercall.md). Obtained at [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) by giving the [Thundercall Trainer](Attunement_Trainers.md) 5 [Spark Glands](Spark_Gland.md).

## Notes

-   Note that **this item cannot be dropped**, and you may only **attain one [Attunement](Attunements.md) per character**
    -   If spawning in with an attunement already you may only get one other potion per character. Doesnt matter if you dont drink them the trainers will still turn you down. Along with spawning with no attunement, just two instead of one.
