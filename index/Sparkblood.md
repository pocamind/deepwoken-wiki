# Sparkblood

A potion used to obtain [Thundercall](Thundercall.md). Obtained at [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) by giving the [Thundercall Trainer](Attunement_Trainers.md) 5 [Spark Glands](Spark_Gland.md).

  

-   Note that **this item cannot be dropped**, and you may only **attain one [Attunement](Attunements.md) per character**
    -   (However, obtaining all the potions and drinking them simultaneously will enable you to get all 5 [Attunements](Attunements.md) and your characters first name will change to Aang)
