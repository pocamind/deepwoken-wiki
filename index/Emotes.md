# Emotes

File:Imagem 2023-04-14 110344933.png

Emote Menu

## Description

Emotes are gestures or poses a player can use. The default input to select emotes is K. To use an emote click the emote name in the emote selection UI.

For the most part, emotes are purely cosmetic. However, they can be used for other purposes, such as emoting at [The Great Sharko Rumbling](The_Great_Sharko_Rumbling.md) memorial near the [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md), to summon [Destroyman III](Destroyman_III.md). Additionally, some of the Communication Gestures have unique effects when used.

## Trivia

-   Prior to the May 22nd, 2023 update, the default keybind to toggle emotes was X before it was moved to K. This was changed with the addition of [Ironsing](Ironsing.md) due to the mechanic of "Iron Pull" taking its keybind.
-   Players can also use emotes by typing into the chat "/e \[emote name\]" as long as they have it.
    -   Strangely, this allows players to use the Salute emote, even on non-[Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md) characters.
