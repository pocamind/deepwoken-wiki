# The Lordsgrove

## Description

The Lordsgrove is a location in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) behind a massive green door, which you can open after washing your face at the fountain in the Lightkeeper temple. This area contains a fountain that gives you the option to invoke, which will transform you into a ball of light and quickly bring you to [**Greathive Aratel**](Greathive_Aratel.md) if you have an [**oath**](Oaths.md).

Quick note as shown in the clip below events that exclusively only spawn in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) have been spotted happening underneath the Hive with a major theory on why, Being that [The Depths](The_Depths.md) are attempting to drag down [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) into the Depths partially proven by the fact pieces of the Hive have been spotted rotting with the same effect as the Drowned enchant appearing all over the Great Hive with [The Divers](The_Divers.md) attempting to halt their advance by fighting off the creatures that have been emerging alongside [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md) other creatures such as [Lionfish](Lionfish.md) have also been spotted.
