# The Lordsgrove

## Description

The Lordsgrove is a location in Starswept Valley behind a massive green door, which you can open after washing your face at the fountain in the Lightkeeper temple. This area contains a fountain that gives you the option to invoke, which will transform you into a ball of light and quickly bring you to [**Greathive Aratel**](Greathive_Aratel.md) if you have an [**oath**](Oaths.md).
