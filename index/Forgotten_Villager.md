## Description

Forgotten Villagers are NPCs that were. Unlike the other villagers, they distrust Alastir and thus cannot be handed in to him. Talking to one of villagers will initiate an event that spawns three waves of [Bandit Leaders](Bandit_Leader.md) and eventually a wave leading up to a [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md).

-   Wave 1/2/3 - Four Bandit Leaders
-   Wave 4 - One Rock Golem

## Moveset

A Forgotten Villager's arsenal only consists of their [Fists](Fists.md), using the [Way of Navae](Way_of_Navae.md) fighting style.
