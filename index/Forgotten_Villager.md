# Forgotten Villager

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Forgotten Villagers are NPCs that spawn in groups of three inside Erisor's Church. Unlike the other villagers, they distrust Alastair and thus cannot be handed in to him. Their only real purpose is to die.
