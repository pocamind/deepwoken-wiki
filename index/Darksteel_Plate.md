# Darksteel Plate

Template:ArmorInfobox**Darksteel Plate** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, five (5) pieces of Erisore, one (1) Strange Claw and one (1) Dark Feather.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 30 and [Fortitude](Attributes.md) 20.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2500 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance and +25% Elemental resistance.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Unbreakable_, which prevents the player from being stunned after their block is broken.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found:

-   Above the Lord Regent's throne in Etris
-   Bandit chests in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md)
