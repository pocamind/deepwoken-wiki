# Darksteel Plate

Template:ArmorInfobox

Darksteel Plate is a Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_

Requirements

This Outfit costs 300 notes and requires (5) Cloth (1) Dark Feather (5) Erisore (5) Fiber and finally (1) Strange Claw

Armour Stats

This outfit has 2500 Durability and grants +25% Elemental Armour and +25% Physical Armour

  
This armour also comes with the talent Unbreakable which when your block broken you are not stunned.

Location

The outfits Schematic can be found in

1.Lower Erisia

2\. On top of the Palace in Etris

Trivia/Notes

-   Being block broken will still damage you
-   If your posture still resets after a block break (I never had this armor), it should be theoretically possible to infinite block [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md) and [Enforcer](Enforcer.md) spin attacks
