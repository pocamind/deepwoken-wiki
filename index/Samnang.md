## Description

Samnang is a [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC. His brother [Sophon](Sophon.md), is a sworn warrior for [Clan Mofisu](Clan_Mofisu.md).

## Location

Samnang is located against a rock ledge near [Vesna](Vesna.md) at [The Lordsgrove](The_Lordsgrove.md).
