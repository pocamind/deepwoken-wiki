# Captain Solaire

## Description

Captain Solaire is a group of three NPCs found in the [Merit Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md). They are dangerous enemies who guard the important parts of the core. Each of them deals a large amount of damage with M1s and Critical Attacks and uses a different double-enchanted weapon.

**Warden's Ceremonial Sword:**

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

This variant of Solaire wields a [Warden Ceremonial Sword](Warden_Ceremonial_Sword.md) and wears an [Authority Helm](Authority_Helm.md) alongside an [Amber Authority Commander Coat](Authority_Commander_Coat.md) and [Silver Fleet Warden Cape](Fleet_Warden_Cape.md). They also wear the [Authority Captain Uniform](Authority_Captain_Uniform.md). This one uses standard [Thundercall](Thundercall.md). They are an [Adret](Adret.md).

## Finisher

Captain Solaire pins their opponent down, before pulverising their skull with their [Sovereign's Punishment](<Sovereign's_Punishment.md>).

## Tips

-   Assassinate them to deal major damage.
-   Avoid being hit by their M1s or Critical Attacks, as they will do major damage to even the tankiest of builds.
-   Their AI is extremely intelligent, however they cannot parry most mantras, use this to your advantage.
-   Their weapons can be enchanted with 2 separate enchantments.

## Trivia

-   Their name Solaire may be a reference to Solaire of Astora, a Dark Souls character.
