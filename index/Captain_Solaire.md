# Captain Solaire

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Captain Solaire is an NPC found in Merit Detainment Core, a dangerous enemy which guards the important parts.

Their appareance consists of an Authority Helmet, an Authority Captain Uniform and an Authority Commander Coat, albeit missing the sleeves.

## Moveset

Their arsenal consists of a Nemit's Sickle along with Thundercall, Flamecharm and Frostdraw attunements.

## Trivia

Their name Solaire may be a reference to Solaire of Astora, a Dark Souls character.
