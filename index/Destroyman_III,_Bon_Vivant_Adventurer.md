## Description

Destroyman III, Bon Vivant Adventurer is a "mischievous wayward traveller" based of off [Destroyman III](Destroyman_III.md).

## Location

Destroyman III, Bon Vivant Adventurer is located atop a spike high up in [The Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md).
