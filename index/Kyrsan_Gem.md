## Description

The **Kyrsan Gem** is a pink crystalline [Deep Gem](Deep_Gems.md) made up of several hexagonal prisms that converge at the bottom, not too dissimilar to the real-world quartz in shape. It also has a pink glow around it.

It is obtained by defeating the [Kyrsgarde Champion](<Kyrsgarde_Champion,_Kyrsa's_Star.md>). You can find the Gem in the chest.

Unknown drop rate.

This Gem is currently **unbankable** and **undroppable.**

## Effect

On hit, slow your opponents for around 6 seconds. This effect is stackable and it has a 1s CD.

Currently, this Gem's CD is bugged, having a 0s CD instead of the 1s CD.
