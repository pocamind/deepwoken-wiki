# Duelist Mask

_If you wish to see useful information on all Equipment, Go to [**Equipment**](Equipment.md)_ Template:EquipmentStats The **Duelist's Mask** is an accessory under the "Face" category. It can be found in chests in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).

## Appearance

The Duelist's Mask is a ovular mask that is pure white with one big circle that has a black outline in the middle of the mask. In the middle column is a black line connecting the circle to the middle of the bottom of the mask.
