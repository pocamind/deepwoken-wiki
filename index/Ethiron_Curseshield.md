## Description

The Ethiron Curseshield is a darkly colored [Shield](Weapons.md), decorated with complex tangling patterns on its front side. It has a requirement of 50 FTD to use, and has 1.9 Weight.

## Trivia

-   This shield bears a heavy resemblance to the [Greatshield of Artorias](https://darksouls.fandom.com/wiki/Greatshield_of_Artorias) from _Dark Souls_.
