## Description

The Hollow Angel Mask is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Face" category. This item worn by the [Hollow Angel](Hollow_Angel.md), and drops from them rarely.

## Trivia

-   While similar to the [Sworn Angel Mask](Sworn_Angel_Mask.md), the Hollow Angel Mask's eyes do not glow when worn.
    -   They however, will glow when dyed.
-   It has the same mouth motif as the [Mudskipper Brute](Mudskipper_Brute.md).
-   The eye sockets are empty and the teeth are missing when held.
