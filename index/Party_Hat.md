## Description

The Party Hat is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category. It was obtained by joining during the [Festival of a Billion Souls](Festival_of_a_Billion_Souls.md) Mini-Event.

## Trivia

-   The Description and Item are both references to Oldschool Runescape, being [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bbfsPzjIXko), in which a Party Hat was the most expensive item ingame.
