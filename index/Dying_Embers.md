# Dying Embers

Ok, first off, dying embers are used to get Flamecharm. Flamecharm is a type of element and can be upgraded to azure flames with 40 willpower and 75 flamecharm. Dying embers drop from anything that uses the magic of Flamecharm. Some of these enemies are the exploding zombies from the zombie event and Erisia, not to be confused by the Mudskippers INSIDE the cave. This is a random event and they will attack bandits if they are closeby. They also drop from golems and I think Flamecharm people at songseeker.
