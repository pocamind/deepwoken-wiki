# Bestiary

The Bestiary is a feature in Deepwoken that allows you to gain damage and resistance buffs against enemies you have defeated multiple times. Currently, there are 19 monsters and 10 bosses that you can get buffs against. To get buffs against those monsters, you have to first kill 10 of that enemy, 25, then 100. For bosses, you are only required to kill 10.
