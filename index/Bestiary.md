# Bestiary

**Note: The Bestiary is a DVM boost across ALL slots, not just the slot you got the challenge completed in.**

The Bestiary is a mechanic in Deepwoken that allows you to gain damage and resistance buffs against enemies you have defeated multiple times. Currently, there are 19 [monsters](Monsters.md) and 10 [bosses](Bosses.md) that you can get buffs against. To get buffs against those monsters, you have to first kill 10 of that enemy, 25, then 100. For bosses, you are only required to kill 10.

It is also important to note that different monsters provide difference damage and resistance buffs. For instance, killing a [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) (of any kind) to a required kill count (10, 25, or 100) awards +10% damage and +5% resistance for each, however, killing an [Enforcer](Enforcer.md) to a required kill count (10, 25, or 100) awards +5% damage and +2.5% resistance for each. Despite this obvious change, it was later confirmed that this **IS NOT** a smaller boost, it was just a way for the game to show that these 'resilient monsters' are tankier.
