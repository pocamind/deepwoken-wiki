# Bestiary

**Note: The Bestiary is a DVM boost across ALL slots, not just the slot the challenge was completed in.**

The Bestiary is a mechanic in Deepwoken that grants damage and resistance buffs against repeatedly defeated enemies (19 monsters, 10 bosses as of 6/23/2025). Buff thresholds are 10, 25, and 100 kills for monsters, and 10 for bosses.

Buff amounts vary; for example, Megalodaunt kills grant +10% damage and +5% resistance per challenge, while Enforcer kills grant only +5% damage and+2.5% resistance. Despite this obvious change, it was later confirmed that this **IS NOT** a smaller boost, it was just a way for the game to show that these 'resilient monsters' are tankier.
