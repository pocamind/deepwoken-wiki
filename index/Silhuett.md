# Silhuett

If the player has Hero reputation with the Ministry, then they can convince him to hand over the Deepspindle, Darkscorn Casque and Darkened Bastion without a fight, but only if they teleported to him through Silhuett. If they reached him through other methods, they will instead face a Black Diver or a Mudskipper Broodlord.
