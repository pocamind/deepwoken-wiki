# 11th Legion Plate

## Description

Template:ArmorInfobox **11th Legion Plate** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, two (2) pieces of Gold and ten (10) pieces of Iron.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 15 and [Fortitude](Attributes.md) 10.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1500 durability and grants +10% Physical resistance and +30% Blunt resistance.
