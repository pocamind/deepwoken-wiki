# 11th Legion Plate

Template:ArmorInfobox**11th Legion Plate** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs 100 and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, two (2) Gold, and ten (10) Iron.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 5 and 10 [Fortitude](Attributes.md).

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1500 durability, grants +10% Physical resistance, and +30% Blunt resistance.

-   Due to resistances being combined multiplicatively instead of additively, the total resistances are: 37% Blunt and 10% Slash.
