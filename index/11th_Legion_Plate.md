**11th Legion Plate** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs 100 and requires five (5) [Cloth](Cloth.md), five (5) [Fiber](Fiber.md) and ten (10) [Iron](Ores.md#iron) to craft.

This Outfit additionally requires the player to be at least [Power](Power.md) 5 and have at least 10 [Fortitude](Attributes.md#ftd) to be crafted.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1500 durability, grants +10% Physical resistance, and +30% Blunt resistance.

-   Due to resistances being combined multiplicatively instead of additively, the total resistances are: 37% Blunt and 10% Slash.
