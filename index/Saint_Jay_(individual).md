## Description

Saint Jay was a prominent [Frostdrawer](Frostdraw.md), a disciple of [Faust](Faust.md), and the enemy of [Urek Felhand](Urek_Felhand.md). He wielded the [Saint Jay's Hammer](<Saint_Jay's_Hammer.md>).[\[1\]](#cite-note-memento-1)

Saint Jay has a [Memento](Mementos.md) linked to him, which focuses on utilization of heavy weapons and [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md) mantras.[\[1\]](#cite-note-memento-1)
