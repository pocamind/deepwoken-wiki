## Description

Saint Jay was a prominent [Frostdrawer](Frostdraw.md), a disciple of [Faust](Faust.md), and the enemy of [Urek Felhand](Urek_Felhand.md).

We are able to play as Saint Jay in Vow of Iron as hes part of the Ironsworn.
