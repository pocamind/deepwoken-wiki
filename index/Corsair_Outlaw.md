# Corsair Outlaw

Template:ArmorInfobox **Corsair Outlaw** is a Starter [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber as well as one (1) [Aeon Logstone](Mantra_Modifiers.md).

This Outfit has no [Power](Power.md) or [Attribute](Attributes.md) requirement.

### _Armor Stats_

This outfit has 1200 durability, grants the user +15% Physical Armor, and +10% Elemental Armor. Additionally, it provides the [Lightweight](Talents.md#natural-armor) Talent.

The [Lightweight](Talents.md#natural-armor) Talent increases the users movement speed by 25% when their armor's durability reaches 0.

## Obtainment

This Outfit's Schematic can be obtained from:

-   [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md)
-   [Bandits](Bandit.md)
