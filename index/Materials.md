# Materials

Materials are items in

[Armor](Outfits.md) or [Quests](Quests.md).

## Wood

### Obtainment

Wood can be obtained chopping down trees. You can obtain more wood from bigger trees. It can also be crafted from 3 sticks. Also, it can be bought at the Blacksmith Store in Etris, Outside the Inn at Isle of Vigils, Antiquarian in the Voidheart, and Balconies area in Greathive Aratel for 2 notes.

### Uses

Crafting a Campfire Pit, 3 sticks, a Lumber Axe or a Pickaxe at a crafting station, and selling it to the Lumberjack Quest giver at the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) or [Etris](Etris.md). Additionally, Pure Ore can be given to the [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) for varying loot.

## Cloth

### Obtainment

Can be bought from the Blacksmith in Etris/Isle of Vigils for or crafted using 3 fiber.

### Uses

Used for schematics.

## Fiber

### Obtainment

Can be bought from the General Store for or crafted with Bamboo Bundles/Spider Eggs.

### Uses

Used in all armor schematics except the Traveler Coats. Can be used to craft a [Fishing Rod](Fishing.md).
