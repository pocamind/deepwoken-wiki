# Brinepool Passage

LocationL Brinepool Passage LocationR

  

  

  
**Brinepool Passage** is a location that is accessible by breaking a wall of ice inside of a fallen tower that leads to [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md). It is a sub-location in the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md).

## Overview

Brinepool Passage leads to the [The Cold Forge](The_Cold_Forge.md) which has some relation to the [Dormant Splinter](Dormant_Splinter.md). The walls are covered in ice and there is a blue mist circulating throughout the cave.

Ice spikes will fall from the ceiling at many parts of this location. There are many falling/disappearing platforms as you travel through this area that drop into the void. There is many not collideable ice chunks, it is advised to not trust any ground in this area.

## Notes

-   You **need** a lot of agility or mobility mantras to get through this area.
-   Monsters can spawn here at extremely uncommon rate.

## Trivia

-   Initially, the location was called Sub-Zero Passage before being changed for unknown reason.
