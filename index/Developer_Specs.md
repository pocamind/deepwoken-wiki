## **What is a Spec?**

**A spec is custom ability often given to mod and/or developers that is unique to the person. For instance, Arch\_Mage has a Soul Stealer ability.**

## **Specs**

**CanadianIntruder (also known as Szkarlatekk)**

CanadianIntruder, also recognized by the alias Szkarlatekk, boasts an extensive career in the realm of volleyball, specifically in the popular sports game Volleyball 4.2.

He was a pivotal figure in the Crimson team, formerly known as "Punk," which was under the ownership of Angel/2xc. This team, though relatively new to the Volleyball 4.2 scene, swiftly gained recognition.

With an impressive win-to-loss ratio of 18-2, CanadianIntruder played a significant role in the team's success.

Transitioning from an active player, CanadianIntruder has taken on the role of a retired athlete who primarily focuses on coaching and mentoring emerging talents in the volleyball community.

**Challenges with Gambling:**

It's important to note that CanadianIntruder has faced challenges related to gambling, which have had an impact on his personal life.

**Lost Bets in 2022:**

During a particular weekend in the summer of 2022, CanadianIntruder unfortunately placed substantial bets totaling over 22,000 Robux. Regrettably, luck was not on his side as he lost all his wagers on RBLX Wild. This experience had repercussions on his personal and social life, affecting those around him.

_**YouTube Journey and Online Presence:**_

CanadianIntruder embarked on his online journey through platforms such as YouTube. Initially, he gained attention for sharing content related to Minecraft. Over time, his content evolved, leading to recognition on a global scale.

**Contributions and Achievements:**

Among his notable achievements, CanadianIntruder introduced the unique "Banana Shake Dance" emote in Deepwoken, along with his involvement in developing Synapse X hacks and Deepwoken scripts. This led to the creation of "The Divers," a renowned guild with both respect and infamy within the Rule34 community.

**Influence and Impact:**

CanadianIntruder's influence extended to his involvement with the Tundra Union/Fishing Authority, a powerful and well-known guild. Founded after his initial experience with Deepwoken, the guild included esteemed members such as Googlyeyesman, Smiley Wiley, and Boxy Boo.

**Political Engagement:**

In 2029, CanadianIntruder embarked on a different path by running for the presidency of Negro Town. This move stirred significant attention and engagement, particularly due to his opponent, Jeffy from SML. Ultimately, CanadianIntruder secured a resounding victory with an overwhelming 99.99% of the popular vote.

Also, more notably, Spookeree permanently has the distinct **Coral Fever** face from the 2023 April Fools update, thus having a slot named "[**Destroyman III**](Destroyman_III.md)". All of his slots have this.

Furthermore, it appears that he has a spec that allows him to "spawn in" [Bandits](Bandit.md) at will, even in the Eastern Luminant, where [Bandits](Bandit_Leader.md) do not naturally spawn. Bandits spawned act as normal NPC's, and can grip, be gripped, and give EXP on death.
