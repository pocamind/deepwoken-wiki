## Description

The Pathfinder Elite is an animated [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The [Shadow Travel](Talents.md#shadow-travel) Talent allows you to teleport to your cursor's location at the cost of health and Ether. The [Omen](Talents.md#omen) Talent allows you to use Shadow Travel with no windup, however your Shadow Travel will always drain your health regardless of the teleport distance.
