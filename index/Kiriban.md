## Description

Vigil Kiriban was a friend of [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) and the father of [Suyuan](Suyuan.md). He was known to be extremely skilled but became estranged from his son until his death, resulting in Suyuan's gaining an obsession with ghosts.[\[1\]](#cite-note-s-1)

Due to Kiriban's status, Suyuan has unrestricted access to the library found within the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-s-1)
