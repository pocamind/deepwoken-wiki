# The Furnace

Template:Location2

**The Furnace** is a Location in _Deepwoken_. It is under the [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) Castle.

## Description

**The Furnace** is the basement of the summer castle. It contains a arena for players to fight each other freely without having to worry about reputation.

## Points of Interest

Jarvis

### Jarvis

[Jarvis](Jarvis.md) is a unique NPC in the Furnace hall. He will allow you to smelt two items into one for you, transferring the stats of one item to another. He is located next to the Brawling Arena.

### Mag

[Mag](Mag.md) will encase bullets in [Ore](Ores.md) to apply unique effects onto them.

#### _Brawling Arena_

-   [Dockson](Dockson.md) allows players to duel in a more controlled fashion above the steaming vents below the Summer Castle.
-   Talking to him will queue you for a duel until another player also queues for a 1v1, in which a battle will be initiated in the said arena.
