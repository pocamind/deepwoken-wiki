## Description

**Pumpkin Pie** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 50% Stomach and 20% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Heal buff, affecting 235% of the Stomach bar. The buff increases health regeneration by 30%.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Egg](Egg.md), 1 [Gathered Wheat](Gathered_Wheat.md), and 2 [Pumpkins](Pumpkin.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
