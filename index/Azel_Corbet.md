## Description

**Azel Corbet** was an [apprentice diver](The_Divers.md) participating in the expedition to the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md) led by [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md). He is the author of the [first Diver Journal](Diver_Journal,_I.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-journal-1)

An aspiring diver apprentice, who met his demise during the Divers' expedition to the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). After being betrayed by [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) he hid in the [Nest Corridor](Nest_Corridor.md) and wrote his last thoughts in his journal before being eaten alive by [bounders](Bounder.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-journal-1)

## Trivia

-   He is not the same person as the [Apprentice Hero](<Mauled_Diver_Corpse_(Apprentice_Hero).md>), as the latter one met his fate from the hands of the parasites, whilst Azel was eaten by the [bounders](Bounder.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-journal-1)[\[3\]](#cite-note-journal-iii-3)
