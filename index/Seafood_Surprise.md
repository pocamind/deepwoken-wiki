## Description

**Seafood Surprise** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Meat**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Vegetarian](Boons_and_Flaws.md#vegetarian) Flaw.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 15% Stomach and 5% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   Any combination of 3, with at least 1 [Urchin](Urchin.md), [Scallop](Scallop.md), [Squid](Squid.md), [Fish Meat](Fish_Meat.md), or [Crustacean Meat](Crustacean_Meat.md), cooked together at a campfire.

## Trivia

-   When held out, stench lines flow out the food.
