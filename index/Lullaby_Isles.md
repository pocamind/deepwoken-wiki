# Lullaby Isles

**Content is not present in-game**  
The subject of this page is mentioned in-game, but is not tangibly implemented into the game.  
E.G. a character mentioned in a book doesn't exist as an interactible NPC.

  

## Description

**The Lullaby Isles** are a tropical archipelago in the [Central Luminant](Luminants.md#central-luminant) and the homeland of [Chrysids](Chrysid.md) with multiple settlements dotted across the region. The archipelago is circulated by a dense mountain range, known as [the Stoneseye](Lore.md#the-stoneseye-range).[\[1\]](#cite-note-distant-light-1)

The Chrysids within speak in a dialect native to the Isles themselves, known as the Moonlit Dialect, which is rarely seen outside its walls.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)

The Lullaby Isles are the territory of [The Moonlit Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md), which means that they are controlled by the [Council of the Moonlit Faith](Council_of_the_Moonlit_Faith.md), which is given orders and overseen by [The Lady Confessor](The_Lady_Confessor.md). If someone's leave isn't sanctioned by [The Moonlit Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md), they aren't allowed back in — they are seen as traitors and are most likely sacrificed by burning alive.[\[3\]](#cite-note-marro-3)[\[4\]](#cite-note-pardoner-4)

[The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) would have conquered the Isles, but finds it a difficult target due to its geographical location. As [the Stoneseye Range](Lore.md#the-stoneseye-range) is incredibly insurmountable to scale, the archipelago has the high-ground advantage, with the caverns being the only way to bypass the mountain range, which are most likely reinforced as well.[\[1\]](#cite-note-distant-light-1)

## Notable Figures

[The Lady Confessor](The_Lady_Confessor.md): The mysterious leader of the Lullaby Isles, where her word is law. Not much is known externally of her location, or condition, but interactions with scholars visiting the [East](Luminants.md#etrean-and-eastern-luminants) suggest a sense of great reverence and sincerity towards her decrees.[\[4\]](#cite-note-pardoner-4)

The Acolyte / [Thal'assis Linnaes](<Thal'assis_Linnaes.md>): The Acolyte is a lead ambassador for the Lullaby Isles, leading groups of scholars to and from the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) in order to exchange knowledge, updates and information.[\[5\]](#cite-note-missives-5)

The Vox: A role in the Lullaby Isles whose position within the totem pole remains widely unknown.[\[6\]](#cite-note-6)

[Council of the Moonlit Faith](Council_of_the_Moonlit_Faith.md): A council residing within the city of Echelon. The highest form of government within the Lullaby Isles, consisting of a handful of the philosophical, theological, and academic members of the Lullaby Isles. Their purpose is to put [The Lady Confessor](The_Lady_Confessor.md)'s decrees into action and ensure the operations within the Echelon and wider archipelago run smoothly.[\[5\]](#cite-note-missives-5)

[Prophet Pariah](Prophet_Pariah.md): A short lived cult leader, driven by his mad beliefs that he would someday find the heavens throughout the Voidsea.
