## Description

A [flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) miniboss found in Firfire, located in a cave behind 3 orange doors. These doors are located near the Kyrsgarde that asks you to sit with him. It should be below the cliff.

The requirements are to have a [resonance](Resonance.md), possess the Talent [Pleeksty's Will](Talent_Quests.md), and to have already [unbounded Flamecharm](Attributes_Unbound.md). You must also do it without a Light Hook.

Upon meeting the requirements you'll initiate a fight with him and upon winning you'll be rewarded the [Pleetsky's Inferno](<Pleeksty's_Inferno.md>).

Be careful fighting him, as most of his moves' hitboxes cover most of the cramped room, and he can also glitch you into the wall which could cause your death, so make sure to get a mantra with a wayward gem on it just incase.
