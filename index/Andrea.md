## Description

Andrea is an adret woman scared for her life, as she tries to decide between facing The trial or remaining in [Celtor City](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).

## Location

Andrea is located inside [Celtor City](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md), sitting outside the gates of the elevator that brings you up the trial.
