## Description

Andrea is an [Adret](Adret.md) NPC.

Some time prior to 1233 CE,[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) Andrea died on the [Surface](Surface.md) and found herself in the [Cathedral of the Interstice](Cathedral_of_the_Interstice.md).

Now the Adretian woman is scared for her life, as she tries to decide between facing the Trial or remaining in [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).

## Location

Andrea is located inside [Cathedral of the Interstice](Cathedral_of_the_Interstice.md), sitting on the floor to the opposite side from the [Shrine of Courage](Shrine_of_Courage.md).
