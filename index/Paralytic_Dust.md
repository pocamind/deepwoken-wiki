# Paralytic Dust

| Paralytic Dust | |
|---|---|
| effect | On hit, Ragdolls target for 5 seconds and applies daze and blindness for ~10 seconds |
| appearance | Unknown |

“ Conjure sensory particles that drop targets into instant, paralytic slumber. ”

― Paralytic Dust

  

## Description

Paralytic Dust is a [Resonance](Resonance.md) that players can obtain and is common to roll.

## Appearance

Unknown

## Usage

Pressing C will activate the **Paralytic Dust** Resonance.

## Resonance Point Usage

-   Sleeping variant:
    -   throw black dust into the air in an aoe around you, ragdolls you and anyone else nearby for 5 seconds and heals them for 5%.
-   Throw varaint:
    -   Throw white dust in front of you ragdolling anyone hit for 5 seconds and dazes for 10 seconds

## Trivia

-   Said to have been manufactored by Lena Kamali
