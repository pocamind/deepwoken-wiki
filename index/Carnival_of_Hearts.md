# Carnival of Hearts

File:Placeholder thing for Carnival of Hearts.png

You can get to the arena for Carnival of Hearts by talking to Aelita that is on the same island as Mortus.

First round:

Someone gets a flaming pomar and if the 30 seconds runout then the last one to have it loses.

  
Second Round:

Everyone is given an infinite supply of Carnival Bombs, and must throw them at each other. Time limit is 2 minutes.

  
Third Round:

Aelita and her brother spawn into the arena and fire large beams at the participants.
