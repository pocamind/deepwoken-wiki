# A Conscious Intersection

Is a small sub-area only accessed after completing Surge's quest of bringing him 1 Dark Feather.

After completing the Quest Surge starts acting weird and when trying to return to the widow's den you instead find yourself in a hellish glowing nightmare with the Nestmind at the end.
