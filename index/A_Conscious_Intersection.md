# A Conscious Intersection

Is a small sub-area only accessed after completing [Surge's](Surge.md) quest of bringing him 1 [Dark Feather](Dark_Feather.md).

After completing the Quest Surge starts acting weird and when trying to return to the [Widow's](Deep_Widow.md) den you instead find yourself in a hellish glowing nightmare with [The Nestmind](The_Nestmind.md) at the end.
