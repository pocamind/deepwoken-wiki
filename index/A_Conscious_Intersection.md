# A Conscious Intersection

LocationL A Conscious Intersection LocationR

TERRITORY OF

I am one of many.

  

## Description

A Conscious Intersection is a small sub-area only accessed after completing [Surge's](Surge.md) quest of bringing him 1 [Dark Feather](Dark_Feather.md).

After completing the Quest, Surge starts acting weird, and when trying to return to the [Widow's](Deep_Widow.md) Den, you instead find yourself in a hellish glowing nightmare with [The Nestmind](The_Nestmind.md) at the end. This area is vital to obtaining the [Visionshaper](Oath%253A_Visionshaper.md) Oath.

It is theorized that this area is not actually inside of [The Depths](The_Depths.md), and may be somewhere inbetween, hence the "Intersection" part. As for [The Nestmind](The_Nestmind.md), this is probably not it's true form, much like how the [Shrine of Yun'Shul](<Shrine_of_Yun'Shul,_Keeper_of_Hearts.md>) isn't actually Yun’Shul, just a device to communicate safely with them.

Falling into the void takes you to [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md).
