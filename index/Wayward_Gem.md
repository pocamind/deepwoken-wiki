## Description

Wayward Gem is a black diamond-shaped [Deep Gem](Deep_Gems.md).

## Effect

Upon landing a Mantra with a Wayward Gem equipped, you will be teleported to your opponent after a 1 second delay. This effect has an 8 second cooldown.

### Notes

-   This Gem is best used on high knockback Mantras or Mantras that displace either your own or your opponent's location. This Mantra is also a great choice for ranged Mantras.
-   Wayward Gem cannot be procced on [Revenge](Attunement-less.md#revenge).

## Crafting

This Gem can also be used to craft a variety of items.

-   [Outfits](Outfits.md): [Heretic's Memento](<Heretic's_Memento.md>), [Inquisition Light](Inquisition_Light.md), and [Justicar's Armor](<Justicar's_Armor.md>).
