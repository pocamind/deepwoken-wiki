## Description

**Inheritor of the Gale** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. They start with the Whistleguard talent, able to use it without a [Whistling Periapt](Whistling_Periapt.md). They can be obtained by defeating the Shogun Specialist and Etrean Apostle NPCS, whose jobs can be found together at the Isle of Vigils job board and the Indigo Citadel job board. Have to be power 15 to take the jobs.
