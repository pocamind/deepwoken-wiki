## Description

Security is a [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC. They are a gatekeeping guard who challenges the player to prove their toughness in [The Pit](The_Pit.md).

## Location

Security is located inside of [The 72 Seasons](The_72_Seasons.md). They can be found standing right outside of [The Pit](The_Pit.md).
