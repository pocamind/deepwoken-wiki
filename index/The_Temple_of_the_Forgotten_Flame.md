# The Temple of the Forgotten Flame

## Description

Template:Areainfobox

Neero inside the temple

A mysterious structure high above the beach in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md). To enter you must traverse The Courtyard or walk along the walls and drop down in the back gardens. The temple is mainly safe, except for the gardens (where regular spawning occurs) and standing infront of the entrance to The Courtyard (where the player can be seen by a watcher). Inside the building, there are mysterious glyphs called ??? and a center platform where Neero stands (it is safe to stand near him). The floor is litered with ruined chandelers and damaged objects and appears to have similar architecture and furnishing to buildings in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). The building also has many purple flames similar to the fires in [The Birdcage](The_Birdcage.md).

## Neero (NPC)

The courtyard behind the temple

Neero is an NPC that tells the player that they have been searching the temple for the 'Forge of Sin', saying that 'someone or something seems to have dragged it away'. He deduces that the object he is looking for was stolen long ago and guesses that it will stolen the last time the island drifted into the Luminants. If the player continues the conversation, he will explain that the islands drift 'in and out of the Voidsea over time', also saying that 'Pathfinders have been looking for Erisia for decades'. He will reveal to the player that the golems appear to be a security system (as they can determine man from monster) that are powered by mysterious magic, not being powered by thundercall or lifeweaver tech as expected. He is intriged by the purple flame, as it appears to be releated to the reason as to why the golems attack players. He later explains to the player his theories that the golems are capturing players (note that the people speaking to you when escape the depths refer to it as a cage) and that someone is controlling the golems and ordering them to send players to the depths (and that they are related to the purple flames found throughout the island).
