# The Temple of the Forgotten Flame

## Description

Template:AreainfoboxA mysterious structure high above the beach in [Upper](Upper_Erisia.md) [Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md). To enter you must traverse The Courtyard or walk along the walls and drop down in the back gardens. The temple is mainly safe, except for the gardens (where regular spawning occurs) and standing infront of the entrance to The Courtyard (where the player can be seen by a watcher). Inside the building, there are mysterious glyphs called ??? and a center platform where Neero stands (it is safe to stand near him). The floor is litered with ruined chandelers and damaged objects and appears to have similar architecture and furnishing to buildings in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). The building also has many purple flames similar to the fires in [The Birdcage](The_Birdcage.md).

## Mysterious Glyphs (???)

Mysterious Glyphs are interactable writings in the Temple Of The Forgotten Flame.

If the player attempts to read the glyphs without a blindfold and five certain willpower talents, it will say '\*The writing on the rock is inscrutable. Whenever your eyes begin to focus on the glyphs, they seem to switch positions. Somehow, it feels like your vision is failing you.\*'.

If you do have the requirements, you will be able to obtain the [Blindseer Oath](Oaths.md). This makes you able to see in a blindfold and gaining a glyph on your blindfold, also giving a lot of other buffs.
