## Description

The Pastry Paster is a Club with a very odd appearance; a rolling pin which is supposed to be in the kitchen, until you wielded it as a deadly weapon.

The Pastry Paster can be upgraded with the [Chef's Toque](<Chef's_Toque.md>) equipment, granting it a 35% chance to steal 1.95 \* ln(D) + 1.075 hunger and thirst from your opponent on weapon hits, where D = scaled damage dealt (calculator [here](https://www.desmos.com/calculator/yktgme7xnc)). This has a 3 second cooldown per proc attempt, going on cooldown even if it didn't proc. This stacks additively with [Gluttony](Gluttony.md).

## Critical

Wind up your rolling pin and strike your target, applying [Daze](Status_Effects.md#dazed) and dealing 22 posture damage if blocked.

This attack has a scaled damage multiplier of 1.25x.

This critical has a cooldown of **5 seconds**.

This critical has the M1 tag, allowing it to proc M1-exclusive [Talents](Talents.md) and [Enchantment](Enchantments.md) effects.
