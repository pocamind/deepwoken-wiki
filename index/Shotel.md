# Shotel

"A blade from Markor prized by warriors for its abillity to effortlessly slip past the guards of foes"

In game it can be equipped when the requirements are achieved those being:

Medium Weapon: 45

Stats:

Damage: 22

Chip damage: 7%

Weight: 2

Range: 8

Swing speed: 0.73x

## Player Notes

-   Based on its description, it may partly ignore blocking, dealing reduced damage or increased poise damage or completely ignore shields as seen in Dark Souls.
