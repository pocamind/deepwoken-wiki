# Sharko Vinyl Figure

## Description

The **Sharko Vinyl Figure** was an item released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration, classified under the "Vinyl Figure" category. Its campaign ran from November 28th, 2024, to December 30th, 2024, before selling out. The vinyl figure measured 8.5 cm in height, 8.0 cm in width, and 7.5 cm in depth. It was made from coloured PVC and finished with oil paint, water-based paint, and varnish.

### Official Description

"Virtual, plush, and now vinyl? This Megalodaunt doesn't know when to stop crossing mediums. The fourth wall might as well just be a door at this point.  
  
Though less puntable (ow) than his other iterations, in vinyl form he's sure to lurk mischievously within any collection.  
  
As always, credit to sycne for the original sticker design!"

## Trivia

-   A total of 500 Sharko Vinyl Figures were sold, and the campaign ended at just 100% of its funding goal.
