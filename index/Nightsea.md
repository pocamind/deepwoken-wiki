# Nightsea

**Content is not present in-game**  
The subject of this page is mentioned in-game, but is not tangibly implemented into the game.  
E.G. a character mentioned in a book doesn't exist as an interactible NPC.

## Description

**The Nightsea** is the sea of [the Heavens](The_Heavens.md). It is inky black, studded with twinkling points of light, known as [Stars](Stars.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1)

When night falls and the [Suncross](Suncross.md) retreats into [the Voidfog](Voidsea.md), the Nightsea with the [Stars](Stars.md) become visible from the Surface below, lighted by the Moonseye.[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1)

## Contents

[Celestial Cities](The_Heavens.md#celestial-cities): Cities of [Lightborn](Lightborn.md), which were gifted to them by the [Celestials](Celestial.md) before the Heavensfall.

[Stars](Stars.md): Points of light, floating in the Nightsea.[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1) Currently, only two major stars are known by names: [the Moonseye](Moonseye.md) and [the Suncross](Suncross.md).
