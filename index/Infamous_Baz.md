## Description

Infamous Baz is Felinor NPC spotted in the back corner of [The King's Garden](<The_King's_Garden.md>) in Etris. He's located in a back corner of the garden to the right of the palace.

## Trivia

-   According to Arch\_Mage, if you keep interacting with him, there is a chance you will get a legendary hidden Felinor-like race. Whether or not he is actually being serious is unclear but the chances of the statement being true are highly unlikely.
-   He is named after infamousbaz, a friend of Arch\_Mage.
-   Canonical winner of the grand griddy.
