“ Hah! Not a penny on you, poor sap. Come back when you're a little, hmm, richer? ”

― Nerin

## Description

Nerin is the NPC who accepts a bribe for [Etrea](Etrea.md); you can pay him up to 1,200 to restore your reputation to neutral.

## Location

Nerin is located at a house near the [Barber](Barber.md) and [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md).
