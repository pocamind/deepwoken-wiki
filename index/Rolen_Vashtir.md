## Description

Rolen Vashtir, first of his name, was the final King of [Etrea](Etrea.md), who reigned through the early 6th century CE.[\[1\]](#cite-note-andy-1)

Rolen later disappeared during an expedition into the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md).

### Legacy

Despite having vanished, many in Etrea believed he would still return as far as 600 years[\[2\]](#cite-note-date-2) after his disappearance.[\[1\]](#cite-note-andy-1) He is also the subject of a prayer meant to ward off evil.[\[3\]](#cite-note-3)
