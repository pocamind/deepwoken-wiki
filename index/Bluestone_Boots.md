## Description

The Bluestone Boots are an **unenchantable**[\[1\]](#cite-note-unenchantable-1) "legs" [Equipment](Equipment.md), and are a part of the Bluestone set. The equipment consists of a pair of black and blue plated boots.

The [I'm Blue](Talents.md#im-blue) Talent increases the effectiveness of your [Blue Gems](Blue_Gem.md) by 40%. This is additive to Blue Gem's innate effect, lowering the base Ether cost of Mantras by 90% instead of 50% and the cost of modifiers by 65% instead of 25%.

## Set Talent

When wearing the full equipment set, you will gain the Set Talent.

Set Talent: [Etherguard](Talents.md#etherguard) - [Warped Blue Gems](Warped_Blue_Gem.md) now provide a bonus effect when landed using a mantra, Etherguard. This effect gives extra resistance to mantras based on how much ether they cost to cast.

Etherguard applies the [Etherguard](Status_Effects.md#etherguard) status effect to yourself for 15 seconds. While you have this status effect, Mantras will deal reduced damage to you based on their Ether cost.

## Obtainment

This item is crafted using the following recipe:

1.  1 [Bluesteel Boots](Bluesteel_Boots.md) + 1 [Warped Blue Gem](Warped_Blue_Gem.md).

1.  [↑](#cite-ref-unenchantable-1-0) This equipment item cannot be [enchanted](Enchantments.md) via [Enchant Grease](Enchant_Grease.md), but there is an extremely small chance that it may be enchanted with a non-exclusive Equipment Enchantment upon being crafted.
