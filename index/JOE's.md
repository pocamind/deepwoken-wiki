## Description

JOE's is an **unobtainable** enchantment with unknown properties.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to a glowing white with cackling black lightning emanating from it.

## Effect

JOE's changes your weapon's critical. On use, plant your weapon in the ground, creating a Stasis Sphere around yourself for 5 seconds. Anyone in the Stasis Sphere, excluding the caster, will not be able to act for the duration of the Sphere. Additionally, you will not be able to see any actions being performed inside of the Stasis Sphere.

Upon being blockbroken, a unique visual effect will play and the guardbreak stun will be negated.
