# Beeswax

Beeswax on a tree

Beeswax in hand

**Locations**

Beeswax can be found hanging off of large trees in Vigil and Etrea, requiring you to climb to harvest them. They usually spawn close to Pomar fruits. Unlike the held item, Beeswax appears as a beehive.

**Usage**

Beeswax is only used for crafting a Canteen, which is achievable by pairing it with a Calabash at a crafting table.

It also serves as an ingredient in potions, giving regeneration effects.
