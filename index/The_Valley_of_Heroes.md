# The Valley of Heroes

Template:Location2

**Valley of Heroes** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark in [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) found in the [Etrean Sea](The_Etrean_Sea.md).

## Overview

Valley of Heroes resembles an icy valley littered with frozen asteroids and statues of forgotten figures. The valley is located above [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md).

## Events

When the player reaches the center of the valley, one of two events will happen depending on whether it is winter or not:

-   If it isn't [winter](Seasons.md), then [Aelita](Aelita.md) will spawn and play a game with the player.
-   If it is winter, white meteorites will start falling from the sky and when they land, 6 [Blizzard Knights](Stone_Knight.md#blizzard-knight) will spawn. Defeating them will give you a chest with several [Hero blades](Hero_Blades.md).

## Trivia/Notes

-   At the end of the valley, there will be a white portal teleporting to a high cliff near [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md).
-   if the player is in danger or if he activated but did not complete the event, the portal will throw him away without letting him teleport away.
-   You can avoid events by sticking to the left side of the wall.
