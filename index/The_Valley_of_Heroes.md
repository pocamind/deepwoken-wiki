# The Valley of Heroes

Template:Location2

**Valley of Heroes** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark in [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) found in [The Etrean Sea](The_Etrean_Sea.md).

## Overview

Valley of Heroes resembles an icy valley littered with frozen asteroids and statues of forgotten figures. The valley is located above [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md).

## Events

When the player reaches the center of the valley, one of two events will happen depending on whether it is winter or not:

-   If the current season is not [Winter](Seasons.md#winter), then [Aelita](Aelita.md) will spawn and play a game with the player.
-   If it is Winter, white meteorites will start falling from the sky carrying six [Blizzard Knights](Stone_Knight.md#blizzard-knight). Defeating them will spawn a chest with several [Hero Blades](Hero_Blades.md).

## Trivia

-   At the end of the valley, there is a white portal teleporting to a high cliff behind the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md) into the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md).
-   It is possible to avoid Aelita's minigame by sticking to the left side of the valley.
