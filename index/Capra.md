# Capra

Template:Races-Main

Capra is a [Race](Aspects.md) in Deepwoken

## Etymology

"Capra" is the scientific name of the genus of mammals called "goats". It's also a word derived from Latin.

## Description

-   Chance of obtaining race: **2.0%**  
    

### **Lore**

They are keepers of ancient secrets who travel the seas sharing knowledge with those who need it.

### **Appearance**

The Caprae have 3 skin color variants: Blue, green, and red. Each variant provides unique passive bonuses, which are listed down below. All Caprae don goat-shaped masks, revealing only their eyes, horizontally rectangular pupils (similar to real world goats) as well as yellow irises. Regardless of variation, their hair color will always be a medium jungle green,

### Racial Abilities

Blue variant - _**Mark of Jurik**_ | Buffs the sanity of one person they choose around them as long as they are at a campfire. |

Green variant - _**Mark of Nemit**_ | Buffs the food of one person they choose around them as long as they are at a campfire. |

Red variant - _**Mark of Ku**_ | Buffs the "rest" (hp regen) of one person they choose around them as long as they are at a campfire. |

### Progression

Upon reaching Power 10 you gain the ability to use the Mark of Jurik, Nemit, or Ku outside of campfires.

### Strengths

-   +2 Intelligence

-   +2 Willpower

### Weaknesses

This isn't a good race to play as for solo progression, as its racial abilities solely apply to other players.

### Notes/Trivia

An objectively goated race.

Each of the marks appear to originate from a different real world source, most closely in definition with Old Greek, and varying regions of Euro-Asia.

This race is likely a reference to the Capra Demon, a boss in Dark Souls.
