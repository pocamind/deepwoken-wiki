# Enchantments

_"I always wanted to see what the LGBTQ enchant did to people!"_  

## Description

Enchants are basically upgrades/power-ups for your weapon. There are 3 variants of Enchants. First, there is your normal Enchant. Second, there are Curses, which is basically stronger enchants but there are downsides to it. Third, there are Blessings, which are basically Curses but without the downside.

## Enchants

### Curse of the Umbral Knight

-   Pros: When landing an M1 on a monster or person, it summons a shadow clone of yourself behind the enemy and does the exact replica of the M1.
-   Cons: Unknown.

### Curse of the bloodthirsty

-   Pros: Deals more M1 dmg (exact amount unknown).
-   Cons: When you miss a M1, you will take damage (dodging and parrying doesn't count).

### Hero Blessing

-   Pros: 3x M1 damage at full HP and has M1 hyper armor.
-   Cons: NONE.

### Combustion

-   Pros: Every time a M1 connects, your enemy explodes.
-   Cons: Unknown.

### Bungee Gum

-   Pros: Every M1 connection will create a purple string that connects both you and the enemy and pulls the victim towards you.
-   Cons: Unknown.

### Obfuscation

-   Pros: The weapon will be invisble, only to be cloaked by particles of white air. Weapon M1 is increased
-   Cons: It's okay on swords.

### Sear

-   Pros: Drains a person's thirst every M1 Connection.
-   Cons: Unknown.

### Providence of Storms

-   Pros: Increases M1 speed drastically. Any parry damage that the other person inflicts will be inflicted back to them. No movement speed penalty.
-   Cons: NONE.
