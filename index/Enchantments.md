# Enchantments

File:Curse of the umbral knight in Infernasu's video.jpg

**Curse of the umbral knight in Infernasu's video**

Enchants are basically upgrades/power-ups for your weapon. There are 3 variants of Enchants. First, there is your normal Enchant. Second, there are Curses, which is basically stronger enchants but there are downsides to it. Third, there are Blessings, which are basically Curses but without the downside. Here is a list of known Enchants.

  
\- Curse of the umbral knight. Pro: when you manage to hit someone, you will summon a shadow of yourself behind the enemy, and the shadow will strike them from behind (can be parried). Con: unknown.

  
\- Curse of the bloodthirsty. Pro: deals more m1 dmg (exact amount unknown). Con: when you miss a m1, you will take dmg (dodging and parrying doesn't count).

  
\- Hero blessing. Pro: 3X m1 dmg. Con: NONE.
