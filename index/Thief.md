# Thief

**Thieves** are a [humanoid enemy](Enemies.md) in _Deepwoken_.

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

A Thief (Also known as a bandit assassin) is an enemy NPC that can be found in [Erisia](Erisia.md) spawning in groups of three. Unlike all other enemies, they don't kill their victims, but steal their money and disappear.

A Thief wears a [Headband](Outfits.md) and a [Shadow Hunter](Shadow_Hunter.md) outfit.

## Moveset

Their arsenal only consists of a [Stiletto](Stiletto.md):

| Name | Description | Parryable? | Blockable? | Danger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Assault | Firmly swings blade at opponent and continuing to combo them until knocked. | ◯ | ◯ | (★☆☆) |
| Critical | Unleash a quick flurry attack on opponent. | ◯ | ✗ | (★★☆) |

### Finisher

-   Smoke Bomb - Releases a smoke bomb after mugging the player before disappearing.

## Tips

-   Be cautious of your blood level during a fight with thieves as daggers can drain your blood very quickly.

## Trivia

-   Despite stealing your money, they still half grip the player, but backing off before the animation finishes.
