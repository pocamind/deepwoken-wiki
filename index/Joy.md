# Joy

Joy is used for currency in the [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md) event.

Joy can be obtained by either:

-   Infecting people with the Curse of the Gourdskipper (1 Joy)
-   Surviving until wave 8 in the [Trial of the Gourdskipper](<Widow's_Wail_Cemetery.md>) trial (15 Joy)
-   Spooking NPCs with the [Hallowtide Mask](Hallowtide_Mask.md) (1 Joy)

You can check how much Joy you have by either talking to [Lysander Spellhardt](Lysander_Spellhardt.md) or [Glaive Spellhardt](Glaive_Spellhardt.md).
