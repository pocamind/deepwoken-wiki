## Description

**Trig** is a [Gremor](Gremor.md) that wears a the [Summer Company Uniform](Summer_Company_Uniform.md). He gives you the [Armor Piercing quest](Quests.md) for gun Talents.

## Location

Trig is located in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md), at the entrance of the Guildhall/Inn. His quest sends you to find [Asius](Asius.md), a merchant who hasn't been paying his dues to the Summer Company. More information can be found about the quest in the [Summer Isle](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Summer_Isle#Trig_%5BQUEST%5D) page.

## Quest Details

Completing his quest rewards you with ability to roll the [Armor Piercing](Talents.md) talent and positive [Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md) reputation.

1.  To start his quest you'll have to just finish his dialogue, where he will ask you to find [Asius](Asius.md).
2.  Asius can be found behind the Inn if you follow the path leading away from the Inn, in an alcove facing the hive.
3.  After getting the papers from him, go back to Trig to end the quest.

Trying to help Asius and saying the first dialogue option "I'll see what I can do." instead of collecting the papers, then coming back to Trig, will cause him to send 3 Summer Company guards after you and give you negative Summer Company reputation.
