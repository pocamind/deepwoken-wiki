# Gale Kata

File:Gale Kata.png

The Gale Kata in the Mantra shop at Etris.

## **Description**

_"A white orb that whispers the ways of the Wind."_

> _**Train your Galebreathing through stances practiced by the Legion.**_

Used to train the Galebreath attunement. It can be used 200 times. Upon activation, the character punches the air as if fighting the air.

_Purchased at any Mantra Shop - **25 [Notes](Notes.md)**_

## Trivia

-   Every attunement has a training item.
-   Can break if overused.
