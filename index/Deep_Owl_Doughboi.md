# Deep Owl Doughboi

## Description

The **Deep Owl Doughboi** was a plush released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration. Classified under the "Doughboi" category, it featured the [Deep Owl](Deep_Owl.md) mob. Its campaign ran from October 17th, 2025, to November 8th, 2025, before selling out. The plush measured 35.0 cm in height and was made from spandex plush fabric with 3D cotton down filling.

### Official Description

"What might possess you to let this thing nest in your home? Curiosity, perhaps. Or maybe it's that the size of it lets you seat it at the table and pretend it's not a nightmare-thing feeding on your thoughts?  
  
We're pretty sure this is just a soft toy, but it might be wise not to turn your back on it. Just in case."

## Trivia

-   A total of 842 Deep Owl Doughboi plushes were sold, and the campaign ended at 421% of its funding goal.
