# Temple Sparring Hall (Chime)

**[Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) Exclusive**

LocationL Temple Sparring Hall LocationR

A solemn sanctuary carved in stone, where warriors hone the dancing steps of war.

  

**Temple Sparring Hall** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the 1v1 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**Temple Sparring Hall** is an enormous hall covered with mostly rock, made in a Japanese-like style, surrounded by stone statues and with the lanterns on the roof.
