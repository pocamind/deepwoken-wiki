# Egg

"You don't have to tell me what happened, you just need to eat these."

Eggs are a food item in Deepwoken. They can be used to make Cooked Eggs, Omelettes, [Mushroom Omelettes](Mushroom_Omelette.md), Noodles, Mushroom Egg Soup, Fish Omelettes, and Cake.
