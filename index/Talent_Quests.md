Talent quests are talents which can only be obtained through completing the request of an NPC. They were added in the Verse 2 update.

If you had these talents before the update on a build you can still do the quest but you will be compensated with a hand of cards upon completion. (2 hands for the engage + critical attack quest)

There are 6 known talent quests present in the overworld:

-   Engage and Critical Attack

-   Aerial Assault

-   Thresher Scales

-   Treefelling Blow

-   Family Recipe

### Engage + Critical Attack

Engage and critical attack come from the same quest, given by an Etrean solider NPC in [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md), at the entrance to [The Garden Reservoir](The_Garden_Reservoir.md)

where they will state that they have lost their friends to a [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md). They will ask you to slay a [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) and when you return to him after killing one, he will award you with the talents Engage and Critical attack.

### Aerial Assault

Aerial Assault can be obtained from a Pathfinder in [Pathfinder's Respite](<Pathfinder's_Respite.md>)(same place as where you get the glider blueprint from [Alastir](Alastir.md)) and after talking to him, he will ask you to gaze into the purple flame in the Temple Of The Forgotten Flame, which is above the bandit camp in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md). Note that you have to go into the building to access the purple flames as the ones outside the temple do not allow you to. Once interacting with the flames, returning to him will get you Aerial Assault, which allows you to perform an advanced dodge mid-air allowing you to travel a bit in the direction of your cursor.

### Thresher Scales

Thresher scales is acquired from an NPC at the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), located at a dilapidated tower below the massive cliff that separates the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md) and [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) from the main Valley. Upon talking to him, he will ask you to kill a King thresher, which can spawn randomly anywhere in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) or at a specific spot in the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md). Once you kill one, returning to him will get you Thresher scales. Be aware that it is not known if you need the talent Bloodiron spirit to obtain this, but it is recommended that you have this before doing this quest. Thresher Scales replenishes your armor while resting at campfires.

### Treefelling Blow

At [Etris](Etris.md), there will be an NPC called Korin at a house near the [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md). You need the Attunement-less strength mantra Strong Left for this one, as that is how you fell trees with your hands. Upon talking to him you realise its about how you punch them and that you need to punch down 5 more trees to sufficiently research this. After you fell 5 trees, talking to him again will earn you the talent: Treefelling Blow, which allows you to gain wood from punching down trees.

### Family Recipe

Located at [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md) in the Eastern Luminant, there will be an NPC called Lucien located at a mine entrance on the island furthest away from the shipyard with the bigger windmill on it. After telling his sad story to you, he will ask you to clear out the Mineskippers. After clearing them and returning, he will give you the Family Recipe talent, which will give you the ability to craft Iron Bombs.

There are 2 more talent quests in the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md): Union hook and Ethiron's Gaze:

Union hook is acquired by talking to the Ignition union NPC in their base after you power on the generator. This allows you to use the metal rods with a hook at the bottom for transportation.

Ethiron's Gaze is obtained at Firfire after retrieving the spear in the Firfire Caverns and then talking to the NPC in the village. This lessens the fog permanently for you and opens up the blocked blue/purple doorways.
