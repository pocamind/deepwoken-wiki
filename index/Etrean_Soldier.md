# Etrean Soldier

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

An Etrean Soldier is an enemy NPC that can only be found in the following events:

-   Etrean Soldiers vs Knives of Eylis Pirates ([The Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md))
-   Etrean Soldiers vs Authority Footsoldiers ([Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md))
-   Invasion of Etris ([Etris](Etris.md))
-   Etrean Soldiers vs Hive Scouts ([Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md))

An Etrean Soldier wears a purple uniform with their race being an [Etrean](Etrean.md) with their gender varying. They can also appear younger or older than 40 years old.

An Etrean Soldier's arsenal only consists of a [Battleaxe](Battleaxe.md) or [Ritual Spear](Ritual_Spear.md):

## Talents

-   Speed Demon - Your attacks now inflict bleed while you have a speed boost.

-   Air Pressure - Dodging an attack or hitting an enemy's block will transform your next dash into a Gale Dash.
-   Suffocating Impact - When flourishing enemies into walls they are suffocated and winded for a short duration.
-   Stifled Jump - Enemies who jump while suffocated are dazed.

## Finisher

An Etrean Soldier executes it's opponent after knocking them:

| Name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Execute | \[[Battleaxe](Battleaxe.md)\] Pins down opponent before slicing their throat with their axe.
\[[Ritual Spear](Ritual_Spear.md)\] Pins down opponent before impaling them through the chest with their spear.

 |

## Trivia

-   They appear to act as naval officers, as they spawn on ships and are found in areas that are easier to reach via boat.
-   Almost all the times they will lose against a Authority Footsoldiers.
