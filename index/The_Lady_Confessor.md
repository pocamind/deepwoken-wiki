## Description

**The Lady Confessor** is the leader of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md), almost nothing is known of her from the outside. Her word is law in the lands of [The Moonlit Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md) and those, who dare oppose her politics are burned alive.[\[2\]](#cite-note-pardoner-2) She forbid leaving the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md), unless the leave is sanctioned by [the Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md)[\[3\]](#cite-note-marro-3), which includes exchange of information with [The Hive](The_Hive.md).

Despite her seemingly ruthless and violent politics, scholars of [the Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md) visiting the [East](Luminants.md#etrean-and-eastern-luminants) treat her actions with great sincerety and reverence.[\[4\]](#cite-note-glossary-4)[\[5\]](#cite-note-liviu-5) Although, speculatively, this is most likely a result of brainwashing.
