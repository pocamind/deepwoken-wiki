# Jack

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Jack was a Monster designed for Deepwoken that was scrapped.

He was a quadrupedal creature, almost frog like in appearance, that would stare down prey from afar and would sprint at them on all fours if he spotted them, much like today's [Bounder.](Bounder.md)

## Trivia

-   He was the second Monster ever designed for Deepwoken.
-   He is considered the prototype to the [Bounder](Bounder.md). The similarities can be seen in the way they attack, both chasing down their prey ruthlessly on all fours.
