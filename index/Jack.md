# Jack

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Jack was a Monster designed for Deepwoken that was scrapped.

He was a quadrupedal creature, almost frog like in appearance, that would stare down prey from afar and would sprint at them on all fours if he spotted them, much like how a [Bounder](Bounder.md) attacks, which makes sense, because this is its precursor.

## Trivia

-   He was the second Monster ever designed for Deepwoken.
-   The head is spiked, almost like a porcupine.
-   He is considered the prototype to the [Bounder](Bounder.md).
