# Minityrsa

_"Ocean water freezes against the stone, as if its trying to climb onto the shore."_

File:Mynistria Picture.png

  

## Description

An island that forever snows. Minityrsa is recommended for Experienced Adventurers that seek glory and power.

Minityrsa, located far from Etris, is recommended for Players that are Power Level 28 or above.

## Notes/Trivia

-   During the Winter, the entirity of Minityrsa is covered in snow. The mix of both sheets of white and permanent snowfall makes the island tedious to traverse.
-   Dark Mage Cultists cannot be assassinated.
-   Since its always snowfall in Minityrsa, its best to place Campfires beneath shelter.
-   To travel to Minityrsa, the easiest way to know where to go is to start from Etris.
    -   Go through the gates that reveals the 4 statues, then sail right to where the right statue is pointing. Until you see an Island with trees and land, that is Minityrsa.
-   Minityrsa can be called by a few names, one of them being "Mini" for short.
