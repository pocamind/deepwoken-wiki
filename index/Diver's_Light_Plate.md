# Diver's Light Plate

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Diver's Light Plate is an Equipment Item that can be obtained from the Hell Mode reward chests. It has innate stats of +10 health and +1 posture. You also get the talent Conquer Your Fears when worn. Diver's Light Plate is one of the few equipment pieces to give "legendary pips". Diver's Light Plate cannot be passed down to other characters.
