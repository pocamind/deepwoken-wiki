# Diver's Light Plate

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Diver's Light Plate is an Equipment Item that can be obtained from the Hell Mode reward chests. It has innate stats of +5 health and +1 posture. You also get the talent Conquer Your Fears when worn.
