# Diver's Light Plate

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Diver's Light Plate is an Equipment Item that is obtained if you successfully complete [Hell Mode](Hell_Mode.md), similar to [Ardour Manifestation](Ardour_Manifestation.md). Ringing the Hell Mode bell and successfully completing is guaranteed to grant you it. Not ringing the bell doesn't prevent you from obtaining it, but it is not guaranteed.

It has innate stats of +10 health and +1 posture. You also get the talent Conquer Your Fears when worn. Diver's Light Plate is one of the few equipment pieces to give natural Legendary pips, and has the highest stat total possible with 3 legendary pips (0 star), and 4 pips + 2 rares as a 3 star. Diver's Light Plate cannot be passed down to other characters, unless smelted.
