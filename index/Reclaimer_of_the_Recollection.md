## Description

**Reclaimer of the Recollection** is an NPC found in both [The Cerebral Spire](The_Cerebral_Spire.md) and [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md). He serves as the guide in [The Cerebral Spire](The_Cerebral_Spire.md) and a counterpart to [Self](Self.md) in the [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md). He tells the player about their objectives and basics of the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md), whilst in the [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md) he wipes the player's character, not being able to bring them back to life.

He's an Aspen [Gremor](Gremor.md) in stage 2 of aging (50-59 years old). He wears the [Light's Final Toll](<Light's_Final_Toll.md>) beads on his arm, the other parts of his outfit are unknown.
