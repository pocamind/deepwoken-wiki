## Description

**Omelette** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is also purchased from the [Travelling Merchant](Travelling_Merchant.md), sold at random.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 25% Stomach and 10% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   2 [Eggs](Egg.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
