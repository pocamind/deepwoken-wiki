# Duke Erisia's Manor

LocationL Lord Erisia's Manor LocationR

What was stolen from below lies within.

  

**Lord Erisia's Manor** is a location found in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark found in [The Garden Reservoir](The_Garden_Reservoir.md).

## Overview

Lord Erisia's Manor is a large manor found above Erisia. It's entrance can be found inside the [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md), where a blue teleporter will lie.

The exterior of Lord Erisia's Manor has little to no content, only architecture and a locked iron gate. Players cannot enter Lord Erisia's Manor without the key given by Lord Regent.

This location plays a substantial part in [Lord Regent's Quest](Etris.md#lord-regent-quest) to eliminate [the Duke](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).

## Potential Enemies

-   [The Duke](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md)
-   Blindfolded Bandits (Attack like Mudskippers, but can parry and block)
-   BrainSuckers
-   Two Rogue stone golems

## Trivia/Notes

-   Not advised to do with a too big of a group, or too small of a group
-   Should only really go if level 30+, max attunment, and group of 3-5 (bigger the group, more health for accelerator, and bigger spirit bomb!!!)
