# Duke Erisia's Manor

LocationL Lord Erisia's Manor LocationR

What was stolen from below lies within.

  

**Lord Erisia's Manor** is a location found in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark found in [The Garden Reservoir](The_Garden_Reservoir.md).

## Overview

Lord Erisia's Manor is a manor that can be found on [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md) which its exterior consists of patches of grass on the corner of the paths with some tall bushes, stone pillars, a distinct tall tree in the center and a blocked gate preceded by a massive bridge that leads to the [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md) where plentiful of [Rock Golems](Rock_Golem.md) reside.

This place is usually visited by going to the bottom gate supposedly at the edge of [Towerstruck Lands](Towerstruck_Lands.md) and traversing the harmful acids and broken architects to the right side corner gap that leads to a short hallway and followed by a distinct turquoise-colored teleporter that will directly send you to another teleporter (you can't go back and exit unless you parkour out of the map or reset) in Lord Erisia's Manor.

Or you can try to gather enough agility or environmental height to get on top of the wall barriers in order to go to Lord Erisia's Manor more time-consumingly. This location plays a substantial part in [Lord Regent's Quest](Etris.md#lord-regent-quest) to eliminate [The Duke](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).
