# Duke Erisia's Manor

Lord Erisia's Manor is a special place you can access after going through the cave near Upper Erisia and then after fully exploring the cave, there will be a blue portal. After stepping through the portal, you are stuck there and cannot go back into the portal. If you go to the right there will be a gate that a golem will be behind of. If you aggro the golem it can destroy the gate and you will have nowhere to run basically. Sharkos and golems alike can spawn there. You can possibly escape by going into a ditch with 2 more inanimate golems that can still shoot beams, but there will be a spot at the end of the cave you can reset in.
