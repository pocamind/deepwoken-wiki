# Guns

File:A vesperian using guns.png

A picture of a vesperian using guns

Guns are a ranged weapon that can be purchased at the inn on the right hand side of Summer Isle. There are sevearl different types of guns

You need Level 25 Light Weapon and the gun itself costs 200 notes (Ammo costs 5 notes)

There is no INT requirement, although it is recommended to have quite a bit since guns use ether if you have no bullets.

You can wield 2 guns at the same time, giving you overall incredible range. Your heavy attack will be replaced with a long range shot that does not break block but is good for poke damage.
