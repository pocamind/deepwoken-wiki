# Guns

Guns are a ranged weapon that can be purchased at the inn on the right hand side of Summer Isle. There are sevearl different types of gun

You need Level 25 Light Weapon and the gun itself costs 200 notes (Ammo costs 5 notes)

There is no INT requirement, although it is recommended to have quite a bit since guns use ether if you have no bullets.
