## Description

**Sticky Bun** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 25% Stomach, but depletes -5% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Ether buff, affecting 30% of the Stomach bar. The buff increases Ether regeneration by 20%.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Sap](Sap.md) and 3 [Gathered Wheat](Gathered_Wheat.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
