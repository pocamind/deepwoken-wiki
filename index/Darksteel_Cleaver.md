## Description

The Darksteel Cleaver is a greatsword in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. Although being one of the best weapons for bosses in the endgame, it is obtainable from any power.

Located where the Chariot's Spire would be in [Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md), near a [Deep Owl](Deep_Owl.md) and a [Prime Owl](Parliament.md). Be aware that the sword spawns very rarely so you might have to server hop several times to find it.

## Talents

Equipping the Darksteel Cleaver grants its unique [weapon Talent](Talents.md#weapon-talents)(s). Weapon Talents will be lost upon equipping any other weapon, including ability-bound weapons, like from the [Crazy Slots](Crazy_Slots.md) Resonance.

**Monster Slayer** grants a 25% damage buff when attacking giant enemies.

## Critical

The user shortly winds up before unleashing an upward slash, knocking down and pushing those hit by it. The critical has True Hyperarmor for the full duration, making it unable to be cancelled through Venting or being Shocked. Alternatively, it can be used as a "pseudo-vent" due to its properties.

This attack has a scaled damage multiplier of 1.15x.

The critical has a cooldown of **5 seconds**.
