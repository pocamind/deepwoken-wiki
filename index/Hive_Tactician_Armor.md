# Hive Tactician Armor

## Description

Template:ArmorInfobox**Hive Tactician Armor** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

### _Requirements_

This Outfit has no cost and requires five (5) Cloth, two (2) pieces of Iron, two (2) Dark Feathers and three (3) Vibrant Gems.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1200 durability and grants +30% Physical resistance, +20% Elemental resistance and +10% Wind resistance.

### _**Armor Talent**_

While 2-handing, you take less posture damage.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   Songseeker Wilds
-   Aratel/Hive Island
-   Minityrsa
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)

## Trivia/Notes

-   This Outfit's schematic is shortened to Tactician in your inventory.
