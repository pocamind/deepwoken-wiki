## Description

An enchant that is acquired through using a Kyrsan Chill Enchant Stone dropped by the [Kyrsgarde Champion](<Kyrsgarde_Champion,_Kyrsa's_Star.md>)

## Effect

On critical hits, apply a slow that increases with each crit. At 5 stacks fully encase your opponent in ice.
