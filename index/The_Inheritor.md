## Description

The Inheritor is the protagonist of _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)'_s [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. They are a member of the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md).

Due to threat posed by [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md) of [Etrea](Etrea.md) to the Eastern Luminant, the Inheritor was tasked by the [Reclaimer of the Recollection](Reclaimer_of_the_Recollection.md) to use [Mementos](Mementos.md) to relive the memories of those who had attempted to foil the Regent in the past in order to see if a weakness could be found.
