# Hardened Enforcer Plate

The Hardened Enforcer Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Its crafted with 1 [Enforcer Plate](Enforcer_Plate.md) + 1 [Enmity’s Armour Piece](<Enmity's_Armor_Piece.md>) + 1 Ethereal Bone Dust at the Lost Forge.
