# Cerebral Requiem

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

LocationL Cerebral Requiem LocationR

Woven memories split and fray before you. This is where their story ends.

  

**Cerebral Requiem** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location in [The Throne of Lies](The_Throne_of_Lies.md).

## Overview

Cerebral Requiem acts as the step-up to the [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md). Players are sent to it when they meet their ultimate fate against [Zi'eer, the Fourth Prophet](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>).

## Contents

### Reclaimer of the Recollection

Located in the center is the [Reclaimer of the Recollection](Reclaimer_of_the_Recollection.md), who tells you about the circumstances of your [Memento](Mementos.md)'s memories and serves to wipe your character similarly to how they do it in the [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md). See their page for more information.

## Trivia

-   Depending on if you defeated Zi'eer or not, the placing of the 'woven memories' will change: If you fell before landing a hit on him, it will continue upwards for a bit before ending, but if you did manage to defeat him, it will instead be below you, as you had surpassed your Memento's memories.
    -   This can also be seen in [The Cerebral Spire](The_Cerebral_Spire.md) and the [Fragments of Else](Fragments_of_Else.md), with the 'woven memories' expanding upwards infinitely, representing the many memories of your Memento that you have yet to live through.
