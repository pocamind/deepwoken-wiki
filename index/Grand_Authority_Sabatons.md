# Grand Authority Sabatons

**Thyme** (/taɪm/) is the herb (dried aerial parts) of some members of the genus _Thymus_ of aromatic perennial evergreen herbs in the mint family Lamiaceae. Thymes are relatives of the oreganogenus _Origanum_, with both plants being mostly indigenous to the Mediterranean region. Thymes have culinary, medicinal, and ornamental uses, and the species most commonly cultivated and used for culinary purposes is _Thymus vulgaris_.
