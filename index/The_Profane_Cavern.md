# The Profane Cavern

LocationL The Profane Cavern LocationR

It's said that the lifeforce of those who have been defeated here has seeped into the walls of the cavern, sprouting forth all manner of insidious flora.

  

**The Profane Cavern** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md).

## Description

**The Profane Cavern** is a large cave with glowing mushrooms, plants, flowers, and liquid.
