## Description

Dravik, the Rat King is an instanced [boss](Bosses.md) fought in a part of the [Rat's Den](<Rat's_Den.md>), near one of the Waterlocks in the Etrean Luminant. He is the first King of the Golden Rats and the one enforcing the Golden Rule. This boss is not optional, as he is mandatory to uncap Power 10. The fight unlocks the Flashing Thunder Memento upon defeat while using the [Sightless](Sightless.md) [Memento](Mementos.md). When Dravik, The Rat King is killed, he spawns as a memory in [The Cerebral Spire](The_Cerebral_Spire.md) with his name ???, and upon talking to him, he will tell you to break the barrier to fight Zi'eer, the Fourth Prophet. To break it you need to defeat True Heart of Enmity in the trials to break it.

He is an old Etrean identified by white beard, with pale, old skin marked with scars on his left eye and scales under his eyelids. He uses a custom version of the [Soulwrought Greataxe](Crazy_Slots.md) as his arsenal.

### Starting the Fight with Dravik

1.  Make your way to the Waterlocks in the Etrean Luminant and enter a small passage, leading to the Rat's Den.
2.  Descend down the Rat's Den until you find a Boundary, that teleports you to the boss area.
3.  Upon loading in and going through his intro, the fight begins.
4.  Recommended but not necessary: Uncap Power 5 by defeating the [Shogun of the Prophet's Guard](<Shogun_of_the_Prophet's_Guard.md>) at the [Temple of Mur](Temple_of_Mur.md).
