# Whaler Initiate

Template:ArmorInfobox**Whaler Initiate** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 15 even though its only obtainable by being a void walker.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1500 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +5% Elemental resistance and +25% Stealth.

## Trivia/Notes

-   This Outfit is granted for free upon starting with the [Voidwalker Origin](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Origins#Voidwalker).
