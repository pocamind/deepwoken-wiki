# Sailing

Sailing can be used to get to different places on water without the risk of dying. To get a boat to sail with, you must first find your villages dock. You must then talk to the dockmaster and press 'Build new boat'. You automatically spawn with the Dinghy boat, a small, fragile but agile boat. Wind speed can be told by the direction of your flag; adjusting your sail perpendicular (like an L) to your flag will provide maximum speed. [Gremor](Gremor.md) is a good race for navigating the sea because of their Mental Compas passive, that always faces north.
