# Spine of the East

## Description

The Spine of the East[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) is a mountain range set in the middle of the [Eastern Luminant](Luminants.md).[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)

[Alsin's Landing](<Alsin's_Landing.md>), as well as numerous Sea Gates are built directly into the Spine, allowing transportation to and from [The Etrean Sea](The_Etrean_Sea.md).
