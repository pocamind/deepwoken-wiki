# Last Wind

The Last Wind is a mechanic that allows characters who have died in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) to get a second chance at life, returning them from [Fragments of Self](Fragments_of_Self.md).

It costs 1000 Robux to use Last Wind; you must interact with a Shrine of the Drowned visible on the Fragments of Self path, and if your character meets the power requirement (above power 10), you will be allowed to pay Robux.

-   Note that Last Wind can only be used once per character.
-   Items in the Hippocampal Pool will stay, and will not be given to the revived character; deposited items will be returned on the next character after wiping.
