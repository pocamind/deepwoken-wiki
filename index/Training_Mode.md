# Training Mode

wip, contribute if possible

## Overview

Training mode is a mode accessable from the main menu, which teleports you to [Temple Sparring Hall (Dungeon)](<Temple_Sparring_Hall_(Dungeon).md>) where there is an instructor to spar with. If accessed through the main menu, you will be given a character with the [Etrean](Etrean.md) race and a [Sword](<Sword_(weapon).md>). The instructor uses a sword and is a novice by default

To enter the training mode on your build, find an instructor npc (like the [Blade Instructor](Blade_Instructor.md) at [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md)) and go through their dialog.

There is a portal that will take you out of training mode.

## Options

### The Player

The players have an option to use a [Stiletto](Stiletto.md), [Sword](<Sword_(weapon).md>), and a [Battleaxe](Battleaxe.md). You can also reset or heal your character. You also wear the [Vigil Initiate](Vigil_Initiate.md) [Outfit](Outfits.md).

If you entered using an actual file, you will have whatever your build is currently using

### The instructor

The trainer has 3 trainer strength, novice, vigil, and instructor. They can use the same types of weapons as the player, and you can reset their position. They also have their properties changed, such as if they can move, parry, attack, etc. By default they can parry and block, everything else is turned off. If your hp gets reduced to 0, you will regenerate hp but not armour. The trainer wears a [Cloak of Winds](Cloak_of_Winds.md) and a white [Deepwoken Cloak](Deepwoken_Cloak.md), and is bald.

They seem to have infinite hp, however after taking enough damage they may remark the players skills, such as "Keep that Up!" and will remark when your hp runs out like "Don't give up yet!"

Novice seems only know how to do basic attacks if in range, but is capable of bocking if the **only** modifier turned on is block/parry, with a rare parry every 20 years.

Vigil seems comparable to a [Bandit](Bandit.md) in terms of difficulty, being able to block and parry somewhat and perform critical.

Instructor seems to be weaker than the original [Blade Instructor](Blade_Instructor.md), with an ai probably similar to a [Bandit Leader](Bandit_Leader.md).
