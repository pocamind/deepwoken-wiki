## Description

The Warmonger is a heavy greathammer. Currently this weapon has the model and stats of the [Steel Maul](Steel_Maul.md), with the only difference being a different base color, texture, and Warmonger having higher requirements.

## Trivia

-   Warmonger has a unique rarity, being a red-orange color.
-   This weapon was likely first released on accident.
-   If you die with this weapon in your inventory, it turns into a regular [Steel Maul](Steel_Maul.md).
-   Warmonger is unenchantable.
