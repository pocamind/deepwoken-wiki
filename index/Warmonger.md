## Description

The Warmonger is a heavy greathammer. In appearance, this weapon is an orange [Steel Maul](Steel_Maul.md) with a stone-like texture. On top of the visual similarity, it shares the same statistical values as the Steel Maul.

Dying while having this weapon equipped will **revert it into a Steel Maul**, removing all of the unique properties.

## Trivia

-   Warmonger has a unique rarity, being a red-orange color.
-   Warmonger is unenchantable.
