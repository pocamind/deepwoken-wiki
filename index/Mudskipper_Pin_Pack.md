# Mudskipper Pin Pack

## Description

The **Mudskipper Pin Pack** was a pin pack released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration. Classified under the "Enamel Pins" category, it featured the [Mudskipper Broodlord](Mudskipper_Broodlord.md) and [Mudskipper](Mudskipper.md) mobs. Its campaign ran from April 11th, 2025, to May 10th, 2025, before selling out. The pins measured 3.81 cm in width and 3.26 cm in depth, weighed 11.4 grams, and were made from plated zinc alloy.

  

### Official Description

"As if drowning wasn't bad enough...  
  
Thankfully, the parasites that puppeteer these fine fellows depicted are purely pictographic, but don't let that stop you from proudly showing your support for Lumen's favourite undead abominations."

## Trivia

-   A total of 203 Mudskipper Pin Packs were sold, and the campaign ended at 101% of its funding goal.
-   This campaign ran alongside the [Thresher Longboi](Thresher_Longboi.md).
