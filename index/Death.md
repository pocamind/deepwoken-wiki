# Death

The death mechanic in this game is very similar to Rouge Lineage. Probably because the Rouge devs worked on this game. Anyways, here's how it works: the first time you die, your health bar will be stained. The second time, you will spawn in the Depths, which is basically like the underworld. Third time you die, you get wiped. Here is a guide on how to escape the Depths: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c4muNkduPs0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c4muNkduPs0)
