# Death

Death is an important aspect of Deepwoken. You technically start with 3 lives in Deepwoken but 2 of those lives are in the overworld and your final life is in the Depths. If you are on your second life and Power Up \[Level Up: Power # <- In the stat sheet (Press Tab)\] Your healthbar will revert and be restored.

If you die once your healthbar is changed and tampered with notifying you are on your second life and if you die on your second life you will be sent to the Depths.

  

## How to regain your life

The Depths is essentially the underworld where you have to redeem yourself and prove your worth. Your goal in the Depths is to enter the Collesuem and stealth past enemy entities called Watchers. If spotted by Watchers you will have to fend off Assassination Angels. Once you stealth past the Watchers and make it inside the Collesuem you will reach an elevator that takes you back up to the Overworld. Note: If you are low enough Power Level you won't have to redeem yourself in battle to re-enter the Overworld. If you are a higher level above 10-15ish you may have to fight an enemy to prove your worth at the top of the Collesuem. If you win and prove your worth you will have all your lives restored and be able to progress as normal. Depths is essentially The Gulag.

  

## Notes

-   Any aggro from guards during your first life will be reset on your second life

-   There are some talents that affect death ( like Boot wallet)
