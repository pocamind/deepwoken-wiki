# Death

_If you want to know more about where you go after dying twice, see [The Depths](The_Depths.md)._

## Description

Death is a core aspect of Deepwoken. You have 3 lives in Deepwoken, the first 2 lives on the Etrean Luminant (The Overworld), and the third in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). When you die, you drop some notes and some items, but the losses can be decreased through certain [Talents](Talents.md). When you're on your second life, your healthbar will be stained. Levelling up will revert the healthbar back to normal essentially giving your third life back. Dying with a stained healthbar drowns you and sends you to the Depths.

[The Depths](The_Depths.md) is essentially the underworld, where you have to redeem yourself and prove your worth. Your goal in The Depths is to enter the sunken Celtorian city and sneak past enemy entities called Watchers. If spotted by the Watchers, you will have to fend off against [Angels](Hollow_Angel.md). While you are sneaking around, you have to go to certain parts of the city and ring bells to open a gate to the centre of the city. Once you make it to the elevator, you will be sentenced by the Voices of the Depths to a [Trial](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/The_Depths#The_Trial) that takes you back up to the Etrean Luminant if successful, restoring all of your lives.
