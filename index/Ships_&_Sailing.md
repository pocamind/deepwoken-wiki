# Ships & Sailing

Boats are the main method of transportation in Deepwoken. There are three seas, the Aratel, the Etrean, and the Void. There are 6 boats, the Dinghy, Sloop, Schooner, Merchant Schooner, Frigate, and Ironclad. If you have a large sum of notes it is recommended to by a big boat. You earn a decent amount of respect if you use any boat but the Dinghy or Sloop.
