# Ships & Sailing

Boats are the main method of transportation in Deepwoken which is used to get to different islands throughout the map. There are three seas, The Etrean, [The Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md), and [The Void Sea](Voidsea.md). There are currently 6 boats which are:

-   Dinghy
-   Sloop
-   Schooner
-   Merchant Schooner
-   Frigate
-   Ironclad.

If you have a large sum of notes it is recommended to by a big boat such as the Merchant Schooner due to it having canons and Lantern(Boat's Campfire) which will be useful during your travel into the Aratel Sea and Void sea. You earn a decent amount of respect by other player if you use large boats which are Sloop and above.

The Boat Man's Appearances.

**As the boats get more expensive, They gain size, Speed, Lanterns, Cannons, and more Rooms and Spaces which make it easier to kills monsters while travelling in the Aratel and Void Sea.** Boats can be purchased from The Boat man which is located in every island's dock that have a town/city on it for example,Etris and Isle of Vigils. When talked to him he would give an option to build your boat (Shipyard) which there you will receive The Dinghy for free as a starter boat. Each ships can be brought with a different sum of notes.

You can find information about Boats Control and Sailing [here](Ships_&_Sailing.md).
