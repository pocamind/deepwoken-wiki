## Description

The **Weapon Manual** is a [Training Gear](Training_Gear.md) used to increase the player's [weapon attributes](Attributes.md#weapon-attributes) depending on what they have equipped.

All characters will spawn in with a Weapon Manual. Additionally, they can be purchased from [Gear Cabinets](Training_Gear.md#gear-cabinet-locations) across Lumen, for 25.

In order to train your weapon attributes, you'll have to use your weapon to complete a short "training exercise" consisting of blocks, parries and dodges.

## Usage

To use the Weapon Manual, either hold it or click it in your inventory. The manual will force whatever weapon you have equipped into your hands and start a quick-time event minigame.

### Minigame

Once you equip your manual, the mini-game will begin. You'll be forced to either block, dodge or parry right as the circle closes in on you. Failing will only result in a hurt animation, and won't damage you.

Each prompt will have it's own designated sound, color and icon to help indicate the action.

Do this five (5) times and you will receive a message on the top center of your screen saying one of three things:

| Message | Results |
| --- | --- |
| You reflect on your mistakes and feel inspired to correct them. | Minimal experience |
| You did well, but you know you can do better. | Moderate experience |
| Your flawless performance inspires further progress. | Maximum experience |
