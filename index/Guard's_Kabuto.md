# Guard's Kabuto

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Guard's Kabuto is an Equipment Armor in the Helmet category_._ This item will give extra randomized stats based on the amount of pips.

## Trivia

-   The Guard's Kabuto Along with Captain's Kabuto, and Royal Guard's Kabuto are almost the same Equipment Armor, with only slightly different colors, and stats.

-   The Guard's Kabuto is the only Kabuto to not give +8 HP
