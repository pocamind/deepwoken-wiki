## Description

The rabbit was a species of animal mentioned in [God in the Machine](God_in_the_Machine.md). With the [book](Books.md)'s removal from the game, rabbits are no longer referenced within _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)_.

They were small, furry creatures found in the woods. They could be hunted for human consumption, but had to be cooked first.[\[1\]](#cite-note-deus-1)
