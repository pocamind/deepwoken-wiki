# Mineskipper

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Mineskippers are a [Mudskipper](Mudskipper.md) variant that wear mining helmets and the [Experienced Adventurer outfit](Outfits.md). Their regular variant holds a pickaxe in its right hand. They are found in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md).
