# Fort Merit

## Description

Template:AreainfoboxWest of the 2nd Hive Island (I think) & North East of Eastern Lighthouse. This is aligned with the Authority, and is comprised of Adretians. You can also buy a spot at the inn.

Contains an Elevator (Clear technological superiority)

Contains Jail Cells filled with Etreans, Free these Etreans for loot
