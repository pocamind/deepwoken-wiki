# Fort Merit

## Description

Template:AreainfoboxFort Merit is an Etrean Prison overtaken by the Authority, now serving as a foward base for them.

It has every necessary npcs other populated islands have, excluding the lumberjack quest and including a rescue quest.

## Location

It is situated on Bluster's Rift side ,right at the edge of it. Usually you sail around the Hive until you see the port in the picture on the right, those are the docks that lead to Fort Merit.

## Points of Interest

### **The Docks:**

The Shipwright can be found inside the little house situated near the water, it is guarded by 1 Authority Peacekeeper and 1 Authority Commander (weaker version of it)

File:Fort Merit2.png

Fort Merit's Port from above.

### **The Prison:**

NPCs:

-   It is heavily patrolled by guards, every corridor has at least one guard walking through it.
-   The actual Authority Commander is inside the prison, being the most intelligent, deadly and durable NPC in Fort Merit.
-   There are 5 Etrean hostages and 1 Nomad hostage within the prison, tied up.
-   Some Legion Soldier named "Brutus", trying to have a dialogue with him will result in the player getting knocked.

Structure:

-   The prison has only one entrance.
-   The layout is very wacky.
-   The halls contain 2 cells each.
-   There are about 3 tunnels the player can slide/crawl through.
-   Every hall and the entrance is connected to a small arena in the center.

### **The Etrean Guardmaster** **(Rescue Quest)**

Location:

-   At the wooden elevator, there is a stairway. Upon following it and going foward you will find the Etrean Guardmaster on a boat.

Quest:

-   Pick up the Etrean hostages within the prison and talk to the Guardmaster while carrying them.
-   You will be rewarded with a chest containing decent gear and loot.

## Tips

-   Not a great location to set your spawn point at.
-   A great location to farm xp/items.
-   Considering that there is a guaranteed Nomad hostage here. Getting [Way Of Navae](Way_of_Navae.md) by picking up the hostage from here and bringing it the whole way back to Etris is possible. (unless.... yes im looking at you lionfish)
-   BY the love of GOD, do not try to assassinate ANY guard within the prison's vicinity. Only one "Hey, Over Here!" will bring the whole fucking security AND the commander to fuck you up.
