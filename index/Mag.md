## Description

Mag is a Birch [Gremor](Gremor.md) NPC working for [The Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md). He can craft [Bullet Casings](Bullet_Casings.md) by converting 200 [Bullets](Bullets.md) into specialized variants using [Ores](Ores.md), for a fee of up to 40. These modified bullets offer unique properties and playstyles.

He wears a Black Hood and the [Summer Company Uniform](Summer_Company_Uniform.md).

## Location

Mag is located in [The Furnace](The_Furnace.md) below the Summer Isle Castle.

## Trivia

-   His name is a reference to gun magazines.
-   The Gunsmith which can be found in the [Guild Base](Guild_Bases.md) is near identical to Mag, with the only exception being their looks.
    -   Furthermore, if the player has less than 200 bullets produced at a time, they will share the dialogue, "I can't encase your bullets with more than one type of casing at a time. If you have the ore, I can make you more \[ORE\] Bullets."
