## Description

The **Strange Claw** is an item only dropped by [Terrapods](Terrapod.md) which can be found on [Erisia](Erisia.md), the [First Layer](First_Layer.md), and [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md).

## Uses

-   Used to craft the [Cloak of Winds](Cloak_of_Winds.md), [Darksteel Plate](Darksteel_Plate.md), and the [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md) [Outfits](Outfits.md)

### Crafting

-   [Equipment](Equipment.md)
    -   [Terraplate Pauldrons](Terraplate_Pauldrons.md): 6x Strange Claws + 1x [Terrapod Shell](Terrapod_Shell.md) + 1x [Warden Pauldrons](Warden_Pauldrons.md) at a [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md)

## Trivia

-   You can summon [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) by offering 5 Strange Claws to the campfire.
