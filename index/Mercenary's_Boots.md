Mercinary Boots are an equipment under part of the boots category. This equipment grants +2 health and a Talent named Endurance Runner, which Negates the speed penalty of critical health.

Mercenary Boot's are part of the "**Mercenary's**" set; with the only other armor piece being the **Mercenary's Grab**.
