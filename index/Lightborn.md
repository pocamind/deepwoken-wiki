# Lightborn

## Description

The stars call you back...

Template:Race Box mark 2

  

## Appearance

Lightborn are fair-skinned Humanoids with crosses in their eyes and White or Gold Halos either on their wrist, neck or head.

## Racial Abilities

-   +2 points in every attribute (except charisma)

## Progression

Currently unobtainable - given by Ragoozer to wave 0 testers only

## Strengths

-   Halos are drippy.(very)

## Notes/Trivia

-   ~Lightborn are probably the most humanoid race in Deepwoken, save for their halos and eyes~.(Canor looks more like a human than lightborn.)
-   Only obtainable from admins
-   This might be based off of the race Seraphs in Rouge Lineage
