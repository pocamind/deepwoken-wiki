## Miscellaneous

**Lore:**

  

Children of **a** Celestial, the Lightborn are considered to be fragments of these once great gods up above. However, in recent times, a family of these people known as the Spellhardts had come forth within the Eastern Luminant, rumored to be housing themselves within a mighty castle in the sky. Although they are of Celestial origin, their reputation among the people is questioned quite often due to their less than favorable actions.

Little is known about their culture and beliefs, but they are known to punish those who are tempted by Drowned Gods, exiling them from their community.
