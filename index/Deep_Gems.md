# Deep Gems

**Deep Gems** are rare Gems that are dropped in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) (rare drop) and by [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) (roughly 50 - 70% chance to drop. Current data: 15 Primadon kills, 9 gem drops, more data has to be collected for a more accurate average). You get more gems from Primadon depending on how quickly you kill it, though, it is not a guaranteed drop even if you _do_ kill it quickly - needs to be proven.

You can equip a Deep Gem by going to a campfire, holding out the Gem and finding the mantra you'd like to equip the gem on. After, click on the black box in the Campfire Menu, below the "Created by \[NAME\]" dialogue.

To remove a Deep Gem from your mantra, you need to **Recall** the mantra. **Clicking destroy gets rid of the Gem entirely**.
