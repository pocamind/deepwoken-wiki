# Broken Gatling Gun

Broken Gatling Gun is a very rare item obtained only from the drop pool of [Mechalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md) at [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md) and [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).

Can be given to [Carrion](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md) to obtain the Mecha Gatling mantra.
