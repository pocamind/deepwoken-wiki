## Description

The True Seraph's Spear is a MED weapon. This spear can be obtained from killing a [Corrupted King Thresher](Thresher.md) or a [Glacial Lionfish](Lionfish.md) in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). It has the joint highest MED requirement out of all spears, alongside the [Kyrsglaive](Kyrsglaive.md), and probably is the most tedious spear to obtain. It's unrivalled in its chip damage. The True Seraph's Spear does 29.6 damage at 100 MED and 28.49 damage at 90 MED (the requirement to use this weapon).
