## Description

The True Seraph's Spear is a MED weapon. This spear can be obtained from killing a [Corrupted King Thresher](Thresher.md) or a [Glacial Lionfish](Lionfish.md) in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). It has the joint highest MED requirement out of all spears, alongside the [Kyrsglaive](Kyrsglaive.md), and probably is the most tedious spear to obtain. It's unrivalled in its penetration, chip damage and swing speed. The True Seraph's Spear does 29.6 damage at 100 MED and 28.49 damage at 90 MED (the requirement to use this weapon).

## Trivia

-   The spear is named after the "Seraph," the highest level of angels in Ancient Judaism.
-   The damage is mediocre at best but the chip damage makes up for it. Stacks poorly with [Obfuscation Enchantment](Enchantments.md) due to the chip cap of 50%.
