**Ferryman's Ring** is a Ring [Equipment](Equipment.md) piece that adds 50% more range to your [Murmur:Rhythm Vision](Murmur%253A_Rhythm.md).

It's decently easy to get from the [Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md).
