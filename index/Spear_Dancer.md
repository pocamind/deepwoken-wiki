## Description

**Spear Dancer** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode.

## Unique Features

Your vent is instead replaced with a buff that increases your next M1s speed, stun, and damage. Missing instead causes you to get stunned and lose tempo.
