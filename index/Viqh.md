| Viqh | |

## Description

Viqh is a Lightkeeper and the only one mentioned by name. Possibly an Etrean female as [Nimea](Nimea.md) was her childhood friend and mentioned her. She is the person who taught [Klaris Llfiend, Mother of Lights](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) to use Radiant Tones, which is responsible for the creation of [Dawnwalker](Oath%253A_Dawnwalker.md). She is currently imprisoned inside the Eastern Interstital Lighthouse by the Central Authority.

## Trivia

-   Her hobby is apparently to write poetry, as stated by Klaris.
