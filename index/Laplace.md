# Laplace

“ ooooo aaaaahah hic waaaaa ”

― Enchant traders on 9/19/22

  
Laplace Diri is a Ganymede located in [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) in the R&D section.

Template:Areainfobox

He lets you trade three [enchantments](Enchantments.md) for a random enchant on your currently equipped weapon. He will explain you through dialogue a tutorial on what he does.

-   Laplace can accept both Enchanted and (somehow) Soulbounded weapons or equipment.
-   For each enchant you gave to Laplace, they tally them to mark how many enchants you given to successfully seal the deal.
-   Laplace cannot enchant legendary weapons (ex. Crypt Blade, Curved Blade of Winds) or any weapon that cannot hold an enchant.
-   Laplace cannot enchant duped weapon. It's impossible to know if your weapon is duped if you trade for it.

## Trivia

-   Laplace's method of transmuting three Enchants into one Weapon enchant is by wrapping all three Enchants together in Linen (presumably rope, not cloth).
-   Laplace was moved from R&D to an area in [Lordsgrove](The_Lordsgrove.md) when he was given the ability to enchant weapons but was then moved back to R&D in the Verse 2 update.
-   His surname, Diri, is unique to him and [yayafino](Developer_Specs.md).
