# Vow of Allegiance

The Vow of Allegiance is a vow you make with players similar to that to the [Vow of Mastery](Vow_of_Mastery.md). You can activate it by saying "Wanna make a vow of allegiance \[name\]?" If they say yes, they will get a window to confirm or deny. If they confirm, they will be treated as an ally, and are able to get buffs from you. There are no requirements for the Vow of Allegiance.

However, you can only make 50 of these vows.(unknown if this is true)

The Vow will carry over even after you wipe, and for the account.

Theoretically, if two guild leaders make a Vow of Allegiance, the two guilds will be treated as allies to each other.

Has no method of being broken.
