## Description

Titus's Cape is an animated **unenchantable** [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Worn by [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md), it is a dark cape with shoulder plates. Equipping it grants you +2 Rare Pips, +1 Legendary Pip, 10% Thundercall Resistance, and the [?](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent.

## Obtainment

After defeating [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md), a crafting bench within the [Merit Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md) will be open to you. This item is only craftable with that bench. The recipes are as follows:

1.  1 [Titus's Cape](<Titus's_Cape.md>) + 1 [Champion's Alloy](<Champion's_Alloy.md>).
