# Purge Shrine

## Description

The Purge Shrine is an interactable tower that can be found atop a small cliff near [Kaide, the Drowned Light](Kaide,_the_Drowned_Light.md) in [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). Interacting with the shrine after wishing for power from [Misérables](Misérables.md) will prompt the screen to shake, signaling the effect has incurred.

## Effects

#### After interacting with the Purge Shrine, the following effects will occur.:

1.  The Scion of [Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) will be buffed, dealing increased damage and no longer spawning [Kyrsan Snipers](Kyrsgarde_Sniper.md).
2.  The time the player has to escape [Layer 2 Floor 2](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) before the spacetime collapse will be increased to 7 minutes.
3.  The [Kyrsan Champion](<Kyrsgarde_Champion,_Kyrsa's_Star.md>) will spawn at the intersection where you will normally find the 4 [Kyrsan Lancers](Kyrsgarde_Lancer.md).

After defeating the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) and entering the floor collapse sequence, going to the center of the city will be a [Kyrsgarde Champion](<Kyrsgarde_Champion,_Kyrsa's_Star.md>).
