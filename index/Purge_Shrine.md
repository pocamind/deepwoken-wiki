# Purge Shrine

File:Purgeshrine.png

The Purge Shrine seen in floor two, in front of [Kaide](Kaide,_the_Drowned_Light.md)

The Purge Shrine is a shrine located atop a small cliff near [Kaide](Kaide,_the_Drowned_Light.md), speaking to it after wishing for power from Misérables will spawn the Kyrsian Champion after defeating Ethrion.
