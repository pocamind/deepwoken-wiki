# Sea Highway

## Overview

Sea Highways are structure found within Etrean and Eastern [Luminant](Luminants.md), upon a ship passing through it, a 'jolt' of energy and powered waves causes it to traverse much faster across the sea while remaining within the highway.

These highways are a masterwork of [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) efficiency, actively maintained and constructed by The Golden Link, a company of Thundercallers originally formed within the Citadel Of Markor.
