# Sea Highway

## Overview

Sea Highways are structures found within the Etrean and Eastern [Luminants](Luminants.md). Upon a [ship](Ships_&_Sailing.md) passing through one, a 'jolt' of energy and powered waves causes it to traverse much faster across the sea while remaining within the highway.

These highways are a masterwork of [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) efficiency, actively maintained and constructed by The Golden Link, a company of Thundercallers originally formed within the Citadel Of Markor.
