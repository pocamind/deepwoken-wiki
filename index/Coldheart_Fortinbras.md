# Coldheart Fortinbras

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

**Coldheart Fortinbras** is an enemy found in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). He can spawn randomly as an event, or more likely as a quest for [The Divers](The_Divers.md) (found on the Quest Board in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) or in/around [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md)). When Fortinbras spawns, he will be in the leaning emote until you approach him.

As a Black Diver, Fortinbras was a scientist of sorts, acting out of [The Cold Forge](The_Cold_Forge.md).

He is wearing a [Black Diver](Black_Diver.md) Outfit and uses the [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md) and [Bloodrend](Bloodrend.md) attunements while wielding the legendary Frostdraw weapon: the [Hailbreaker](Hailbreaker.md), with his version capable of having [Enchantments](Enchantments.md) on it.

## Trivia

-   Despite being a Diver, Fortinbras has Bloodrend, which he learnt on his path to creating a weapon out of his lover Deirdre by seeking out [Vyrelda](Vyrelda,_Master_of_Blood.md) with the help of [Chaser, Scholar of the Crimson Contract](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md).
-   The person Fortinbras mentions is his old lover Deirdre who was a fellow Diver of unknown rank with him. He was shunned from the Divers as the sinister nature of his experiments leading him to build and continue his experiments within the hidden reaches of [The Cold Forge](The_Cold_Forge.md).
-   The culmination of his experiments lead to Deirdre becoming the [Dormant Splinter](Dormant_Splinter.md), one of his biggest regrets to this day.
-   His bell is frozen and produces a white mist, although what the bell does is unknown.
-   He has the same eyes as [Nell, Master of Frost](Nell,_Master_of_Frost.md), with dark sclera and blue irises.
