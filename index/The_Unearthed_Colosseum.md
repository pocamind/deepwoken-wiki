# The Unearthed Colosseum

LocationL The Unearthed Colosseum LocationR

Overcome the challenges ahead and you shall be greatly rewarded.

  

**The Unearthed Colosseum** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the 2v2 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**The Unearthed Colosseum** is a large colosseum built above spikes that protrude out the sides.
