# Gunsmith

| Gunsmith | |
|---|---|

## Overview

The Gunsmith is an NPC, who is found in the Armory of your guild base, and can be hired for. He has the exact same function as [Mag](Mag.md) but inside of your guild base.

His main use is to produce [Bullet Casings](Bullet_Casings.md) by converting two hundred Bullets into specialized Bullets using Ores for a price of up to, giving them different properties and playstyles.

### Dialogue:

(1) Gunsmith: Looking to buy some casing? For some Notes and a little ore I can encase your ammunition

(2) Gunsmith: Gonna need you to show me the ore you want me to use... (If you were not holding a compatible ore.)

(3) Gunsmith: How many bullets do you want encased? It's 1 Note for every 5 bullets, rounded up to the nearest Note.

## Trivia

-   The Gunsmith is assumed to be an Ironsinger hired from the Summer Isles.
