# Diver's Journal: Journeyman Skeli

## Description

_**Diver's Journal: Journeyman Skeli**_ is a [Book](Books.md) found in the first floor of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md), written by and collected from the corpse of [Journeyman Skeli](<Abandoned_Diver_(Journeyman_Skeli).md>).

## Text

### 

Diver's Journal: Journeyman Skeli

> \- Eternal Gale Expeditionary Log What date do I even put here?
> 
> This snow sticks like tar and doesn't even melt by a fire.
> 
> Curse Klaris for sending us down here. There was no reason to lose more than a single expedition team. You're throwing our lives away, you blind fool.
> 
> According to the locals, a [Diver](Glossary.md#the-divers) has taken residence in the tower above.
> 
> Chaser, I'm sure of it. When a man like that volunteers to lead the first team, it can't mean anything good. And if he's the only one left alive, I can only imagine what became of the others.
> 
> These [Black Divers](Glossary.md#black-divers) are blinded by their own arrogance. If you're so strong, where's the damn help?
> 
> How could they be so stupid? Once Chaser finds what he's looking for, we're all goners.
