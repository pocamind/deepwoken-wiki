# Grudge

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Grudge is a hostile NPC that has a low chance to be randomly encountered as an event in specific locations. Current known locations are [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md), [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), [The Depths (Scyphozia)](First_Layer.md), [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md) and [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md). It is a green, ghostlike figure that wields the legendary weapon [Wraithclaw](Wraithclaw.md) and utilizes [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md), specifically it's Specter Path.

The only requirement for this NPC to spawn is having uncapped [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md).

Killing them will give you the [Wraithclaw](Wraithclaw.md) along with a low tier chest.
