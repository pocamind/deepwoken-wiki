# Gliders

-   Schematics are found in a cave in Lower Erisia. (I dont recall exactly where, but if you go to the big gate at lower ersia and go left you should come across some parkour, where you will talk to a lady who gives you the schematics)
-   Allows you to glide. (indefinite glide duration)
-   15 uses.
-   Recipie = 2 sticks, 2 cloth (at crafting table)
-   Must be in the air to use it.
    
    Etrean adventurer starting his journey!
