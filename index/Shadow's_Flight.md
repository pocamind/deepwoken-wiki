## Description

Shadow's Flight is a craftable consumable that allows the user to teleport to their mouse cursor in exchange for health and ether, functioning almost identically to the rare Talent [Shadow Travel](Talents.md#shadow-travel), but with less range and at a greater health and ether cost.

Notably, this allows the user to enter [The Understrand](The_Understrand.md) without being a [Shadowcaster](Shadowcast.md).

## Obtainment

Shadow's Flight is created by crafting together 2 [Void Feathers](Dark_Feather.md#void-feather) and an [Umbral Obsidian](Umbral_Obsidian.md).
