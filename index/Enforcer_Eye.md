## Description

Enforcer Eye is used to gain the "Enforcer Pull" mantra from [Carrion](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md), who resides in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) after talking to him in the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md).
