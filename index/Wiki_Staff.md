# Wiki Staff

The [Deepwoken Wiki](Deepwoken_Wiki.md) is provided with a team of staff members responsible for its management and safety to provide a welcoming environment for readers and editors. Users in such a team are granted special abilities and rights based on their role inside the platform.

These users are commonly known as _"staff members"_, _"staff team"_, or _"staff"_ in the community. Such terms refer to the whole of the following groups:

-   [Bureaucrats](Bureaucrats.md)
-   [Administrators](Administrators.md)
-   [Content Moderators](Moderators.md)
-   [Discussion Moderators](Discussion_Moderators.md)
-   Enforcers
