## Description

Volu is a [Capra](Capra.md) affiliated with [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md).

## Bloodfouler

Upon initiating a dialogue he will hand you the first volume of the Strange Tomes. Upon gathering all 3 of them you can place them in the pedestal to obtain [Bloodfouler](Bloodfouler.md).

-   If you talk to him after making or having the [Bloodfouler](Bloodfouler.md) he takes it from you
