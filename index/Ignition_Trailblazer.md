# Ignition Trailblazer

Template:ArmorInfobox**Ignition Trailblazer** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, two (2) Megalodaunt Hides and one (1) Rock.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 10.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1000 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +25% Elemental resistance, +5% Blunt Armor and +5% Wind Armor

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Cards](Talents.md) _Perseverance_ and _Low Stride_

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:
