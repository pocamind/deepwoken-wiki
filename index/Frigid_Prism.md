# Frigid Prism

File:23rq3fasdfew.png

Etrean holding his new frigid prism

-   Use : Certain crafting recipes, NPC in upper Etris to the west of palace in the shack asks for five of them (Giving them to her unlocks frostdraw)
-   Consumable : yes
-   Sellable : yes
-   Where to find : Defeat nomad tribe to the right of the bridge/tunnel above the Etris docks, nomad tribe NPC have a chance to drop the prisms.
-   The nomad tribe can spawn at anytime and is peaceful until you attack it.
-   Any NPC that uses Frostdraw has a chance to drop the prisms.
