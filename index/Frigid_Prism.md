# Frigid Prism

File:23rq3fasdfew.png

[Etrean](Etrean.md) holding his new frigid prism

-   Use : Certain crafting recipes, NPC in upper [Etris](Etris.md) to the west of palace in the shack asks for five of them (Giving them to her unlocks frostdraw)
-   Consumable : yes
-   Sellable : yes
-   Where to find : Defeat [nomad tribe members](Eastern_Nomad.md) to the right of the bridge/tunnel above the [Etris](Etris.md) docks, [nomad tribe NPCs](Eastern_Nomad.md) have a chance to drop the prisms.
-   The nomad tribe can spawn at anytime and is peaceful until you attack it.
-   Any NPC that uses [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md) has a chance to drop the prisms.
-   An easy way to get the prisms is to go to [summer isle](Summer_Isle.md) and kill the [nomads](Eastern_Nomad.md) there, as they are peaceful and easy to defeat.
