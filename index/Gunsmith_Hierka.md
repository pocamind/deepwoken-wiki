## Description

The former protégé of [Gunsmith Rosen](Gunsmith_Rosen.md), Hierka was really loyal to the [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md)'s [Bureau of Research](Bureau_of_Research.md) and had aligned himself with [The Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md) to steal their secrets. Under Rosen's watch, Hierka achieved the rank of gunsmith. Hierka modified Rosen's designs to produce the [Rifle Spear](Rifle_Spear.md), but his deception was eventually discovered.[\[1\]](#cite-note-rs-1)[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)
