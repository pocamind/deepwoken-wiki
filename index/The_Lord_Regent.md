# The Lord Regent

## Description

Lord Regent is NPC Type enemy that is found in the throne room of the Palace in Etrea. He can't be defeated or attacked.

Lord Regent would be sitting in the throne in the Palace.

### Moves

-   **Needle Stab:** Will one shot anyone.

### Notes

-   Don't go to him unless you have the quest needed to talk to him.
-   He is mentioned by the teacher, in the charisma story as sacrificial king
