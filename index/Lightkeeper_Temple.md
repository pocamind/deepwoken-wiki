# Lightkeeper Temple

For now, Lightkeeper Temple is mainly just a place people go to wash their face in order to open the gate (permanently, client-side) at [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) so they can access [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) (a place with many benefits). It is located somewhere beyond Widow Cave and after you get there, you will have to fight a croc. Killing that croc and going left will take you to another croc. After you kill that, continue on the left side to get to this purple area where you wash your face. On the right side there is a mother croc, so do not go right unless you want to fight one of these monstrous beings.
