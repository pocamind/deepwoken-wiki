# Flamecharm Obelisk

| Flamecharm Obelisk | |
|---|---|
| location | Saramaed Summit |

## Description

The Flamecharm Obelisk is an NPC located in a small hidden cave with lava flowing out in [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md). It must be located and interacted with, along with 4 other [element-based obelisks](Saintsworn_Obelisks.md), to obtain the [Saintsworn](Oath%253A_Saintsworn.md) [Oath](Oaths.md). It can only be interacted with if you have at least 15 [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md). When interacted with, Jace, the [Hero of Flame](<Hero's_Blade_of_Flame.md>), will speak to the player.
