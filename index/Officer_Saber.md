## Description

The Officer Saber is a Medium type weapon that can be obtained from [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md), [The Depths](The_Depths.md) and [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md)'s chest.

## Critical

Perform a flurry of quick slashes without losing momentum.
