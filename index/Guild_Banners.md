# Guild Banners

The building at **[Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md)** with a banner

Capture these to get money in your Guild Treasury

2 people hitting the griddy next to a banner

**Guild Banners** are banners found around the map. They reward the guild controlling them with _(?)_ notes per minute, and give higher note rewards if there is more competition in the server. If one guild has multiple banners under their control they will make notes from all of them, however it may become difficult to defend all of your banners if you have many under your guilds control.

(All known) Locations:

-   Etrean Luminant:
    -   There is one on top of a rock out the front of Erisia. Go straight from the shore and you should see it.
    -   There is one outside of Erisor's Church in Erisia.
    -   One in the ruins next to the Ministry bandit camp above Outpost Antumbra
    -   One at the top of the ice waterfall in Minityrsa, near the rogue hive mech and the jetstriker orb.
    -   One near Imel (the Ministry NPC by the statues in the highway)
    -   Near the Tomb of the Nameless Warrior, closer to the gate to Etris
    -   Sail to Lower Erisia straight across from Etris, near the part with the entrance into Pathfinder's Respite (the mudskipper cave)

-   Eastern Luminant:
    -   One in a cave under Lordsgrove.
    -   One nearby the Derelict Church.
    -   One in Songseeker nearby the windmill
    -   One atop a rock in Starswept, Near the Starfield Grove (Iblis spawn)
    -   One up a cliff near the Starfield Grove.
    -   In the shore of the Ancient Rotlands, near the gate to Monkey's Paw/Greathive
    -   On the mini-island near the bridge to the Starfield Veldt in Miner's Landing
    -   One on the mini-island across from Summer Isle
    -   In the Starfield Veldt, on the Crimson Sharko spawn
    -   On the bridge to Starfield Veldt
