## Description

**Tankard of Ongo Juice** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is also purchased from [Fabantou](Fabantou.md) for 20.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 2% Stomach and 25% Water.
