## Description

The Vanguard Brace is an equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 3 Health, 1 posture, 1 Pip, and the [Talent](Talents.md) Replenishing Knockout, Which increases the amount of health and posture gained from downing enemies. It is commonly found in chests from [Erisia](Erisia.md), and can also be obtained from the [tutorial](Tutorial.md).
