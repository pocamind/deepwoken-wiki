# Hollow Forge

| Hollow Forge | |
|---|---|
| usage | A furnace for Ignition Delvers to convert 3 Pluripotent Alloys to Smith's Alloys. |

## Description

The Hollow Forge is a in appearance a furnace; in the middle its the window with on-going lava and valves on the top. It can be found in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>). It was likely created by [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md), hence why it is only usable by them.

**It can only be used with the Ignition Delver [Origin](Origins.md), and both Smith's Alloys and Pluripotent Alloys cannot be dropped. Smith's Alloys can be banked but Pluripotent Alloys cannot be banked.**

## Usage

The Hollow Forge can be used to convert [Pluripotent Alloy](Pluripotent_Alloy.md) into [Smith's Alloy](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) and [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) as well as converting Smith's Alloys into Armorer's Needles and vice versa.

The Hollow Forge has the following recipes

-   3 Pluripotent Alloy -> 1 Smith's Alloy
-   9 Pluripotent Alloy -> 1 Armorer's Needle
-   3 Smith's Alloy -> 1 Armorer's Needle
-   1 Armorer's Needle -> 3 Smith's Alloy
