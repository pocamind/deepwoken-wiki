# Hollow Forge

## Description

The Hollow Forge is a in appearance a furnace; in the middle its the window with on-going lava and valves on the top. It can be found in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) and [The Chariot's Spire](<The_Chariot's_Spire.md>). It was likely created by [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md), hence why it is only usable by them.

While the Forge can **only be used by players with the [Ignition Delver](Ignition_Delver.md) Origin**, you can perform a workaround for a few of the conversions. [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) and [Armorer's Needles](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) can be banked, meaning you can transfer these Relics to a slot with this Origin to convert them into the opposing type. [Pluripotent Alloys](Pluripotent_Alloy.md), on the other hand, are unable to be banked.

## Usage

The Hollow Forge can be used to convert [Pluripotent Alloy](Pluripotent_Alloy.md) into [Smith's Alloy](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) and [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) as well as converting Smith's Alloys into Armorer's Needles and vice versa.

The Hollow Forge has the following recipes

-   3 Pluripotent Alloy -> 1 Smith's Alloy
-   9 Pluripotent Alloy -> 1 Armorer's Needle
-   3 Smith's Alloy -> 1 Armorer's Needle
-   1 Armorer's Needle -> 3 Smith's Alloy
