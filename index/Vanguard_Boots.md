## Description

Vanguard Boots are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can be found through chests in [Erisia](Erisia.md) and [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md).

Vanguard Boots are part of the "**Vanguard**" set; with the only other armor piece being the **Vanguard Brace**.

## Trivia

-   These boots correspond to the Vanguard Brace you get from the [Tutorial](Tutorial.md).
