## Description

The Scoundrel's Saber is a Medium Sword weapon sold exclusively by [Traan Zakshun](Traan_Zakshun.md) at a price of 650 notes.

## Critical

The user rushes forward, seemingly preparing to slice with the blade, instead delivering a fake blow that leads into a strong right.

The second hit automatically guardbreaks and Dazes for 2.5 seconds, and has a 1.1x scaled damage multiplier. If the user lands a hit on the opponent's back, it will ramp up the damage multiplier to 1.21x of its scaled damage.

The critical has a cooldown of **4 seconds**.

## Trivia

-   This is part of the Saber weapon family, along with the [Officer Saber](Officer_Saber.md) and [Cavalry Saber.](Cavalry_Saber.md)
-   The critical has a very inconsistent hitbox, which will likely be patched in a later date.
-   Despite the weapon's description stating Trahn striking foes with the pommel, this has no reflection in-game.
