# Deepwoken Cloak

Template:EquipmentStats

## Info

**Deepwoken Cloak** is a legendary armor piece in the arms category. Equipping it grants you the [Blade Dancer](Talents.md) talent, including the normal stats that come with the armor piece. You can obtain the cloak inside [Primadon Chests](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md). There's 2 variants of the Deepwoken Cloak, white and black.

## Trivia

-   The **Deepwoken Cloak** was added along with the game's release but was soon removed for some reason; there were only 3 known people that had it after it's removal. Fast forward to the **10th of May 2022** and the cloak has been re-added by the developer Arch\_Mage.
