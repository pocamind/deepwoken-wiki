## Description

The Chef's Toque is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category. Equipping it grants you 10 Health, one guaranteed Legendary Pip, and the [Talent](Talents.md) Gourmand and the unique equipment [Talents](Talents.md#equipment-talents) _Umami_ and _Gourmand_. It is found exclusively in chests from [Chef Odiolavoro](Chef_Odiolavoro.md)'s quest.

_Umami_ grants a 35% chance for all chef weapons ([Long Tong of The Law](The_Long_Tong_of_The_Law.md), [Pastry Paster](The_Pastry_Paster.md), and [Flippers of Fate](The_Flippers_of_Fate.md)) as well as [Fondant Splitter](Fondant_Splitter.md) to steal 1.95 \* ln(D) + 1.075 hunger and thirst from your opponent on weapon hits, where D = scaled damage dealt (calculator [here](https://www.desmos.com/calculator/yktgme7xnc)). This has a 3 second cooldown per proc attempt, going on cooldown even if it didn't proc. This stacks additively with [Gluttony](Gluttony.md).  
_Gourmand_ grants increased sustenance from consuming food.
