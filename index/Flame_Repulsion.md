**Flame Repulsion** is a basic Combat Mantra used by [Flamecharmers](Flamecharm.md). You must have the _Flamecharmer_ [Talent Card](Talents.md) to use this.

## Usage

When using **Flame Repulsion**, the user will wind-up an attack, being unable to move for about two seconds before releasing a wave of flame in all directions. The player takes reduced damage when winding up.

**Flame Repulsion** deals moderate damage, knocks players back and inflicts burn upon targets.

Blocking this spell will still knock you back, but you will not be inflicted with damage or burn.

## Modifications

[Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md) will result in their stated effects. There are no Sparks that apply to this Mantra.

This Mantra can be improved with the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Thorns of Fire_.
