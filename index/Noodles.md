## Description

**Noodles** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is also purchased from the [Travelling Merchant](Travelling_Merchant.md), sold at random, and obtained from [Chef Odiolavoro](Chef_Odiolavoro.md) as a quest reward.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 30% Stomach and 5% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Egg](Egg.md) and 2 [Gathered Wheat](Gathered_Wheat.md), no [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) required.
