# Fisher Hut

The fisher hut is an location in the [Etrean Luminant](Luminants.md). It can be found when sailing from [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) to [Erisia](Erisia.md). The hut itself is on an small island with a [boat spawner](Ships_&_Sailing.md).

### Overview

There's a hut and few palms on the island as well as an [boat spawner](Ships_&_Sailing.md). Inside of the hut, you can buy a [Fishing Rod](Fishing_Rod.md) for 30 notes and [Chum](Chum.md) for 10 notes, also [Mortus](Mortus.md) can be found here. Next to the hut is a [Gear cabinet](Training_Gear.md). If you go left from the hut, you'll pass trough a small alley and youl'll be at a [fishing spot](Fishing.md).

### Trivia

-   Generally, this place is good for [Fishing](Fishing.md), because people don't come here often and if you fish at the fishing spot you're tehnically uninteruptable.
-   You can set up to 3 [Monster Baits](Monster_Bait.md) here alongside being able to buy them here.
-   The place is in the open sea but still secluded by a mountain
