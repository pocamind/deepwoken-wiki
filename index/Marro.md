## Description

Brown is a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) NPC who resides on the islands of [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), he is apparently unrelated to any faction. You can find him sitting in the grass near the mine entrance trying to find something for his village's Elder. When talking to him, he tells us a little about the culture of his people and especially about the Chrysid Elders and their characteristics.

## Dialogue

Talking to him:

Hum, hum...

1.  What are you doing here?

Ah! Excuse me. I'm simply looking for little kinck knacks for my Elder. He requested, ah... something full of life.

1.  Elder?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

The Chrysid Elder of my village, yes. Traditionally they are often deemed an Elder once blessed with true wisdom, as evident by their neck mane.

1.  What do you mean?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

When a Chrysid reaches a point of great wisdom, they soon are bleesed with a neck mane as proof o f their knowledge. Very few Chrysids tend to possess one, it's a great honor to be able to serve him.

1.  \[End Dialogue\]
