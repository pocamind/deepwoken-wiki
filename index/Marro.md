## Description

Brown is a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) NPC who resides on the islands of [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), he is apparently unrelated to any faction. You can find him sitting in the grass near the mine entrance trying to find something for his village's Elder. When talking to him, he tells us a little about the culture of his people and especially about the Chrysid Elders and their characteristics.

## Dialogue

Talking to him:

Hum, hum...

1.  What are you doing here?

Ah! Excuse me. I'm simply looking for little knick knacks for my Elder. He requested, ah... something full of life.

1.  Elder?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

Elder of our little community out here. Traditionally, elders are given their title once they're considered 'blessed with true wisdom', as evident by their neck manes - but we ain't really ones to stick to that tradition out here.

1.  What do you mean?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

You may have heard tales of the Lullaby Isles ... the Moonlit Faith ... all that sort. We're kinda like that, but we do things our own way. Every one of us from our little community hails from that place. Whether they left out of anger, sadness, or pure curiosity - they're all welcome.

1.  Why did you leave the Lullaby Isles?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

Had a tiny spat with a scholarmate of mine. Nothing serious, just some choice words said. He absolutely did not take it well - threatened to name me a heretic! Blackmail me over _nothing!_ And, well ... if you don't know what happens to heretics ... it ain't pretty. So, I hopped out. Decided to find my own way without fearing for my life, 'cause of some reckless idiot. Best choice I've ever made.

1.  \[End Dialogue\]
