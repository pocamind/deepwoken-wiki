## Description

Sentinel Faris Kesset is a [Tiran](Tiran.md) NPC. She was part of the Shock Trooper division for [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md).

She wielded the [Duskguard Axe](Duskguard_Axe.md). As signified by her [resonance](Resonance.md), she was moderately strong.

She was eventually slain by [Titus Fulminus Destellus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md) and was succeeded by [Sentinel Augustus](Sentinel_Augustus.md).

## Location

Sentinel Faris Kesset is located on a balcony at the large castle in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).
