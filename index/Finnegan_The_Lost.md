# Finnegan The Lost

## Description

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0 The Drowned Delver is an enemy found in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md). It spawns as a job for [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md), much like [The Fisherman](The_Fisherman.md). They use the [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) attunement and a [Boltcrusher](Boltcrusher.md). They have a somewhat large health pool and do a decent amount of damage. Because it is a job for [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md), it can spawn at [The Chariot's Spire](<The_Chariot's_Spire.md>).

The Drowned Delver uses their signature weapon, the [Boltcrusher](Boltcrusher.md).

## Tips

-   Like [The Fisherman](The_Fisherman.md), you can bring them to [Light's Causeway](<Light's_Causeway.md>) to make the fight easier, and just parry until they are killed, or use the enchantment [Providence Thorns](Enchantments.md).
-   The [Boltcrusher](Boltcrusher.md) is a 100% drop chance, so it is easy to farm to get more stars on the weapon.
