## Description

Pathfinders are NPCs that can appear in [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md) alongside a [Teacher](Teacher.md).

A Pathfinder wears a [Pathfinder Cloak](Pathfinder_Cloak.md) with their [aspect](Aspects.md) varying.
