## Description

**Alsin** is a [Celestial](Celestial.md), and a very prominent figure in the religion of [the Justicar](The_Justicar.md). He's supposed to be the judge of every Justicar's Path, after their life ends.[\[1\]](#cite-note-polis-1)

As [The Justicar](The_Justicar.md) are taught to interpret The Path in their own way, nobody knows what they have to do in order to get **Alsin**'s approval. The only thing [the Justicar](The_Justicar.md) can do after they finish their path is pray for Alsin to have mercy on them.[\[1\]](#cite-note-polis-1)
