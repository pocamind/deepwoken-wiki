## Description

**Doom of Caeranthil's Scale** is a direct drop from [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md). Somewhat rare drop chance.

## Uses

-   Trading with [The Gaunt Man](The_Gaunt_Man.md) for an [Aegis Gem](Aegis_Gem.md).
