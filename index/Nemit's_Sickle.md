## Description

The Nemit's Sickle is a dagger that boasts a 30% penetration against armor and an extremely high swing speed like most of the other daggers. It does less damage than few other daggers, however its high penetration and high scaling make up for it.

## Talents

Equipping the Nemit's Sickle grants its unique [weapon Talent](Talents.md#weapon-talents)(s). Weapon Talents will be lost upon equipping any other weapon, including ability-bound weapons, like from the [Crazy Slots](Crazy_Slots.md) Resonance.

[**Curved Edge**](Talents.md#curved-edge) grants +30% chip damage to all [Light Weapon Mantras](Attunement-less.md#light-weapon-mantras).

## Critical

The user winds up for 0.45 seconds and then unleashes a quick upward slash before unleashing a strong side kick, dealing high damage and high posture damage on block. This knocks the target back, even if the move is blocked and deals about 16 posture damage.

The first hit has a 1.2x scaled damage multiplier and applies [Bleed](Status_Effects.md#bleed). The second hit has a scaled damage multiplier of 0.65x.

The critical has a cooldown of **4 seconds**.
