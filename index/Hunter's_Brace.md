# Hunter's Brace

_If you wish to see useful information on all Equipment, Go to [**Equipment**](Equipment.md)_ Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Hunter's Brace is an item under the “Arms” category, purchased on the first floor of the castle on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md). It allows you to parry attacks that can only be dodged including [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) Kick, [Thresher](Thresher.md) Burrow, as well as other unparryable attacks.

## Trivia

-   The item is temporary when used, it disappears when you logout if you chose to put it on.
-   This item increases your chances of surviving more than 1 monster that uses non-parryable moves up by a whole lot since you'll actually be able to avoid ALL damage and not get unlucky when you are hit by an unparryable move with your roll on cooldown.
