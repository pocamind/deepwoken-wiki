# Hunter's Brace

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Hunter's Brace is an item purchased on the first floor of the castle on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md). It allows you to parry attacks that can only be dodged including [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) Kick, [Thresher](Thresher.md) Burrow, as well as other unparryable attacks.

## Trivia

-   The item is temporary, it disappears when you logout.
-   Outclassed by the "Force Your Way" talent from the [Ignition Deepdelver](Ignition_Deepdelver.md) outfit.
