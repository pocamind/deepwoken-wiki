# Memorial Masons

“ they seemed a little scared of me, like a death omen. i mean... i guess we \*do\* wear all black, have rugged hands, and come off as rather nonchalant in such a morbid place... ”

― Simeon Rasimof recounts an encounter with a group of new Divers

The Memorial Masons, coloquially known as the "Deathscribes", are a subdivision of [The Divers](The_Divers.md) consisting of stonemasons. Their job is to carve the names of retired or, more likely, deceased Divers into stone monuments. They are led by a [High Mason](High_Mason.md), or "Head Deathscribe". The current High Mason is [Horus Helsen](Horus_Helsen.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-0-1)[\[2\]](#cite-note-dj-2)

Their uniform is all black.[\[2\]](#cite-note-dj-2)
