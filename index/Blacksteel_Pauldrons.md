## Description

The Blacksteel Pauldrons are an equipment item in the arms category. The stats and pips gained from equipping them have not been documented. They are commonly found in chests from [Erisia](Erisia.md), and can be dropped from [Blacksteel Pirates.](Blacksteel_Pirate.md)
