## Overview

The Lost Forge is a hidden forge within [Lost Forge of Leiuna](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md). It is mainly used to craft and transform some weapons into a similar [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) variant of said weapon.

To access the Lost Forge, the [Interluminary Parasol](Interluminary_Parasol.md) must hit the door with one of its tentacles, after that the door will be destroyed and players will be allowed to enter it.

  

## Convertible Weapons
