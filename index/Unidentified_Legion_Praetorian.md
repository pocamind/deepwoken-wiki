## Description

A Legion Praetorian is said to have used the [Imperial Staff](Imperial_Staff.md) while in service to [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md). The Praetorian is believed to have used it to destroy entire towns during the Legions' expansion.[\[1\]](#cite-note-is-1)

The Praetorian and his weapon have since attained a legendary reputation, though the truth regarding their feats are a matter of some debate.[\[1\]](#cite-note-is-1)
