# Cathedral of the Interstice

LocationL Cathedral of Insterstice LocationR

TERRITORY OF Lost Celtor

  

The **Cathedral of the Interstice** is a sub-location in the depths. It is the main way for a player to escape the [Depths](The_Depths.md) through the Depths Trial. It contains the [Shrine of Courage](Shrine_of_Courage.md) that heavily increases the difficulty of the trial, often adding a [Bone Keeper](Bone_Keeper.md) onto what you normally need to fight but giving you enough experience to invest 15 stat points. There are few ways to enter this area, the first being a gate in the direct of commerce in the [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md) that is opened after ringing 4 bells located around the city. The second is coming up the walls and jumping in. There are other more obscure methods, such as using the [Dimensional Travel Resonance](Dimensional_Travel.md).

  
Once you use the lift to exit the depths, you may face certain enemies. For more info on the trial go to this page: [First Layer](First_Layer.md).
