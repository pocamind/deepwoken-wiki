## Description

A personal use lantern one can lug around to combat the parasites of the Eternal Gale at a cost of some movement speed. This item provides the [Wormwarder](Talents.md#wormwarder) Talent, which makes you completely immune to parasites in the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md) at the cost of movement speed.

To craft this item, you can obtain the recipe from interacting with a Lantern in the Second Layer, and then crafting the item with 2 [Galewax](Galewax.md), 4 [Irithine](Ores.md#irithine), and 4 [Spark Glands](Spark_Gland.md). Because you cannot bring Galewax out of the Second Layer, you have to use [Master Craftsman](Talents.md#master-craftsman) or the crafting table at [The Frontier Furnace](The_Frontier_Furnace.md).

## Trivia

-   You are unable to have Galewax outside of the Second Layer, meaning you must craft this item within the Second Layer. A crafting table is available in the Frontier Furnace, else, the Master Craftman talent will let you craft it without the table.
-   This [Equipment](Equipment.md) piece is somewhat redundant due to the existence of the [Grand Fisher Helm](Grand_Fisher_Helm.md), which not only gives the same effect without a downside, but is also obtainable without even stepping foot into the Gale and is far easier to get.
-   Due to the movement speed debuff from using this item, it is only useful for long expeditions within the Eternal Gale.
-   The Wormward Lantern does not actually clear parasites, it only disables their ability to continue growing- meaning you will keep any parasites you had before equipping it.
-   You cannot store this item in a safe deposit box, or drop it.
