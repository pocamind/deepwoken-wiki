## Description

The Circle of Honour is an organization whose main goal is to defeat [Zi'eer, the Fourth Prophet](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>) of [the Ministry](The_Ministry.md). They go through [Mementos](Mementos.md): Memories of the fallen Circle of Honour members to kill him in the past and find a way defeat him in the present.

All of the deceased [Mementos](Mementos.md) were said to be capable of defeating [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md), but failed to do so and ultimately fell to his hand. Now the only known surviving members of the Circle of that time are [Reclaimer of the Recollection](Reclaimer_of_the_Recollection.md) and [Warden Jericho](Warden_Jericho.md).

The Circle is allied with the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md), due to both sharing the cause of wanting to take down Zi'eer.

## Notable Members

[The Inheritor](The_Inheritor.md) - The main protagonist of [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md). They have the ability to go through memories of fallen Circle members via [Mementos](Mementos.md). Their main objective is to find [Zi'eer](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>)['s](The_Lord_Regent.md) weaknesses through these mementos.

[Reclaimer of the Recollection](Reclaimer_of_the_Recollection.md) - One of the known surviving member of the Circle of Honour. He serves as the tutorial, progression and conclusion NPC in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md). He puts the player in [the Recollection](The_Recollection.md) and helps them go through the [Mementos](Mementos.md) with the ultimate goal of defeating [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md).

[Warden Jericho](Warden_Jericho.md) - The other known surviving member of the Circle. He serves as the Warden of the Eastern Luminant for the [Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md). He fights against [Etrea](Etrea.md) mostly to get rid of [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md) and free the people. He is one of the known users of the Warmaster [oath](Oaths.md).

[Maestro Alkhurst Legato](Maestro_Alkhurst_Legato.md) - The previous leader of the Isle of Vigils before [Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md). He is a senior member of the Circle of Honour. He lived longer than most of the other members due to being unable to go fight [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md). Although most of his deeds are unknown, he is considered to have made many major contributions by the [Reclaimer](Reclaimer_of_the_Recollection.md). He met his end fighting against Zi'eer's forces, permanently driving them away from the Isle of Vigils. In game, he gives context on what is going on in [Etris](<Etris_(Vow_of_Iron).md>). He is also a wielder of the Warmaster [oath](Oaths.md).
