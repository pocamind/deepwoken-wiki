## Description

Circle of Honour is an organization, whose main goal is to defeat [Zi'eer, the Fourth Prophet](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>) of [the Ministry](The_Ministry.md). They go through [Mementos](Mementos.md): Memories of the fallen Circle of Honour members to kill him in the past and find a way defeat him in the present.

The Circle is allied with the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md), despite the player having Enemy [Reputation](Reputation.md) with them from the start. It is explained that's because of the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md) assuming the player is "another sellsword".
