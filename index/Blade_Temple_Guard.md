# Blade Temple Guard

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Blade Temple Guards are reputable NPCs that can be found on the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) guarding the front of the temple gate and entrance to the temple.

## Moveset

A Blade Temple Guard's arsenal consists of a [Katana](Katana.md) along with the [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md) Attunement:

## Finisher

A Blade Temple Guard executes the player after knocking them:

| Name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Execute | User pins down the opponent and slices their throat with their blade. |

## Trivia

-   Easier to kill than [Etrean Guards](Etrean_Guardsmen.md) because unlike them, they are affected by [PVE Scaling](Power.md).
-   When the game first released, they did not have Mantras.
-   Their dialogue won't change depending on the player's reputation with Etrea.
