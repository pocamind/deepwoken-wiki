# Blade Temple Guard

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Blade Temple Guards are reputable NPCs that can be found on the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) guarding the front of the temple gate and entrance to the temple. Occasionally, they will defend themselves once some [Bandits](Bandit.md) spawn in front of the temple.

## Moveset

| Name | Description | Parryable? | Blockable? | Danger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Assault | Firmly swings sword at opponent and finishes the combo by kicking them. | ◯ | ◯ | (★☆☆) |
| Critical | Sheathes blade before dashing forward and unleashing a quick slash. | ◯ | ✗ | (★★☆) |

### Boons

-   Experienced Fighter - Can parry and block common attacks and mantras.

## Finisher

A Blade Temple Guard executes the player after knocking them:

| Name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Execute | User pins down the opponent and slices their throat with their blade. |

## Trivia

-   Despite being affiliated with Etrea, they are highly inclusive, as well as staying away from their war with The Authority.
-   Easier to kill than Etrean Guards
-   Despite having a small health pool, they appear to be extremely resistant to most forms of damage, thus making them formidable enemies even at higher levels.
-   Their dialogue won't change depending on the player's reputation with Etrea.
