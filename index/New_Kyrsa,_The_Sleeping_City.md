## Trivia

-   You do not have to escape floor 2 via defeating the Scion of Ethiron in order to keep your quest progress. Talking to Carrion will still make him appear in castle light even if you lighthook up or die within lighthook's time frame. (Confirmed)
-   The hostile NPCs worshipping the fifth pylon are actually Black Divers who have been corrupted by the mist present on floor 2.
-   The Krysgarde artillery on the outskirts of the city can be avoided by standing under cover. The spears do not go through objects.
-   You have a total of 30 minutes to activate all the Pylons before dying. After the first 15 minutes, you will be sent back to the start but all the Pylons will remain active. On the second, you will instantly die. The time can be tracked by the Old Diver Watch you gain after entering the floor.
-   After activating all the Pylons, time will halt and you may freely explore the floor without being reset.
-   The Krysgarde Artillery may be a reference to the chase scene with Undyne in the game Undertale.
