## Contents

New Kyrsa is a beautiful and huge place with tons of buildings and Canticlysm technologies, such as trains or artillery. Currently, Kyrsa doesn't have any human dwellers, since it got sunk deep into The Depths and suffers from Space-time collapse. You can meet alot of Kyrsgardes here and only one is not hostile, his name is Naerotiv. Kyrsa contains alot of pink glowing chests, aka Medallion chests. They contain from 5 to 7 Kyrsan medallions, which you can exchange for dyes, enchant stones, enchant armors, knowledge or Dawnwalker oath. In order to get out of New Kyrsa, you have to activate all 5 Obelisks and defeat [Scion of Ethiron.](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) This place is currently most challenging places in-game, since you can meet alot of Kyrsgardes that can easily kill you, and you can die here easily overall. Even some Black Divers got stuck here forever or died. If you die here, you will instantly wipe, or if you still have Light hook you will get back to [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md).

## Trivia

-   Before New Kyrsa sunk deep into The Depths, it was a massive golden age (or Canticlysm) city built by other people, with it's own railways and brand new technologies, such as teleports, mechanic doors, artillery, or even trains. After New Kyrsa sunk, most people died, and city got inahbited by Kyrsgardes. Now it's under control of Scion of Ethiron, due to failed sealing ritual, called "The Ninefold Blessings of Marrow". New Kyrsa became "bedchamber" and "shrine" to Scion of Ethiron, "puppet" of Ethiron, the Eye of Calamity
-   You do not have to escape floor 2 via defeating the Scion of Ethiron in order to keep your quest progress. Talking to Carrion will still make him appear in castle light even if you lighthook up or die within lighthook's time frame. (Confirmed)
-   The hostile NPCs worshipping the fifth Obelisk are actually Black Divers who have been corrupted by the mist present on floor 2.
-   The Krysgarde artillery on the outskirts of the city can be avoided by standing under cover. The spears do not go through objects.
-   You have a total of 45 minutes to activate all the Obelisks before dying. After the first and second 15 minutes, you will be sent back to the start but all the Obelisks will remain active. On the third, you will instantly die. The time can be tracked by the Old Diver Watch you gain after entering the floor.
-   This is because New Kyrsa suffers from time dilation, like whole Second Layer. It's also called Space-time collapse, and the reason why it exists is unkown. That means New Kyrsa exists in infinite amount of realities at the same time, and every human stuck here is doomed to stay here forever and suffer from Space-time collapse.
-   Looks like you can find some old city ​​dwellers who have lost their minds and gone insane from infinite amount of time resets.
-   After activating all the Obelisks, time will halt and you may freely explore the floor without being reset.
-   The Krysgarde Artillery may be a reference to the chase scene with Undyne in the game Undertale.
