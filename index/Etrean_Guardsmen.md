# Etrean Guardsmen

## Description

Although not fully considered an enemy unless you provoke them or have bad reputation inside Etrea, the Etrean Guardsmen are NPC type of enemies that are found in Etrea. Doing actions such as hitting them three times or spitting on them (yes I'm not kidding) will cause the guard to aggro on you. Typically after defeating them more will come after you. After defeating a lot of the Etrean Guards, the King's Guards will come after you.

Etrean Guardsmen are found all around Etrea in different locations.

### Moves

-   **Different Weapons:** Fast Normal M1 hits

-   **Galebreath:** Gale Lunge.

### Tips

-   Just don't get bad reputation or do any of thr actions considered by the guards to be "disrespectful".
