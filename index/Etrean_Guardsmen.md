# Etrean Guardsmen

## Description

Etrean Guardsmen are NPC Type enemies that are found in Etrea. Typically after defeating them more will come after you.

Etrean Guardsmen are found all around Etrea in different locations.

### Moves

-   **Different Weapons:** Fast Normal M1 hits

-   **Galebreath:** Gale Lunge.

### Tips

-   Just don't get bad rep.
