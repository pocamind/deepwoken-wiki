# Etrean Guardsmen

## Description

Although not fully considered an enemy until you provoke them or approach them with bad reputation with Etrea, the Etrean Guardsmen are NPC type of enemies that are found in [Etris](Etris.md). Doing actions such as hitting them three times or spitting on them (yes I'm not kidding) will cause the guard to aggro on you. Typically after defeating them more will come after you. After defeating a lot of the Etrean Guards, the King's Guards will begin to come after you.

Etrean Guardsmen can be found all around [Etris](Etris.md) at different locations.
