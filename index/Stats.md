# Stats

Stats or Attributs are numerical representations of a character abilities in certain domains. Each stat grant different benefits the more they are leveled, as well as beeing necessary to equip certain items or weapons.

## Purpose of stats

-   **Statistical advantage:** Having higher stats means that your raw numbers like health or ether are higher, making you more efficient in combat or for other tasks.
-   **Armor, weapon and item requirements:** Certain items require the player to have a high enough stat of a given field (sometime 2) to be equipable/purchaseable.
-   **Talent pool:** Certain talent are locked to certain stats thereshold and will not be drawn upon a Power Up. Leveling up a stat allow you to obtain more varied talent cards related to that skill. (Someone with too low Charisma would not be able to get Vow of Mastery for example)

## How does upgrading a stat work?

Upgrading a stat require the player to:

-   perform actions related to that stat
-   have used/equipped that stat specific training item

Each stats have an associated EXP value (that is not shown to the player). Performing certain actions give EXP to the related stat. Once the stat is ready to level up and is being trained, it will level up.

You have 2 possible moment to use training items:

-   Before working on the stat. The message "You feel your (stat name) improving..." means that once that stat reach the next EXP thereshold, it will automatically level up
-   After working on the stat. The message "You feel like your ability in (stat name) has potential... With the right equipment, you could draw it out..." whenever you reach that stat current EXP thereshold (or is near) without having used the training item. Using the training item now or later will immediately level up the stat.
    -   Even if the stat is not leveling up due to the lack of training item, EXP gains are still accounted, which can potentially lead to multiple level up in a row with multiple use of the training item.

**Note**: You may eventually reach a point where you receive a message saying "I've learnt what I can from training right now, I should put it into practice" when using a training item. If that's the case, you must explore and kill hostile NPCs, monsters or players to progress further.

## Strength

"Your raw physical strength"

Strength affect your ability to carry items in your inventory. Every point of strength increase your maximum weight capacity, at a rate of 1/1.

Actions that grant Strength EXP: Cutting trees, mining, carrying objects, using the Boulder (passive gain while in use)

Training item: Dumbell, Boulder

## Fortitude

"Your ability to resist damage and diseases"

Fortitude affects your ability to subtain damage. Every point in Fortitude increase your maximum HP, at a rate of 1/1.

Actions that grant Fortitude EXP: taking damage, using the Boulder (passive gain while in use)

Training item: Training Vest, Boulder

## Agility

"Your slight hand and nimbleness"

Agility affect your ability to manoeuver around and your ability to sneak up on enemies. Every point in agility increase Wall climb Height and Stealth, which make you harder to notice from enemy while crounched.

Actions that grant Agility EXP: Vaulting, Wall Climbing, Wall Jumping

Training Item: Ankle Weight

**Note**: Certain talents grant "Passive Agility" which is only used to improve mobility and stealth and is not accounted for in armor/weapons requirements.

## Intelligence

"Your logical processing and problem solving skills"

Intelligence affect your ability to cast Mantras for longer periods of time. Every point in intelligence increase Ether and Reservoir, at a rate of 1/2 for both.

Actions that grant Intelligence EXP: Gathering, using the Math Book and getting the right answer.

Training Item: Encyclopedia, Math Book

## Willpower

"Your inner strength and ability to keep a steady mind"

Willpower affect your ability to handle yourself psychologically while in dangerous areas. Every point in Willpower increase Sanity at a rate of 1/? and increase Reservoir at rate of 1/1.

Actions that grant Willpower EXP: Using the Praying Beads (passive gain while in use), being in dangerous areas such as the Valley of Heroes or The Depth

Training item: Praying Beads

## Charisma

"Your ability to influence others with your personality"

Charisma affect your ability to manipulate certain dialogue options with certain NPCs. Every point in Charisma grant Ether, at a rate of 1/1.

Actions that grant Charisma EXP: Small talk with "How to make friends", selling items to the Antiquarian

Training item: How to make friend

## Heavy/Medium/Light Weapon

"Your ability to wield Heavy Weapons such as Greataxes, Greatswords and Greathammers/Medium Weapons such as Swords, Axes and Spears/Light Weapons such as Daggers, Guns and Fists"

Heavy/Medium/Light weapon affect your ability use a type of weaponary. Weapons have base damage + extra damage based on a % of their given stat.

Actions that grant Heavy/Medium/Light EXP: Hitting/blocking/parrying with a weapon of that specific family.

Training item: Weapon Manual
