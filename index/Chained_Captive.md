## Description

The Chained Captive is an [Adret](Adret.md) NPC. He is an [Authority Soldier](Authority_Soldier.md) captured by [Emar Vend](Emar_Vend.md).

He is required to rescue in order to obtain [Chainwarden](Oath%253A_Chainwarden.md).

## Location

The Chained Captive can be found in the abandoned castle on a side island from [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md).
