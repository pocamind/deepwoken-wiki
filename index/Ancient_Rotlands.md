# Ancient Rotlands

LocationL Ancient Rotlands LocationR

The scent of decay suffocates this murky land, once the home of a fallen Celestial.

  

The Ancient Rotlands is location in the East Luminant accessible through the [Songseeker wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). The area looks like a large swamp, with large objects that look and beat like hearts suspended on chains over the ground.
