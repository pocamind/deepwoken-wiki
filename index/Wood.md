# Wood

“ How much wood would a woodchuck chuck if a woodchuck could chuck wood? ”

― A conspicuous Celtor

Template:ItemTemplateNEW

**Wood** is an essential resource used to craft tools and other materials.

## Obtainment

Wood can be can be obtained in a few ways:

-   Chopping trees with an [Lumber Axe](Lumber_Axe.md).
-   Obliterating trees with Strong Left (With the [Treefelling Blow](Talents.md#treefelling-blow) Talent).
-   Bought for or depending on the vendor.
-   Crafting together three (3) Sticks.

## Uses

Wood can be used for the following:

-   Crafting a [Campfire Pit](Campfire_Pit.md).
-   Crafting a [Lumber Axe](Lumber_Axe.md) or [Pickaxe](Pickaxe.md).
-   Crafting a [Repair Kit](Repair_Kit.md).
-   Crafting into three (3) [Sticks](Stick.md).
-   Turning it in to [Korin](Korin.md) or [Drefa](Drefa.md) for [Etris](Etris.md) [Reputation](Reputation.md).
-   Repairing Boats.

## Locations

Wood can be sold from the following locations:

**Etrean Luminant:**

**Etris:**

**Blacksmith:**

The wood at the [Etris](Etris.md) [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) is located at the corner of the room.

-   Costs.

**Docks:**

The wood at the Etris Docks is located near the Repair Hammer.

-   Costs.

**General Store:**

The wood at the Etris General Store is located on two tables opposite of each corner.

-   Costs.

**Isle of Vigils:**

The wood at the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) is located outside of the Inn.

-   Costs.

**Voidheart:**

The wood at the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md) is located on a table next to the [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md).

-   Costs.
-   The Voidheart can be accessed from both the Etrean and Eastern [Luminants](Luminants.md) by [Voidwalkers](Origins.md).

**Eastern Luminant:**

**Greathive Aratel:**

The wood at [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) is located on a bench in the Canopy Plaza's balcony.

-   Costs.

**Miner's Landing:**

The wood at [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) is located on top of a barrel next to the Antiquarian.

**Summer Isle:**
