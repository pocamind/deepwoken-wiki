The Bluesteel Pauldrons are an equipment item in the arms category. The stats and Pips gained from equipping them have not been documented. They are commonly found in chests from [Aratel Island](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md), [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md), and [Erisia](Erisia.md).
