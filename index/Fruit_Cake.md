## Description

**Fruit Cake** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 25% Stomach and 12% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   2 different [Fruits](Ingredients.md#fruits) and 1 [Gathered Wheat](Gathered_Wheat.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
