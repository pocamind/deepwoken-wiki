# Korilfiend, the Inferno's Provenance

**Content is not present in-game**  
The subject of this page is mentioned in-game, but is not tangibly implemented into the game.  
E.G. a character mentioned in a book doesn't exist as an interactible NPC.

  

## Overview

**Korilfiend, the Inferno's Provenance** is the Drowned God of Heat, slumbering deep within The Vents, the Third Layer of The Depths.

[Black Diver](Black_Divers_division.md) [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md), before being reported as missing in action, merged with a meteor flower that was part of Korilfiend, becoming a [Deepwoken](Deepwoken.md) and inventing [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) as a result. Korilfiend is also the creator of the Forge of Sin, an [artifact](Artifacts.md) formerly held at [Temple of the Forgotten Flame](The_Temple_of_the_Forgotten_Flame.md), stolen by [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) for his own nefarious purposes.

## Trivia

-   The [Deepfire Ring](Deepfire_Ring.md) is described to be Korilfiend's; if it was used or created by them is unknown.
-   [Crimson Megalodaunts](Crimson_Megalodaunt.md) appear with a dark red color because they have been exposed to The Vents and have mutated enough to inherit [Flamecharming](Flamecharm.md). [Venn Kaelhand](Venn_Kaelhand.md), the former owner and likely creator of the [First Light](First_Light.md) is known to have met their demise to one.
