# Korilfiend, the Inferno's Provenance

**Korilfiend, Drowned God of Fire,** is a Drowned God.

## Overview

Nothing is known about Korilfiend besides their name, title, and that the apparently rogue Black Diver, Amorus Pleeksty, has eaten a part of them in the Third Layer. What role they will play in the lore, if any at all, is unknown.
