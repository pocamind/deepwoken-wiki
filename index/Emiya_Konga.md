## Description

Emiya Konga was an infamous thief,[\[1\]](#cite-note-k-1) who a wore a [dark coat](<Konga's_Parka.md>).[\[4\]](#cite-note-4)[\[5\]](#cite-note-5) Konga was the master of an artform known as the Slider Style,[\[6\]](#cite-note-kongaiii2-6) which was utilized for a variety of mischief.[\[3\]](#cite-note-kongaiii1-3)

They possessed [a ring](<Konga's_Clutch_Ring.md>) but eventually lost it.[\[1\]](#cite-note-k-1)

## Trivia

-   This character, as well as their great grandson [Konga The Third](Konga_The_Third.md), [Konga's Clutch Ring](<Konga's_Clutch_Ring.md>) and [Konga's Parka](<Konga's_Parka.md>), is primarily a reference to bcongeboy5, a tester during _Deepwoken'_s development who was notorious for spamming sliding movements and attacks.
