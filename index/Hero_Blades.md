# Hero Blades

**The Hero Blades** are a set of weapons available in Deepwoken. They take the shape of swords, which are brightly colored in their given element, with runes writtens on them.

## Obtention

The Hero Blades are obtainable in the Valley of Heroes, as a guaranted drop (element is random) in the chest spawned by defeating the [Snow Knights](Stone_Knight.md).

## Elemental blades

There are 5 different Hero Blade, each one representing a given [Attunement](Attunements.md):

-   Hero Blade of Flames, requiring 75 Flamecharm to wield
-   Hero Blade of Frost, requiring 75 Frostdraw to wield
-   Hero Blade of Lightning, requiring 75 Thundercall to wield
-   Hero Blade of Wind, requiring 75 Galebreath to wield
-   Hero Blade of Shadow, requiring 75 Shadowcast to wield

## Builds

-   The Hero Blade shines in builds that put a great emphasis in their attunement, going for the cap of 75 or even 100 with Lord Regent's quest usually. This is because, despite them taking the shape of swords, their scaling is based on the user's Attunement level and not their Medium Weapon level.
    -   By omitting the usage of points to level up Medium Weapon, you can save up to 100 points to spend on your other Attributes, such as your core attributes or a second attunement.
