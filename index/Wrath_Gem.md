## Description

Wrath Gem is an light red diamond-shaped [Deep Gem](Deep_Gems.md).

## Effect

Upon landing a Mantra with a Wrath Gem equipped, apply the [Wrath](Status_Effects.md#wrath) status effect for 9 seconds, increasing your critical attack damage by 7.5% per player combat tag you have. This effect has a 10 second cooldown.

### Notes

-   If you are using the [Reinforced War Set](Reinforced_War_Helmet.md), then the Wrath Gem is best used on [Strength Mantras](Attunement-less.md#strength-mantras), to proc their equipment Talents. If not, then this Gem will be most effective on any easy-to-hit Mantra with a low cooldown.
-   If you have the Brain [Food Buff](Status_Effects.md#food-buff) from the [Grilled Parasol Brain](Grilled_Parasol_Brain.md), Wrath Gem's damage buff per player combat tag will be increased from 7.5% to 15%.
-   As previously mentioned, the Wrath status effect can gain additional effects through the equipment Talents in the [Reinforced War Set](Reinforced_War_Helmet.md).
-   For usage with [Critical Engine](Talents.md#critical-engine), the damage buff will not be applied to the hit that procced the Deep Gem, but will be applied to subsequent hits after granting the Wrath status effect. It is highly suggested that you use a multi-hitting critical for this Gem.
-   This damage buff is affected by the damage modifier cap.
