# Unidentified swordsman

| Unidentified swordsman | |
|---|---|

“ I'M PARRYING IT! ”

― The swordsman parrying the Duke's attack

## Description

The swordsman was part of an [adventuring party](Unidentified_adventuring_party.md) who were tasked by the population of an island to slay [their former Duke](<The_Duke_(manga).md>), who had become corrupt and threatened to destroy the land.

The group arrived at the [Northern Temple](Northern_Temple.md) and confronted the Duke.

## Trivia

-   The swordsman, alongside the Duke, features on the cover of the manga collection.
-   Due to being unnamed in the manga itself, the community has taken to nicknaming the swordsman "Perry the parrier", stemming from his infamous quote and the character of Perry the Platapus.
