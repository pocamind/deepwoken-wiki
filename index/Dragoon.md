## **Description**

The Dragoon is a [gun](Guns.md) that can be bought in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) for 500 [Notes](Notes.md) and requires 65 Light Weapon points to equip.

The Dragoon has a base damage of 20 and scales of 12 light weapon. Much like other guns it has a unique attack pattern and has ~12 range~ (used to have 12 range =( ). It fires slower compared to it's revolver and flintlock counterpart, but it packs more of a punch, and carries the same attack speed as its contemporaries. The Dragoon has a damage of 44 at 100 LHT, and 38 at 75 LHT.
