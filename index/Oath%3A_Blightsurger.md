## Description

**[Oath](Oaths.md): Blightsurger** is an Oath associated with [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md).

## Obtainment

To obtain Blightsurger, you first have to defeat Titus. Afterwards select his 2nd dialogue option and go and talk to Brutus. Go back to Titus and he will give you the oath.

Requires Hero Rep with [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md)

## Abilities

-   **Sovereign Drive - Mantra**
    -   Lunge forward and strike with your fist.
    -   Dazes on hit.

-   **Skycrash - Mantra**
    -   Leap forward and stomp with devastating force.
    -   Ragdolls on hit.
    -   Can be used twice in quick succession if the user is in the Sovereign State.

-   **Warp Kick - Mantra**
    -   Reel back and perform a crushing kick.
    -   Teleports to opponents within range.
    -   Applies [Wither](Status_Effects.md#wither) and Blightshock on hit.

-   **Sovereign State - Talent**
    -   Landing physical hits builds your Blight Meter. At full gauge, enter a Blight State that changes your critical and empowers Blightsurge abilities.
    -   Landing attacks that deal physical damage builds up a meter similar to [Jetstriker's](Oath%253A_Jetstriker.md) Momentum meter.
    -   While in Sovereign State, your meter will passively decay.
    -   Replaces your critical with a leaping slam attack, applying knockdown and Blightshock.
    -   Skycrash can be used twice in quick succession while in this state.

-   **Blight Pierce - Talent**
    -   Your Blightshock attacks pierce through your enemies hyperarmor and gain more penetration.

-   **Blighted Touch - Talent**
    -   Landing M1's during Sovereign State now applies Blightshock.

-   **Anchor Shock - Talent**
    -   Your Blightshock prevents targets from using mobility mantras.
