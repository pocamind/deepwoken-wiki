## Description

The Sanguine Transfuser is a support-based Relic Dagger weapon, fused with the [Dormant Splinter](Dormant_Splinter.md). The dagger has a build consisting of spiraling helix of crystallized blood with a pointy edge on the top, constantly emitting mist-like particles of blood. The guard is circular and flares outward with jagged, petal-like projections, resembling a flower. The handle is wrapped in a red leather-like material, and the pommel resembles a crystal.

It is crafted within [The Cold Forge](The_Cold_Forge.md) with:

-   1 [Hemobloom](Hemobloom.md)
-   1 [Ancient Dagger Remains](Ancient_Dagger_Remains.md)
-   1 [Dormant Splinter](Dormant_Splinter.md)

## Critical

The user stabs themselves, applying 40 [Wither](Status_Effects.md#wither) to themselves and draining their blood before spraying it onto the ground. Anyone who stands on the blood pool, including the user will be given healing over time. This does not count as passive regeneration, allowing you to proc [Hemorrhaging Blow](Talents.md#bloodrender) off the healing.

The blood pool will last for 5 seconds before dissipating.

The critical has a cooldown of **19 seconds**.

## Trivia

-   This weapon is held in reverse grip.
-   If the Sanguine Transfuser is brought back to [Seroth](Seroth.md) on the same character that made it and is equipped, he will unlock a unique set of dialogue.
-   This weapon reuses the animations of the [Spectral Grasp](Spectral_Grasp.md).
