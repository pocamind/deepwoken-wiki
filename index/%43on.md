## Description

Con is a Khan NPC that owns a Pawn shop where he sells all kinds of crafting materials. His [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) counterpart is [Hale](Hale.md).

## Location

Con is located at [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) inside the main castle.
