“ Woah, hey. Don't linger around me for too long. You're gonna make 'em suspicious. ”

― Rashet

## Description

Rashet is the NPC who accepts a bribe for [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md); you can pay him up to 1,200 to restore your reputation to neutral.

## Location

Rashet is located by a pillar at [Merit's Reach](<Merit's_Reach.md>) from across the river.
