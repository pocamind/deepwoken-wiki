# Regalia

**Regalia** are passive abilities that positively affect progression.

## Obtainment

-   Regalia can be randomly obtained every time your power increases.
-   The chance of obtaining a Regalia is increased the more progressed your last character was.
-   **You may only have 1 Regalia per character, and your character acquiring a Regalia is not guaranteed.**

## Regalia

A list of all Regalia.

### Mute

Mute, or less formally known as 'Animal King' causes all monster-type mobs to not be able to detect you. All [Monsters](Monsters.md) (with the exceptions of 'Constructs' such as [Golems](Rock_Golem.md), [Blizzard Knights](Stone_Knight.md) and [The Enforcer](Enforcer.md)) will be unable to see the player and therefore wont attack unless provoked. This includes [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) and the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md). Monsters get an AI similar to Town Guards when aggroed; they will unaggro the player if they do not deal damage to the monster for 15 seconds. Most humanoid enemies will still aggro the player on sight. If you wipe with a low enough level, an unknown level under 7, you will get to keep Mute for your next life.[\[1\]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0zHNjRDTsiU)

Activating [Hell Mode](Hell_Mode.md) will disable Mute until it ends.

-   [Immortal Guardians](Immortal_Guardian.md) are affected by Mute.
-   [Brainsuckers](Brainsucker.md) encountered in [Duke Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>) are not affected by Mute.

### Prodigy

Increases [XP](Power.md) gain and [Attribute](Attributes.md) training speed by an unknown amount.

Very similar to the removed [Talents](Talents.md) Adept and Mark of the Lone Warrior.

### Inspired

Grants all [Resonance](Resonance.md) [Paths](Sibex.md) instead of only one.

### Worth

Grants special dialogues with [Sibex](Sibex.md) and the [Legendary Boot](Legendary_Boot.md).

May have more to it, needs testing.

## Trivia

-   For almost the entirety of the game's lifespan, Mute was the only Regalia known to exist, until Arch\_Mage told the community about the others and the obtainment method.
-   Regalia, as a definition, are the emblems or insignia of royalty, especially the crown, scepter, and other ornaments used at a coronation.
-   Mute does not apply to any monsters or humanoids encountered in [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md).
