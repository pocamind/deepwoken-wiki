# Regalia

## Theorized Ways of Obtainment

warning, these are not guaranteed and there is no official known way to obtain these.

Wiping at a high level

Chance after completing the [Trial Of One](Trial_of_One.md)  

## Animal King

> _"Animals will not attack one of their friends, unless the animal is threatened or attacked first." -Fallout 3 Animal Friend Description._

Ability to be neutral with animals. Any creature that is nonhumanoid **Will Not** attack the player.

-   NPC's that resemble humans **Will Still** attack the player. The only known NPC's that resembles humans that will not attack those with the talent are [Ministry Scouts](Ministry_Scout.md) and [The Enforcer](Enforcer.md).
-   Animal Bosses **Will Not** attack you including Primadon
-   Possible reduced progression / XP gain (from personal experience, I did Trial of one and got it afterwards and the "mark of the lone warrior" has not made a difference at all)
