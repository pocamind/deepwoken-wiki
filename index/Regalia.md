## Animal King

> _"Animals will not attack one of their friends, unless the animal is threatened or attacked first." -Fallout 3 Animal Friend Description._

> Animal king isn't really an accurate name since it's all depths related mobs (shadow magic humanoid npcs) but the name was popularized by LordSendo's video

Monsters and certain enemies will be neutral to the player unless provoked. Any creature that is nonhumanoid **Will Not** attack the player. (This excludes [Stone Knights](Stone_Knight.md) & [Erisia Golems](Rock_Golem.md), possibly others.)

-   NPC's that resemble humans ([Enemies](Enemies.md)) **Will Still** attack the player. The only known NPC's that resembles humans that will not attack those with the talent are [Ministry Scouts](Ministry_Scout.md) and [The Enforcer](Enforcer.md).
-   Animal Bosses **Will Not** attack you including Primadon
-   ~Possible reduced item drops in chests? (I'm power 30 and still only get 8 amethyst / 5 sapphire in chests, not sure if I am unlucky.)~ Chest loot seems to be completely unaffected, but we all get unlucky sometimes.
-   The best talent for Sharko-simps to get up close and personal
