# Regalia

**Regalia** are passive abilities that positively affect progression and gameplay. These are mysterious passives which can only be seen through testing or gameplay.

## Obtainment

-   Regalia can be randomly obtained every time your power increases. [\[1\]](#cite-note-archinfo-1)
-   **You may only have 1 Regalia per character, and your character is never guaranteed to obtain a Regalia.**[\[1\]](#cite-note-archinfo-1)

## Regalia

### Mute

Mute, also known as "Animal King" by the community, causes all [Monsters](Monsters.md) (with the exceptions of 'Constructs' such as [Golems](Rock_Golem.md), [Blizzard Knights](Stone_Knight.md) and [The Enforcer](Enforcer.md)) to be non-hostile to you, meaning they won't attack unless provoked. This includes bosses such as [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) and the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md). Monsters get an AI similar to Town Guards when aggroed, as they will de-aggro off the player if they do not deal damage to the monster for 15 seconds. The majority of humanoid enemies remain unaffected by Mute. [Immortal Guardians](Immortal_Guardian.md) are an exception as they are still affected by Mute.

  
Mute is disabled in dungeons, meaning it wont work in [Duke Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>) or [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md) (The Eternal Gale & New Kyrsa). This is also the case in [Hell Mode](Hell_Mode.md), as it temporarily disables mute until its over.

Wiping below an unknown Power (~3) is known to keep the Regalia even if wiped. It is unknown whether this is exclusive to Mute or occurs with all Regalia.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)

### Prodigy

Increases [XP](Power.md) gain and [Attribute](Attributes.md) training speed by an unknown amount.[\[1\]](#cite-note-archinfo-1)

Functions similarly to the removed [Talents](Talents.md) Adept and Mark of the Lone Warrior, as well as the Maverick boon.

### Inspired

Grants all [Resonance](Resonance.md) [Paths](Sibex.md) instead of only one. It is unknown what dialogue will be seen when talking to Sibex with this Regalia. [\[1\]](#cite-note-archinfo-1)

## Trivia

-   For almost the entirety of the game's lifespan, Mute was the only Regalia known to exist, until Arch\_Mage told the community about the others.[\[1\]](#cite-note-archinfo-1)
-   Regalia, as a definition, are the emblems or insignia of royalty, especially the crown, scepter, and other ornaments used at a coronation.
