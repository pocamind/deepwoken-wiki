## Description

The Aspirant Mask is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Face" category. It can be dropped from the Songseeker Enemies in [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md) and [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md). They can also be found via [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md), in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) and [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) Chests.

Each Aspirant Mask has a triangular shape. At the top, two horns gradually curve out, and three cylindrical slits on each side are incised into the mask for sight.
