# Songseeker Wilds

Songseeker Isles is just like 3 islands where monks spawn that use an element that varies with their mask color (red=fire, frost=blue, thunder=yellow). There's also a cave on one of em with just iron, coal, and rock

This island also contains a fire temple that has two monks at the front and inside is the flamecharm trainer

Out of the monk variations I'd say that frost npcs are the easiest and flame ones are the hardest. This is because the flame npcs use high damaging skills that (usually) cant be cancelled, like flame repulsion or rising flame. They can use any of their 3 skills (flame blade, repulsion, rising) whenever they want, so they can spam rising flame or repulsion and if you aren't careful you have a high chance of death. Flame blade is their weakest skill arguably because you can just m1 to cancel it and they dont combo it off of anything. Thunder npcs are medium difficulty imo because they are the only npc that actually uses m1s, heavy, and block but in exchange they have mediocre skills and you can just parry their m1s rather easily after they cast a skill. the frost npcs are the easiest because they spam cancellable skills with long windups so if you just spam m1 on them you will pretty much win no matter what
