## Description

Ms Stick is a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) and is a worker for Lance Leshi

Ms Stick sells [Mushroom Omelettes](Mushroom_Omelette.md) for 15

## Location

Ms Stick can be found standing behind Lance Leshi inside of Lance's Food at [Etris](Etris.md).

## Trivia

-   Stick is an NPC added in the 16/02/2026 update where [Mystics](Mystic.md) were removed due to being obsolete with the new foretelling mechanic. This npc is meant to poke fun at how Mystics now need to seek other forms of employment.
-   Her name is also a reference to her former job as Ms Stick is pronounced like Mystic.
-   Her position as a cook may also be a nod to the term "flipping burgers" which means resorting to entry level jobs due to unemployment.
