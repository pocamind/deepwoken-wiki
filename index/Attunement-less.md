# Attunement-less

## Description

Attunment-less mantras so far consist of mantras that are chosen based of your stats like talents rather than you attunments.

**Sing** - _Charm_ those around you with you powerful voice. (Requirment: Charisma)

**Taunt** - Taunt those around you, causing them to be unable to jump and to take more damage. However, in their rage they do extra damage. (Requirment: Charisma)

**Adrenaline Surge** - An adrenaline rish of agility. (Requirment: Agility)
