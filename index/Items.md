# Items

Down Below are links to a list of items in Deepwoken

## [Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md)

**Mantra Modifiers** are items that can be used to expand the potential of the player's [Mantras](Mantras.md). These can easily be found by killing most enemies and can also be found in Erisia Chests.

## [Weapons](Weapons.md)

Tools constructed with the intent to harm others, polished to be efficient killing devices. They can be bought from shops or obtained from chests.

## [Equipment](Equipment.md)

An essential mechanic in Deepwoken, primarily used to resist damage from most sources, while also giving many different benefits. Though it can also be used for cosmetic purposes. Found in chests, exchanged for using an [Artifact](Artifacts.md) or by various other means.

## [Gems](Treasures.md)

Glistening gemstones that can fetch a modest price. They can commonly be obtained from chests.

## [Fish](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Fishing#Fish)

Creatures of the sea, usually cooked into delicious delicacies. They can be obtained by fishing.

## [Books](Books.md)

Books are items that are found in the world of Deepwoken and are a separate category from loot, tools, weapons, etc. Books can be obtained by looting the various bookshelves around the world (10%-15% chance) or as a guaranteed item sold at a Traveling Merchant Ship.
