## Description

The Specialist Boots are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Legs" category, and is a part of the Specialist Set. The Specialist Boots are a pair of black plate leggings, made to protect the wearer's legs. When worn, this grants +1 Posture, +1 Uncommon Pips, and the [?](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent.
