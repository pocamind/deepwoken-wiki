# Seamstress's Attire

Template:ArmorInfobox**Seamstress's Attire** is a Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires ten (10) Cloth and Fiber, five (5) [Iron](Ores.md), and two (2) [Megalodaunt Hide](Megalodaunt_Hide.md).

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 8.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2000 durability and grants +20% Elemental resistance, +25% Physical resistance.
