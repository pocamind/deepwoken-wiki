# The Celtorian Horror

The Celtorian Horror was an eldritch monster that is supposed to have taken place in the Drowning of [Celtor](Celtor.md) to the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of the [Depths](The_Depths.md), which is now the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md) and [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md), remains of the [City of Celtor](City_of_Celtor.md), now home to the [Lost Celtor](Lost_Celtor.md). This is said to have occurred at the end of [The Canticlysm](The_Canticlysm.md), making way for the time period known as the [Tides](Lore.md).

## Trivia

-   Although it has a major part in the lore of the pre-Tides era of the world of Deepwoken and the Depths, not much is known about it, including it’s appearance.
