# The Celtorian Horror

## Description

The Celtorian Horror was an eldritch monster that played a major role in the Drowning of the [City of Celtor](City_of_Celtor.md) to the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of the [Depths](The_Depths.md), which is now the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md) and [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md). The latter of which being remains of the City of Celtor, although now home to the [Lost Celtor](Lost_Celtor.md). This event is said to have occurred in 904 CE[\[1\]](#cite-note-0-1).

## Trivia

-   Although the Celtorian Horror has a major part in the lore of the pre-Tides era of the world of Deepwoken and the Depths, not much is known about it, including its appearance.
-   Lead Developer Arch\_Mage stated that if the Celtorian Horror were to be added ingame it would most likely "smush you with a single tentacle."[\[2\]](#cite-note-arch-2)
    -   From this, we can assume that it is a cephalopodic life form, potentially resembling the likeness of a Kraken.
-   The Celtorian is referred to by male pronouns by Arch\_Mage, seemingly hinting at it’s gender.[\[2\]](#cite-note-arch-2)
-   Despite fan speculation, the Celtorian Horror is infact a literal sea monster[\[1\]](#cite-note-0-1) and not a metaphor for the events which took place.
