# Terrapod

Template:Creature Info Box v2

The Slasher is a monster in Deepwoken notorious for shredding health quickly and for having very high mobility.

## Description

Slashers are purple and have 4 legs equipped with sharp claws on each leg. They have a hard shell on top with a mouth on the bottom for eating victims. It uses it claws to rapidly slash enemies and put pressure on anyone in its way without hesitation. It has the resemblance to a headcrab from the Half-life series.

## Behavior

Slashers are pretty mindless as they follow the same pattern and repeat the same attacks. Though, don't let it catch you off guard as it's attacks can completly destroy your health very quickly. It will constantly attack until you deflect enough of it's attacks to where it gets tired. In this time frame you are allowed to freely attack it until it begins attacking you again. It struggles with reaching high places due to not having the ability to jump high.

## Abilities

-   **Relentless Strikes:** The Slasher rapidly attacks anything in its path including the air until it gets tired
-   **Quick Slice:** The Slasher does a singular slice at it's opponent and retreats to recover. (Rare)

  

## Notes

-   Slashers spawn scattered around Erisia and are often inconsistent.
-   Strange Claw is used in some armors and can be sold for a decent amount of notes.
-   Purples Crabs can be recognized by the slashing sound they make each time they attack.
-   You **can't** sneak up on Slashers
