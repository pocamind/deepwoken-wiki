# Crews

## Description

On the top left of your screen, next to the Help button, there will be an icon of three people, with an orange-ish background, labeled "Crews." Clicking this will allow you to create a Crew with any player in the server, as long as they do not have an [Origin](Origin.md) that aligns them with a faction. This is very similar to party systems in other games.

Being in a Crew will automatically ally yourself with all other players in the Crew.

Notably, this feature is extremely similar to [Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md)'s Squads feature.
