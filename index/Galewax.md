# Galewax

Gale wax is a mysterious substance found within [Layer 2 F1,](Second_Layer.md) and it can be used to light Soul Candles which opens a hole in the ground beneath them to a chamber containing chests with [Enchant Stones](Enchant_Stone.md) and [Krys-weapons.](Weapons.md)

There are a total of 9 Galewax in Floor 1, a guide for all their locations can be found [here.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9RfD_RxO0T4)
