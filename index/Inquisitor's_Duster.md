## Description

The **Inquisitor's Duster** is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item in the "Arms" category. Equipping it grants you 2 Rare Pips. It's found occasionally inside the [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md)'s chest once defeated.

## Trivia

-   Given it's name, it's likely tied to Authority Inquisitors in some way.
    -   Used to be unobtainable exclusive to Moderators/Developers, now obtainable from [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md)'s loot pool.
        -   This previously didn't work since the addition of [World Events](World_Events.md), but was fixed on the same update the [Fang and Coil](Fang_and_Coil.md) fist style was added.

-   Famously worn by the YouTuber Nogo, while it was unobtainable.
    -   Nogo's Inquisitor's Duster is the smoke variant dyed black.

-   The animations for this equipment updated to be dynamic, just like every other animated equipment.
