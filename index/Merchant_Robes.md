# Merchant Robes

Template:ArmorInfobox**Merchant Robes** is a Starter [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires requires five (5) pieces of Cloth and Fiber.

This Outfit has no [Power](Power.md) or [Attribute](Attributes.md) requirement.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1200 durability and grants no resistances.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Mates' Rates_, which increases selling prices at Antiquarians and [Mantra Collectors](Mantra_Collector.md).

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)
