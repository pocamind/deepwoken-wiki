# Merchant Robes

Template:ArmorInfobox**Merchant Robes** is a Starter [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires requires five (5) pieces of Cloth and Fiber.

This Outfit has no [Power](Power.md) or [Attribute](Attributes.md) requirement.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1200 durability and grants no resistances.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Merchant's Accord;_ Merchants and Antiquarians will do business with you no matter how poor your reputation is.

-   This talent does NOT allow you to use the antiquarian in the depths if you are not deepbound.

## Trivia/Notes

-   The outfit is worn by Lance Leshi and his brother at Isle Of Vigils who sells food near the docks.
