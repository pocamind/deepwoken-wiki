# Golden Warrior

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

The Golden Warrior is a rare random encountered enemy NPC that spawns leaning on random walls in [Erisia](Erisia.md). It is also interactable by pressing the \[E\] key which will also prompt you with his dialogue.

"Ah hello! Would you like to duel? You may take the first swing! Ha, I'll even give you 3."

He will start fighting back after the 3rd hit. The fight isn't too difficult as long you can parry half of the time. A chest will appear will upon killing it.

It wears a Goldleaf Helm, Warden Pauldrons, an \[Unknown\] outfit and a pair of \[Unknown/Unlisted\] boots.
