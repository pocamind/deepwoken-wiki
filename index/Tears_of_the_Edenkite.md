## Description

Tears of the Edenkite is a damage dealing [enchantment](Enchantment.md) that strikes your opponent with bubbles on critical attack and flourish.

## Effect

On flourish or critical attack, Tears of the Edenkite creates 5 bubbles that home in on your target after a delay, dealing damage based on your weapon investment and applying hitstun. This has a 20 second cooldown.

The bubbles deal 11 typeless damage each at 100 weapon investment, bypassing your opponent's armor resistances. Additionally, these have 7.25x PvE scaling.

### Notes

-   The bubbles are parryable, blockable, and dodgeable.
