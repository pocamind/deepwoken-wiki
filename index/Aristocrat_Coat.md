# Aristocrat Coat

## Description

Template:EquipmentStatsAristocrat Coats can be found in Summer Isle Chests. They come in 3 different styles, which are Beige, Burgundy, and Slate. This armor piece grants the talent, Art of the Deal, which decreases the price of an item by around 12%.
