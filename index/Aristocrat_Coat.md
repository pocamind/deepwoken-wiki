# Aristocrat Coat

“ After all this time, I have the 3 of them... ”

― Duke cosplayers after getting the Celtor race, eyeglasses, and the Aristocrat Coat

  

## Description

Template:EquipmentStats

  
The Aristocrat Coat is an Equipment item of the Arms Catagory. Equipping it grants you the talent, [Art of the Deal](Talents.md), which decreases the price of items by around 12% as well as randomized stats based on the Pips you get. Aristocrat Coats can only be found in Summer isle.

## Trivia

The Slate variant of this coat is used by the [Duke of Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).
