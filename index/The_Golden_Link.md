## Description

**The Golden Link** are a company of [Thundercallers](Thundercall.md), originally formed within [Markor](Markor.md). They specialized in constructing [Sea Highway](Sea_Highway.md) gates across the [Luminants](Luminants.md) and maintaining them. They seemingly profited off of the Highways.[\[1\]](#cite-note-attunement-1)

The vast utility and demand for these highway gates have caused a large expansion of their company, now spreading throughout almost all the [Luminants](Luminants.md). [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) is used to power these large structures, generating the energy needed to provide this ‘jolt’ of speed to all ships that pass through. Without [Thundercallers](Thundercall.md), these gates would be useless, making [Thundercallers](Thundercall.md) one of the most highly valued workers across all [Luminants](Luminants.md).
