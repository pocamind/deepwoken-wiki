# Summer Dragoon (NPC)

## Summer dragoon

A summer dragoon

### Description

Summer dragoons are higher ranking members of the [Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md) that wear their respective armor [Summer dragoon](Summer_Dragoon.md) and use [Rosen's peacemakers](<Rosen's_Peacemaker.md>), their armor grant them the talent [Sunset ricochet](Talents.md) which makes their bullets ricochet from enemy to enemy. (Even though all summer company npcs have this talent as innate)
