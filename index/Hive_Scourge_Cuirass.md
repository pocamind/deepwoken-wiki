## Description

The Hive Scourge Cuirass is an animated equipment item in the Arms category. Equipping it grants you 8 Health, 2 Posture, 2 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip), and the [Talent](Talents.md) Lowstride, which greatly reduces the speed penalty received from crouching. It is commonly found in chests from [Duke Erisia](Fort_Merit.md), [The Depths](The_Depths.md) ([Second Layer](Second_Layer.md)), and can also be obtained from [Merchant Ships](Travelling_Merchant.md).
