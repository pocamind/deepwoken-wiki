# Deep Shrines/Shrine of Conceit

| Shrine of Conceit | |
|---|---|

“ DELVE INTO YOUR LONG FORGOTTEN PAST. WHAT POSSIBILITIES HAVE YOU SHED? ”

**If your looking for another shrine return to [Deep Shrines](../Deep_Shrines.md) or head to [Navigation](#navigation).**

## Description

Located in [Saramaed Summit](../Saramaed_Summit.md) at [Miner's Landing](<../Miner's_Landing.md>) above the Ignition Union vs Summer Company event near the Resonance Door.

## Effects/Details

### Effects

For 1 Knowledge, unburn a card of your choice. Note that the Ace is **NOT REFUNDED**.

### Details

-   Lets you reenable a card to be drawn if you accidentally burnt it.
