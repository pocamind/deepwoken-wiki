## Description

Located within the [Saramaed Summit](../Saramaed_Summit.md) at [Miner's Landing](<../Miner's_Landing.md>) above the Ignition Union vs Summer Company event near the Resonance Door.

## Effects/Details

### Effects

For 1, unburn a card of your choice. Note that the Ace is **NOT REFUNDED**.

### Details

-   Lets you reenable a card to be drawn if you accidentally burnt it.
