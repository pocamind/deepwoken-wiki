# Spider Egg

Spider eggs are common in Lower Erisia. They are found in low spaces such as caves or hollow logs. They are rare and hard to find. They are mainly used to make useful [potions](Alchemy.md) and to (although extremely wasteful) craft fiber.

A total of 7 spider eggs can be found below the drop into [Widow's Hollow](<Widow's_Hollow.md>). You'll likely receive even more than just these 7 with the "harvester" talent. It's recommended anyone trying to farm spider eggs server hop for them here as each of them can be collected without alerting the widow.
