## Description

Tone is a [Tiran](Tiran.md) [NPC](NPC.md). He sells starter equipment aimed towards new and early game players.

## Location

Tone is located at the docks in [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) leaning against a wooden pillar in front of the stairs.
