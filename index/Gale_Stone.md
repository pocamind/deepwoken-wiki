# Gale Stone

**Item Obtainability:**

Holding a Gale Stone.

You can obtain Gale Stones by killing Elisia Guards

**Item Description:**

"A faint whisper of wind can be heard from within."

**What It Does:**

Can be used to obtain the GaleBreath Magic

  

## Trivia

You can accidentally eat these so be careful
