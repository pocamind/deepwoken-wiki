## Description

A Gale Stone can be acquired by slaying NPCs that use [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md), in which there is a high chance you will obtain one. It can be used to complete the [Lost Brother](Temple_of_the_Blade.md) quest in the Isle of Vigils

(Allowing you to go past the lvl 75 limit for Weapon Mastery). You can also use Gale Stones (5) in [Friid's](Friid,_Master_of_Gale.md) quest on the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) in order to learn and unlock the [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md) attunement for a character (One that has another element).

## Tips

-   Killing [Legion Soldiers](Legion_Soldier.md) located in the [Viper's Jaw](<The_Viper's_Jaw.md>) as they have a high chance on dropping Gale Stones and spawn often, etrean gaurds do drop gale stones however unless you are killing them in erisia or at a vs. event you will lose rep, also the etrean guards at etris dont drop gale stones.

-   Also, the bloated Mineskippers found within the cave on Songseeker will occasionally drop Gale stones; though it's a bit rare or uncommon for them to do so, you could try to farm them for one as well.
