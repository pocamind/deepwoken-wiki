## Description

Weird Trader is a seemingly inconspicuous [Ganymede](Ganymede.md) that can rarely be spotted sailing on his Dinghy just about anywhere in the sea, this include the waters of the Etrean Luminant, the Eastern Luminant and even the Void Sea.

## Item Request

Weird Trader will make requests for 3 items, from a long list of possible choices. Providing him with these 3 items will reward the player with a very rare weapon or an Astral Enchantment Stone. This trade can only be performed once per encounter, a new Weird Trader need to be found to attempt another trade.

**Claiming you have the items without having them in your inventory causes Weird Trader to uppercut you, sending you extremely high in the sky and giving you the potential of being knocked or dying if you arent wearing a [Tiran Pendant](Tiran_Pendant.md).**
