## Description

Weird Trader is a seemingly inconspicuous [Ganymede](Ganymede.md) that can rarely be spotted sailing on his Dinghy just about anywhere in the sea, this include the waters of the Etrean Luminant, the Eastern Luminant and even the Void Sea.

## Item Request

Weird Trader will make requests for 3 items, from a long list of possible choices. Providing him with these 3 items will reward the player with rare items. This trade can only be performed once per encounter, a new Weird Trader need to be found to attempt another trade.

### Requested items

-   Weapons, including:
    -   [Dragoon](Dragoon.md)
    -   [Inquisitor's Thorn](<Inquisitor's_Thorn.md>)
-   Any of the Voidzone Lotuses
-   Ingredients, Including:
    -   Redds
    -   Browncaps
    -   Alestrian Coral
    -   [Erisore](Ores.md)
    -   ???
