# Glaive Spellhardt

| Glaive Spellhardt | |
|---|---|
| name | Glaive Spellhardt |
| location | The Floating Keep |

## Description

Glaive is a male [Lightborn](Lightborn.md) located in the Upper Hall/Second Floor of [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md). He sells you various [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md)\-related items for [Joy](Joy.md), though they are usually all combat-related.

Here are all the possible options that you can buy from **Glaive Spellhardt**:

**2024:**

With 2024 Hallowtide, came with 3 newly-added Hallowtide-exclusive weapons on top of the weapons from last years. Though, they were only released on the second week, on the [November 8th, 2024](Version_History/Uncollapsed.md#november-8th-2024) update.

1.  [Master Schematic](Outfits.md#master) \[20 Joy\] - Gives you a random master outfit schematic.
2.  [Skyreap Blade](Skyreap_Blade.md) (Greataxe) \[100 Joy\]
3.  [Hallowscleave](Hallowscleave.md) (Sword) \[100 Joy\]
4.  [Skull Piercer](Skull_Piercer.md) (Rapier) \[100 Joy\]
5.  [Putrid Edenstaff](Putrid_Edenstaff.md) (Greathammer) \[100 Joy\]
6.  [Bloodtide Trident](Bloodtide_Trident.md) (Spear) \[100 Joy\]
7.  [Drakemaw Gauntlets](Drakemaw_Gauntlets.md) (Fist) \[100 Joy\]
8.  [Gourdguard](Gourdguard.md) (Shield) \[80 Joy\]
9.  [Wretched Mawblades](Wretched_Mawblades.md) (Greatsword) \[100 Joy\]
10.  [Death's Reverie](<Death's_Reverie.md>) (Twinblade) \[100 Joy\]
11.  [Spectral Grasp](Spectral_Grasp.md) (Dagger) \[100 Joy\]
12.  [Harrowing](Enchantments.md) [Enchant Stone](Enchant_Stone.md) \[150 Joy\]
13.  Pumpkin Pitch (Mantra) \[300 Joy\]
    

**2023:**

With 2023 Hallowtide, came with 3 newly-added Hallowtide-exclusive weapons on top of the weapons from last year. You could also get the Pumpkin Pitch mantra. The Gourdguard and Harrowing Enchant Stone came on the second week of hallowtide, on the [November 3rd, 2023](Version_History/Uncollapsed.md#november-3rd-2023) update.

1.  [Master Schematic](Outfits.md#master) \[20 Joy\] - Gives you a random master outfit schematic.
2.  [Skyreap Blade](Skyreap_Blade.md) (Greataxe) \[60 Joy\]
3.  [Hallowscleave](Hallowscleave.md) (Sword) \[60 Joy\]
4.  [Skull Piercer](Skull_Piercer.md) (Rapier) \[60 Joy\]
5.  [Putrid Edenstaff](Putrid_Edenstaff.md) (Greathammer) \[100 Joy\]
6.  [Bloodtide Trident](Bloodtide_Trident.md) (Spear) \[100 Joy\]
7.  [Drakemaw Gauntlets](Drakemaw_Gauntlets.md) (Fist) \[100 Joy\]
8.  [Gourdguard](Gourdguard.md) (Shield) \[80 Joy\]
9.  [Harrowing](Enchantments.md) [Enchant Stone](Enchant_Stone.md) \[150 Joy\]
10.  Pumpkin Pitch (Mantra) \[300 Joy\]
    

**2022:**

With 2022 Hallowtide, came with 3 Hallowtide-exclusive weapons, and a random master outfit schematic. All of the weapons used to cost 60 [Joy](Joy.md), but were increased to 70 a few minutes later on the [November 7th, 2022](Version_History/Uncollapsed.md#november-7th-2022) update.

1.  [Master Schematic](Outfits.md#master) \[20 Joy\] - Gives you a random master outfit schematic.
2.  [Hallowscleave](Hallowscleave.md) (Sword) \[70 Joy\]
3.  [Skull Piercer](Skull_Piercer.md) (Rapier) \[70 Joy\]
4.  [Skyreap Blade](Skyreap_Blade.md) (Greataxe) \[70 Joy\]
