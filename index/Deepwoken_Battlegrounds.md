## Overview

Deepwoken Battlegrounds was an update to _Deepwoken_ released on April 1st, 2025 to celebrate April Fools Day, upholding the tradition of annual April Fools' events. The Battleground mode could be accessed by clicking "BATTLEGROUNDS" on the main menu, taking the player into the battlegrounds servers. In Battlegrounds, players would spawn either on [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md), above the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) map, or down on the Battle Royale map itself, with the player having 50 points in all [stats](Character_Stats.md) besides the three Weapon stats which all had 100, access to all (non-oath) [mantras](Mantras.md) freely, a base [Sword](<Sword_(weapon).md>), and the exclusive Ether Fly mantra.

On the Battle Royale map, players could find excessive amounts of [Deep Totems](Battle_Royale.md#deep-totems) and [Loot Chests](Battle_Royale.md#chests), along with the chance to find any weapons in the base game and outfits to wear. Death had very little penalty in the battlegrounds game mode, with people only losing their weapon, enchant effect, and any held items such as Bombs. Killing players was rewarded by granting the killer copies of their victim's [Talents](Talents.md), and the ability to equip their weapon.

## Trivia

-   Deepwoken Battlegrounds was the fourth annual April Fools' event.
    -   The three events prior were, in order of release: [Mudwoken](Mudwoken.md), [Coral Fever](Destroyman_III.md), and [Warring States Era](Warring_States_Era.md).
-   The Battlegrounds gamemode had a bug when first released where dying removed the Ether Fly Mantra & the Flimsy Glider from your inventory.
    -   This made the only safe way to get down to the Battle Royale map was to use mantras that prevented fall damage such as Wind Passage or Neural Pathway, as otherwise you would instantly die from the fall.
-   In Battlegrounds, upon taking an Oath, the only way to change it afterwards was to log from the server.
    -   Despite this, when you killed someone, it would often say you gained their Oath talents, despite not being able to use them.
-   For unknown reasons, certain Oaths were unobtainable from Oath Shards. This includes some of the more recently released Oaths at the time, being [Saintsworn](Oath%253A_Saintsworn.md), [Soulbreaker](Oath%253A_Soulbreaker.md), and [Bladeharper](Oath%253A_Bladeharper.md), as well as [Linkstrider](Oath%253A_Linkstrider.md), [Silentheart](Oath%253A_Silentheart.md), and [Saltchemist](Oath%253A_Saltchemist.md). Silentheart and Saltchemist were made unobtainable intentionally.
-   As early as a few hours after the update, players would clamor for the spawn locations in each map to be randomized as to prevent spawnkilling, because they weren't before.
