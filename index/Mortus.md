# Mortus

File:Mortus.png

Mortus's appearance

Mortus is a Capra with gray hair that is wearing the same outfit as [Lower Erisa bandits](Bandit.md).

He is located in a fishing hut, on the closest small rocky island in front of Lower Erisia's main beach.

## Features

Mortus is capable of crafting fishing baits that can help you fish different monsters.

These monsters are:

[Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md), [Threshers](Thresher.md) and [Terrapods](Terrapod.md) _(needs verification)_

In order to craft the Megalodaunt bait, you'll need to bring him 2 megalodaunt hide.

In order to craft the Thresher bait, you'll need to bring him 2 Thresher Spines.

In order to craft the Terrapod bait, you'll need to bring him 2 Strange Claws.
