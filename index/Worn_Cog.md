## Description

A Worn Cog is an item only dropped by [Mechalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md#mechalodaunt) which can be found on [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) and [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md) or in the [Diluvian Mechanism](<Diluvian_Mechanism_(NPC).md>). It serves as a material to make the [Ignition Deepdelver](Ignition_Deepdelver.md) outfit.
