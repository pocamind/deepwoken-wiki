# Locker

| Locker | |
|---|---|
| usage | Accessing the player's menu |

## Description

Lockers are a utility item that solely allows the player to open their menu, similar to [Crafting Stations](Crafting_Station.md) and [Campfires](Campfire_Pit.md). However, they do not have the same function as the latter two.

They can be primarily found in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md), such as [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md), [Marauder's Entrance](<Marauder's_Entrance.md>), [Diver's Camp](<Diver's_Camp.md>), and [The Cold Forge](The_Cold_Forge.md).

## Usage

Lockers can be used by pressing E near one. This will open the player's menu, accessing their stats.
