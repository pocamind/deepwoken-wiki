# The Hidden Grove

File:Hidden grove.png

Located behind a door in summer isle. Not much is known about its purpose, but looks very similar to Greathive Aratel fountains. When interacted with, the player is prompted with "I have nothing to water it with"

When interacted with a canteen with more than 0/5 in it around 6 lotuses will spawn in the grove and the player is prompted with "It seems its harvest has blossomed all it can for now."
