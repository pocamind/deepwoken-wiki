# The Hidden Grove

LocationL The hidden grove LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Summer Company

  

The Hidden Grove is a cave located behind a **destructible wall** (mantras like Strong Left or Shoulder Bash will break it) behind [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md), consisting of a small cave area with a waterfall, the grove itself, and a flower fountain. (Not to be confused with [Greathive Shrines](Fountain_Gates.md), since they are visually similar.)

When interacting with the shrine, while having no water or while holding a canteen, the player is prompted with "I don't have anything to water this..."

When interacting with the shrine while your canteen has any kind of liquid in it, 6 [lotuses](Ingredients.md#lotuses) will spawn around the fountain. The Lotuses last 45 seconds before disappearing and are randomized based on the season that is currently ongoing in the game, those being;

Spring: [Zephyr Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#zephyr-lotus) (Green), [Miasmic Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#miasmic-lotus) (Pink)

Summer:

Autumn: [Zephyr Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#zephyr-lotus) (Green), [Skyvalor Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#skyvalor-lotus) (White)

Winter:

Upon interacting with the shrine after already activating it, the shrine says _"It seems its harvest has blossomed all it can, for now."_ The cooldown for this fountain is 60 minutes per player serverwide.
