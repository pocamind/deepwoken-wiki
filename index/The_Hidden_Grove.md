# The Hidden Grove

LocationL The hidden grove LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Summer Company

  

The Hidden Grove is a location located behind a d**estructible wall** (mantras like lvl 2 Strong Left will break it) in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md), consisting of a small cave area, the grove itself, and a singular fountain. (Not to be confused with [Greathive Shrines](Fountain_Gates.md), although they are visually similar.)

When the fountain in the center of the room is interacted with an empty canteen, the player is prompted with _"I have nothing to water it with."_ When interacting with a canteen (any amount of water), around 6 [lotuses](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients#Lotuses) will spawn around the fountain. The text upon interacting reads _"It seems its harvest has blossomed all it can for now."_ The cooldown for this fountain is currently 60 minutes
