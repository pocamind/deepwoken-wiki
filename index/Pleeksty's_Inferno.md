## Description

The weapon can be obtained by going into The Eternal Gale without a Light Hook and using the Flamecharm Attunement. Once these requirements are met you will face an enemy called [Aska](Aska.md). Defeating him will grant you the weapon.

It is not able to be banked and is not droppable, being permanently bound to the slot you fought Aska with.
