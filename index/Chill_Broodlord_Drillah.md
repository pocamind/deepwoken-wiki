# Chill Broodlord Drillah

## Description

Aura.

Chill Broodlord Drillah is honestly, the **COOLEST** Broodlord in all of Deepwoken (self-proclaimed).

No one knows how, but it got the Spongebob Chain from the Layer 3 smuggling ring for an undisclosed price, as well as [The Guy](The_Guy.md)'s eyeglasses by passing the Charisma check.

## Location

It is a real location.

This Broodlord can be found on the top-left corner of the Eastern Luminant's map.

-   Essentially, before you follow the waves leading to [Simforea](Simforea.md), if you look to the left you should see some sort of "shadow" of a tall pillar. Follow it, and you'll arrive at Chill Broodlord Drillah's pillar of truth.

Upon reaching the area, you will find a tall pillar of stone.

In order to get to the top, you will have to time your climbing and use your glider to continue climbing ([tutorial](https://youtu.be/_dWpsIovNMo)).
