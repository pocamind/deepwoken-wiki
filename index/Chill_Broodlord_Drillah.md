# Chill Broodlord Drillah

| Chill Broodlord Drillah | |
|---|---|

## Description

Chill Broodlord Drillah[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) is honestly, the **COOLEST** [Broodlord](Mudskipper_Broodlord.md) in all of _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)_. He exists only as an [Easter egg](References_and_Easter_Eggs.md) for any brave pathfinder willing to kneel before him alongside his [Calabash](Calabash.md). Forever unmoving for he knows one move could end all of [Lumen](Luminants.md).

No one knows how he got the Spongebob Chain from the Layer 3 smuggling ring for an undisclosed price.

## Location

This Broodlord can be found on the top-left corner of the Eastern Luminant's map.

-   Essentially, before you follow the waves leading to [Simforea](Simforea.md), if you look to the left, you should see some sort of "shadow" of a tall pillar. Follow it, and you'll arrive at Chill Broodlord Drillah's pillar of truth.

-   Upon reaching the area, you will find a tall pillar of stone.

In order to get to the top, you will have to time your climbing and use your glider to continue climbing ([tutorial](https://youtu.be/_dWpsIovNMo)). But you could also just use [Dimensional Travel](Dimensional_Travel.md).
