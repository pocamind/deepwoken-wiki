# Chill Broodlord Drillah

## Description

Chill Broodlord Drillah[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) is honestly, the **COOLEST** [Broodlord](Mudskipper_Broodlord.md) in all of _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)_. He exists only as an [Easter egg](References_and_Easter_Eggs.md) for any brave pathfinder willing to kneel before him alongside his [Calabash](Calabash.md). Forever unmoving for he knows one step could end all of [Lumen](Luminants.md).

## Location

Chill Broodlord Drillah can be found on the top-left corner of the Eastern Luminant. Before you follow the waves leading to [Simforea](Simforea.md), if you look to the left, you should see some sort of "shadow" of a tall pillar. Upon reaching the area, you will find a tall pillar of stone.

In order to get to the top, you will have to time your climbing and use your glider to continue climbing ([Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QYPAiY5hq7k&feature=youtu.be)). But you could also use [Dimensional Travel](Dimensional_Travel.md).
