## Description

The Gold Plate is a [Treasure](Treasures.md) dropped from [Rat Fighters](Rat_Fighter.md) and [Golden Warriors](Golden_Warrior.md). 5 of them can be traded to the Golden Bouncer in exchange for a chest containing mid-tier [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md) loot.

## Trivia

-   The Gold Plate shares its description with the [Gold Bar](Treasures.md) and [Platinum Bar](Treasures.md), and its sell price with the Gold Bar.
