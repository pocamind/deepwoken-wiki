## Description

The Mercenary's Garb is an equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 6% Stealth, 2 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip), and the [Talent](Talents.md) Kick Off, which increases the upwards distance wall jumps send you, as well as giving a speed boost after jumping over a wall. It is commonly found in chests from the [Crypt of the Unbroken](Crypt_of_the_Unbroken.md) and [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).

## Trivia

-   The Mercenary's Garb is part of the "Mercenary's" set; with the only other piece being the [Mercenary's Boots](<Mercenary's_Boots.md>).
