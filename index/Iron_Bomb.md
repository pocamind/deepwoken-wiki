# Iron Bomb

## Description

File:Iron bomb.png

Credit to Attking1234#4691

A Ball Shaped Explosive used to damage monsters and players.

## Obtainments

You are able to get this recipe when you interact with an NPC in [Songseeker Wilds,](Songseeker_Wilds.md) He will then give you a quest to clear a mine beside him filled with [Mineskipper.](Mudskipper.md) When finished return to him and he will gave you a talent called "Family Recipe" which allowing you to craft bombs.

### Recipe

2 Iron + 1 Coal near a crafting table

(Iron can be purchase or mined from a cave while Coal is only obtained through mining)

### Uses

Crafted bombs deal damage when they explode. You are able to press M1 to place it on the ground directly below you with a timer to explode or you can press E to throw it in the direction of your cursor, with the bomb exploding on impact.
