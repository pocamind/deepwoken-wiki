# The Golden Rat

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

LocationL The Golden Rat LocationR

Off with your hats, and three times three!

  

## Overview

The Golden Rat is home to [The Golden Rats](The_Golden_Rats.md) and [Dravik, The Rat King](Dravik,_The_Rat_King.md) with the location being a Tavern inside of a cave. [Golden Warriors](Golden_Warrior.md) can be found at every corner with some even drinking [Tankards of Plumfruit Juice](Tankard_of_Plumfruit_Juice.md).

Outside, the [Golden Bouncer](Golden_Rat_Bouncer.md) will tell you to enter.

## Contents

[The Beard](The_Beard.md) can be found selling [Plain Boiled Brined Aged Fermented Hotdog](Plain_Boiled_Brined_Aged_Fermented_Hotdog.md), [Tankard of Plumfruit Juice](Tankard_of_Plumfruit_Juice.md) and the [The Golden Fool](The_Golden_Fool.md)

A mystery box can be found near the [Group of Etrean Nobles](Group_of_Etrean_Nobles.md)

This is the area where you must fight [Dravik, The Rat King](Dravik,_The_Rat_King.md) to progress your level past 5-10
