# Blacksteel Pirate Captain

| Blacksteel Pirate Captain | |
|---|---|
| health | 3000 |
| drops | Darkalloy Greatsword, Blacksteel Pauldrons, Blacksteel Helm |
| weapon | Darkalloy Greatsword |
| moves | Assault, Critical, Umbral Slash, Eclipse Kick, Shade Bringer, Pressure Blast, Shadow Assault, Shadow Roar |

## Description

The Blacksteel Pirate captain can be encountered during the Hostage Mission for the [Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md) Origin. This NPC spawns alongside 5 [Blacksteel Pirates](Blacksteel_Pirate.md). This enemy is very strong, be aware.

They wear a [Darksteel Plate](Darksteel_Plate.md), a dyed black [Silver Knight's Helm](<Silver_Knight's_Helm.md>), a Black [Plain Cape](Plain_Cape.md), and a pair of [Blacksteel Pauldrons](Blacksteel_Pauldrons.md).

## Passives

-   **Perpetual Speed Boost** - The Blacksteel Pirate Captain moves incredibly fast at all times. Running is not an option.

## Tips

-   It is highly recommended that you take out all of the [Blacksteel Pirates](Blacksteel_Pirate.md) first before attempting to fight the Blacksteel Pirate Captain.
-   As it is not required to kill them for the mission to complete, it may be more advisable to simply assassinate them or distract them using other enemies and then grab the hostage and run.
