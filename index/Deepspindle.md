## Description

The Deepspindle is a shadow rapier "grown out of the darkest scoria of the abyss", making it a naturally created weapon instead of one manmade. It possesses a twisting-shaped blade that intertwines together into a sharp point. Currently, the weapon is still being researched, and there is not much information on it.

## Obtainment

1.  Obtain Shadow's Flight or Shadow Travel by crafting 2 [Void Feathers](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Feather#Void_Feather) and an [Umbral Obsidian](Umbral_Obsidian.md) together.
2.  Find the Capra Diver NPC in The Depths ([Hell Mode](Hell_Mode.md) increases rate, random encounter from The Depths, chance unknown yet.)
3.  Talk to the NPC, then use Shadow's Flight or Shadow Travel at his location and you should be teleported. (There is a bug where you can get teleported under the ground and wiped, make sure you clip it to be restored.)
4.  After using Shadow's Flight or Shadow Travel, you will be teleported to an arena where you will fight the NPC waiting for you to obtain the weapon Deepspindle, and you should get teleported back to where you found the NPC. If you die, you will be wiped.

## Critical

**TWO CRITS**

On standing crit, user stabs opponent once afterwards makes needles eject from target repeatedly, procing shadow each stab, while striking a pose.

On running crit, user stabs weapon into themselves sending out projectiles to the enemy, which if landed will tp the user to the receiving enemy.

On flourish, procs shadow.
