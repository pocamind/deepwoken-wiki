## Description

The **Boltcrusher** is a Legendary [Greathammer](Weapons.md): a heavy industrial and charged pick-hammer picked up as a weapon to infuse its booming power on bam. It utilizes explosive electrical charges made by the top engine to drive energy that will drill rock or down foe. Its designation: to destroy. It scales off both Thundercall and Heavy Weapon.

  

## Obtainment

It is obtained via the "Drowned Delver" job on the Job Board in Layer 1 of the Depths. It drops from the Delver NPC itself.
