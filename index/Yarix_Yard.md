# Yarix Yard

Template:Location2

**Yarix Yard** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique areas exclusive to the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) gamemode.

## Overview

Yarix Yard is a small market located around the outskirts of [Kingmaker's Isle](<Kingmaker's_Isle.md>).
