# Derelict Highchurch

LocationL Derelict Highchurch LocationR

High above the ancient rot, a place of worship to the unimaginable resides.

  

## Overview

The Derelict Highchurch is a location in the East Luminant, it can be found above the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md). It can be accessed from a broken bridge connecting to near the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md) in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). It appears normal on the surface, however, one can open a hatch beneath the lectern, leading into a library, You can interact with some bookcases, to open a secret entrance, or just use a terrain breaking mantra on the bookshelves guarding the entrance to a room with a crucified Celestial, and Samael, who is crucial to receiving the [Starkindred](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md) [Oath](Oaths.md). this also where you can find the surface shadowcast trainer (she doesnt give shadowcast you still have to get 5 umbral and go the the trainer in the depth.
