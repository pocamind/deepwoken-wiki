# Derelict Highchurch

LocationL Derelict Highchurch LocationR

High above the ancient rot, a place of worship to the unimaginable resides.

  

The Derelict High Church is a location in the East Luminant, it can be found above the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md). It can be accessed from a broken bridge connecting to near the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md) in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). It appears normal on the surface, however, one can open a hatch beneath the lecturn, leading into the library, You can interact \[with some bookcases, need clarification on specifics\] to open a secret entrance, leading to a room with a crucified Celestial, and Samael, who is crucial to receiving [Starkindred](Oaths.md).
