# Derelict Highchurch

LocationL Derelict Highchurch LocationR

High above the ancient rot, a place of worship to the unimaginable resides.

  

## Overview

The Derelict Highchurch is a location in the East Luminant, it can be found above the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md). It can be accessed from a broken bridge connecting to near the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md) in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), or from two [Union Hooks](Talent_Quests.md#union-hook) on the backside. Four Songseekers will spawn at the front of the church. It appears normal on the surface, however, one can open a hatch beneath the lectern, leading into a library, You can interact with some bookcases, to open a secret entrance, or just use a terrain breaking mantra on the bookshelves guarding the entrance to a room with a crucified [Celestial](Celestial.md), and [Samael](Samael.md), who is crucial to receiving the [Starkindred](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md) [Oath](Oaths.md). The Celestial's blood is also a crucial part of obtaining the [Saltchemist](Oath%253A_Saltchemist.md) [Oath](Oaths.md). You can also find the [Shadowcast Trainer](Beatra.md), in the garden to the left of the church. (She doesn't give [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md), [Nostor, Master of Shadow](Nostor,_Master_of_Shadow.md), will.)
