# Erisor's Church

LocationL Erisor's Church LocationR

Why does this name speak to me and why do I know it so well?

  

**Erisor's Church** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark found in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).

## Overview

Erisor's Church

Erisor's Church is a small ruined church. It can be found by the entrance to the [Towerstruck Lands](Towerstruck_Lands.md).

## Enemies

-   [Bandit](Bandit.md)
-   [Bandit Captain](Bandit_Leader.md)
-   [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md)

## Events

File:Erisor's Church 2.png

Forgotten Villagers inside the Church

### Forgotten Villagers Mission

-   Talking to the [Villagers](Forgotten_Villager.md) for a bit will result in waves of [Bandits](Bandit.md) spawning.
-   These [Bandits](Bandit.md) will progress in difficulty until a [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md) spawns.
-   This event will end as soon as the [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md) is eliminated.
-   If any of the [Villagers](Forgotten_Villager.md) survive, a chest will be spawned at the end of the event with typical [Erisian](Erisia.md) loot.
-   Alternative to this quest, you can knock out these villagers and take them to [Alastir](Alastir.md) in [Pathfinder's Respite](<Pathfinder's_Respite.md>).

## Tips

-   The forgotten villagers typically take ~20-30 minutes to respawn every time you beat / loose the event.
-   If left alone without any help, they will loose as they are severely outnumbered.
-   If all of the villagers die, you cannot claim your reward.
-   There is always more bandit leaders than forgotten villagers. Always agro a Bandit leader to fight you instead of fighting one that the Villagers are already fighting. You will hit the villagers and agro them on you.
