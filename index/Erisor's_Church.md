# Erisor's Church

## Description

Template:Areainfobox

File:Erisor's Church 2.png

Forgotten Villagers inside the Church

A small. run-down church below/nearby the Burning Gardens. Inside the church are 3 forgotten villagers sitting by a fire. When talking, the villagers will be grateful that you are not a bandit and warn you about the Royal Pathfinder. One of the villagers will say that he is a false ally and is truly loyal to the Lord of this land, explaining that his true mission is to look for 'all of those that lived here before the island froze in time.' The villager will ask the player if they heard something outside and the conversation will be ended. Once this dialogue ends 3 waves of bandits and a golem will appear and start attacking the forgotten villagers.

To take them to the [Royal Pathfinder](Alastir.md), attack them, knock them, and carry them to the pathfinder. They use fist style to fight.
