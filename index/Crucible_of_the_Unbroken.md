# Crucible of the Unbroken

**Crucible of the Unbroken** is a Location in _Deepwoken_. It is the unique arena exclusive to the Gladiator [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**Crucible of the Unbroken** is a massive decorated arena with hanging chandeliers, teams are separated onto two different sides and firstly sent in duos, eventually cycling through players as others are killed.
