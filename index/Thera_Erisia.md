## Description

As a member of the [Erisia family](Erisia_family.md), Thera served as the duchess of [Erisia](Erisia.md). According to surviving records, in the form of statues, she was the third of her family name to bear this title.[\[1\]](#cite-note-pr-1) Thera was well renowned for wielding a rapier, earning her her title of "The Needle's Eye". She had a son, [Nir Erisia](Nir_Erisia.md), who would go on to inherit the duchy from his mother.[\[2\]](#cite-note-ne-2)

The exact circumstances of Thera's death are unclear. However, it's widely believed that she found herself in a dispute that turned violent, during which she was attacked by numerous other individuals. Allegedly, she survived the assault, only to succumb to her wounds in the arms of her son.[\[2\]](#cite-note-ne-2)

[Needle's Eyes](<Needle's_Eye.md>) were produced as replicas of her famed rapier.[\[2\]](#cite-note-ne-2)
