## Description

Winter Corps Parka is an equipment item under the Arms category. Equipping it grants you 2 pips. It is commonly found in chests from [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md) and [Crypt of the Unbroken](Crypt_of_the_Unbroken.md). The Talent Winter's Protection states "Your tightly-bound winter gear negates elemental damage buffs from weather on damage against you. Also seems to provide some resistance to the Gale." This talent is (likely) exclusive to the Winter Corps Parka.

## Trivia

-   The Winter Corps Parka is a part of the Winter Corps set which currently only include the [Winter Corps Boots](Winter_Corps_Boots.md).
