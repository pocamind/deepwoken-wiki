# Levit

This race is a Vow of Iron EXCLUSIVE and has a 1% chance of being rolled.

It only has one variant, Jack, and 3 types of ears

It has the innate Talent Retrograde, which, upon use, it will set a checkpoint, where it will save your current health and position, and either once you have used the ability again, or the time limit runs out, you will be sent back to that checkpoint, returning you to the position you were standing at, and the % of health you were at.

-   This also means you can both LOSE and REGENERATE health, depending on your checkpoint health and your current health

You can obtain this race without rolling if you use the Strange Merchant Memento, it will be bound to that slot, however, and will be replaced if a different memento is equipped.
