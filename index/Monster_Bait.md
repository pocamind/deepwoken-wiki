# Monster Bait

Megalodaunt Bait is a craftable item in Deepwoken. It costs 2 chum to make.

Like the name suggests, it is used to spawn [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md). Megalodaunt Bait may also spawn Red Megalodaunts, but it is with a rare chance.

Note: The item is currently disabled temporarily.

## **Trivia**

"Winter 1291 marks that fateful night, when the rumbling of a million Megalodaunts thundered in our ears. They laid waste to our homes... And what little lands we had left to squabble over.

But, in the strange and inscrutable ways of the Deep, an age of plenty soon followed. So soon after a fall, our people were once again prospering..."

On 13th March, 2022, Megolodaunt Bait was added into the game. However, there was a glitch where it spawned infinite Sharkos, so servers were filled with many in Etris. It caused the developers to shutdown servers to disable the item, and Chests were doubled for 3 days to commemorate this event. This is now considered lore, as said by the developers.

There even were entire gangs of high levels server hopping and beelining to Megalodaunt hotspots to try to protect freshspawns and kill the Megalodaunts.
