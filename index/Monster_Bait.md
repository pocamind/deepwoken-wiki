# Monster Bait

“ Winter 1291 marks that fateful night, when the rumbling of a million Megalodaunts thundered in our ears. They laid waste to our homes... And what little lands we had left to squabble over. ”

― The dangers of over fishing

  
Megalodaunt Bait is a craftable item in Deepwoken. It costs two Chum to craft.

Like the name suggests, it is used to spawn [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md). Megalodaunt Bait may also spawn Red Megalodaunts in their place, but very rarely.

## **Trivia**

-   On 13th March, 2022, Megalodaunt Bait was added into the game. However, due to improper testing, there was nothing to prevent the item from many of the item being used at once, causing comically large numbers of Megalodaunts to spawn, so servers were filled with many, especially in Etris. It caused the developers to shutdown servers to disable the item, and Chests were doubled for 3 days to commemorate this event. This is now considered lore, as said by the developers, trying to cover up the mistake.
    -   There were even entire gangs of high levels server hopping and beelining to Megalodaunt-filled hotspots to try to protect freshspawns and kill the Megalodaunts.
    -   Many refer to the event as 'The Sharko Rumbling,' a reference to the rumbling from Attack On Titan, which happened to be airing around that time. The latest version log in the wiki reflects this disastrous event as **"The Dreaded Winter of 1291."**
