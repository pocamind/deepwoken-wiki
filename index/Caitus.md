## Description

Caitus is a [Khan](Khan.md) NPC. They are a member of [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md) and currently belonging to the 11th Scouting Legion. They stay with their group composed of [Atra](Atra.md), Lars, [Gaius](Gaius.md) and [Amara](Amara.md).

Caitus tasks the player with delivering [a message](Legion_Intelligence.md) about a [certain someone](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md) in an [Authority Prison](Merit_Detainment_Core.md). They will teach the [Imperium Kata](Imperium_Kata.md) style to the player upon reporting back after delivering said message.

Taking upon Caitus' quest requires above Ally reputation with The Hundred Legions.

## Location

Caitus is located in [Erisia](Erisia.md) inside [The Viper's Jaw](<The_Viper's_Jaw.md>) in [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md) hideout with other legion scouts.

## Trivia

-   Some words said by Caitus are derived from Latin terms:
    -   [Nemo](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/nemo#Latin) is a Latin word for "nobody", which they refer to the player as under Ally reputation.
        -   Nemo was a name used in place of the Outis alias in Latinized versions of _The Odyssey_ for the hero Odysseus, who is referred to as Ulysses in the Latin version.
    -   [Salve, in a Latin context](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/salve#Latin), is used for greetings and farewells.
        -   Salvete is the plural form of this word, which is mostly (and incorrectly) used by Caitus.
    -   [Teneō](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/teneo#Latin) has various meanings, but in this context, may be used for "understand".
    -   [Spondeō](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/spondeo) literally means "I promise".
-   You don't need to speak to Caitus before defeating Titus in order to get Imperium Kata.
