## Description

Songmaster Tzaraan is the [Songseeker](Songseeker.md) Mistress. It is speculated, she believes in reincarnation by returning to the song, just like all other [Songseekers](Songseeker.md). She is said to encourage freedom of mind for her students, but only if it aligns with her ideals.
