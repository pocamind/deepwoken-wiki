## Description

**Iron Cestus** is a Light Weapon that requires no LHT to use. It has no penetration, 7.5 scaling, 5 weight, 6 range, and 0.86x Swing Speed. It also has a base damage of 13.5, being the third highest damaging fist cestus alongside the [Legion Cestus](Legion_Cestus.md). Considered as of one of the best [Fist](Fists.md) weapons for starters.

## Notes

-   Best cestus for early game.
