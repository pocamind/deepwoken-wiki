## Description

**Iron Cestus** is a Light Weapon that requires no LHT to use. It has no penetration, 7.5 scaling, 5 weight, 6 range, 1.11x Swing Speed, and a base damage of 13.5. The first [Fist](Fists.md) weapon for players.
