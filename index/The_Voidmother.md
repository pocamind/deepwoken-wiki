## Description

The **Voidmother** is a formless, shapeless being. Once was a [Whaler](Knife_of_Eylis.md), and now "has ascended". It is located in [Voidheart](Voidheart.md), and teleports you to [Lower Erisia.](Lower_Erisia.md) Then she teleport you to the place where you used Voideye last time.

## Trivia

It every so slightly bares resemblence to Ya'alda from [Rogue Lineage](https://rogue-lineage.fandom.com/wiki/Rogue_Lineage_Wiki), another game Ragoozer & Arch\_Mage worked on.
