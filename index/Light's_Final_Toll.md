## Description

Light's Final Toll is a Legendary spirit lantern that floats beside the player. The player also wears a string of prayer beads around their arm. It can be obtained from [Layer 1](First_Layer.md) or [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md). It is currently the only weapon to scale off of Willpower, and the only weapon to scale off attributes that are not Attunement or Weapon.

## Trivia

-   This is the first weapon to scale from an attribute other than weapon or an attunement.
-   This comes from SudoMesh's entry (Light's Final Toll) from the Discord's art contest to have their weapon concept added into the game.
    -   As the winner of the contest, he was also given the exclusive [Aberrant Capra](Aberrant_Capra.md) subrace.
-   The only weapon that can gain scaling past 100, as with +2 WLP races, you can get to a "weapon stat" of 102.
-   With its abysmally small base damage of 12, Akira's ring will only provide an extra 1.44 damage at 100 WLP.
-   **Is not compatible with Jus Karita.**
-   Has misleading animations that extend the weapons visual reach way outside of it's effective hotbox.
-   Can switch between Way of Navae and Legion Kata critical attacks.
-   As this weapon is Legendary, it is completely unenchantable, though it was able to be enchanted for a bit.
