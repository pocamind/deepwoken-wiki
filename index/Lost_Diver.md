# Lost Diver

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Lost Divers are NPCs found in the [Kyrsan Wilds](Kyrsan_Wilds.md), worshipping an Obelisk. They remain docile from afar, but will attack players who get near the Obelisk.

A Lost Diver's appearance only consists of a [Black Diver](Black_Diver.md) outfit with their [race](Aspects.md) varying. Their bodies are slightly transparent.

## Trivia

-   The Lost Divers will continuously chant the following phrases when they are not aggroed.
    -   From softer skies...
    -   To fearsome gales...
    -   Our lord Ethiron...
    -   Will wake to this world...
-   When they are alerted to the player's position, they will say these lines before attacking:
    -   Help us.
    -   Help us. Please.
-   They only use [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md), fitting as they are trapped in the Layer 2, The Eternal Gale.
