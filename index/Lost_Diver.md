# Lost Diver

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

A Diver is an NPC that can only be found in [Kyrsan Wilds](Kyrsan_Wilds.md). They remain docile from afar, but when trying to get too close to the Obelisk, they will become hostile after all of them say, "Please save us." They also seem to be transparent, resembling some kind of ghosts. They all have the same weapon being the [Markor's Inheritor](<Markor's_Inheritor.md>) and all of them use [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md).

A Lost Diver's appearance only consists of a [Black Diver](Black_Diver.md) outfit with their [race](Aspects.md) varying.
