## Description

Auto-Repair is a cut content equipment enchantment that had an unknown effect.

This enchantment applies vertical white particle effects to the equipment piece.

## Effect

Auto-Repair's effects are unknown, though the name implies that it may have passively restored your armor value.

## Trivia

-   This enchantment was accidentally left in on Deepwoken's release week by developer Arch\_Mage. It has been unobtainable ever since.
