## Description

The **Pernach** is a medium blunt weapon in the [Club](Clubs.md) category. This flanged mace, considered to be the endgame version of the [Mace](Mace.md), has innate bleed and high armor penetration, making it one of the stronger weapons within its subclass.

## Critical

Pernach uses the default Club critical, an overhead swing. It has a scaled damage multiplier of **1x** and a cooldown of **5 seconds**. On hit, it [dazes](Status_Effects.md#daze) opponents and will insta-guardbreak them if they attempt to block it.
