# Karliah

“ Oh, sorry, I didn't see you there. I'm Karliah, apprentice Diver extraordinaire! ”

― Karliah

Template:NPC name

## Description

Karliah is an apprentice Diver in [Etris](Etris.md) who, by a quest, allows you to unbind your Charisma. She is quite inexperienced in combat, and was easily beaten down by [Mudskippers](Mudskipper.md) during her quest. Karliah's quest has numerous Charisma checks, and you can only unbind the attribute if you have it at max. Interestingly, she is considered as a love interest by many players.

More details about the quest can be found [here.](Etris.md#diver-escort-quest)

After the quest, Karliah quits [The Divers](The_Divers.md) and is now resting in her house, and is wearing different clothing. She is grateful for your insight on the realities of life, and reflects on how friendly the people of Etris are with her. Notably, she seems to show some romantic interest with your character.
