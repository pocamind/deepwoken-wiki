**Hero's Regalia** is a Unique [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit has no material crafting requirements.

This Outfit additionally requires the player to be at least [Power](Power.md) 15 and have at least 45 [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) to be crafted.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 3000 durability and grants +15% Physical Armor, +35% Elemental Armor, and +75% Ether Regen.
