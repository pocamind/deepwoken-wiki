# Cascabel Bay

LocationL Cascabel Bay LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Central Authority

  

## Description

Cascabel Bay is a Location in _Deepwoken_. It is a small sea making up the body of water surrounding [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) and part of [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md).

## Trivia

-   Although released along with many other seas, Cascabel Bay is the only one to be controlled by a faction.
