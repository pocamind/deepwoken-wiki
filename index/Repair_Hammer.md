# Repair Hammer

| Repair Hammer | |
|---|---|
| usage | Repairs your boat's health by 100 for one Wood. |

The **Repair Hammer** is a utility item in _Deepwoken_ that is used to recover the health of damaged boats.

## Overview

The Repair Hammer is a tool that can be found useful when traversing [the Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md) on low-end [boats](Ships_&_Sailing.md). It can be bought for in the [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md) next to [Schnitzel](Mantra_Collector.md).

## Usage

Using M1 on a damaged boat will spend one wood to recover 100 of its health points.
