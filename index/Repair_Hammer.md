# Repair Hammer

| Repair Hammer | |
|---|---|
| usage | Repairs your boat's health by 100 for one Wood. |

The **Repair Hammer** is a utility item in _Deepwoken_ that is used to increase the health of your boat.

## Overview

The Repair Hammer is a tool that can be found useful when traversing [the Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md) on low-end [boats](Ships_&_Sailing.md). It costs in [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md), next to [Schnitzel](Mantra_Collector.md).

## Usage

Using M1 on a damaged boat will recover 100 of it's health points at the cost of one Wood.
