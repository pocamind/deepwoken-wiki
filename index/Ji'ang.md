## Description

Lord Guardian Ji'ang was the final legitimate [Lord Guardian](Lord_Guardian.md) of the throne of [Etrea](Etrea.md) before the current regime. His rule came to an end during a coup led by [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md).
