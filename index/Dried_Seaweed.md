## Description

**Dried Seaweed** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is the result of cooking [Seaweed Bundle](Seaweed_Bundle.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) [CraftingCampfire](Campfire_Pit.md).

It is a type of **Plant**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Carnivore](Talents.md#carnivore) Talent.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 10% Stomach and 0% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Seaweed Bundle](Seaweed_Bundle.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
