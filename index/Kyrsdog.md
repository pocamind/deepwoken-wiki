## Description

**Kyrsdog** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Meat**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Vegetarian](Vegetarian.md) Flaw.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 50% Stomach and 20% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Bread](Bread.md), 1 [Frozen Membrane](Frozen_Membrane.md), and 1 [Ongo](Ongo.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) [CraftingCampfire](Campfire_Pit.md).

## Trivia

-   The **Kyrsdog** is a reference to the [Hot dog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_dog) from [real life](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth).
