## Description

**Trinity Galenwarde** was [the Hero of Wind](The_Five_Heroes.md). She wielded the [Hero's Blade of Wind](<Hero's_Blade_of_Wind.md>), and bound her soul to it along with the other heroes. Her soul is now located in an obelisk, located in the [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-obelisk-1)

## Trivia

-   The first Hero of Wind was named Sven Corbet, however this was later retconned into Trinity Galenwarde.[\[2\]](#cite-note-citation-2)
-   Trinity is one of the two Heroes that have their surname revealed, the first one being [Urek Felhand](Urek_Felhand.md).
