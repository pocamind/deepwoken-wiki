**Trivia and Notes**

For anyone wondering, the "wildcard mantra" slot is for any extra mantra you have. For example, you are able to use up to 4 combat mantras, with 3 being placed within the combat mantra category and 1 being placed in the wildcard mantra category

Thundercall moves are very ether demanding, so it's best to use a character rich in ether.
