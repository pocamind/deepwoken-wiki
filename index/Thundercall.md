-   **Training**

Can be trained by using [Self-Conducting Loop](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Self-Conducting_Loop?so=search)

Mantras can be upgraded at the Summer Isles for 100 notes (also is where firearms are purchased from). Be sure to level your thundercall high enough
