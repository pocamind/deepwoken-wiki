# Might Gem

| Might Gem | |
|---|---|

| weight | 1 |
| stack | x25 |
| type | Deep Gem |
| bankable | Yes |

| pass down | No |
| desc | A sign of strength...Increases a Mantra's posture damage by 10%. |

## Description

Might Gem is an orange diamond-shaped Deep Gem.

## Effect

The posture damage of your Mantra will be increased by 10%.

### Notes

-   This Gem is best used on high posture damage Mantras, due to it being a percentage increase in posture damage.
-   Might Gem can gain additional effects and more potency on its posture damage buff through the equipment Talents in the [Ascended Outlaw Set](Ascended_Outlaw_Hat.md).

## Crafting

This Gem can also be used to craft a variety of items.

-   [Equipment](Equipment.md): [Ascended Outlaw Hat](Ascended_Outlaw_Hat.md), [Ascended Outlaw Robes](Ascended_Outlaw_Robes.md), [Ascended Outlaw Boots](Ascended_Outlaw_Boots.md), and [Ascended Outlaw Mask](Ascended_Outlaw_Mask.md).

## Obtainment

The Might Gem is exclusively obtained from [Titus Fulminus Destellus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md).
