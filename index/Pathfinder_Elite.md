## Description

The Pathfinder Elite is an animated equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 5% Damage Vs Monsters and 2 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip). It can only be obtained as a rare drop from [Nautilodaunts](Nautilodaunt.md).
