## Description

The Imperator's Fury is a [Relic](Relics.md) [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Torso" category.

Equipping it grants the [Brute Strength](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent, replacing your Vent with a slam attack that deals damage and knocks enemies back. This attack has a longer cooldown than your regular Vent.
