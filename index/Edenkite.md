# Edenkite

File:Jurnal of an adventurer.png

Artistic representation of the Edenkite, as shown in [Pleeksty's](Amorus_Pleeksty.md) journal.

## Description

The Elder Edenkite are ancient, giant, serpent-like creatures capable of flight that live in the Northern Luminant. We have never seen an Edenkite before _(They probably look somewhat like [Dread Serpent)](Dread_Serpent.md),_ although they are referenced in the [Drakkard](Drakkard.md) race and the [Tears of the Edenkite enchantment](Enchantments.md). The Edenkite taught the [Drakkard](Drakkard.md) how to meditate, allowing them to greatly slow their hunger consumption and passively gain EXP.

Considered prophets of infinite wisdom. After an Elder Edenkite gave The Mad Steward a prophecy that the fate of the world will be its inevitable destruction, The Mad Steward decapitated the Edenkite that gave him the prophecy and ate its heart, filling his head with every tradgedy that ever occured and transforming him into the first [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md). The remaining Edenkite retreated back into the tundra of the Northern Luminant, never to be seen again, and the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md) can be fought in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md).

Deepwoken has a very interesting pattern when it comes to powerful beings. When you consume a part of a powerful being, you gain a part of their power, examples being [Starkindred](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md) (the [Celestial](Celestial.md)s), [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md) and the First Ministry Prophet are _[Deepwokens](Deepwoken.md)_ (Drowned Gods). When you serve under, or are a hybrid of the being(s), you also gain, albeit less, power. Examples of this are the [Lightborn](Lightborn.md) and the [Krulians](Krulian.md). For the Edenkite, consuming a part of them turns you into a Dread Serpent, and serving under them makes you a [Drakkard](Drakkard.md), as far as we know.
