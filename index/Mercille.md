## Description

Mercille is a Vesperian NPC in [Bluster Rift](Bluster_Rift.md), Summer Company Camp near the [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md).

You can turn sacks found in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) and [Bluster Rift](Bluster_Rift.md) to her for a chest, much like the [Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md)'s explosive crates found on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) that you deliver back to them.
