## Description

**Candy** is a [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

It can be obtained from opening a [Goodie Bag](Goodie_Bag.md).

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 10% Stomach and 5% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Full buff, affecting 10% of the Stomach bar. The buff reduces natural Stomach loss by 10%.
