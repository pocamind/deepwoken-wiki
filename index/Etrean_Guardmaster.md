## Description

Etrean Guardsmaster is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) [NPC](NPC.md). The player is able to break off their handcuffs if they have been sent to execution by an Authority NPC or an [Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md).

## Location

Etrean Guardsmaster is located around [Port Merit](Port_Merit.md), on a [Dinghy](Dinghy.md) in the water. Their location changes periodically between three different spots.

## Trivia

-   If you talk to them when one minute remains, it reads "1 minutes remain."
    -   If you talk to them when less than a minute remains, no remaining time is displayed.
-   The Shipping Schedule dialogue takes priority over the Handcuffs dialogue, and because you cannot hold any items while handcuffed, this softlocks you.
