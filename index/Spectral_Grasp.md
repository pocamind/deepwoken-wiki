## Description

**Spectral Grasp** is a light dagger Hallowtide exclusive weapon. Bought from [Glaive Spellhardt](Glaive_Spellhardt.md) for 100 [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md) Joy or from any [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) for 7 Knowledge if you have the [Hollowtide 2024 Medal](Seasonal_Medals.md), and can also be found in chests in the [Trial of the Gourdskipper](<Widow's_Wail_Cemetery.md>).

This hand appears ancient and decrepit, as though it had been lifeless for untold ages. Its cold, vile grasp withers the very soul of all those who fall victim to it.

## Critical

After a short wind-up, the user slashes twice with the weapon, applying 10 Wither and dealing 1x scaled damage for each hit. This critical has innate bleed on both hits.

The critical has a cooldown of **5 seconds**.

## Trivia

-   The M1 attacks have a special ghastly sound effect that is unique to this weapon.
-   This weapon might be inspired by Karambit and its M1 and crit may be a nod to a Indonesian martial arts called 'Silat' which usually paired with a Karambit
-   Like all other Hallowtide exclusive weapons, this is not droppable.
-   When it first released, Spectral Grasp had the [Whaling Knife](Whaling_Knife.md)'s critical and no unique animations, this was changed the in a hotfix soon afterwards.
