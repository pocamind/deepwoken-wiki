## Description

Adretian Aegis is a blue and gold mid-game shield that bears the icon of [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md).

## Trivia

-   Adretian Aegis cannot be dropped nor banked.
-   Adretian Aegis cannot be purchased if you are not using the [Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md) Origin.
-   It **can** be [passed down](Hippocampal_Pool.md) however, allowing you to equip it on your next character, provided you meet its requirements.
