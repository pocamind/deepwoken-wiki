## Lore

A race with feline-like qualities, the Felinor are a capricious lot. Often vagarious and of swinging moods, they are experts at self-preservation and survival - boasting sharp claws on paw-like hands, the Felinor are renowned for their combat skill.

Fighters through and through, the Felinor do not build magnificent castles or form enormous kingdoms - instead they live in gregarious nomadic communities of modest size, wandering from place to place, seeking new resources to ensure their survival.

Naturally curious and of sharp senses, the Felinor also make for incredible explorers - though, their innate disdain for water, and their proneness for seasickness often hinders their attempts at seafaring. Nonetheless, numerous Felinor explorers have carved their names into the cornerstones of history...

As a result, the Felinor have spread far and wide - roaming hills and valleys, deserts and tundras, they are a cunning and hardy race of capricious feline-like warriors.

## Appearance

Felinors are cat-eared humanoids with yellow/green eyes. They have light gray hair/grayish/bage hair.
