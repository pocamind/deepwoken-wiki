# Authority Soldier

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

The Authority Flamecharmer is an enemy NPC found in [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md). They are the lowest ranking, and thus the most incompetent, members of The Central Authority's army. As a result, they have a standard appearance, sporting Authority Peacekeeper armor and an Authority Helm.
