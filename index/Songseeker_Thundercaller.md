# Songseeker Thundercaller

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0 **Thundercaller Monks** are a [humanoid enemy](Enemies.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Appearance

Thundercaller Monks wear dark wraps and a unique dark green mask. Their [race](Aspects.md) and gender can vary.

## **Locations**

Thundercaller Monks can appear in [Random Encounters](Random_Encounters.md) and in [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md).

## Enemy Information

### Health

Thundercaller Monks have 1000 health.

### Weapons

Thundercaller Monks are armed with their [fists](Fists.md), and use the Way of Navae fist style.

### Combat

Thundercaller Monks have the Way of Navae fist style, but alternatively use [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) Mantras against their opponents. These Mantras have little to no cooldown.

-   **Jolt Grab**: Teleports into the opponent and slams them into the ground. Deals damage and stun. Can be blocked and parried.
-   **Thunder Kick**: The monk sends out a powerful kick forward. Deals damage, stun and knockback. Can be blocked and parried.
-   **Lightning Stream**: Grapples onto the player and pulls them in. Deals damage and stun. Can be blocked and parried.

Thundercaller Monks cannot be assassinated and have no Ether cost to their Mantras. These Mantras combined with the fist style and stun can make this enemy very difficult to battle.

### Drops

Thundercaller Monks don't drop anything on death. If fought in a [Random Encounter](Random_Encounters.md), they will spawn a chest on death.

## Trivia/Notes

-   It is not advised to fight these below [Power](Power.md) 30, as they're extremely difficult to fight without spare health.
-   Despite being immune to [assassination](Stealth.md), the player can still sneak behind and use a powerful mantra to pile up some good damage.
-   Constantly flourishing them/using Mantras will prevent them from spamming Mantras.
-   So far the most intelligent Monk as they have a fighting style.
-   Whenever they land Lightning Stream, they are likely to use Thunder Kick right after.
