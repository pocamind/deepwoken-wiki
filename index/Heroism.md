## Description

Heroism is a buffing [enchantment](Enchantments.md) that grants the user damage buffs if their health is within a certain range.

## Effect

When your health is at 75% or higher, Heroism grants a damage buff to your weapon attacks scaling on how close to 100% health you are. When your health is at 25% or lower, Heroism grants a damage buff to your weapon attacks scaling on how close to 0% health you are.

Heroism's damage buff is lessened in PvE.

Being under certain negative status effects will apply them to your opponent when you hit them with your weapon attacks. Note: this does not remove the status effect from yourself, it only applies it to your opponent.

### Notes

-   Heroism's damage buffs start at +4% damage when at 75% or 25% health, and it gains +4% damage for every 5% health you are towards your respective goal. This means you gain a +20% damage buff when at 100% or 0% health.
    -   In PvE, Heroism grants a 1% damage buff for being at 75% or 25% health, and gains +1% for every 5% health you are towards your respective goal. This means you only gain a +5% damage buff when at 100% or 0% health in PvE.
-   Heroism's status effect transfer works on the following [Status Effects](Status_Effects.md): [Burn](Status_Effects.md#burn), [Chill](Status_Effects.md#chill), [Suffocation](Status_Effects.md#suffocation), [Winded](Status_Effects.md#winded), [Shock](Status_Effects.md#shock), [Fear](Status_Effects.md#fear-the-dark) (from the [Fear the Dark](Talents.md#fear-the-dark) Talent), [Anti-Heal](Status_Effects.md#anti-heal), and [Taunt](Status_Effects.md#taunt).
-   The status effect transfer applies with you having the ownership of the status effect. This means if you were to transfer [Burn](Status_Effects.md#burn) for example, it'd scale on **your** [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) investment and it'd be affected by **your** Flamecharm Talents, not your opponents.
