# Songseeker Temple

LocationL Songseeker Temple LocationR

There are those who seek to wield the Song as those of the Old World once did, as an artform to be cultivated into something transcendent.

  

**Songseeker Temple** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark found in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md).

## Overview

Songseeker Temple resembles a dojo on the interior. It can be found higher in the island with the windmill on it.

Inside the Temple is Amashi, a [spelltrainer](Attunement_Trainers.md).

## Contents

### NPCs

-   Players can find [Amashi](Amashi,_Master_of_Flame.md), a [spelltrainer](Attunement_Trainers.md) who can help you acquire [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md), or upgrade your existing [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) [Mantras](Mantras.md).
-   Just outside the temple, players can find [Yamaketzal](Yamaketzal.md), who can either give you the [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) or remove your [Tarnished](Boons_and_Flaws.md#flaws) Flaw.

## Quests

### Flamecharm Spelltrainer Quest

-   If you are unattuned with [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md), the [spelltrainer](Attunement_Trainers.md) will give you a quest.
-   He will ask for five [Dying Embers](Dying_Embers.md), obtain them and give them to him. This is reduced to 2 Dying Embers if you have previously unlocked Flamecharm on your account.
-   Doing this will result in a potion that will attune you with [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md).
