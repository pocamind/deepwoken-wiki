The Kyrswynter is a legendary Ice Spear, forged in a forgotten era down in Kyrsa. The creation of this spear was long before Kyrsa fell under the wrath of Ethiron. It's design is similar to that of a Japanese Naginata. It's intricate design dictates on how experienced Old Kyrsa was with their technology.

1.  Get **75** Frostdraw and obtain the talent [Ethiron's Gaze](Talent_Quests.md).
2.  Speak to [Naerotiv](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). He is on the right path of the first Boss Shrine.
3.  Defeat the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md). (May be changed in the future to not require this)
4.  Go to [Frosviernr Joraelnaero](Frosviernr_Joraelnaero.md) as the Eternal Gale is collapsing and speak to him. You need the 75 Frostdraw to **obtain** the Kyrswynter but you need points in Medium Weapon to **equip** the Kyrswynter.

## Trivia

-   This weapon cannot be enchanted.
-   Kyrswynter running crit cannot be parried, however CAN be blocked, but deals INSANE posture damage.
