# Bandit Leader

## Overview

Template:Creature Infobox V3 Bandit Captains are hostile NPCs. They are found around a campfire accompanied by 2 other [bandits](Bandit.md) as an event and at the abandoned village in a group of 5. One at the tower, one just outside the entrance, one patrolling between the closest 2 houses, one at the campfire, and one inside the supplies found by going up the staircase at the right of the entrance and heading up the slope. They can also be encountered in the Summer Isle guards vs Bandits event.

Their appearance consists of the Duelist's Attire.

## Tips

-   You can assassinate them.
    -   Assassinations will one shot if you have the "Assassin" talent.
