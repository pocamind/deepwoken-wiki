## Description

Curse of Repulsion is a buffing curse enchantment that drains your opponent's blood on hit.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to a glowing blue and gives it a Vent-like electricity particles.

## Effect

Upon blocking an attack Curse of Repulsion will push your opponent backwards. This has a 2.5 second cooldown with no range limit.

### Curse

When this enchantment procs, you take 30% chip damage from your opponent's attack. This stacks additively with your opponent's chip damage.

### Notes

-   Chip damage does not have PEN, making this enchantment's curse not as bad as it seems on paper.
