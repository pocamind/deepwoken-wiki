# Vesque

| Vesque | |
|---|---|
| aspect | Etrean |
| location | Isle of Vigils |

Vesque is an NPC in the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). You can get [Fang and Coil](Fang_and_Coil.md) by talking to him. To get Fang and Coil, you need 20 Light Weapon, **Friend** [Etrea](Etrea.md) reputation or higher, and have killed [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md).
