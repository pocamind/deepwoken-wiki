## Description

Smouldering Hallow is a Heavy Greatcannon Weapon with a pumpkin motif.

Like all other Hallowtide exclusive weapons, it cannot be dropped to other players.

## Critical

On critical, the user fires a volley of pumpkin projectiles out of their cannon.
