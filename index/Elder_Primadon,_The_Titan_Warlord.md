Template:Creature Infobox V3Elder Primadon, the Titan Warlord is a stronger version of [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), with a larger body, darker skin tone, and glowing eyes.

## Description

Similar to Primadon, can be summoned by making an offering. ( Most relics work, but be careful not to spawn regular Primadon. )
