## Description

The Foreman is a [Canor](Canor.md) NPC. He runs Nagatony's Picture Booth.

He is a short, balding man.

## Location

The Foreman can be found in Nagatony's Picture Booth, inside of a wall hidden behind the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md).
