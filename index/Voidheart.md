# Voidheart

LocationL Voidheart LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Knives of Eylis

Halls of inscrutable architecture stretch as far as the eye can see. No human could've constructed this, almost defying the laws of the realm itself.

  

**The Voidheart** is a location in _Deepwoken._ It is a landmark in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) and the current forward base of the Knives of Eylis, who signed contract with Eylis herself.

## Overview

Inside Voidheart

Voidheart looks like room inside castle, having alot of rooms in it and high ceilings. Everything is gray here. Voidheart reminds Trial of One, having antiquarian, training gear to buy and blacksmith. It's location inside Voidsea, where you can actually buy anything you need for early game.

## Points of Interest

-   A bounty board that's always full (Knives of Eylis rep is catching up to a shit ton of people now)
-   The [Dusklit Garden](Dusklit_Garden.md)
-   A chess board
-   The Voidmother, can teleport you to [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md)
-   Antiquarian, as well as General/Mantra Shop items
