## Description

The Overcoat is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. They are thick coats made to protect its wearers from the environment. Black Overcoats can be found in [Hell Mode](Hell_Mode.md), while the Brown & White Overcoats can be found in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) and rarely, [Erisia](Erisia.md).

## Trivia

-   As seen above, Brown Overcoat is the only coat with its own unique description, whilst the Black & White Overcoats use the same description as the Parkas. This is simply a writing error in the game's code.
-   All 3 Overcoats have different stats when compared to one another. You can see their stat/pip info here: [Arms Equipment](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Equipment#Arms)
    -   The Black Overcoat is slightly better than its counterparts in terms of stats.
-   Overcoats look similar to Parkas.
-   Their descriptions are flavor text; they do not actually provide any resistance against environmental effects.
