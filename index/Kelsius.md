## Description

Kelsius is an Adret NPC who works as the librarian at the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). Upon accepting his quest, he tasks the player into retrieving his lost books for a mantra hand and access to books to instantly gain a random attribute point. If the player has already obtained an extra mantra hand, completing his quest will instead reward 1 [Knowledge](Knowledge.md).

He is a [Thundercaller](Thundercall.md) and former member of [the Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md)'s Shock Corps.

## Location

Kelsius is located inside the library at [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

## Lost Books Quest

This quest tasks the player with retrieving the missing books from the cabin located under [Etris](Etris.md). Upon interacting with the NPC blocking the doorway, they will move aside and allow entry inside the cabin, the player will then have to speak to [Khan](<Khan_(NPC).md>), who reveals Kelsius as a "murderer" and knocking the player out. Upon returning to Kelsius, the player gains a mantra hand and the ability to use the attribute books for notes.

If the quest was done on the account previously, Kelsius will instead ask the player to find the books in Isle of Vigils. These books can be located behind the building that resides the [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) and [Banker](Banker.md) on a pile of rocks.

## Trivia

-   Despite their record, Kelsius was accepted by the Maestro to work as the vigils librarian.
-   You are able to completely skip the quest and unlock special dialogue if you have completed the [Castaway](Castaway.md) tutorial on the same character.
-   Oddly enough, having the [Authority Ensign](Authority_Ensign.md) [Origin](Origins.md) doesn't unlock new dialogue from him, despite his past as a member of the Shock Corps.
