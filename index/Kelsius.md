## Description

Kelsius is an Adret NPC located in the **[Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md)** library that gives you a quest for a free mantra hand. After you complete that quest, you can buy a random [attribute](Attributes.md) point for a random amount of Notes. A Khan refers to him as a "murderer".
