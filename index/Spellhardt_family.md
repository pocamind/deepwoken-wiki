## Description

The Spellhardt family are a noble family of [Lightborns](Lightborn.md), originating from the city of [Constellia](Constellia.md), currently residing in [the Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md).

Before the fall of Constellia, the Keep was very well-manned. Shortly after the aforementioned event, the Keep was left solely in the hands of the family, to be managed by themselves.

The Spellhardt Family has a tradition, called [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md), which they still keep going purely for their own entertainment, as it gets lively in the Keep.
