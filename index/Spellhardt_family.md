# Spellhardt family

## Description

The Spellhardt Family are a noble family of [Lightborns](Lightborn.md), originating from the city of [Constellia](Lore.md#constellia), currently residing in [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md), which appears in both [Luminants](Luminants.md) every [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md).

Before the [fall of Constellia](Lore.md#constellia), [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md) was very well-manned. Shortly after the aforementioned event, [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md) was left at the hands of the family, to be managed by themselves.

The Spellhardt Family has a tradition, called [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md), which they still keep going purely for their own entertainment, as it gets lively in [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md).

## Reputation

Unlike many non-agressive [factions](Factions_&_Groups.md), the player cannot gain, or lose reputation with The Spellhardt Family.

The Spellhardt Family does not have relations to any [factions](Factions_&_Groups.md), besides Constellia, which is not present in-game, but mentioned in the [lore](Lore.md).

## Notable members

[Lysander Spellhardt](Lysander_Spellhardt.md): Presumably, the head of the family and the oldest in the family. Makes the [Hallowtide Mask](Hallowtide_Mask.md) for the player, if they have all the [ingredients](Hallowtide_Mask_Ingredients.md) for it. Sells [Seasonal Medals](Seasonal_Medals.md), Hallowtide [aspect](Aspects.md) variant, Hallowtide-exclusive armor, equipment and [Relics](Relics.md) for [Joy](Joy.md).

[Galpin Spellhardt, Musicologist Extraordinaire](Galpin_Spellhardt,_Musicologist_Extraordinaire.md): The second oldest person in the family. Sells different [songbooks](Books.md) and a special Spectral [Instruments](Instruments.md) set for [Joy](Joy.md). Used to be a faculty chair at The Empyrean College of Cadence.

[Glaive Spellhardt](Glaive_Spellhardt.md): Sells Hallowtide-exclusive [weapons](Weapons.md), Master [outfit](Outfits.md) schematics, the [Harrowing](Enchantments.md#harrowing) [Enchant Stone](Enchant_Stone.md) and the [Pumpkin Pitch](Pumpkin_Pitch.md) [mantra](Mantras.md) for [Joy](Joy.md).
