## Description

Vigil Isaac is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC. He is a newly appointed Vigil.

Isaac is brother to [Apprentice Kj](Apprentice_Kj.md), having recently become a Vigil under [Evengarde Rest](Evengarde_Rest.md). Giving him a [Gale Stone](Gale_Stone.md) allows the player to complete the quest "Vigi's Savior" and speak to Apprentice Kj to receive a [Curiosity](Quests.md) [Talent](Talent.md) hand and an audience with Evengarde Rest.

## Quest

### "Vigil's Savior"

1.  To start the quest, the player has to finish Apprentice Kj's dialogue. He will then ask the player to find and rescue Vigil Isaac.
2.  Vigil Isaac can be found in a house at Upper Erisia, following a path leading from the waterfall at [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md) connected to the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md).
3.  After giving a Gale Stone to Vigil Isaac, head back to Apprentice Kj to complete the quest.

## Location

Vigil Isaac is located initially at [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md), sitting injured in a run-down house. He can later be found inside of the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md) near his brother.
