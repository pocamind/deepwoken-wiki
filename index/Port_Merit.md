# Port Merit

LocationL Port Merit LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Central Authority

A bustling hub of trade and entertainment situated on the margins of Fort Merit.

  

**Port Merit** is a sub-location in _Deepwoken_. It is landmark on [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md) found in the [Aratel Sea](Aratel_Sea.md).

## Overview

Port Merit is the port leading to [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md). The Port is filled with a multitude of buildings such as a bank, Inn, and Antiquarian.
