## Description

Meemaw is a [Felinor](Felinor.md) NPC. She is the grandmother of [Lucien](Lucien.md).

## Location

Meemaw is located at [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), standing outside of the mineshaft.

## Trivia

-   Meemaw is an affectionate term for one's grandmother. Given Lucien calls her "meemaw" with an uncapitalized "m", it's unclear if Meemaw is really her name or simply a title.
