# Ivory

_Note: this page is not complete, as Verse 2 has only come out recently. I also do not know the proper format._

## Description

Ivory is a [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC questgiver that can be found in the [Pathfinder's Respite](<Pathfinder's_Respite.md>). Ivory gives the player a quest to go up to the highest peak in Erisia, where the Watcher is, and to gaze into the purple flame.

Ivory sitting down in the small shrine in the middle of Pathfinder's Respite

## Quest

### The quest

Ivory tells the player that they are a simple new pathfinder, and says that they can teach you new movement if you go to the highest peak in Erisia and gaze into the purple flame.

### Completion

To complete the quest, head to the Temple Of The Forgotten Flame and into the Temple Courtyard. Go inside the temple and interact with one of the purple fires. It will then prompt you to go back to Ivory. Finish the quest by going back

### Reward

Ivory congratulates the player for completing the quest and teaches them a new move. The reward for completing this quest is the new Air Dash[\[1\]](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Of_The_Forgotten_Flame) ability. Press Q in the air to dash a short distance.
