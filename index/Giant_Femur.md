## Description

The **Giant Femur** is used to gain the "Brachial Spear" [Monster Mantra](Monster_Mantras.md) from [Carrion, Father of Evolution](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md) in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) after he has been interacted with in [Floor 2](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). It can be obtained as rare drop from a [Bonekeeper](Bone_Keeper.md), and cannot be dropped alongside every other rare monster part.
