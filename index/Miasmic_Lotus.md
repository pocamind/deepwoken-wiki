# Miasmic Lotus

A Miasmic Lotus

The Miasmic Lotus can be found in the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md) in a hollow tree and next to a vine, 2 Miasmic Lotuses are used to make the Flask of Charisma at a [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md).
