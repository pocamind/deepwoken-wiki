# Bone Keeper

File:UnknownTwo.png

## Description

Unknown Two, also known as Giant [Nautilodaunt](Nautilodaunt.md), is a new monster in Layer 2 of [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

Unknown Two appears to be a giant, armored humanoid creature with (somewhat) normal proportions, wearing what seems to be some kind of loincloth or tunic, and with a helmet. It has clearly clawed feet, however it's arms appear to be either stubs with metallic claws, or it's "hands" are four claws, one on each sidee of their hand.

Unknown Two is INCREDIBLY fast, being able to leap from across the room it's in in less than a second, towards any unfortunate victims.

There's a few interesting things about Unknown Two: It uses a ground-pound attack similar to Nautilodaunt, stomping the floor to attack targets, however instead of shockwaves, Barnacles (the same ones found on the head and backs of [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md)) will burst from the ground and throw players up. This along with their similar size might mean some correlation, especially moreso considering Megalodaunts and Nautilodaunts are already related with eachother, and Unknown Two seems to be related with both of them. This also may draw some correlation with Unknown One.

Finally, Unknown Two appears to be in the same area/location as Unknown One, in a very cramped and rocky tunnel system.

Some extra information regarding this enemy, Infernasu in this [clip](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AnjsiWxeR6A&t=68s) from his live stream called it "the bone one". This might mean that the shockwave it does is not actually causing barnacles to burst from the ground but bones. He also mentioned it being as annoying as a Megalodaunt.
