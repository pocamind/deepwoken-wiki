# Bone Keeper

File:UnknownTwo.png

## Description

Bone Keeper, also known as Giant [Nautilodaunt](Nautilodaunt.md) and Garry, is a new monster in Layer 2 of [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

Bone Keeper appears to be a giant, armored humanoid creature, with inhuman proportions, having extremely short legs and large arms. It has clearly clawed feet, however it's arms appear to be either stubs with metallic claws, or it's "hands" are four claws, one on each side of their hand. It appears to be wearing clothes, including a skirt, and even armor, however it's unknown if these are actually clothes/armor, and not just it's biological body. It's head is a different color than it's main body, though.

Bone Keepers are INCREDIBLY fast, being able to leap from across the room it's in in less than a second, towards any unfortunate victims.

There's a few interesting things about Bone Keeper: It uses a ground-pound attack similar to Nautilodaunt, and is also found within the same location as [Bounders](Bounder.md), who are similar to Megalodaunts (who are similar to Nautilodaunts) so it's just one giant chain of similarities.

Some extra information regarding this enemy, Infernasu in this [clip](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AnjsiWxeR6A&t=68s) from his live stream called it "The Bone One". He also mentioned it being as annoying as a Megalodaunt.
