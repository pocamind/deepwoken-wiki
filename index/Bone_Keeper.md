# Bone Keeper

_Also known as The Boner_

File:UnknownTwo.png

## Description

The Bone Keeper is a new monster in Layer 2 of [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

Bone Keeper appears to be a giant, armored humanoid creature, with inhuman proportions, having extremely short legs and large arms. It has clearly clawed feet, however it's arms appear to be either stubs with metallic claws, or it's "hands" are four claws, one on each side of their hand. Its lower half appears to be a large bell. It appears to be wearing clothes, such as armor, however it's unknown if these are actually clothes/armor, and not just it's biological body. Its head is a different color than it's main body, though.

Bone Keepers are INCREDIBLY fast, being able to leap from across the room in less than a second, towards any unfortunate victims.
