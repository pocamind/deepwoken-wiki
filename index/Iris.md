## Description

Iris is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) NPC. They task the player to place a [Delicate Flower](Delicate_Flower.md) on the grave of their deceased. Upon completion you will be granted a [Vibrant Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md) and [Hive](The_Hive.md) reputation.
