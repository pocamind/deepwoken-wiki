## Description

Iris is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) NPC. Talking to them will start the Grave Tending [quest](Quests.md) where they ask you to bring a flower to their brother's grave.

## Location

Iris is located in [The Lordsgrove](The_Lordsgrove.md), following the path that trails left.

## Trivia

-   When damaging a flower and receiving a new one, the new flower will NOT be destroyed by any sources of damage. This can be abused to get a durable flower when doing the quest. (Particularly achieved by activating fall damage in the same room the quest giver is, then talking to him right afterwards)
