**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.

Gives you the [Delicate Flower](Delicate_Flower.md) and an identical quest to [Suri's](Suri.md) one with it.

Upon completion you will be granted a [Vibrant Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md), [Hive](The_Hive.md) reputation and courage.
