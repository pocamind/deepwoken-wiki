# Marauder's Entrance

Marauder's Entrance is a location in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of [The Depths](The_Depths.md), Located at one of the sides of the map, the way to Marauder's Entrance has a small parkour section, which is quite simple, but risky because of the fact that the void hitbox is just a few studs of height from the parkour, meaning that a simple mistake may cause you to fall into either [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md) or your [Death](Death.md). depending on the requirements of entering the second layer[\[1\]](#cite-note-1).

As of currently, this place has no real use and was speculated that it would be the entrance to [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md), but it has been left untouched and abandoned by the developers (To my extent of knowledge at least), Even the [Resonance Door](Resonance.md) that leads to Marauder's Entrance cannot be interacted with.

Although there is barely any footage of the inside of this place, there is at least 1 video that was created over a year ago. The surrounding looks like an Abandoned [Trial of One](Trial_of_One.md)'s chamber, that has a pillar in the middle, and above the pillar is something that looks like [The Voidmother](The_Voidmother.md), and above it a flashing light similar to the one above the Depths Trial
