# Marauder's Entrance

LocationL Marauder's Entrance LocationR

  
  

## Overview

Marauder's Entrance is a location in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of [The Depths](The_Depths.md), located at a side of the map near the [District of Commerce](District_of_Commerce.md). The way to Marauder's Entrance has a small parkour section, which is quite simple, but risky because of the fact that the kill bricks/void are just a few studs below the parkour, meaning that a simple mistake may cause you to fall into either [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md) or your [Death](Death.md), depending on your [Power](Power.md) and [Drowns](Death.md).

After entering, there is a hall inside which is where [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md) is located. Upon conversing with the mechanism, you'll go within it and face trials of strength and willpower, that reward great amounts of treasure.

## Trivia

-   The Marauder's Entrance was one of the most abandoned places in the depths, with most players never coming close to it. It used to have a resonance door but now is replaced with a hallway to the Diluvian Mechanism. Allowing any level to test their strength.
