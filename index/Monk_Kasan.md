## Description

Monk Kasan is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC. He is a monk of the Monastery of Mur and a member of the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md).

## Location

Monk Kasan can be found sitting inside of [The Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md) beside [High Monk Junsui](High_Monk_Junsui.md).
