## Description

Astral is a PvE-based buffing [enchantment](Enchantments.md) that increases your damage against NPCs.

## Effect

Astral increases the damage of your M1s by 20% in PvE.

Additionally, upon parrying three times in quick succession or landing a critical attack, a star will rain down, dealing damage in an AoE. This deals 100 PvE damage or 4% of a player's health in PvP.

### Notes

-   Astral's damage buff bypasses both the soft and hard damage modifier caps.
-   The [Celestial Boots](Celestial_Boots.md) increases Astral's damage buff from +20% M1 damage to +25%.

## Trivia

-   Astral previously required having met a [Wishmaker](Developer_Specs.md#iltria-leafre-spellhardt-retired) to obtain the enchant.
