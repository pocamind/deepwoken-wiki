## Description

Rudral is a [Capra](Capra.md) NPC, who is the [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) for [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>). He is an essential part for unlocking [Ironsing](Ironsing.md).

## Location

Rudral is located near the [Inn](Inn.md) and Pub at Miner's Landing.
