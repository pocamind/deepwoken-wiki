# Avian

Template:NPC name

## Description

Avian is a Etrean NPC in [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

During the first meeting, he asks you to bring him a Weapon Manaul so that he can become stronger and defeat [the guy on the opposite side](Evengarde_Rest.md). Choosing to give him the Weapon Manual will deduct one use from your Weapon Manual and gain a little experience.
