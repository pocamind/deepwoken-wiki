# Avian

Template:NPC name

## Description

Avian is a young [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC in [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

During the first meeting, he asks you to bring him a Weapon Manual so that he can become stronger and defeat [the guy on the opposite side](Evengarde_Rest.md). Choosing to give him the Weapon Manual will deduct one use from your Weapon Manual and grant a little experience.

## Trivia

-   The person Avian speaks about beating, is in fact, [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md), considered one of the most powerful mortals in Deepwoken. Needless to say, no matter how much training he puts in, it is unlikely he can actually beat [Maestro](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md).
-   His quest will not remove your weapon manual, only deduct one usage from it. So this is considered one of the easiest quests in the game (since it's basically just dialogue spam).
