# Black Diver

Template:ArmorInfobox**Black Diver** is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, one (1) Blessed Gem, five (5) Vibrant Gems, one (1) Megalodaunt Hide, one (1) Odd Tentacle and one (1) Thresher Spine.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 45 and [Willpower](Attributes.md) 20.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2600 durability and grants +30% Physical resistance, +30% Elemental resistance, +20% Lightning resistance, +20% Shadow resistance, +16 Sanity and +300% Ether Regen.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
