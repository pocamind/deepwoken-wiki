## Description

The Hivelord Mask is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Face" category. It can be found in [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) chests as well as being directly dropped from the [Hive Constructs](Hive_Construct.md) at [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) with a low chance. It can also be found in chests dropped by [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md), [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md), and [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md).

The Hivelord Mask is a sand-colored ovular mask with five spike jutting out from the top, gradually tapering into a point. Two eyes and an irregular nose shape are painted onto the mask. When worn, the Hivelord Mask completely covers the wearer's face.
