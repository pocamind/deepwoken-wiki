# Hivelord Mask

## Overview

Template:EquipmentStats The **Hivelord Mask** is an accessory under the "Face" category. It can be found in [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) chests as well as being directly dropped from the [Hive Constructs](Hive_Construct.md) at [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) with a low chance.

## Appearance

The Hivelord Mask is a sand-colored ovular mask with five spike jutting out from the top, gradually tapering into a point. Two eyes and an irregular nose shope are painted onto the mask. When worn, the Hivelord Mask completely covers the wearer's face.
