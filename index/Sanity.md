# Sanity

_"Do you know the definition, of insanity?" - Crazed Stranded_

## Description

Sanity is your character's mental integrity in Deepwoken. You lose sanity from encountering Sea Monsters enough to mentally damage your character, or through other sources.

Blue-like vignettes (4 corners of your screen) is an indication of your character's insanity. Full insanity deals constant damage until you gain back Sanity.

To regain sanity, the only known method (as of now) is by resting in a Campfire for a period of time.

There are certain [Talents](Talents.md) that benefit or reduce the progression of Insanity. Such talents like "Lose your Mind" or "Breathing Exercise".
