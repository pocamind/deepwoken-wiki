# Celtor Wastes

LocationL Celtor Wastes LocationR

  
  

  

  
**Celtor Wastes** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is a region in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of [The Depths](The_Depths.md) and takes up a large part of the First Layer

## Overview

The area encompassing much of [the Depths](The_Depths.md), located outside the [City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).
