## Description

**Remaz** was one of the side-characters in the [book](Books.md) [Lament of Cirryn](Lament_of_Cirryn.md). He is a [Lifeweave](Lifeweave.md) scholar of [The Hive](The_Hive.md), who was sent out on a mission along with 2 other scholars to revive [Cirryn](Cirryn.md) by a hivelord [Ximac Diri](Ximac_Diri.md).

**Remaz** was a very snooty man, constantly making fun of [Ehkusa](Ehkusa.md) and not realizing how much he hurts her feelings. Although, after he crossed the line and making fun of [Ehkusa](Ehkusa.md)'s dead friend, [Ryrda](Ryrda.md), he apologized to her and stopped. He was also very caring, rather sacrificing himself, than letting his friends die.
