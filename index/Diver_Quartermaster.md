# Diver Quartermaster

Diver Quartermaster

The Diver Quartermaster is the leader of an expedition to retrieve a create found in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).

Returning the create to the Diver Quartermaster rewards you with an [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>), a [Smith's Alloy](<Smith's_Alloy.md>), and a [Knowledge](Knowledge.md) point.
