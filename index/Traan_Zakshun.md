## Description

Trahne Zakshun is an NPC merchant that can be found on a cliff in [Erisia](Erisia.md). he sells various outfit schematics labelled as "Trahnes Salvaged Stock". He wields an [Iron twinblade](Iron_Twinblade.md) on his back, and he wears the Carefree Garments outfit.
