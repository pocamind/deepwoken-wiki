# Ignition Union Uniform

Template:ArmorInfobox**Ignition Union Uniform** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit has no cost and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, three (3) pieces of Irithine and one (1) Lionfish Scale.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 6.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2000 durability and grants +22% Physical resistance and +22% Elemental resistance.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md)
-   [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) at the bank
-   [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) Chests
-   [Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)

## Trivia/Notes

-   One of the best early/mid game armors, as it gives fairly high physical and elemental armor, which rivals some expert tier armors while remaining cheap to make.
-   Better than the [Summer Company Uniform](Summer_Company_Uniform.md). (Debatable)
