# Ether

## Description

**Ether** is the magical essence used to cast [Mantras](Mantras.md), though some [Murmurs](Murmur.md) like [Murmur: Ardour](Murmur%253A_Ardour.md) will drain your ether upon using it. It is represented by the blue-ish green bar. If you try to cast a Mantra without enough Ether, it will make an error sound and not cast.

## Interactions

-   It is raised by [Intelligence](Attributes.md#int) (+2 per point), [Charisma](Attributes.md#cha) (+1.5 per point), [Erudition](Traits.md#erudition) (+25 per point), [Equipment](Equipment.md) stats, and certain [Talents](Talents.md).
-   Regenerates faster with higher [Tempo](Combat_Mechanics.md).
-   Combining all Ether sources, the maximum theoretical Ether is 1,173 (120 base, 200 from Intelligence, 150 from Charisma, 150 from Erudition, up to 150 from [Devastating Power](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md), and a 403 Ether [kit](Equipment.md).
    -   [Contractor](Oath%253A_Contractor.md) or [Saintsworn](Oath%253A_Saintsworn.md) must be used, as they grant the highest amount of Ether when Devastating Power is taken into consideration).
    -   Because of stat restrictions (largely regarding [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md)), this amount of Ether is nearly impossible to reach in practice, with the highest possible Ether being 1,163 instead.
