# Ether

## Description

**Ether** is the magical essence used to cast [Mantras](Mantras.md). Also some [murmurs](Murmur.md) like Ardour will drain your ether upon using it. It is represented by the blue-ish green bar. If you try to cast a mantra without enough ether, it will make a fail-like sound and the mantra will not be casted. Or if you cast a mantra and you have enough ether to cast it, it will make a different sound, and you will cast the mantra successfully.

## Interactions

-   It is raised by [Intelligence & Charisma](Attributes.md).
-   It is also raised by [Erudition](Traits.md).
