# Ether

## Description

**Ether** is the magical essence used to cast [Mantras](Mantras.md) and use certain other abilities. It is represented by a light green bar below the health bar. If you try to cast a Mantra while having insufficient Ether, it will make an error sound and not cast.

## Interactions

-   It is raised by [Intelligence](Attributes.md#int) (+2 per point), [Charisma](Attributes.md#cha) (+1.5 per point), [Erudition](Traits.md#erudition) (+25 per point), [Equipment](Equipment.md) stats, and certain [Talents](Talents.md).
-   Regenerates faster with higher [Tempo](Combat_Mechanics.md#tempo).
-   Excluding Ether gained from [Equipment](Equipment.md), the maximum obtainable Ether is 770 (120 base, 200 from Intelligence, 150 from Charisma, 150 from Erudition, and up to 150 from [Devastating Power](Talents.md#devastating-power).
    -   [Contractor](Oath%253A_Contractor.md) or [Saintsworn](Oath%253A_Saintsworn.md) must be used, as they benefit the most from [Devastating Power](Talents.md#devastating-power).
