# Ether

## Description

**Ether** is the magical essence used to cast [Mantras](Mantras.md), though some [Murmurs](Murmur.md) like [Murmur: Ardour](Murmur%253A_Ardour.md) will drain your ether upon using it. It is represented by the blue-ish green bar. If you try to cast a Mantra without enough Ether, it will make an error sound and not cast.

## Interactions

-   It is raised by [Intelligence](Attributes.md#int), [Charisma](Attributes.md#cha) and [Erudition](Traits.md#erudition).
-   Regenerates faster with higher [Tempo](Combat_Mechanics.md).
-   Maximum possible Ether is 1204 (120 base, 206 from Intelligence, 153 from Charisma, 180 from Erudition, up to 140 from [Devastating Power](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md), and a 405 Ether [kit](Equipment.md). [Contractor](Oath%253A_Contractor.md) or [Saintsworn](Oath%253A_Saintsworn.md) must be used, as they grant the highest amount of Ether when Devastating Power is taken into consideration.)
