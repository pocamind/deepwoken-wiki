# Ether

## Description

**Ether** is the magical essence used to cast [Mantras](Mantras.md). It is represented by the blue-ish green bar.

## Interactions

-   It is regenerated by the [Reservoir](Reservoir.md).
-   It is raised by [Intelligence & Charisma](Attributes.md).
