## Description

Tayna is a [Adret](Adret.md) NPC. She sells [Sparks](Mantra_Modifiers.md) for 350 Notes each.

## Location

Tayna is located at [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md), she can be found next to a cart near the [Job Board](Job_Boards.md).
