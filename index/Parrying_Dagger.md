# Parrying Dagger

Template:WeaponInfoBox

## Description

The Parry Dagger is an Off-hand tool used for better parry capability. Found in the weapons shop in Etris (Starting Island) and can be purchased for 25 notes and requires 10 light weapon training. While blocking, you can left click to parry, instead of having to perfect block an attack to parry it. Cannot be used with two handed weapons, (heavy weapons), or weapons that take up two hands. Unless a two handed weapon has a one handed stance, (switch by pressing Y), you cannot use the parry dagger in tandem with them.

Faceless Gang Banger with a Sai.
