# Parrying Dagger

Template:WeaponInfoBox

## Description

The Parry Dagger is an Off-hand tool used for better parry capability. Found in the weapons shop in Etris (Starting Island) and can be purchased for 25 notes and requires 10 light weapon training. ~While blocking, you can left click to parry, instead of having to perfect block an attack to parry it. Can only be used with one-handed weapons or in a one handed stance.~ It doubles your parry frames.

Adret with a Sai.
