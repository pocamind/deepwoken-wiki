# Rotskipper

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Mindless enemies that can be found in [The Hidden Village](The_Hidden_Village.md) and [Tomb of the Nameless Warrior](Tomb_of_the_Nameless_Warrior.md). They are a variant of the [Mudskipper](Mudskipper.md). If parried, its limbs will fly off one by one, eventually followed by its head, killing it instantly.

## Tips

-   Very good for grinding when first starting, due to their low HP and low damage output.
    -   Stat Check Level: Any.
-   Surprisingly, they have far more HP than regular mudskippers.
-   During the 2023 Hallowtide event, parrying their attacks would only cause 1 arm to fall off and they could be infinitely parried.
