## Description

The Night Axe is a [Heavy Weapon](Weapons.md) in the Greataxe category. It can be obtained from [Ministry Necromancer](Ministry_Necromancer.md). Its defining trait is having 80% chip damage, making blocking almost completely useless against it.

## Critical

The user performs a Shadowcast-empowered upwards swing that guard breaks and steals Ether on hit. The critical is compatible with Shadowcast talents that **does not** specify Mantras, like [Sightless Still](Talents.md#sightless-still) and [Twisted Puppet's](Talents.md#twisted-puppets) Shadowcast burn for example.

This critical deals **1.25x of your weapon's scaled damage**, meaning if your Night Axe has 30 damage, this critical will deal 37.5 damage. Additionally, this critical deals Slash and Shadow damage, meaning it is more easily resisted. It has a **windup of 0.6s** and **0.3s of endlag**.

The critical has a cooldown of **3 seconds**.
