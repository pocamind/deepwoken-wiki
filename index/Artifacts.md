# Artifacts

_"Is that...Rock Johnson?!" - Bank Teller_

  

## Description

Artifacts in Deepwoken are valuable pieces of technology or objects that can be sold to Antiquarians for high rewards such as Rare Armor and Mantra Ingredients.

Deepwoken Artifacts are not Rogue Lineage Artifacts.

Artifacts usually are guarded by Monsters or Enemies, but rarely it can be completely unguarded.  

## Artifacts

### Pieces of the Forge

An Adret carrying a piece of The Forge.

  
Rewards

-   400 Notes
-   10 Common to Rare Items (Armor & Weapons)

Locations & Hints

-   Any Island (Erisia Island)
-   Sound Ques and Purple Rings flying upwards (Sounds of an echoed pulse)
