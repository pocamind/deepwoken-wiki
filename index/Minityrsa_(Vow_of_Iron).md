## Overview

This Minitrysa is the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md)'s variant of the current Minitrysa, which takes place 30 years ago, opposed to the current time, in which [Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) takes place.

Minitrysa is covered with snow, with not much life around, aside from a few buildings that belong to [Clan Kobura](Clan_Kobura.md).
