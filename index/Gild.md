## Description

An old [Celtor](Celtor.md) man NPC in Depths located inside a building in the [Varicosa District](Varicosa_District.md), in the [first layer](First_Layer.md) of The Depths. [\[1\]](#cite-note-1) He wears Merchant Robes and has apparently been stuck in the depths ever [since Celtor City sank](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Lore?so=search#The_Great_Drowning_of_Celtor).
