## Description

Gild is an old [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC. He wears [Merchant Robes](Merchant_Robes.md) and has apparently been stuck in the depths ever since Celtor sank below the Tides.

## Location

Gild is located in the [Varicosa District](Varicosa_District.md) of [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md), standing inside one of the buildings.
