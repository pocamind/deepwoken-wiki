# Tempestmaker's Threads

Template:ArmorInfobox**Tempestmaker's Threads** is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in Deepwoken.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, five (5) [Gale Stones](Gale_Stone.md), five (5) [Vibrant Gems](Mantra_Modifiers.md), eight (8) [Iron](Ores.md), one (1) [Wind Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md).

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 15 and 45 [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md).

This Outfit isn't droppable but is bankable.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2600 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +30% Elemental resistance, +30% Wind resistance, and +75% Ether Regen.

-   Due to resistances being combined multiplicatively instead of additively, the total resistances are: 25% Physical, 47.5% Galebreathe, and 30% Elemental.

It gives the [Tempest Evolution](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent, which grants a 20% damage bonus in the air for a few seconds after landing [Wind Blade](Galebreathe.md#wind-blade) with the [Tempest Wind](Talents.md#gale-duelist) Talent. Additionally, this increases the damage of your Tempest Wind.
