# Familiar Heretic's Armor

Template:ArmorInfobox

**Familiar Heretic's Armor** is a Unique [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, five (5) Fiber, and one (1) Rock.

This Outfit's requirements is being [Power](Power.md) 5.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1300 durability and grants +15% Physical Armor, 10% Slash Armor, and 25% Fire Armor.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Lowstride_, which lets you draw your weapon silently and move faster + roll further while crouching.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic is obtainable from:

-   [Widow's Wail Cemetery](<Widow's_Wail_Cemetery.md>)
-   [Lysander Spellhardt](Lysander_Spellhardt.md)

## Trivia/Notes

-   This outfit is based on Rogue Lineage's [Wraith Knight's](https://rogue-lineage.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith_Knight) armor, the [Wraith Knight](https://rogue-lineage.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith_Knight) armor.
-   This outfit is not droppable or bankable.
-   This outfit was bugged, attempting to use it at a Blacksmith would result in the schematic being deleted from the player's inventory.
