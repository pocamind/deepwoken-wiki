## Description

Brutus is an imprisoned [Khan](Khan.md) in the [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) prison that allows you to unbound your [Fortitude](Attributes_Unbound.md). Talking to him will prompt a beatdown that'll knock you and leave you at barely any health, along with possibly draining you of all your armor.

## Location

Brutus is located in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) inside of a prison cell that can only be accessed through a small hole in the wall, or by breaking the walls around the bars with a mantra.

## Trivia

-   Initially, there were no bars on Brutus' cage. While it was initially speculated he was afforded certain freedoms, an interview with Arch\_Mage revealed that there were meant to be bars there, hence why there's a secret tunnel into his cell that seemingly provided no function due to the cell being wide open anyway. This lead to builder Zartania constantly being asked "Can you put bars on Brutus' cage?" by a discord user which finally culminated in their addition during the 3/8/2024 update.
-   You can use a training vest and let him beatdown you to increase fortitude.
-   If you have positive [Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) [Reputation](Reputation.md), he'll say "Oh a treaty-breaker makes his way into my den... Your superiors will regret the day they broke our pact."
-   His hair ID is 5143078089.
