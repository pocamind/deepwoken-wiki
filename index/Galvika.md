## Description

One of the previous Acolytes of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-missives-1)

In the [Strange Missives](Strange_Missives.md) he wrote to [The Hive](The_Hive.md) about a festivity of [the Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md), known as the "Nostri Eclipse". He urged [the Hive](The_Hive.md) to stay away from their territories, or else "<the all-too-familiar matercrypt will receive those who do not heed this warning into its sunk'n shores".[\[1\]](#cite-note-missives-1)
