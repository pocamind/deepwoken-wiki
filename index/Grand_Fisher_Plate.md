## Description

The Grand Fisher Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The Grand Fisher Set is a guaranteed drop [The Fisherman](The_Fisherman.md).

The [Diver's Resilience](Talents.md#divers-resilience) Talent allows the player to parry certain attacks that are normally unparryable from [Monsters](Monsters.md). After parrying an unparryable attack, this goes on cooldown for 20 seconds. This comes with the downside of moving 3% slower at all times. This functions similarly to [Force Your Way](Talents.md#force-your-way).

## Trivia

-   The Grand Fisher set was made as the winner for the 2nd Official Equipment Concept Art Contest, won by TU5SK, making it one of the first community made armor sets in the game.
    -   Some of the inspiration for this armor comes from the character Sajin Komamura from the anime Bleach. This is directly referenced in the concept art. It may also be based on the [Arcwarder](Oath%253A_Arcwarder.md) Oath.
    -   It has the same stats as a [Diver's Light Plate](<Diver's_Light_Plate.md>), but is comparatively easier to obtain.
