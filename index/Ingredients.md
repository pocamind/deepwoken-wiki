# Ingredients

## Description

Ingredients are scattered throughout Deepwoken. Where it is in the wilderness, in loot and other sources, Ingredients are used for crafting.

Effects of Food and Ingredients that can be consumed are located [here](Food.md).

Recipes & Alchemism are located here.

### Color Coding

-   Mint Green Items can be craftable, cooked or used in Alchemism.
-   Yellow Items are generic items, such as Materials, Gems, Food and Harvesting Tools.
-   Light Blue Items are Schematics, and can be brought to a Blacksmith to learn the recipe.
-   Light Orange (Or Canvas colored) Items are Training Equipment.
-   Light Red Items are equipable, such as shields or weapons.
-   Violet (or Purple colored) Items are Mantra Ingredients.
