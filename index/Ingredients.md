# Ingredients

## Description

Ingredients are scattered throughout Deepwoken. Where it is in the wilderness, in loot and other sources, Ingredients are used for crafting. Effects of Food and Ingredients that can be consumed are located [here](Food.md). Recipes & Alchemy are located here.

### Color Coding

-   Mint Green Items can be crafted, cooked or used in food recipes and potions.
-   Yellow Items are generic items, such as Materials, Gems, Food and Harvesting Tools.
-   Light Blue Items are Schematics and can be brought to a Blacksmith to learn the recipe.
-   Light Orange Items are Training Equipment.
-   Light Red Items can be equipped, such as shields or weapons.
-   Violet Items are Mantra Modifiers and recalled Mantras.

## Lotuses

“ A primordial Lotus, capable of producing the rawest form of power itself. ”

― Lotus Flavour Text Description

Although technically an ingredient, Lotuses are Attribute-Rich ingredients that can only be used in a crafting station. Each 2 lotuses crafts a Flask of an Attribute (ex. 2x Brilliant Lotus = Flask of Intelligence).
