# Hemafrost

Hemafrost in the mantra shop at Isle of Vigils

## **Description**

_"A glowing sphere that emits ice energy."_

Used to train the frostcall attunement. It can be used 200 times. Upon activation, the character is slowed and surrounded by a frosty glow.

_Any Mantra Shop - 25 Notes_

## Trivia

-   Every attunement has a training item.
