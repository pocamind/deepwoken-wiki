# Hemafrost

The Hemafrost in the mantra shop at Isle of Vigils

## **Description**

_"A glowing sphere that emits ice energy."_

Used to train the Frostdraw attunement. It can be used 200 times. Upon activation, the character is slowed and surrounded by a frosty glow.

_Purchased at any Mantra Shop - **25 [Notes](Notes.md)**_

## Trivia

-   Every attunement has a training item.
-   Can break if overused.
