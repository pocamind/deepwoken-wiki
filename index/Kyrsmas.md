## Description

This is [Deepwoken](Deepwoken.md)'s equivalent of [Christmas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christmas), strongly resembling it in real life, with decorated pine trees, wrapped presents, and also being from the 22nd of December to the 5th of January the next year. The name "Kyrsmas" is most likely named after the [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md). During this time, chests are converted into presents, islands like [Etris](Etris.md) and [Erisia](Erisia.md) are decorated festively, and double the loot can be found in all chests.

Kyrsmas has only taken place once, from the 22nd of December 2023, to the 5th of January 2024. It is unknown if it will occur again.

## Trivia

-   Unlike [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md), Kyrsmas appears to have no in-universe justification.
