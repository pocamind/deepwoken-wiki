## Description

This is [Deepwoken](Deepwoken.md)'s equivalent of [Christmas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christmas), strongly resembling it in real life, with decorated pine trees, wrapped presents, and also being from late December to early January the next year. The name "Kyrsmas" is most likely named after the [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md). During this time, chests are converted into presents, islands like [Etris](Etris.md) and [Erisia](Erisia.md) are decorated festively, and double the loot can be found in all chests.

Due to developmental constraints, a downplayed version was released for 2024-2025. There were minimal changes, with only the central tree in Etris being changed to a Kyrsmas theme, and the release of the [Kyrsmas Hat](Kyrsmas_Hat.md).

## Trivia

-   Unlike [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md), Kyrsmas appears to have no in-universe justification, especially given the average denizen of Lumen would not know what a Kyrsgarde is.
