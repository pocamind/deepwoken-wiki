## Description

The Harnessed Thresher is [Sordinos](Sordino.md) companion he raised using a [Vow](Vows.md) of Companionship.

The Harnessed Thresher is located at [The Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), laying beside Sordino.
