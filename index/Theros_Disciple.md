## Description

**Theros Disciple** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. Titus can be cheesed by sitting next to a whirlpool while Titus tries to teleport to you. Eventually, he will fall into the whirlpool and be voided.
