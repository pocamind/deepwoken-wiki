**Arachnid's Weave** is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs 1,000 and requires ten (10) Cloth, two (2) Fiber, five (5) [Spider Eggs](Spider_Egg.md), two (2) [Dark Feathers](Dark_Feather.md), one (1) [Bloodless Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md), and five (5) [Vibrant Gems](Mantra_Modifiers.md).

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 15.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2100 durability, grants +20% Physical Armor, +35% Elemental Armor, +15% Blood Armor, +15% Iron Armor, +10% Lightning Armor, 10 Sanity, and +55% Ether Regeneration. Additionally, it provides the [Corrosive Touch](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent.

-   Due to resistances being combined multiplicatively instead of additively, the total resistances are: 20% Physical, 44.75% Ironsing, 44.75% Bloodrend, 41.5% Thundercall, and 35% Elemental.

The [Corrosive Touch](Talents.md#equipment-talents) Talent makes your Critical attacks and Flourishes corrode your opponent's armor, draining an additional 5% of their armor on proc. Corrosive Touch has a 10 second cooldown.

Corrosive Touch being applied to the target

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be obtained from the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) as a direct drop.

## Trivia/Notes

-   This outfit has the lowest Physical resistance out of any Master armor, with a measly 20%.
-   Arachnid's Weave originally had 15% Physical resist and a staggering 40% Elemental resist. This, along with its name (it was originally called Arachnid's Corset) were shadow updated on the same day this outfit was added to the game.
