# Hivekin

## Description

Term representative of the aspects Vesperian, Ganymedes, and Chrysids.

A kinship formed by once similar views of advances of technology. Currently though not all the aspects agree to it as the majority of the Chrysids live isolated away from the Great Hive.

The Hivekin are the inhabitants of the Great Hives and in the faction The Hive.
