# Traits

## What is a Trait?

Traits are attributes given to you to further increase your strength beside basic attributes, gears and talents.

Every players have a maximum of 12 trait points, given at the first 10 [Power](Power.md) and subsequently the 15th and 20th for the last two points.

Each trait can only be increased up to 6 points.

### Vitality

-   Increased maximum Health by 10 per point.
-   Maximum of 60 Health at 6 points.

### Erudition

-   Increase max [Ether](Ether.md) by 30 and max [Tempo](Combat_Mechanics.md) by 10 per point, also increase Tempo gain by 15% each point.
-   Maximum of 180 Ether and 60 Tempo.
-   Increase Tempo gain up to 90% at max.

### Proficiency

-   Increase [Weapons](Weapons.md) scaling for M1's and Criticals by 5% per point.

### Songchant

-   Increase [Mantra](Mantras.md) scaling by 5% per point.
