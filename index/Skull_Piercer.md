## Description

The Skull Piercer is a LHT weapon in the rapier category. It is obtained by getting [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md) Joy and talking to Glaive. Only obtainable second week of [Hallowtide.](Hallowtide.md) This is the only rapier that procs bleed and has 8.5 range, making it better than [Crucible Rapier](Crucible_Rapier.md).
