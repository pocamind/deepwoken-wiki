# Commander Rayner

Template:NpcInfobox

## Description

Commander Rayner was an NPC at [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) that requested your help, should they be shown an Officer's Note, with a situation at the Isle of Crows, and gave you the key to the basement. The Isle of Crows was an island that appeared in the Deepwoken direct.

## Trivia

-   Bizarrely, despite being a member of [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md), they wore the [Hive Adjudicator](Hive_Adjudicator.md) outfit.
