## Description

The Grand Fisher Helm, along with the [Grand Fisher Plate](Grand_Fisher_Plate.md), are guaranteed drops from an enemy in the [Depths](First_Layer.md) called "[The Fisherman](The_Fisherman.md)." Equipping it grants 5 health, along with 5% Ice and Lightning Armor, making it a good option for those vulnerable to said attunements.

The helmet comes with an innate talent, _Coldseep Reactor_: "By cultivating the localized chemosynthetic microorganisms within the Depths and utilizing them as a power source, your helm thrums with a protective field of static electricity and heat." This talent grants the user **full immunity to the parasites of the [Eternal Gale](Second_Layer.md) without any downside.**

  

## Trivia

-   The Grand Fisher set was made as the winner for the 2nd Official Equipment Concept Art Contest, won by TU5SK, making it one of the first community made armor sets in the game.
    -   Some of the inspiration for this armor comes from the character Sajin Komamura from the anime Bleach. This is directly referenced in the concept art. It may also be based on the [Arcwarder](Oath%253A_Arcwarder.md) oath.
-   The description for Coldseep Reactor effectively suggests that the helm uses energy generated as a result of microorganisms from the Depths consuming the Eternal Gale parasites.
-   This is often considered one of the best armor pieces in the game due to granting parasite immunity, good pips, unique elemental resistance, and having a comparatively easy obtainment method.
-   The discovery of the obtainment method for this set is credited to [StarPhil29](https://www.youtube.com/@StarPhil29) by Agamatsu.
