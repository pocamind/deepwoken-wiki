# Iron Mask

Template:EquipmentStats The **Iron Mask** is an accessory under the "Face" category. It can be found in chests in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).

## Appearance

The Iron Mask is a mask made out of an unknown alloy (supposedly iron) inlaid with a black fabric in the interior. Two circular holes are bored into the mask. Additionally, four slits faced diagonally are on each side of the mask. When worn, the Iron Mask completely covers the wearer's face.

## Trivia

-   The Iron Mask is seen at the [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) at [Etris](Etris.md). However, the player cannot interact or buy it.
-   May or may not be the Mark 1 armor from Ironman
