# Guilds

The Guildhouse in Etris

Guilds are associations created by others, allowing players to come together and explore the game with one another. Guilds can be seen like [Houses](https://rogue-lineage.fandom.com/wiki/Houses) from _Rogue Lineage_, another game by _Monad Studios_.

Once you are **Power Level 15**, a guild can be founded for 100 [Notes](Notes.md) (This is not true, forming a guild is free) by talking to the Guild Clerk in the Guild House in Etris, east of the large tree in the center. Talking to the Guild Clerk will prompt you with a window. This window allows you to name your Guild and set it's primary color.

Once you've founded your guild, you are given the Guild Officer tool. This tool allows you to invite, promote or exile a member from your guild.

Members of a guild will stay in their guild after death. Currently you can either get exiled by someone with the Guild Officer tool, or leave by talking to the Guild Clerk. Other benefits/changes from being in a guild are unknown currently, but [Canor](Canor.md) might have a damage reduction buff for their allies within the same guild.
