## Description

The Icarus Sun Shield is a [side weapon](Weapons.md) in the Shield category.

Currently the only obtainment for the Icarus Sun Shield is through defeating [General Titus Juventius](General_Titus_Juventius.md) by lowering his health below 90% and through talking to him when he's kneeling. Cheesing him via whirlpools will not reward you with the shield. The Shield requires 60 Fortitude to equip and it's special effect is that it reduces posture recovery from parrying by 40%, and gives a max posture bonus of 10.8
