## Description

Drowned is an equipment enchantment that bolster's your equipment's stats.

This enchantment applies floating purple mist particles to the equipment piece.

## Effect

Drowned grants raw stats to your equipment piece. Drowned gives: +10 Health, +10 Ether, +5% Damage Vs. Monsters, and +5% Monster Armor.

### Notes

-   This is one of very few ways to obtain the Damage vs Monsters stat and the only way to gain the Monster Armor stat.
