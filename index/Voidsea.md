# Voidsea

> _"Here, the dying light collects like dregs at the precipice of a waterfall. The ultimate fate of all things become apparent."_

> \- In-game flavour text upon entering _**The Void Sea**_.

## Overview

Template:AreainfoboxThe Void Sea is one of the main areas in Deepwoken. It is speculated to be an ecological deadzone, where some vicious sea creatures reside. it is incredibly easy to stumble into during travelling. It covers a fairly large part of the sea and can appear out of no where so while going long distances be very careful. There is actually two different sections of Voidsea that has been discovered so far, a patch behind The Isle Of Vigils, and a patch to the side of Minityrsa.

## Description

The Void sea has dark and gloomy waters. It is relatively dangerous place for low-levels to enter, however the chance of being attacked by anything is very unlikely. It's very easy to get lost as the Voidsea is far away from land making it hard to create or use landmarks to get back.

Whirlpool also appears in the Voidsea, specifically around The Boatman's Watch and around [Ardour](Murmur%253A_Ardour.md) when it spawns. They instantly destroy any boat that touch them, and can bring a player directly to [The Depths](The_Depths.md) without them needing to die. Note that dying in the Depths will still cause you to wipe even if this method is used.

## Notes

-   Especially dangerous monsters spawn in this area, such as: [Flying demons](Brainsucker.md), [Ice Dragons](Murmur%253A_Ardour.md), and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md).
-   There are patches of bubbles around the Void Sea and hitting one can cause a Sea Dragon (bigger bubbles) or a Lionfish (smaller bubbles) to spawn.
-   Dying in the Voidsea only instantly takes you to The Depths if you didn't die to another player.
