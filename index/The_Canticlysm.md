# The Canticlysm

**The Canticlysm** is the name of a time period the ended when [The City of Celtor](Celtor_Wastes.md) drowned, and what caused most of the resulting Old World to spiral into the sea.

The entity that ended the Canticlysm is referred to as "the Celtorian Horror."

Naktigonis referring to the Canticlysm in the "The Sun, Gleaming" Deepwoken OST youtube video

A Comment by a random youtube user: "is it me or does something feel a little off about the track? the track itself is good yeah but it just gives you a vibe that somethings terribly wrong"

Naktigonis: "if i were to hazard a guess, i'd probably say it's just whiplash - compared to the rest of the OST, the track sounds weirdly happy and cheerful. that's probably why it sounds jarring. but you raise a good point! considering that the Songseeker Wilds are a congregation point for those seeking to learn how to use the Song like mantracasters did in the days before the Canticlysm, the off-vibe is more than understandable. they are trying to glean pieces of a world that is no more - and will never be again. considering how the ways of the Old World are directly to blame for the Canticlysms end and the current state of Deepwoken's world, the facts start to paint a much darker picture than the song might suggest by its tone."
