# The Canticlysm

"'''The Canticlysm''' is the name of a time period the ended when [The City of Celtor](Celtor_Wastes.md) drowned, and what caused most of the resulting Old World to spiral into the sea. The entity that ended the Canticlysm is referred to as "the Celtorian Horror." Naktigonis referring to the Canticlysm in the "The Sun, Gleaming" Deepwoken OST youtube video A Comment by a random youtube user: "is it me or does something feel a little off about the track? the track itself is good...")
