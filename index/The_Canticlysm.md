# The Canticlysm

## Description

The Canticlysm was an era of considerable social upheaval and innovation that ended with the arrival of the Tides. It is the last era of the Old World. Considered by many to be the Golden Age of the Song and of humanity. It is revealed in several [Books](Books.md) that the reason the Canticlysm ended was because of misuse or overuse of the song, and so therefore, the Tides were a punishment by the gods for this. During this period of time, the Song was used for quite literally everything, such as a [Flamecharmer](Flamecharm.md) using their abilities to cook food, and it was also the time when the Song and Humanity was at it's most powerful, which is why it is referred to as "The Golden Age of the Song".
