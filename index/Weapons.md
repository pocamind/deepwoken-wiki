This page will list the different weapons that you will use in Deepwoken / Drowned Gods.

There are three different main types of weaponry:

Strength weapons {which are governed by the strength attribute}, these weapons are characterized by their large hitboxes, slow weapon attacks and high damage. These also govern Flintlocks, the current only known gun type.

Agility weapons (which are governed by the agility attribute}, these weapons are characterized by their small hitboxes, fast weapon attacks and low damage.

  

Spells (which are governed by Intelligence, potentially Willpower, and the different elements), unlike Rogue Lineage, tomes are not needed to wield spells and as such you can use both a weapon and a spell simultaneously, all spells are player-made, and there are no scrolls to pick up,

**Note:ALMOST ALL WEAPONRY HAVE 1 AND 2 HANDED STYLES (I.E option between holding a greatsword with both hands or one hand and an extra item (for instance a parrying dagger)**

**Weapons with 1 handed styles can be equipped with other weapons**
