# Browncap

Template:Food Template V1

## Description

A brown mushroom with a white stalk. Commonly found in [Etris](Etris.md), [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md), and [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), in groups of 3.

## Recipes used in

-   [Mushroom Soup](Mushroom_Soup.md) (Any 2 muchrooms)
-   [Mushroom Omelette](Mushroom_Omelette.md) (2 eggs, 1 mushroom)
-   Mushroom Bisque (1 of Any Mushroom, 1 Crustacean Meat)

## Trivia

-   One of 4 mushroom types, along with the [Gobletto](Gobletto.md), [Dentifilo](Dentifilo.md), and Bluecap.
    -   Also one of 2 mushroom types to have a color in their name, the other being the Bluecap.
-   If used in [Alchemy](Alchemy.md), the primary affect applied is **Disgusting**; Secondary effect is **Strengthening**.
