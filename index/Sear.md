## Description

Sear is a debuffing enchantment that increases your opponent's incoming PEN and drains their water.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to yellow and with bright yellow particles.

## Effect

Sear increases your weapon's PEN by 5%. On hit apply the [Cauterize](Status_Effects.md#cauterize) status effect, increasing your opponent's incoming PEN, up to 40%.

The amount of PEN given per hit varies based on your weapon type, granting +5.45% incoming PEN per hit on Light Weapons, +8% incoming PEN per hit on Medium Weapons, and +10% incoming PEN per hit on Heavy Weapons.

Stacks of Cauterize last 15 seconds. Applying a new stack does not refresh this timer.

Additionally, your weapon attacks drain your opponent's water meter, with the drain scaling on your weapon's scaled damage. The water drain is 20% of your weapon's scaled damage.

### Notes

-   Sear grants 1 stack of Cauterize per critical hit. This is exceptionally good on certain multihit criticals like [Trident Spear](Trident_Spear.md)'s, as they can max Sear's PEN bonus with one critical attack.
-   Sear bypasses the 50% PEN cap.
-   Cauterize's PEN bonus is applied to Mantras as well.

## Trivia

-   Prior to August 2nd, 2022, Sear would steal thirst on hit, similar to [Gluttony](Gluttony.md).
