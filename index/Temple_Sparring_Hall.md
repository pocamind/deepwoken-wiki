# Temple Sparring Hall

Template:Location2

  
**Temple Sparring Hall** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark of the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

## Overview

Temple Sparring Hall is a arena located in the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

## Content

### Das

A [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) NPC who can send players into [Training Mode](Training_Mode.md) on their current character.

### Maestro Evengarde Rest

For more information see the [original article](Evengarde_Rest.md).

## Trivia

-   Although the location was in the game since it's original release, it never was treated as separate location until a shadow update on 23 January 2024.
