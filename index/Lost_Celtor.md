# Lost Celtor

| Lost Celtor | |

## Description

A once great kingdom ruling over the [Southern Luminant](Luminants.md), now reduced to a scrambled mess of soldiers aimlessly patrolling [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md), their former capital. Their once great capital city, now [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md), was sunk in it's entirety during The Great Drowning, lost to the tides, which [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md) was responsible for, as an offering to Yun’Shul.

## Reputation

They are the only faction which can be found in [The Depths](The_Depths.md) other than [The Divers](The_Divers.md) and [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md), both of which they are neutral towards, meaning their relations with almost all other factions are unknown.

Ringing the Celtor Bells gains Positive Reputation with the faction, but there is a limit, capping out at high Friend tier, just before Ally tier.

Their dialogue does not change with reputation.
