## Description

The Legion Intelligence is a quest item obtained from [Titus](General_Titus_Juventius.md). It is required for the obtainment of the [Imperium Kata](Imperium_Kata.md) fighting style. which can be done through defeating [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md) inside the [Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md).

Upon defeating Titus, the Legion Intelligence will become readable.
