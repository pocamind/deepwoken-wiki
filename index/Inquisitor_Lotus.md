## Description

The individual known as Lotus was an Authority Inquisitor and [Thundercaller](Thundercall.md) assigned to an operation seeking to undermine the kingdom of [Etrea](Etrea.md). Lotus partook in [Operation 'Puppet Master'](<Operation_'Puppet_Master'.md>), which saught to obtain intel on [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md). Lotus initially attempted to obtain information from the civilian population, which proved ineffective. They later organized the kidnapping of an Etrean guard, replacing him with an Authority colleague, and interrogated them using Thundercall shocks to the guard's Hippocampus. As the guards responses differened if the memory of their initial responses were erased, Lotus suspected foul play and thus increased their voltage output, successfully breaking the mind veil that had been applied to the guard but also killing him and giving Lotus a vision of the Lord Regent, who had been alerted to their presence. Lotus later noted being plagued by whispers, seemingly from their, now MIA, colleague, and a feeling as if his mind was being probed by hands. Lotus was able to ensure the report reached [Warden Jericho](Warden_Jericho.md) but either died or disappeared before they could return to base.[\[1\]](#cite-note-1)
