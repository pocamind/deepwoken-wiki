# The King's Garden

LocationL The King's Garden LocationR

TERRITORY OF Etrea

The smell of blossom permeates the quiet gardens that surround the Lord Regent's place.

  

## Overview

The King's Garden is a landmark in [Etris](Etris.md).Template:AreainfoboxIt is guarded by 2 Etrean Royal Guards who will attack players that attempt to get near the [Palace](Etris_Palace.md) if they don't have the [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) [Quest](Quests.md) active.

At the left side of the garden, there is a small maze with a Hive Fountain at its center, interacting with it after washing your face will teleport you to the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md), assuming the slot that interacts with the Fountain has been to [the Eastern Luminant](Luminants.md) during that life.
