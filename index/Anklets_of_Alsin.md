## Description

The Anklets of Alsin are a light weapon that can be found in all depths chests, [Chaser's](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) chest, [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) chests, [Deep Widow's](Deep_Widow.md) chest, and [Duke's](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) chest. The only requirement is a Kick Style. As of now, the only Kick Style is [Jus Karita](Jus_Karita.md).

## Notes

-   This is the first weapon released for the [Jus Karita](Jus_Karita.md) fighting style.
-   This is also the first weapon to be modelled on the player's legs instead of hands and or arms.

## Benefits

Serves as a stat-stick to give players:

-   Access to [Stars](Weapons.md).
-   Access to [Enchants](Enchantments.md).
-   +10% Chip Damage.
