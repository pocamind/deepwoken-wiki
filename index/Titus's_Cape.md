## Description

Titus's Cape is an animated [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping it grants you 3 Pips and 10% Thundercall Resistance. It is found in the exclusive chest from [Titus Fulminus Destellus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md).

## Trivia

-   This is the only cape accessory to belong in the "Arms" category.
