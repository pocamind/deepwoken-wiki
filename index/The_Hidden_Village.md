# The Hidden Village

This is a village that is hidden behind a wooden door in the desert.

(The door can be broken with any heavy weapon m3 or fire)

Once in, you can go in the town. Located in the town are two npcs; one of them is named Nimea who mentions the term "Lightkeepers," highlighted in blue text.

File:Nimea's Dialog.png

Nimea's dialog prompt upon interaction with the player.

The other npc, Cestis, who appears to be a Ganymede, comments about the player being a goody-two-shoes and that they best flee before they get hurt.

File:Cestis.png.png

Cestis' interaction dialog when talking to the player.

There is also a green fountain that teleports you to [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) if you have already opened the [Lordsgrove](The_Lordsgrove.md) Gate and have obtained an oath.
