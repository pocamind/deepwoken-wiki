## Description

Rat Fighters, not to be confused with the [Golden Warrior](Golden_Warrior.md), are exclusively found in the [Rat's Den](<Rat's_Den.md>), a sub-location of [Erisia](Erisia.md).

They wear a [Goldleaf Helm](Goldleaf_Helm.md), [Warden Pauldrons](Warden_Pauldrons.md), a [dyed](Dye_Packet.md) [Steel Plate](Steel_Plate.md) outfit and a pair of [Flameguard Boots](Flameguard_Boots.md).

Rat Fighters drop a [Gold Plate](Gold_Plate.md) upon being killed, of which 5 can be turned in to the [Golden Rat Bouncer](Golden_Rat_Bouncer.md) for mid-tier Erisia loot.
