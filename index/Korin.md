## Description

Korin is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) living in Etris near the Antiquitarian he is as a lumberjack and is commonly used to upgrade your relation with [Etris](Etris.md).

-   [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md) uses his appearance when showing himself to the player.
