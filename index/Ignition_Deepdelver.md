# Ignition Deepdelver

Template:ArmorInfobox **Ignition Deepdelver** is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, one (1) [Blue Gem](Deep_Gems.md), five (5) [Vibrant Gems](Mantra_Modifiers.md), one (1) Bounder Claw, one (1) Frozen Membrane and one (1) Worn Cog.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 15.

### _Armor Stats_

The outfit has 3000 durability, grants +15% Ice Armor, +35% Elemental Armor, +20% Ether Regeneration and +30% Physical Armor. Additionally, it provides the [Force Your Way](Talents.md#ignition-deepdelver) Talent which allows the player to parry typically unparryable attacks from giant monsters, at the cost of a significant amount of armor durability.
