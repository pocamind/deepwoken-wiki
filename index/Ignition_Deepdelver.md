# Ignition Deepdelver

**Ignition Deepdelver** is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

Template:ArmorInfobox

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber, one (1) [Blue Gem](Deep_Gems.md), five (5) [Vibrant Gems](Mantra_Modifiers.md), one (1) Bounder Claw, one (1) Frozen Membrane and one (1) Worn Cog.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 15.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 3000 durability and grants +30% Physical resistance, +35% Elemental resistance, +15% Ice resistance and +20% Ether regen
