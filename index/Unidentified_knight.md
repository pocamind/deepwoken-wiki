## Description

Unidentified knight is a knight with a horned helmet that appeared to kill the [adventuring party](Unidentified_adventuring_party.md) in the last few pages of the [deepwoken manga](<Deepwoken_(manga).md>) for an unknown reason other than they were "boring".

They also have a special power to preform magic without "chants" and have lighting powers due to the black lighting following the knight.
