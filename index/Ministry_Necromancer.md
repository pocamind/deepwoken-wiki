# Ministry Necromancer

## Description

Cultist Necromancers, or just commonly refered as Necromancers, are NPC Type enemies that are frequently found in Minityrsa. Typically after deafeating a single Necromancer will yield a chest with decent loot and gear.

Necromancers are found sitting on the ground, and are invulnerable to being assassinated. Necromancers are commonly seen holding a dagger and possess the attunement of Shadowcast. They also can summon mudskippers. Necromancers have around the same HP as a Bandit Captain.

### Moves

-   **Dagger Attack**: Common M1 dagger hits, easy parryable and don't do significant damage.
-   **Mudskipper Summons**: Summons three Mudskippers that are dressed in either blue or red robes, signifying their attunement. The Necromancer will be invulnerable and not attack until these Mudskippers are gripped. These Mudskippers have the same HP as a normal Mudskipper.
    -   Red-robes are Flamecharm mudskippers that throw fireballs.
    -   Blue-robes are Frostdraw mudskippers that summon four swords of ice that spin around them.
-   **Shadow Breath**: Breathes in air made out of pure Shadowcast, doing quick, but low damage. It is able to be blocked and parried.

### Tips

-   Easily able to be killed at a high enough weapon or magic level.
-   Mudskippers are easy to kill and free health packs.
-   Block and parry all of his moves until the Shadow Breath attack, and then you can get a free flourish.
-   Every Cultist Necromancer is a [Ganymede](Ganymede.md).
