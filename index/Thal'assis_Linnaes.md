## Description

The current [Acolyte](Glossary.md#the-acolyte--thalassis) of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). A lead ambassador, who works under the [Council of the Moonlit Faith](Council_of_the_Moonlit_Faith.md), who's work is leading groups of scholars to and from the Greathive Aratel in order to exchange knowledge, updates and information.[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1)

In the [Strange Missives](Strange_Missives.md) he wrote about one of the academics of [the Hive](The_Hive.md) "running astray" while exploring the crypts of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md) and trying to steal the heritage, laid upon the walls of the crypts. He was captured and executed (presumably, by burning alive).[\[2\]](#cite-note-missives-2)
