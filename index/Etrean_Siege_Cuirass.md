## Description

The Etrean Siege Cuirass is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The Etrean Siege Cuirass, along with the [Etrean Siege Sabatons](Etrean_Siege_Sabatons.md), is part of the Etrean Siege Set.

The [Battle Tendency](Talents.md#battle-tendency) Talent increases the speed at which posture is regenerated by 20%.
