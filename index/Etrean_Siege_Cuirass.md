## Description

The Etrean Siege Cuirass is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping it grants you 8 Health, 2 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip), and the [Talent](Talents.md) Battle Tendency, which increases the speed at which posture is regenerated by 20%. It is commonly found in chests from the [The Depths](The_Depths.md) ([First Layer](First_Layer.md)), the [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md), and [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).

## Trivia

-   The Etrean Siege Cuirass is part of the "Etrean Siege" set; with the only other piece being the [Etrean Siege Sabatons](Etrean_Siege_Sabatons.md).
