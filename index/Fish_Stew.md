## Description

**Fish Stew** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is also purchased from [Mess Chef](Guild_Bases.md) for 20.

It is a type of **Meat**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Vegetarian](Boons_and_Flaws.md#vegetarian) Flaw.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 18% Stomach and 10% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Squid](Squid.md) and 1 [Fish Meat](Fishing.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
-   Or 1 [Seaweed Bundle](Seaweed_Bundle.md) and 2 [Fish Meat](Fishing.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
