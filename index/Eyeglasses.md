## Description

The Eyeglasses are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Face" category. Eyeglasses and Polarized Eyeglasses can be obtained in Minityrsa and Songseeker Chests. Aristocrat Glasses can be found from Duke Erisia or Miner's Landing. They provide a random stat buff when worn.
