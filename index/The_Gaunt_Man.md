# The Gaunt Man

The Gaunt Man is a new NPC in the depths that gives access to some new content and items.

The items are relatively expensive, with 2 new enchants, and 1 new equipment (not sure parasol blight might be a weapon), A new deep gem, and a new dungeon that contains at least 1 new mob (needs more proof for other mobs) referred to as an Prime Meglodaunt.
