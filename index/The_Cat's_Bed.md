## Description

The Cat's Bed is an NPC. They are one of many entities that reside in [The Hall of Shattered Wills](The_Hall_of_Shattered_Wills.md).

Their appearance is unclear, it is assumed that they are a large feline. They are also an [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) that has unique dialogue.
