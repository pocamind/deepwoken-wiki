## Description

The Oni Robes are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category and is a bright red recolor of Monastery Champion Robes. It is rarely found within chests rewarded by [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) and the [Etris: Siege](Cinders_of_Etris.md) event. (This is most likely a glitch as it seems to belong to the [Wandering Demon Memento](Wandering_Demon.md) in VOI)

## Trivia

-   The Monastery Champion set was designed by J3STTR, being the winning entry of the "Vigils Equipment Art Concept Contest", leading to its addition.
