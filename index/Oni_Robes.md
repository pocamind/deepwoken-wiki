## Description

The Oni Robes are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category and is a bright red recolor of Monastery Champion Robes.

## Trivia

-   The Monastery Champion set was designed by J3STTR, being the winning entry of the "Vigils Equipment Art Concept Contest", leading to its addition.
-   It is related to the upcoming [Wandering Demon](Wandering_Demon.md) [Memento](Mementos.md).
