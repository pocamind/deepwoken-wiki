## Description

The Sorcerer's Brim is a Unique tier [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The [Sorcerer's Skill](Talents.md#sorcerers-skill) Talent reduces the cooldown of your [Vent](Combat_Mechanics.md#tempo) by 2.5 seconds, down to 7.5 seconds. The [Overflowing Dam](Talents.md#overflowing-dam) Talent grants a 10% damage buff to your M1s while your Ether bar is full. Overflowing Dam's damage buff scales on your Intelligence investment, losing 0.125% per point of Intelligence below 40. At 5 Intelligence, you will only gain a 5.625% damage buff.

## Set Talent

When wearing the full equipment set, you will gain the Set Talent.

Set Talent: [Sorcerer's Supply](Talents.md#sorcerers-supply) - Increase the amount of [Tempo](Combat_Mechanics.md#tempo) you have by 30.
