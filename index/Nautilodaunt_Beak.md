## Description

Used to obtain [Abyssal Ridge](Monster_Mantras.md) \[Monster Mantra\] by talking to [Carrion, Father of Evolution](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md) in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) after he has been interacted with in [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md) Floor 2.

It is obtained by killing [Nautilodaunts](Nautilodaunt.md) with a low chance. The beak will drop near its body. It cannot be dropped, like all other Monster Mantra ingredients.
