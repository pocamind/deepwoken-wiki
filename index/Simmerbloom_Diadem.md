## Description

The **Simmerbloom Diadem** is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category.

[Simmerbloom](Talents.md#simmerbloom) augments your [Pleeksty's Inferno](<Pleeksty's_Inferno.md>), allowing your M1s to apply burn or proc Eruptions on hit.

This includes inflicting burn on downed people, making it a good option to keep up pressure in multiplayer fights, especially in tandem with the [Deepfire Ring](Deepfire_Ring.md) item and [Corpse Explosion](Talents.md#corpse-explosion) Talent.

## Trivia

-   This was the second equipment item with to have the 'Unique' rarity.
-   Was previously first piece of equipment to require investment into an Attunement, and the first to require investment into [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) by extension. However, this requirement was removed in the [May 2nd, 2026](Version_History/2026.md#may-2nd-2026) (Free Week) update.
