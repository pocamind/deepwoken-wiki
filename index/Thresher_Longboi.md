# Thresher Longboi

## Description

The **Thresher Longboi** was a plush released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration, classified under the "longboi" category. Its campaign ran from May 3rd, 2025, to May 10th, 2025, before selling out. The plush measured 12.0 cm in height and 13.0 cm in length. It was made from polypropylene cotton and plush fabric (ultra soft fleece).

### Official Description

"Burrowing crocodilian nightmare from below, the Thresher is here to season your home with a small pinch of terror. And maybe a few handfuls of dirt.  
  
Perfect for those who're always asking "Why can't my draft excluder bite my ankles, too?"  
  
But we're sure you can maybe get some kind of value from this guy, too!"

## Trivia

-   A total of 1,500 Thresher Longboi plushes were sold, and the campaign ended at 750% of its funding goal.
