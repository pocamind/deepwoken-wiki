# Crypt of the Unbroken

LocationL Crypt of the Unbroken LocationR

"The dimly lit halls echo with voices of those who once walked."

  

File:Crypt of the unbroken entrance.png

Crypt of the Unbroken.

The **Crypt of the Unbroken** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md) found in the Etrean Luminant.

## Overview

The Crypt of the Unbroken is a building in the highest part of [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md). The inside is filled with tall, narrow intersecting halls inhabited by hostile enemies.

  

## Points of Interest

File:Crypt entity.png

Entity in the crypt.

At the end of a hall in the crypt, there is an entity sitting on a pedestal against the wall. It appears that you can talk to it, but more information needs to be found.
