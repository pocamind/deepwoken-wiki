# Sharko Plush

## Description

The **Sharko Plush** was a plush released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration, classified under the "Plushie" category. Its original campaign ran from November 24th, 2023, to December 31st, 2023, before selling out. However, due to its popularity, the Sharko Plush was added to the Makeship Hall of Fame and became a permanent item available for purchase. The plush measures 13.3 cm in height, 13.0 cm in width, and 17.8 cm in depth, and is made from polypropylene cotton and plush fabric (ultra soft fleece).

### Official Description

Once a screenpet, this fearsome Megalodaunt has made good on his promise and broken out of the confines of your computer screen - this time for good!  
  
Whether you decide to befriend, punt, arrest, or start a band with this creature, he's sure to take it in stride with his vacant smile and lack of moral responsibility.
