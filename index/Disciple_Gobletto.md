## Description

Disciple Gobletto is a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) NPC. He is a new member of [The Justicar](The_Justicar.md).

He was given the name Gobletto, after the [common mushroom](Gobletto.md), which has been a cause of embarrassment and misfortune for him. While he was in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md), he was accosted by Pathfinders who mocked his name and assaulted him. He was eventually rescued by the Justicars and joined the organization, devoting himself to [Alsin](Alsin.md).

## Location

Disciple Gobletto is located on [Alsin's Landing](<Alsin's_Landing.md>), standing under a tent beside [Neophyte Jefferson](Neophyte_Jefferson.md) and [Elder Sasha](Elder_Sasha.md).
