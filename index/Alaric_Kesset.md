## Description

Alaric Kesset is an [Adretian](Adret.md) Navy Captain serving in [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) navy, serving as captain of the CASS 'Shallow Grave'. They had previously fought in conflicts in the Eastern and Etrean Luminants.[\[1\]](#cite-note-opdlr-1)

Kesset was assigned to [Operation 'Distant Light'](<Operation_'Distant_Light'.md>), where the 'Shallow Grave' carried out reconnaissance on the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). The operation was a success, and Kesset reported their findings to Fleet Admirial [Thorm Erebet](Thorm_Erebet.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-opdlr-1)
