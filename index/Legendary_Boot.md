# Legendary Boot

## The Legendary Boot

### Summary

The legendary boot can be found in lower Erisia. It is not an event, but a stationary NPC which does not despawn. It can be found by going through a waterfall in lower Erisia

### Location

Waterfall Entrance

Go past the bandit camp in lower Erisia. Then take a left until you reach the cliff. There will be a waterfall in the cliff and it is just through there
