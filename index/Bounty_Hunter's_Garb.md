## Description

The Mercenary's Garb is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The [Piercing Shot](Talents.md#piercing-shot) Talent grants a Talent tool that enhances your next critical attack made with any Rifle or Pistol weapon. Enhanced critical attacks have 100% chip damage and apply the [Pierced Armor](Status_Effects.md#pierced-armor) status effect for a few seconds, increasing incoming PEN. The [Kick Off](Talents.md#kick-off) Talent grants various bonuses to climbing and parkour.
