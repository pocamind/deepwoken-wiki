## Description

The Ranger's Brace is an equipment item under the Arms category. Equipping it grants you 3 Health, 1 Posture, and 2 Rare Pips. It is commonly found in chests from [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) and [Erisia](Erisia.md).

## Trivia

-   The Ranger's Brace is part of the "Ranger's" set; with the only other piece being the [Ranger's Boots](<Ranger's_Boots.md>).
