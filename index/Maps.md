## Overview

Navigation in _Deepwoken_ can be confusing at first. Use these [maps](https://map.deepwoken.net/) if you are ever in need of easy navigation. Remember to look at waypoints such as Statue Island below.

### Note to Readers:

_As of the Verse 2 Update, almost all maps that include Aratel Island and/or the Depths are incorrect. Please be patient._

## Trivia/Notes

-   Islands named Lungfish Keep, Dink Island, Pov Land, and Geoduck Cave do not exist or have been removed.
-   [Boatman's Watch](<Boatman's_Watch.md>) exists where Dink Island would have been.
-   Future Luminants are confirmed, it is just going to be a while before we see such a massive update.
