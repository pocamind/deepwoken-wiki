# Shrine of the Drowned

Template:NPC name

“ You know why it stands here-it offers an alternative. It beckons you to offer a part of who you are to the cold voice - a voice that’s always been at the back of your mind. ”

― Shrine of the Drowned when requirements are met for Drowned Favour

  

## Description

The Shrine of the Drowned is an NPC located in the [Fragments of Self](Fragments_of_Self.md) when wiping, found just in-between the two archways that lead to the Self NPC. It allows you to buy the Drowned Favour gamepass for 1000 robux if Power 2 or above. Upon buying the Drowned Favour, your character will be revived and will be returned to the [Etrean Luminant](Luminants.md) with all 3 lives, regardless of being in the Depths. Note that this can only be done **once** per slot.

## Tips

-   It is recommended that you do not buy Drowned Favour on a low power slot, as it is most likely a waste and should be saved for when your slot is more valuable. Only purchase if you really need to.

-   If you pass down an item and then buy Drowned Favour, you will **not** get your item back and will be passed down to the next slot upon wiping as normal.

## Trivia

-   The Drowned Favour gamepass used to cost 500 robux. It was changed at a later date to 1000.
