## Description

The Shrine of the Drowned is an NPC located in the [Fragments of Self](Fragments_of_Self.md) when wiping, found just in-between the two archways that lead to [Self](Self.md). It allows you to buy the Drowned Favour for 1000 [Robux](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Robux) if Power 2 or above. Upon buying the Drowned Favour, your character will be revived and will be returned to the [Etrean Luminant](Luminants.md) with 2 of your 3 lives, regardless of being in the Depths.

"NOTE: This product will bring the current character back to life, but it cannot be used on the same character twice. Deepwoken is a game where characters _cannot last forever_ - please spent responsibly" --_Note on the purchase page, next to the popup_

The Shrine can be used once per character instead of once per slot, as depicted in an image above. Occasionally, this will be reset (Around once a half year, usually at huge updates or New Year).

## Trivia

-   Previously, the Drowned Favour product used to cost 500 [Robux](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Robux). It was changed at a later date to 1000 [Robux](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Robux).
    -   However, whenever there is a sale on Deepwoken, the Drowned Favour product goes on sale too, costing 500 [Robux](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Robux).
-   The Drowned Favour product is often reset upon big updates, many of these big updates happen around [Kyrsmas](Kyrsmas.md).
    -   This means that even if you have already revived a character with Drowned Favour before, you may revive that same slot again with it.
-   It is recommended not purchase a Drowned Favour on a low power character, as it is most likely a waste and should be saved for a more valuable character.
-   If you pass down an item and then buy Drowned Favour, you will **not** get your item back. Your item will be passed down to the next slot upon wiping as normal.
