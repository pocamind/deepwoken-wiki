## Lore

The Adrets are a historically recentley arrived race of spiritually adept individuals that make up the bulk of the greatest force in the world; The Adretian Authority. The spirituality derives from the markings that are upon their face, representing future in life/what they are to be. When gaining these markings, it is up to the Adret on what they mean. Others will always interpret their own markings one way or another.
