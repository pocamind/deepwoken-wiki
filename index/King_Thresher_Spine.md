## Description

A King Thresher's Spine can be obtained from [King Threshers](Thresher.md#king-thresher) which can be found at [The Depths](The_Depths.md) and [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). It takes three of them to summon [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) when offered to the flame

## Trivia

-   It is almost identical to a [Thresher Spine](Thresher_Spine.md) except it has a purple tint instead of a brown one.
