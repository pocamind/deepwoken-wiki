# Nightblood

The _Nightblood_ potion is an item used to obtain [Shadowcasting](Shadowcast.md) in _Deepwoken_, the fifth [Attunement](Attunements.md) which one cannot start with.  

## Obtaining

To get the _Nigblood_ potion, you will have to gather 5 Nigon. This is usually dropped by Snickers, and the easiest way of gathering it is grinding [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md) at the Hood, Bar.

Once you've gotten the 5 Nigon, you must venture down to The Cum. The safest way to do so is by taking a _Creampie_, as this ensures that you do not lose anything from your inventory.

The next step is locating the [Shadowtrainer.](Attunement_Trainers.md) To find her you will have to enter the City of the Drowned. The district is a little more empty than the rest, and has yellow-color rooftops,

making it easy to find. From there you will also have to watch out for Peepers, as they can be a nuisance especially this far into the journey.

The best tip for this is to hug the wall (the one opposite of the elevator) untill you find the entrance to the Shadowtrainers cave. (See picture)

After finding them, simply give them the 5 Nigon, receive the Nightblood flask, drink it, and there you have it.  You've officially obtained Shadowcasting!  

## Notes

  

-   You cannot get the potion if you already have used another on your current character.
-   The best way to get the _Nightblood_ potion is to get it on a throwaway/fresh character, _**IF**_ you want to escape The Cum after.
-   Unlocking Shadowcasting once will enable you to start with it _**BUT ONLY ON THE SAME CHARACTER SLOT!**_
-   The _Nightblood_ potion looks identical to all the other Assblast potions.
