## Description

**Gale Enhanced Earrings** are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Ears" category. The [Way of the Wind](Talents.md#way-of-the-wind) talent increases the speed of your Mantra casts while airborne. You need to have been in the air for more than 0.5 seconds for this effect to activate. This has a wind pulse fx on proc.
