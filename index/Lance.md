# Lance

“ LANCE LESHI! ”

― Lance Leshi

  

## **Info:**

Lance Leshi is an etrean NPC, located in [Etris](Etris.md), inside a building in front the the General Store. He sells various food items like [Mushroom Bisque](Food.md), [Megurger](Food.md) and [Mushroom Omlette](Mushroom_Omelette.md), with various prices ranging from 15-25 notes.

-   Lance Leshi has a brother located in [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), but I forgot what his name was...
