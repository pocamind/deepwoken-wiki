# Lance

“ LANCE LESHI. ”

― Lance Leshi

  

## **Info:**

Lance Leshi is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC, located in [Etris](Etris.md), inside a building in front the the General Store. He sells various [food](Food.md) items like Mushroom Bisque, Megurger and Mushroom Omlettes, with various prices ranging from 15-25 notes.

-   Lance Leshi has a brother located in [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), but I forgot what his name was...
