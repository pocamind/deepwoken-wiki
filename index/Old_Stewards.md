# Old Stewards

| Old Stewards | |
|---|---|
| subunits | Society of Luminance |
| territory | Adrofalis' laboratory |
| goals | Preserve & research knowledge of the Old World, Prevent knowledge of the Old World from falling into the wrong hands |

The Old Stewards are a secret organization who preserve and research knowledge of Old World before the Tides.[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) The group has had several, now defunct, precursor organizations, including the Society of Luminance.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)

As the Stewards possess valuable yet dangerous knowledge, many groups such as [The Divers](The_Divers.md) and [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md) seek to infiltrate their ranks, ultimately leading to disaster when such happens.[\[3\]](#cite-note-0-3)

Members of the Old Stewards take on a Vow of Stewardship, which is to be taken seriously.[\[3\]](#cite-note-0-3) In an event that a Steward is interrogated, they are to die before giving up their knowledge.[\[3\]](#cite-note-0-3) Joining the Stewards is a difficult process, as recruits are accepted once every year at a different location each time, and must find these locations by themselves.[\[3\]](#cite-note-0-3)
