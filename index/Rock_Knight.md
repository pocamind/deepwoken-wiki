# Rock Knight

A tankier version of the [Snow Knight](Stone_Knight.md) that spawns near the large gate at [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). This monster falls from the sky in a meteor similar to the Snow Knights except he is alone. He much more tankier than the Snow Knights since he is alone.
