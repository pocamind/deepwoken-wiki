## **Description** (unconfirmed)

The Enforcer is a special enemy in Deepwoken that only appears in The Depths serving as one of the main threats to your survival. This enemy is impervious to any forms of damage meaning it cannot be stopped from clapping you down the middle. It's supposed purpose is to thwart any players seeking passage from [The Depths](The_Depths.md) back to the OG realm. The Enforcer wields a large axe capable of dealing devastating blows and executing a KO'd player instantly.
