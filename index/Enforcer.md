# Enforcer

Template:Creature Info Box v2

_"You see this guy your not boned cuz they dont one shot" - Some tester_

## **Description**

A large armored beast with an oversized axe that appears in The Depths after you successfully escape if you are extremely high level (Somewhere after level 40).

Drops the Enforcer Axe

**Attacks**

-   **High Swing -** Lifts its axe high in the air and brings it down on the enemy
-   **Low Swing -** Swings its axe around knee length at the opponent
-   **Kick -** Deliver a kick at the opponent
-   **Spin -** Spin with your axe and deals tons of damage but is slown

**Finisher**

-   **Headsplitter** - Raises his axe overhead and slams it down, splitting your skull into two.
-   **Pull -** If The Enforcer senses that you are knocked he will pull you in and instantly execute you.

## **Notes**

-   It is widely considered easier than the corrupted sharko trial or 2 angels. Due to having no ranged attacks meh damage output and meh hp
-   A common combo that the Enforcer uses, are two high swings in quick succession before going for a low swing
-   You can block all of its attacks and sometimes The Enforcer stands still allowing for a few hits.
-   The Enforcer can also feint.
-   Rumored to be the spawn of a Lad daddy long since gone. The Enforcer's however are only able to reach Thad level due to the lack of attention they got in their childhood. Page of Lad Daddy [here](https://saltandsanctuary.wiki.fextralife.com/Kraekan+Cyclops)
