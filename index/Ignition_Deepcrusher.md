## Description

The Ignition Deepcrusher is a Legendary weapon introduced in the same update as Ironsing. It is unique in that its two handed stance is a club that inflicts Rods, and its one handed stance is a sword that inflicts Bleed.

The club benefits more from Ironsing talents due to inflicting Rods, however the Blade alone is a better choice.

## Trivia

-   The Ignition Deepcrusher is the first weapon to be part of "two categories" as it's stances are both Clubs and Blades. This is similar to the difference shown by the Curved Blade of Winds, however to a larger extent.
