## Description

The Curved Blade of Winds is a Legendary Weapon and is currently the best Medium Weapon in the game. It is a very rare drop from the chests in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). It has 19 Base Damage and is unique in that it scales off of two stats, Medium Weapon, and [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md). This weapon also has the Galebreathe passive, After Cut, applied onto it at all times, signified by the wind particles it has. After an attack, there will be an "after cut" that hits the opponent again.

  

## Notes

-   You can stack Maestro's Blade with the passive of the weapon, meaning there will be two after cuts on the opponent.
