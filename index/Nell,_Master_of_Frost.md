# Nell, Master of Frost

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0In order to obtain [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md), you must bring 5 [Frigid Prisms](Frigid_Prism.md) to Friid. Alternatively, if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, you must kill them in order to get one of five oath talents. They are found near the guildhouse (follow the path to the right) in [Etris](Etris.md).
