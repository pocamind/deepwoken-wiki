# Nell, Master of Frost

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0Nell, the **Black Diver** is an [Adret](Adret.md) who appears to have mastered the [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md) Attunement. Considering she has risen to the rank of [Black Diver](The_Divers.md) it can be assumed she is quite powerful although does not spend nearly as much time doing field assignments anymore, although she does not appear too old it isn't too bold to assume she is retired from the [Divers](The_Divers.md) just as her fellow attunement trainer [Funke](Funke,_Master_of_Thunder.md) is assumed to be. She is quite powerful giving a good fight to the Player she appears wielding the [Gran Sudaruska](Gran_Sudaruska.md) meaning in lore she has inherited her will.

  
In order to obtain [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md), you must bring 5 [Frigid Prisms](Frigid_Prism.md) to Friid. Alternatively, if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, you must kill them in order to get one of five oath talents. They are found near the guildhouse (follow the path to the right) in [Etris](Etris.md).
