## Description

The **Frozen Membrane** is an ingredient dropped from all [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md) and is used to make the [Ignition Deepdelver](Ignition_Deepdelver.md) master outfit. It can also be used to make the Kyrsdog [food](Food.md) item, a unique and mighty dish. This ingredient is only obtainable in [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) and [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md).

## Trivia

-   This is effectively a severed head.
-   The **Kyrsdog** is a reference to the food [hot dog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_dog)
