## Description

A Frozen Membrane is an ingredient dropped from all [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md) which is used to make the [Ignition Deepdelver](Ignition_Deepdelver.md) outfit. It can also be used to make the Kyrsdog food item, a unique and mighty dish.
