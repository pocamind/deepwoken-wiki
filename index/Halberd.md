## Description

The Halberd is a Greataxe type weapon used by the Central Authority. The Halberd is also [Alloyable](Pluripotent_Alloy.md), turning it into an endgame weapon by increasing its stats while giving it a purple tint.

## Talents

Equipping the Halberd grants its unique [weapon Talent](Talents.md#weapon-talents)(s). Weapon Talents will be lost upon equipping any other weapon, including ability-bound weapons, like from the [Crazy Slots](Crazy_Slots.md) Resonance.

[**Spinal Splinter**](Talents.md#spinal-splinter) grants your Halberd critical +15 true damage if it hits your opponent's back. This is dealt as a separate instance of damage. This does not proc on [Spine Cutter](Talents.md#spine-cutter) hits.

## Critical

The user charges forward before performing a circular slash. This critical deals **1.25x** the weapon's scaled damage.

The critical has a cooldown of **3 seconds**.

This critical has the [M1 tag](M1_tag.md), allowing it to proc M1-exclusive [Talents](Talents.md) and [Enchantment](Enchantment.md) effects.

## Trivia

-   The Halberd's critical is possibly a reference to Champion Gundyr's second phase running attack from _Dark Souls III_.
