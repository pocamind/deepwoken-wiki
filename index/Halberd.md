**Base:**

## Description

The Halberd is a Greataxe used by the Central Authority. It has a Base Damage of 22 and a scaling of 7, and can be obtained though chests in and [Erisia](Erisia.md) and [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

**Alloyed:**

## Description

The upgraded version of the Halberd. Just a normal Halberd with increased stats. It can be obtained through chests in [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md), or by using a [Pluripotent Alloy](Pluripotent_Alloy.md) on a Halberd.

## Trivia

-   Since the alloy update, this weapon is really good, the critical has a full radius of 360, it steals blood and stuns.
-   The Halberd's critical is possibly a reference to Champion Gundyr's second phase running attack from [Dark Souls 3](https://darksouls3.wiki.fextralife.com/Dark+Souls+3+Wiki).
