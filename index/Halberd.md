## Description

The Halberd is a Heavy Greataxe used by the Central Authority. It has a Base Damage of 22 and a scaling of 7, and can be obtained though chests in and [Erisia](Erisia.md) and [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

## Notes & Trivia

-   Since the alloy update, this weapon is really good, the critical is a full radius of 360, it steals blood and it stuns.
-   The Halberd's critical is possibly a reference to Champion Gundyr's second phase running attack from [Dark Souls 3](https://darksouls3.wiki.fextralife.com/Dark+Souls+3+Wiki).
