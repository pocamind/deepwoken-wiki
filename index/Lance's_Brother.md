# Lance's Brother

Template:NPC name

## Description

Lance's Brother is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC, located in at the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), inside of a food stand near the docks. He sells various [food](Food.md) items. Nobody seems to know his name...

-   Mushroom Bisque -

## Crate Delivery Quest

To accept the quest choose "You need some help around here?" When talking to him.

After accepting the quest, (?) Will spawn a FOOD crate at the back of his shop. To finish the quest, talk to [Stratos](Stratos.md) crate in hand.
