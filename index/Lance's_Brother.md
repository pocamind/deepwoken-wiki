# Lance's Brother

Template:NPC name

## Description

**Lance's Brother**, also known as **(?),** is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC, located in at the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), inside of a food stand near the docks. He sells various [food](Food.md) items.

## Prices

-   Cheese -
-   Mushroom Omelette -
-   Mushroom Bisque -

## Crate Delivery Quest

To accept the quest, choose "You need some help around here?" When talking to him.

After accepting the quest, (?) will spawn a food crate at the back of his shop. To finish the quest, talk to [Stratos](Stratos.md) with the crate in hand.
