# The Pit

LocationL The Pit LocationR

Only one can stay at the center of this place.

  

## Description

**The Pit** was a limited-time combat area introduced during the [Metallica x Deepwoken '72 Seasons' Listening Party](<Metallica_x_Deepwoken_'72_Seasons'_Listening_Party.md>). It functioned as a small arena where players could knock each other out of it to accumulate points and eventually earn the Headbang [Emote](Emotes.md) and Metalhead [Badge](Badges.md).

All damage taken whilst in The Pit was heavily reduced. On the other hand, knockback was greatly increased, and any attack would send players ragdolled flying.

## Trivia

-   [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md) could sometimes appear around The Pit, seen as a spectator watching the Metallica band play.
