# Surface

**Game-adjacent article**  
This game content bears more significance to _Deepwoken'_s world than to the game.  
Thus, this article focuses on that.

## Description

**The Surface** is the half of [Lumen](Lumen.md), in which the player resides in-game, unless they are in [the Depths](The_Depths.md). The surface is made up of different [Luminants](Luminants.md) and the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) which surrounds them.

[The Voidfog](Voidsea.md) surrounds the skies of the Surface, resembling the clouds. The [Suncross](Suncross.md) lights up the day, while the [Moonseye](Moonseye.md) and the other [Stars](Stars.md) of the [Nightsea](Nightsea.md) light up the night.[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary0-1)

## Contents

[Luminants](Luminants.md): Akin to continents, the [Luminants](Luminants.md) are the remaining regions of Lumen that haven't been claimed by the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md). In order to secure safe passage through the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md), the work of a [Lightkeeper](Lightkeepers.md) is required.[\[2\]](#cite-note-glossary-2) Currently, there are only 2 [Luminants](Luminants.md) in-game: Etrean and Eastern, which are actually a singular [Luminant](Luminants.md), since they are only separated by the Sea Gates.[\[3\]](#cite-note-ploom-3)[\[4\]](#cite-note-citation-needed-4)

[The Voidsea](Voidsea.md): An anomalous and near-insurmountable stretch of water that represents the gradual dissolution of Lumen into [the Depths](The_Depths.md). Conventional laws of time and physics do not work fully in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md).[\[5\]](#cite-note-glossary1-5)[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary0-1)
