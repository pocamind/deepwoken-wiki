## Description

Okram is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC. They teach the player the basics of [Fishing](Fishing.md).

## Location

Okram is located in the same house as the [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) at [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md).
