## Description

High Monk Junsui was a figure of note among the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md).

## Location

High Monk Junsui is located inside of [The Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md), standing between [Monk Nako](Monk_Nako.md) and [Monk Kasan](Monk_Kasan.md).

## Trivia

-   Junsui is a Japanese name meaning "pure".
    -   It was partially chosen due to resembling the name "Jinshi", a character in _The Apothecary Diaries_, a historical drama novel, manga and anime series set in a fictionalized version of Tang Dynasty China.
