## Description

The Big Herbalist's Hat is an Equipment Armor in the Helmet category. Equipping this item will give you the [Iron Gut](Talents.md) and [Herbivore](Talents.md) talents, along with random stats.

## Notes

-   The Herbalist Hat and Big Herbalist Hat are stat wise the same and only differ in appearance.
-   Often used by light users to hide their animations.
