# Authority Peacekeeper

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

The Authority Prison Guard is a type of enemy NPC found patrolling in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md). They may look intimidating but in actuality they are surprising weak compared to an [Authority Footsoldier](Authority_Officer.md).

Their appearance consists of a Authority Peacekeeper Uniform and an Authority Helmet. They are almost identical to the [Authority Flamecharmer](Authority_Soldier.md), but are easily identifiable by their signature rifle spear.
