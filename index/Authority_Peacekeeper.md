# Authority Peacekeeper

## Description

Authority Peacekeepers are the guards found patrolling at [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md), they somehow are "dumber" in combat than their weaker version, the Authority Footsoldiers, although a noticeable increase in their health.

Their appearance consists of a Authority Peacekeeper Uniform and an Authority Helmet.

## Moves

Their arsenal is composed of a [Rifle Spear](Weapons.md) and a "[Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md)" mantra:

### Moves:

-   **Rifle Spear's Critical Attack:** Points the spear at the player and fires at them almost in an instant. _Blockable, can be parried._
-   **Flame Blade:** The Guard thrusts down with their blade coated in flame. _Blockable, can be parried._

### Boons:

-   **Numbers:** Although fighting one guard is simple enough, there are a _lot of them_ within Fort Merit (around ~15) and one simple "Hey, Over Here!" escalates the situation quickly.

### Flaws:

-   **Inexperienced:** They dont block/parry that much (from my experience), making them relatively weak even with their increased health.

## Tips

-   Fighting them head-on is probably the best way of attack, considering they are pretty "dumb" in combat and to not trigger the "Hey, Over Here!".
-   If their numbers have increased by a lot, it's best to keep backing off and to knock all of them one by one.
-   Try to NOT get the Authority Commander (Prison Warden) into the fight aswell.
