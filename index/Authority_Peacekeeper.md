# Authority Peacekeeper

Template:Creature Info Box v2

## Description

An Authority Prison Guard is a type of enemy NPC found patrolling in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md). They may look intimidating but in actuality they are surprising weak compared to an [Authority Footsoldier](Authority_Officer.md).

Their appearance consists of a Authority Peacekeeper Uniform and an Authority Helmet.

### Boons:

-   **Bodyguard Detail:** When attacking one, when near other Authority Peacekeepers. All nearby Authority Peacekeepers in range of the one you are attacking will rush to help their comrade. This includes the Authority Warden. You can easily get overwhelmed by the their numbers and if the Authority Warden joins it's game over.

### Flaws:

-   **Inexperienced:** They don't block/parry that much (from my experience), making them relatively weak even with their increased health.

## Tips

-   Fighting them head-on is probably the best way of attack, considering they are pretty "dumb" in combat and try not to let them alert the others by calling "Hey, Over Here!".
-   ~If their numbers have increased by a lot, it's best to keep backing off and to knock all of them one by one.~ It's actually better to run and heal instead of trying to pick them off one by one due to lag and other server-side issues. Especially if your not good at fighting multiple opponents at once.
-   Avoid grabbing the attention of an Authority Warden into the fight, as he will pose a great threat.
