## Description

The Golden Rats are honorable warriors, residing in the Golden Rat taverns. They find joy in challenging each other to fights and drinking together afterwards.[\[2\]](#cite-note-bouncer-2)

Seemingly, they do not harbor any feelings towards each other, as the [Golden Rat Bouncer](Golden_Rat_Bouncer.md) calls the [Rat Fighters](Rat_Fighter.md) in the [Rat's Den](<Rat's_Den.md>) losers and tells the player to kill them and bring him their [Gold Plates](Gold_Plate.md) for a reward.[\[2\]](#cite-note-bouncer-2)

They have only 3 rules, as the Golden Rats usually forego the "No brawling in the tavern" rule: Paying for your beverage up front, _no_ ruining the floorboards and the golden rule: Toasting only to [King Dravik](<Dravik,_the_Rat_King_(NPC).md>)'s name.[\[1\]](#cite-note-beard-1)

## Notable Members

**[Dravik, The Rat King](Dravik,_The_Rat_King.md)**: King Dravik the First, Duke of the Battleaxe, the Count of Gold, Monarch of the Tavern, and lastly, the King of [the Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md). Previously a vigil instructor, now the ruler of the Golden Rats, and notably, the strongest one of them. For yet unknown reasons he knows a lot about [Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md)'s power and how to break the barrier protecting him.[\[1\]](#cite-note-beard-1)[\[3\]](#cite-note-dravik-3)
