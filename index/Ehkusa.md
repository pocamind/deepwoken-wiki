## Description

**Ehkusa** was the main character of the [book](Books.md) [Lament of Cirryn](Lament_of_Cirryn.md).

She was a [Lifeweave](Lifeweave.md) scholar of [The Hive](The_Hive.md), who was sent out on a mission with 2 other scholars to revive [Cirryn](Cirryn.md) by a hivelord [Ximac Diri](Ximac_Diri.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-lament-1)

In the end, the mission ends in partial success, with [Cirryn](Cirryn.md) coming back to life, but going rogue and wreaking havoc in the Greathive.

Ehkusa a very peaceful woman, trying to make amends and communicate with her companions. She really values her friends, seemingly losing spirit when [Ka'al](<Ka'al.md>) dies, but finding power in herself to keep battling. She tries to stop [Remaz](Remaz.md) from sacrificing himself by luring [Cirryn](Cirryn.md) into the lifeless chamber in order to stop him from killing more people by offering herself instead. In the end, she follows with [Remaz](Remaz.md)'s plan and lets him sacrifice himself to save the Greathive from rogue [Cirryn](Cirryn.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-lament-1)

She also has a deceased friend, [Ryrda](Ryrda.md), a part of who's mask she always keeps with herself.[\[1\]](#cite-note-lament-1)[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)
