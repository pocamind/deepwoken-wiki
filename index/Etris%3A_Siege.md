# Etris: Siege

# Etris vs Authority Raid Event

Also referred to as, ''[Etris](Etris.md) Burning Event'', ''[Etris](Etris.md) War Event'', ''Etrea VS [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) Event''

''Highest amongst the peaks of the mountain, a proud fortress of a city stands against the world itself.''

## Description

The Etris raid event is an event in which [Authority NPC's](The_Central_Authority.md) will invade Etris, signified by a large number of flames spouting from various buildings in the area.

At the beginning of the event, various buildings will explode into flames, signified by a very loud explosion, as the flames crawl up towards the center of the city, Authority guards will rise up from the Etris docks, slowly making their way up to the main town of Etris while fighting off Etrean Guards in rapid succession. As the fight rages on, the clouds will make room for ash, smog, and smoke, darkening the sky until the inevitable end of the war.

  

If you or other players decide to help [Etris](Etris.md), and defeat all of the Authority Guards, 3 Large chests will spawn at the city center at The Tree, yielding **high-tier [Depths](The_Depths.md)** Loot, sometimes including [Deep Gems](Deep_Gems.md), and Rare Rings.

Polis, The [Jus Karita](Jus_Karita.md) Trainer will aid in the Event, usually found fighting off the Authority Warden. He can be found using the [Rapid Punches](Attunement-less.md) mantra. Speaking to him will provide unique dialogue.

''Ah, if it isn't the hero of Etrea. How goes your wary travels?

## Trivia

**NPC's will have special dialogue during this event, such as:**

-   ''Get to the town center, Hurry!''
-   ''The town is burning! It's burning! Help!''
-   ''We need all the help we can get! Get to the town and clear out the Authority! Please!''

**Authority Guards will also have special dialogue nearing the end of the event, such as:**

-   ''ONE FINAL PUSH! TO THE CENTER, ROUT THEM!''

One of the only places that will never burn in the Etris Raid Event is [Lord Regents](The_Lord_Regent.md) Palace.
