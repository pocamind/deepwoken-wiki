# Beatra

## Description

Beatra is an NPC who acts as [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) trainer for those who dont want to risk to wipe on [The Depths](The_Depths.md), They can be found to the left of the [Derelict Highchurch](Derelict_Highchurch.md)

In order to obtain [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md), it is similar as [Nostor, Master of Shadow](Nostor,_Master_of_Shadow.md), you must bring 5 [Umbral Obsidian](Umbral_Obsidian.md) to Beatra to unlock [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md)

## Trivia

-   if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, she will refuse to fight and say "Don't you give that look, dearie. You know these old bones are too frail to put up a fight..."
-   She also mentions that she can see the darkness in your heart, but that it wont do anything until you decide to draw it out
