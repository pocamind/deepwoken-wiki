# Megalodaunt

The Megalodaunt, commonly refered to as Sharko (not to be confused with the shark like creature, the [LionFish](Lionfish.md)) is a Giant Type enemy that can appear in The Etrean Luminant and The Depth.

It is the most common Giant enemy to spawn. Usually, killing a Megalodaunt grants a chest with decent gear, usually better than what you can find commonly.

This creature is hostile to every other creature but not itself. It will however still go for his rarer, Red version.

## Move list

-   **Slash:** A short range slash. Blockable and parriable. Heavy posture damage.
-   **Kick:** Sends the target flying. Break blocks, but can be dodged.
-   **Jump:** If the target is above, the Megalodaunt can jump, which incur a multihit attack above and in front of it. Blockable and parriable.
-   **Spit:** a multi hit projectile targetted at a single target. Blockable and parriable.
-   **Roar:** can roar at nearby targets, putting them in combat and granting a small amount of insanity
-   **Thousand needle:** an multi hit AOE, often used against groups of targets. Blockable and parriable.

## Comboes

-   Slash + Slash: if you block the first hit instead of parrying, the second hit will guard break.
-   Slash + Quick: You can block the first hit instead of parrying it, but the kick must be dodged.
