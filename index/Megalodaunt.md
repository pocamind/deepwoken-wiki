# Megalodaunt

Slang term for the Megalodaunt that spawns on Lower Erisia and The Depths.

It has some ranged attacks that can be blocked(not sure) and 2 close ranged moves. 1 of the close ranged moves is a kick that can only be dodged and another one is a swing that can be parried.
