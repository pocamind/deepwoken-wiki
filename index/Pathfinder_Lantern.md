## Description

The Pathfinder Lantern is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Torso" category. It can be crafted on the [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md) using the following materials:

-   (1) [Dying Ember](Dying_Embers.md)
-   (1) [Iron](Ores.md)
-   (1) Fiber

## Trivia

-   The glow of the lantern remains when smelted onto another torso equipment, allowing one to have the light of the lantern without compromising on stats
-   Dyeing the lantern does not effect the glow's color.
