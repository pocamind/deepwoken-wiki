# Training Gear

_"Why is there so many people in the sparring arena?!?" - Clueless Felinor_

  

## Description

Training Items, or Training equipment, can be bought and used to enhance your attributes; depending on the equipment. There are other ways than performing through equipment; such as eating poisonous foods to increase your Fortitude.

## Training Equipment

Dumbbell - Strength Training

Boulder - Strength and Fortitude Training

Training Vest - Fortitude Training

Ankle Weights - Agility Training

Mathematics Book - Intellegence Training

Encyclopedia - Intelligence training

Prayer Beads - Willpower Training

"How to Make Friends" - Charisma Training

Weapon Manual - Light, Medium, or Heavy Weapons Training

  

## Notes/Trivia

-   The Mathematics Book makes you do complicated Math Problems under a time limit (6 Seconds?)
-   Encyclopedia does not need your brain to work.
-   The "How to Make Friends" Training Item requires you to go outside and talk to NPCs.
-   Boulder Training damages you.
-   All Training Items have durability, indicated by \[#\] on their item description.
-   Training Items _CAN_ be used multiple times to increase your stat, but at a slow rate. You can alternatively make use of Training Items by performing them once and gain experience via PVE or PVP.
-   The Weapon Manual increases one of the weapon stats depending on the current weapon equipped.
-   All training equipment do not weigh anything.
