# Crafting Recipes

_"Man, this Brown Mushroom tastes bussin'!" (Pukes everywhere) - Random Adret_

## Description

One of the most obvious mechanics in the game, crafting. Recipes can be learned either through experimentation or other means of learning via dialogue. Food Recipes can be made on the go or a Campfire.

## Recipes

-   _1 Egg, 2 Gathered Wheat -_ Noodles, can be made anywhere
-   _1 Browncap, 1 Dentifillo -_ Mushroom Soup, Campfire required
-   _3 Wheat -_ Bread, Campfire required

## Alchemy

-   1 browncap, 1 gobletto, 2 dentifillo - soothing potion (temp blindness for like 3 seconds)

1 Bluecap, 1 Calabash, 1 Spider Egg - Heartening Elixir (?? Effect - probably healing or blood regeneration though)  

## Notes/Trivia

-   Sadly, you cannot use the Graceful Flame Mantra (Flamecharm exclusive) to cook Food.
