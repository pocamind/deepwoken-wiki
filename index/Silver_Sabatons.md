# Silver Sabatons

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

Silver Sabatons are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can be found through chests in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md).

Silver Sabatons are part of the "**Silver**" set; with the only other armor piece being the **Silver Pauldrons**.
