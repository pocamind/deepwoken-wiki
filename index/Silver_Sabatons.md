## Description

Silver Sabatons are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can be found through chests in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md).

Silver Sabatons are part of the "**Silver**" set; with the other 2 armor pieces being the **Silver Pauldrons** and **Knight's Helm**.
