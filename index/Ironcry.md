## Description

The Ironcry is a MED weapon in the Bow category. It is obtained by spending 25 [Echoes](Echoes.md) on the "Starting Weapon: Bow" [Echo Unlock](Echo_Unlock.md).

## Critical

Ironcry uses the default Bow critical, pulling back the bow before firing a ranged shot. It has a scaled damage modifier of **1.25x** and a cooldown of **7 seconds**.
