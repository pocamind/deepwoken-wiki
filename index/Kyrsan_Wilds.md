# Kyrsan Wilds

LocationL Kyrsan Wilds LocationR

Deep cavern walls painted with the serene glow of starlight emanating from a city lost to time.

  

## Points of Interest

-   Naerotiv
-   Plath

## Contents

Kyrsan Wilds is a sub-location to [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md), located in the Second Floor of the Second Layer. It's a small location with one of five obelisks, that are used to open gate and halt time in Second Floor. With a certain chance a pink chest with Kyrsan medallions spawns near to Naerotiv. Kyrsan Wilds is being defended by artillery. If you are spotted by it, you will be fired at for 20–30 seconds with ice spears.

Be warned that the wilds are above a dangerous void, and due to the usage of smooth terrain, it can be quite easy to fall in, prematurely ending your run, and most likely, your slot.

## Trivia

-   In order to get spotted by artillery, you have to step in pink circles on the ground. Ice spears fall from above, so you can just hide under something. These spears can be annoying sometimes and deal medium-high damage.
-   Naerotiv tells about New Kyrsa and Scion of Ethiron lore, also mentioning Kyrsgardes ritual called "The Ninefold Blessings of Marrow", used to lull him to sleep. He refers to New Kyrsa as a "not only a shrine to Ethiron, but to ensure it remains asleep" and "bedchamber". Naerotiv tells us that ritual must be completed and we must seal Scion of Ethiron.
