# The Meat Lord

The Meat Lord in all his glory.

The Meat Lord is a [Random Encounter](Random_Encounters.md) and a merchant in Deepwoken. He sells three types of meat dishes at low prices. The available items seem to be randomly selected from a larger pool of food items.

## Wares

-   Calamari
-   Fish Omelette
-   Glazed Fish - Boosts your M1 Attack Damage.
-   Seafood Boil - Makes your hunger go down slower for a limited time.
-   Sushi
-   Mushroom Bisque
-   Cooked Fish

## Notes/Trivia

-   The Meatlord sells arguably the best food option in the entire game, Calamari. Calamari has no weight on your character and has no maximum amount, meaning you can have 99 Calamari without any inventory space being used.
-   Choosing the "Hey" option when speaking to him will make him stop selling to you
-   Hostile NPCs do not attack him
-   Talking to The Meat Lord while possessing the vegetarian flaw will give you the dialogue option to tell him that you are a vegetarian, to which the meat lord will respond with "Did I ask?"

-   Trying to buy something from him with not enough money will make him stop telling to you too
