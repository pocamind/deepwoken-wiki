# The Meat Lord

The Meat Lord in all his glory.

The Meat Lord is a [Random Encounter](Random_Encounters.md) and a merchant in Deepwoken. He sells three types of meat dishes at low prices. The available items seem to be randomly selected from a larger pool of food items but **vegetarians cannot eat any of his food as all of them contain meat.**

## Wares

-   [Calamari](Calamari.md)
-   Fish Omelette - Boosts your Mantra Damage.
-   [Glazed Fish](Glazed_Fish.md) - Boosts your Weapon Damage.
-   Seafood Boil - Makes your hunger go down slower for a limited time.
-   [Sushi](Sushi.md)
-   Mushroom Bisque
-   Cooked Fish
-   [Megurger](Megurger.md) - Boosts your Weapon Damage.

## Trivia

-   The Meatlord sells some of the highest tier food, including Mushroom bisque which gives you a quite large amount of hunger and thirst, while also reducing the amount of hunger you lose for a short time.
-   Acting in ways he deems disrespectful will result in him refusing to sell items to you. This includes:
    -   Choosing the "Hey" option when speaking to him.
    -   Trying to buy something from him with not enough money.
-   Talking to The Meat Lord while possessing the vegetarian flaw will give you the dialogue option to tell him that you are a vegetarian, to which the meat lord will respond with "Did I ask?"
-   He sells the meat at very low prices, it is only 2 notes for an item.
