## Description

The **Parasol Membrane** is a chest drop from the [Interluminary Parasol](Interluminary_Parasol.md). It is a vital part of the Parasol used to bring horrors of [the Depths](The_Depths.md) into the overworld.

## Crafting

The Parasol Membrane can be used to craft into other items with the [Lost Forge](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md) found at the [Lost Forge of Leiuna](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md).

    -   [Regenerative Pendant](Regenerative_Pendant.md): 1x [Curseblood Pendant](Curseblood_Pendant.md) + 5x [Curse of the No Life King](Curse_of_the_No_Life_King.md) [Enchant Stones](Enchant_Stone.md) + 1x Parasol Membrane
