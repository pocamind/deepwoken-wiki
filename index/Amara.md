# Amara

## Description

Template:Creature Info Box v2 Amara is the Centurion of the 11th Legion, located in the Eastern Luminant, She is the main Source of Lore dedicated to the Legion, She's not the only Centurion in the East as she mentions the presence of other Centurions in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), Being the [Legion Captain](Legion_Captain.md) and the [Legion Javelin Captain](Legion_Sniper.md), the last one she warns you about, This one is Hostile no matter your Reputation with the Legion.

She also grants you [Legion kata](Fists.md) after you help the [Legion Captains](Legion_Captain.md) in their encounters.

Amara is a Khan, she's around 40 years old due to her wrinkles and hair color, She has the [Legion Centurion Outfit](Legion_Centurion.md)

## Lore

Amara and the 11th Legion seem to come all the way out west, as suggested by Gaius in [Viper's Jaw](<The_Viper's_Jaw.md>) ranting about how he was posted in the East, Even though the description of the [11th Legion Plate](11th_Legion_Plate.md) suggests that they've has always been there. Apparently the whole Legion is made of scouts as that's Amara's task in the East.
