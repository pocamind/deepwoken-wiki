# Chef Odiolavoro

Chef Odiolavoro is a new npc that gives quests or “orders” aswell as multiple food recipes. These quests only give xp, 10 notes and random food recipes. After a few orders there is an unknown cooldown. He's found in Etris across from the Banker.
