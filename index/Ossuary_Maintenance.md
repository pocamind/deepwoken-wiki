# Ossuary Maintenance

## Description

_**Ossuary Maintenance**_ is a [Book](Books.md).

## Text

### 

Ossuary Maintenance

> A set of esoteric notes
> 
> \[The book is soft to the touch and comfortingly weightless, crisp iridescent sheets flick past as you browse. It seems to be a record of mundane yet foreign maintenance logs, and you flick to a page at random. No author is listed, and the text is printed carefully - It just seems to be an archived copy.\] Crypt surv'ys and maintenance, due for our last eclipse of the year. As of this surv'y - Crypts stand at -6800 active passages, with an increase of 7 vacant ossuaries since. Confessor's Decree - \[Sub-Echelon and Rustic divisions.\] Satisfact'ry. There is to be no change to major routes for nostri Eclipse this year, may she guide us.
> 
> \---
> 
> Magna. Sub-Echelon routes 'Lunar Majora' et 'Lunar Minora' \[Directionem, est. Magna Selpulchrum.\] have been temporarily repair'd and reinforc'd - It seems that continued construction on archive expansion has caus'd extensive and irreversible pressure on our 'occupied' ossuaries, these need to be review'd before the tunnels can be safe for passage again; Un-noticed and whole osteodebris may obstruct the paths of our aecti. -- Consult the guidance of our Confessor and th'counsell upon possible delay regarding development until the pilgrimage. -- Consult descendency of osteodebris for possible replacement of the matter upon stronger vacant crypt lots before demolition post-eclipse. Retrieved unpermitted osteodebris from accessible outermost vacant crypts \[Directionem, ultramontes.\] beyond known occupied perimeter. -- Consult the guidance of our Confessor and th'counsell upon concerns of increased subterranean passage beyond known rural passage. - Consult descendancy of casualty during retrieval. Map all subterranean change to crypt routes and positions. External op'nings of moved crypts \[Directionem, ultramontes.\] measured and concealed. 2 new op'nings were located by retrieval aecti, presumably cause by subterranean change. Seal'd.
> 
> \---
>
