## Overview

Calamus is an NPC found in the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). He appears to be a member of [The Hive](The_Hive.md). When you first talk to him, he tells you to kill a [Monster](Monsters.md) claiming that he will reward you. If you complete his quest, he will give you a chest with average loot, and grant positive rep with The Hive. If you talk to him once again, he will tell you to kill another Monster, meaning you can take this quest multiple times.

If you haven't completed his task and you talk to him, he can give you the option to give up the quest, letting you take it again, but this time, with another monster.

After completing 30(?) quest from calamus

After completing a certain number of quests, he will give you a task to set up a device at [Bluster Rift](Bluster_Rift.md).
