# Iblis, The Fallen Angel

“ ﻿ THE PATH YOU CHOOSE TO FOLLOW IS WROUGHT WITH LIES. DECEIT. YOU ARE THE TRUE FOOLS, PLAYING AS GOD. ”

― Iblis

Template:Creature Info Box v2

[Iblis](Iblis,_The_Fallen_Angel.md) is a powerful angel located in Starfield Grove [Voidzone](Voidzones.md). It is used to get [Starkindred](Oaths.md) It uses an alternate [Divine Longspear](Crazy_Slots.md), and is an elemental angel of fire.

## Trivia

-   The name "Iblis" means "The leader of the devils" in Islamic culture. Iblis was an angel thrown out of heaven, so it's perfectly fitting that he is a red angel.
