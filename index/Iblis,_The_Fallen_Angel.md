# Iblis, The Fallen Angel

“ ﻿ THE PATH YOU CHOOSE TO FOLLOW IS WROUGHT WITH LIES. DECEIT. YOU ARE THE TRUE FOOLS, PLAYING AS GOD. ”

― Iblis

Template:Creature Info Box v2

Iblis, The Fallen Angel, is a powerful angel located in Starfield Grove [Voidzone](Voidzones.md). It is used to get [Starkindred](Oaths.md), uses an alternate [Divine Longspear](Crazy_Slots.md), and is an elemental angel of fire. It bears a red [Hivelord Mask](Hivelord_Mask.md).

## Trivia

-   In Christianity, Iblis was an Djinn (basically a spirit) that was ascended to the rank of an angel for his good deeds. Later on, for believing he was superior to Adam and refusing to accept Adam as Allah's new creation due to his superiority complex, he was cast out of the heavens and vowed to bring all of mankind to hell.
-   Iblis respawns shortly after dying.
