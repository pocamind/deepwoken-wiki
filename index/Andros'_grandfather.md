# Andros' grandfather

## Description

The grandfather of [Bookkeeper Andros](Bookkeeper_Andros.md). He was one of the [Old Stewards](Old_Stewards.md) and seemingly a bookkeeper like his son, since he left him books of the Old World.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)
