# Seasons

## Description

There are 4 seasons in Deepwoken: Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter. Each season lasts 1 real life day and transition between one another at midnight GMT+0.

The difference between all seasons is the visuals and interactions to Items that grow in the environment. Such examples is Wheat not growing in the Winter, and certain fruits unable to grow from trees.
