# Pathfinder Thaumaturge

Template:ArmorInfobox

**Pathfinder Thaumaturge** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken._

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires (5) Fiber, (5) Cloth and (1) Rock.

This Outfit requires 10 Intelligence and [Power Level](Talents.md) 15.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1000 Durability and grants the wearer +10% Physical Armor and +15% Elemental Armor.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) "_Now You See Me_ ", which grants the player 50% more stealth whilst crouched.

## Location

This outfit can be found in:

-   [Erisia](Erisia.md)
-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md)

## Trivia/Notes

-   The Pathfinder Thaumaturge is an inferior version of the [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer.](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md)
