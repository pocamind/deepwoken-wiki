# Pathfinder Thaumaturge

Template:ArmorInfobox

**Pathfinder Thaumaturge** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken._

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber as well as one (1) [Rock](Ores.md#rock).

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 5 and 10 [Intelligence](Attributes.md#intelligence)

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1000 Durability, grants +10% Physical Armor, and +15% Elemental Armor. Additionally, it provides the [Lowstride](Talents.md#assassin) Talent.

The [Lowstride](Talents.md#assassin) talent increases your [Stealth](Stealth.md) & Speed while crouching, as well as removing the noise equipping a weapon makes.

-   The Pathfinder Thaumaturge is a recolored and superior version of the [Pathfinder Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Sorcerer.md)
-   The Pathfinder Thaumaturge is an inferior version of the [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md).
