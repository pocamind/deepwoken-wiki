# Maestro Evengarde Rest

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Maestro Evengarde Rest is the Maestro of the Vigil Guard. He's a person who will help train and fight for the better result of mankind.

Upon challenging Maestro to a fight you'll be teleported to the Temple Sparring Hall. After winning the fight you'll receive a chest of rewards, and the new medium weapon [Purple Cloud](Purple_Cloud.md). However if you lose, you'll be instead sent to the depths, and gain the flaw disgraced. Where you'll have to fight him again to get rid of the flaw.
