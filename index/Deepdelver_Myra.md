## Description

Deepdelver Myra is a [Tiran](Tiran.md) NPC who is hesitant about their scheduled expedition into the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md).

## Location

Deepdelver Myra is located at the back of the Deepdriller room in [The Chariot's Spire](<The_Chariot's_Spire.md>)
