## Description

A helmet with a chance to be dropped by Mineskippers. This equipment's talents, Rock Blaster and Excavator, allow you to "Doubles your ore yields when mining." and "Chance to receive two sets of ore when mining." You also mine things quicker.

## Trivia

-   The light is switched off while it is held.
-   The first and currently only piece of equipment to give talents related to mining.
