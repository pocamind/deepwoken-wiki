# Combat Tag

## Deception

Combat tag, or In Combat _(In Danger)_ is the state you enter when being parried by an enemy or attacked by an enemy in any way, and can be Prolonged by being Hit multiple times resetting the timer, or added with being attacked by multiple Enemies.

Main Purpose is to punish Rage-Quit and Combat Log.

#### Indicated By:

-   The White/Red Skull popping up at the top of the Screen.
-   Combat Music of the Location your in will start playing.

Hovering over the Skull will show you the Name of The Entity that put you into Combat Tag or Prolonged it and The Timer you will be in Combat Tag for, it will also show the number of Entities that activated it.

## PVE and PVP

PVP Combat Tag Differs from PVE Combat Tag.

#### PVE White Skull

The White Skull Means your in PVE Combat Tag, this Combat Tag will only Last 30 Seconds or by killing all the Mobs that caused you Harm, This comes with Limitations.

1.  You can Not Switch Weapons nor Armor During Combat
2.  if you Leave the Game or Disconnect during this, as long as you are above 40% Health, you will not get Punished in any way, but if you were under 40% the Game would treat it as Death, even sending you to depths and wiping you if you already in it
