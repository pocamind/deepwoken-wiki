# Fleet Warden Cape

## Description

Template:EquipmentStats The Fleet Warden Cape can be obtained from fishing and Summer Isle chests. When equipped, you gain various buffs. Requires Power 40 to wear it. It has 6 variants which are Emerald, Rose, Scarlet, Shade, Silver & Onyx.
