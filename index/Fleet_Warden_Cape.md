# Fleet Warden Cape

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Fleet Warden Cape is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Torso" category. It can be obtained from [Fishing](Fishing.md) and [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) chests. When equipped, you gain +3% Elemental Armor and 1 Common [pip](Equipment.md). Requires Power 13 to wear it.

It has 6 variants which are: Emerald, Rose, Scarlet, Shade, Silver & Onyx.
