## Description

Marcus is inarguably the coolest Golem, if not the coolest NPC, in the game. He is of a modest size, unlike his braggart cousins who prefer to attack you instead of relaxing over a nice warm beverage. He can be found in the Hidden Village. Marcus canonically solos every Drowned God at once, with relative ease. (Confirmed by Ragoozer)

The only entity that can even compare to Marcus' incomprehensible power would be [The Meat Lord](The_Meat_Lord.md), as both have risen to levels above the powers of [Celestials](Celestial.md).

ALWAYS interact with Marcus whenever you see him, or you aren’t a real one.

## How To Find Marcus

When entering the hidden village, go to the right side and through a hallway to find an area with a campfire. When reaching the campfire spot, looking to your left; there will be a house that has a crawl space under it. Inside the space there will be a hole, jumping into the hole will take you to the humble cave Marcus lives in. Make sure to say hello. What a great guy.
