# Flint

Flint is essential for survival if you don't have Discovery of Fire, Graceful Flame, or Produce Spark. The purpose of flint is to light up campfires. The amount of uses flint has is 15. It can be crafted with one Rock and one piece of Coal. Or, you can buy it for 3 notes at the Etris General Store, Isle Of Vigils Blacksmith, and at Con's Pawn in The Summer Isle. There is also an upgraded flint with ten times the uses, the Umbral Flint, which is crafted with two umbral obsidian and one coal.
