# Words of Light

## Description

_**Words of Light**_ is a [Book](Books.md) obtained after defeating the [Kyrsgarde Champion, Kyrsa's Star](<Kyrsgarde_Champion,_Kyrsa's_Star.md>).

## Text

### 

Words of Light

> A Kyrsan Manifesto
> 
> \[The same text appears to be written twice on the scroll. The first instance is written in a tongue unknown to you. But the second instance is unmistakably penned in familiar Gatherhand. The letters tower imposingly upon the parchment, evenly spaced, and inimitably uniform. The way the ink sits upon the page suggests that the message was written by hand - but you would be forgiven for believing that its form was ordained by some universal constant, not unlike the forming of quartz crystals, or high pillars of basalt. The Gatherhand portion reads as follows.\]
> 
> * * *
> 
> HEAR ME NOW, AND DEIGN TO LEND AN EAR. BEHOLD NOW THUS, OUR MANIFESTED DISCONTENT.
> 
> LONG AGO DID I FIRST SERVE NEW KYRSA, WHEN IT WAS FREE OF THE USURPER'S UNYIELDING THRALL. WHEN ITS SPIRES REACHED EVER HIGHER AND THE CITY DREW ITS BREATH, I BREATHED ALONGSIDE IT, AND GAZED TOWARD THE SKIES. I WAS ITS SENTINEL AND GUARDIAN, BOUND BY A VOW UNENDING.
> 
> I SERVED IT STILL WHEN THE CHAINS FELL UPON US, AND THE CREEPING MISTS ROLLED IN TO BLANKET ALL. WHEN THE STORM TURNED OUR MIGHT AGAINST US, AND BOUND US TO ITS SERVITUDE. WHEN THE SPIRES OF OUR TRIUMPH BECAME THE WATCHTOWERS OF OUR CAPTIVITY. WHEN THE STORM'S EYE WATCHED, PEERING DEEP INTO OUR MINDS.
> 
> I SERVED IT STILL WHEN OUR CAPTOR BID US MARCH BEYOND THE FRINGES. ITS REGIMENTS I LED BENEATH A TATTERED STANDARD, LIKE CLOTHEN FINGERS RAKING THROUGH THE STORM. PROUD WARRIORS DEBASED TO WANTON PILLAGE. BENEATH A BLOODWORN FLAG, WE KILLED. WE CONQUERED. INNOCENTS WERE SLAIN. FOR YEARS WE ROVED, FOR CENTURIES WE REAPED.
> 
> I SERVED IT STILL WHEN IN THE RUINS OF CONSTELLIA, AMONG THE ASH AND FLAMES OF OUR DESTRUCTION, I FOUND A RADIANCE. A BLESSING FAR BEYOND OUR RECKONING AND KEN, AND ALIEN TO US. A HUMMING NOT UNLIKE A SOUL. A PRESENCE LINGERING, INVITING.
> 
> * * *
> 
> THIS ESSENCE SPOKE TO ME BY NAME.
> 
> IT DREW ME CLOSE, AND PIERCED MY VERY SOUL.
> 
> IT SPOKE THE TRUTH IN SOFT AND DULCET TONES.
> 
> ITS LIGHT WAS BRILLIANT AND BLINDING, ALL THE SAME.
> 
> ITS RADIANCE EASED THE SHACKLES OF MY MIND.
> 
> IT IS GLORIOUS. IT IS WONDERFUL. IT IS RADIANT. IT IS BEAUTIFUL.
> 
> THE ESSENCE IS NAMED LIGHT, AND I AM ITS APOSTLE.
> 
> * * *
> 
> THE LIGHT MADE ME AN INSTRUMENT OF LIBERATION. I TOOK UPON A WEAPON OF THE FALLEN, AND SWORE MY HEART TO KYRSA AND THE HEAVENS: I SHALL BECOME A BLADE OF RETRIBUTION; BRING DEATH UPON THE SCION AND HIS STORM.
> 
> THE TYRANT NOW APPROACHES, COME TO TAKE ME PRISONER. I SENSE IT. HE SEEKS WITHIN A TIMELESS CAGE TO BIND ME. TO QUELL THE LIGHT, AND SHROUD OUR WORLD IN DARKNESS.
> 
> SO HEAR ME NOW, AND DEIGN TO LEND AN EAR.
> 
> LEARN NOW THE NAME, 'RAEVA FERASH KENESTOS!
> 
> HEAR NOW HOW MUCH THE BLOOD-SOAKED HEAVENS WEPT.
> 
> HEAR NOW THE PRAYERS OF OUR KINFOLK TO THEIR MASTER, THE SAME WHO GRIPS THEIR CHAINS AND HOLDS THEM ALL IN THRALL.
> 
> IF YOU ARE FREE - BE YOU A KYRSAN OR FRINGEWALKER, - AWAIT MY MARK, AND JOIN IN MY RETURN.
> 
> * * *
> 
> AND TO THE FOUL USURPER AND ITS SCION: HEAR NOW MY ONLY SOLEMN WORDS OF WARNING.
> 
> KNOW MY NAME.
> 
> KNOW THAT I APPROACH.
> 
> BEHOLD THE LIGHT OF HEAVENS - KNOW AND FEAR ITS MIGHT.
> 
> DO WHAT YOU MAY MY ARRIVAL IS INEVITABLE.
> 
> THE ELEVENTH HOUR STRIKES. YOUR RECKONING IS COMING.
> 
> * * *
