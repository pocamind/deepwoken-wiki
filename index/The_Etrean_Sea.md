# The Etrean Sea

LocationL The Etrean Sea LocationR

These waters are tamer than most, but the stillness makes many uneasy.

  

**The Etrean Sea** is a location in _Deepwoken._ It is a region in the Etrean Luminant.

## Overview

The Etrean Sea is a region that all players who create their character will spawn in. This location is typically where beginners are found.
