# Lives

## Lives Mechanic

People have 2 lives when spawning in the game. When you die your health bar will be spotted, however leveling up will restore a life and return the health bar to its original state. Upon dying with 1 life (spotted health bar) You will see your ghost getting up from your body, but falling in it again. After that, you will spawn in [The Depths](The_Depths.md), There, you will have chance to live again(both lives will get restored.) You have to cross border in center and reach elevator. Dying will result in a wipe
