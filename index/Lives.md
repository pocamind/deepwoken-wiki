# Lives

## Lives Mechanic

People have 2 lives when spawning in the game. When you die your health bar will be spotted, however leveling up will restore a life and return the health bar to its original state. Upon dying with 1 life (spotted health bar) You will see your soul rise from your body, but falling back to it's vessel. Afterwards, you will spawn in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). Dying in The Depths will wipe your character, and you will be sent to the Fragments of Self. If you have fallen there, you could talk to the clone of yourself and wiped or pay 1000 robux to the statue to get revived in Fragments of Self area.
