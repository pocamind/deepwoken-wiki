# Lives

## Lives Mechanic

People have 2 lives when spawning in the game. When you die your health bar will be spotted, however it will go back to normal after some time, indicating that you regained your lost life. Upon dying with 1 life (spotted health bar) You will see your ghost getting up from your body, but falling in it again. After that, you will spawn in The [depths](The_Depths.md), There, you will have chance to live again(both lives will get restored.) You have to cross border in center and reach elevator. Dying will result in a wipe
