# Lives

## Lives Mechanic

Players have 2 lives when spawning in the game. When you die in the 'overworld', you will lose a life and your health bar will have small, translucent blotches on it. Leveling up will restore all your lives and return the health bar to its original state (Leveling up in the depths wont restore lives until you escape). Upon dying with 1 life left, You will see your soul rise from your body. Afterwards, you will spawn in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). Dying in The Depths will send you to the [Fragments of Self](Fragments_of_Self.md). If you go there, you can either talk to the replica of yourself and wipe ALL your character data for the specific slot or pay 1000 robux to the Shrine of the Dead located not far from the duplicate of yourself to get second chance at life. The only methods for restoring your lives legitimately are leveling up and escaping the depths.
