# Dusklit Garden

LocationL Dusklit Garden LocationR

TERRITORY OF The Knives of Eylis

Frozen in time, the leaves on the trees fall still. The grass doesn't wave with the wind. Crumbling debris of a lost time are frozen in the still atmosphere.

  

The **Dusklit Gardens** are a sub-location of the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md). It's a floating island, with small parkour on it's way. As Boatman's Watch, everything here is frozen in time, like everything in Voidsea. This location has blacksmith in it.
