# Sky Statue

| Sky Statue | |
|---|---|
| location | Derelict Highchurch, Etris Palace, Lower Erisia |

## Description

The Sky Statues are a small collection of statues that are used to obtain the [Bladeharper](Oath%253A_Bladeharper.md) [Oath](Oaths.md).

## Location

The Sky Statues are located separately throughout the Etrean and Eastern Luminant, their locations are:

-   [Derelict Highchurch](Derelict_Highchurch.md), on top of the large mountain to its side.
-   [Etris Palace](Etris_Palace.md), behind the palace on a small hill.
-   [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md), on a nearby mountain connected to an arch near the shores.
