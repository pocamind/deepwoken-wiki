# Sky Statue

A collection of statues that are dispersed around the map and are looking at the sky.

Sky Statue located next to Lower Erisia

## Dialogue

-   _Sky Statue: \*A statue of a person looking skyward.\*_
-   _Player: \[Inspect\]_
-   _Sky Statue: \*It's an interesting statue but the significance escapes you.\*_
-   If you have the requirements for [Oath: Bladeharper](Oath%253A_Bladeharper.md), the dialogue will change and you will be teleported to the arena.

## Locations

1.  On top of the gate between the sea with lower erisia and isle of vigils and the sea with minitersa and the waterlocks
2.  Behind Lord Regent's Manor
3.  On top of songseeker's mountain by the MINITYRSA gate

Sky Statue located in Etris behind Lord Regent's Manor.

These are relevant to the [Bladeharper Oath](Oath%253A_Bladeharper.md).
