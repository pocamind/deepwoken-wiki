# Saramaed Crossing

LocationL Saramaed Crossing LocationR

LAWLESS TERRITORY OF The Ignition Union

A derelict passage that bears the scars of industry.

  

## Overview

The Saramaed Crossing is a giant, broken passage, located between the [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) and the [Starfield Veldt](Starfield_Veldt.md). The passage has a number of static [Brainsucker](Brainsucker.md) spawns, and a [random encounter](Random_Encounters.md) with 2 [Summer Company Guards](Summer_Company_Guard.md) fighting a [Mechalodaunt](Mechalodaunt.md).

Inside a tower on the left side at the end of the bridge can be found [Adar, Master of Metal](Adar,_Master_of_Metal.md), who serves as both an [Ironsing](Ironsing.md) trainer and a part of [Silentheart](Oath%253A_Silentheart.md) progression.

The player will have to use the [glider](Gliders.md) in order to get from the end of the passage to the [Starfield Veldt](Starfield_Veldt.md).
