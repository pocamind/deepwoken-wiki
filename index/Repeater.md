# Repeater

[Revolver](Repeater.md) is a [gun](Guns.md) that can be bought in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) for 100 [notes](Notes.md).

Requirements: 65 l.weapon and 200 [notes](Notes.md) (for one gun)

The [Revolver](Repeater.md) has a base damage of 13 and scales with l.weapon. Much like other guns it has a unique attack pattern and has 12 range. the [Revolver](Repeater.md) fires much faster than the [Flintlock](Flintlock.md) and Dragoon. You could see it as a direct upgrade from the [Flintlock](Flintlock.md). As with all the [guns](Guns.md) it has 1.3x attack speed.
