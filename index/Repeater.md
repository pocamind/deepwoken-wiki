# Repeater

## **Information:**

The [Revolver](Repeater.md) is a [gun](Guns.md) that can be bought in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) for 200 [Notes](Notes.md). Yet, you also need 65 Light Weapon points to equip it.

The Revolver has a base damage of 13 and scales off of 15 light weapon. Much like other guns it has a unique attack pattern and has 12 range. It fires much faster than the [Flintlock](Flintlock.md) and Dragoon. You could see it as a direct upgrade from the Flintlock. As with all the [Guns](Guns.md), it has 1.3x attack speed.
