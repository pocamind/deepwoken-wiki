## Description

The [Revolver](Repeater.md) is a [gun](Guns.md) that can be bought in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) for 200 [Notes](Notes.md). It requires 65 points in Light weapons to be utilized.

The Revolver has a base damage of 13 and scales off of 15 light weapon. Much like other guns it has a unique attack pattern and has 12 range. It fires much faster than the [Flintlock](Flintlock.md) and [Dragoon](Dragoon.md). You could see it as a direct upgrade from the Flintlock. As with all the [guns](Guns.md), it has 1.3x attack speed.
