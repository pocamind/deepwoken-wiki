## Description

Pete is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) NPC who was part of [Cap'n Orlandeau](<Cap'n_Orlandeau.md>)'s crew in [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>). His position on the ship was not known.
