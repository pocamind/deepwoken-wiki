# Shanties

Shanties are a series of songs that you and others can play all at once to stave of hunger and thirst, slowly regain blood and HP, and to pass the time with your friends. Currently, there are four instruments you can play, being the Drums, the Trumpet, the Violin and the Accordion. When you purchase one of these at the [Etris](Etris.md) [antiquarian](Antiquarian.md), you get a Worn Songbook, which will ask you which instrument you want to play and which Shanty to play along to. If there are other people playing a Shanty nearby, you can join in in time with them.

There are currently 3 shanties to choose from, being

-   A Memory of Tides, obtained with your Songbook
-   The Gideshu March, obtained by reading the [book](Books.md) of the same name
-   The Song of Fathoms, obtained by reading the [book](Books.md) of the same name
