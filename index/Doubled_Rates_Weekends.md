# Doubled Rates Weekends

Every real life Weekend (Spanning from Saturday 00:00UTC - Monday/Tuesday 00:00UTC, starting times may vary from earlier to later due to how it starts when an **Update Drops**, but always ends consistently.) Certain changes to the player-quality of deepwoken will occur.

## Changes

-   All [Chest](Chests_&_Sacks.md) sizes are visually doubled in size and give double the amounts of items, usually wielding much higher rates for high demand [Relics](Relics.md), [Legendary Weapons](Weapons.md), or a much higher sell price for loot farming areas.
    -   Chest sizes may remain doubled for extended periods.
-   [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) is open to be played.
-   [Guild Points](Guilds.md) are doubled, so a boss such as [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md) will instead give 6 points instead of 3 points towards your guilds "PVE Score"

## Tips

-   Farming bosses may prove incredibly lucrative, as well as ganking players who may have recently come out of bosses, as due to much higher rates bosses will most likely have dropped an incredibly high amount of [Deep Gems](Mantra_Modifiers.md) or potentially Legendary Weapons
-   Grinding to high [Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md) waves with a guild will provide incredibly high amounts of guild points that may lead to ascending up to higher ranks to be much easier.
