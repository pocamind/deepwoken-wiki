# Llevyn

Template:NPC nameLlevyn is a NPC, in the first floor (Eternal gale) of the Second layer, once you power up the generator if you talk to him, he can call you a deepdriller which will arrive at the end of the Nest corridor. Keep in mind that if you escape this way you will NOT keep the loot you picked in the Second layer.

The deepdriller escaping method is mainly used by players that got here without a lighthook and dont wanna risk wiping at the chaser fight.
