# Shogun of the Prophet's Guard (Temple of Mur)

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

**Shogun Captain** is a Boss exclusive to Vow of Iron. It is a humanoid that's larger than the player.

## Description

The Shogun Captain is required to be killed in order to power up past power 5. It is located in a dungeon server. In order to access said dungeon, there is a white portal near the Deep Thresher Nest or Viridian Sanctuary.

## Attacks

When **Shogun Captain** highlights green, it will move a bit backward, then it does 2 spiral slashes to the player. This attack is parryable.

When **Shogun Captain** highlights red, it will do 2 spiral slashes. This attack is unparryable, so you have to dodge roll twice.

When **Shogun Captain** highlights white, it will do a stomp. You have to jump in order to avoid this attack.

When **Shogun Captain** highlights blue, it will have a long windup, afterwards it will do one hefty slash to the player. This attack is parryable.

**Shogun Captain** will do a kick and then slash the player, this gives no highlights to **Shogun** and the attack is parryable.

**Shogun Captain** without a windup do 2 slashes to the player, this gives no highlights to **Shogun** and the attack is parryable.

**Shogun Captain** can quickly do 1 slash to the player with tons of particles. The attack is parryable

## Tips

**Shogun Captain** can movestack (it's crazy)

Its attacks are in a random order, so it's best to keep an eye on which move he will do next.

The sixth letter of the alphabet is F. By pressing it, you initiate something called a parry. Use this to your advantage when fighting as **Shogun's** attacks do a hefty amount of damage.
