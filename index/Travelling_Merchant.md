# Travelling Merchant

“ You want buy? ”

― Chili to a potential customer

Template:NPC name

## Description

Chili is a Crow [Tiran](Tiran.md) that appears in [Merchant Schooners](Ships_&_Sailing.md) spawned around [the Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md). At the bottom deck, he can sell a [Food](Food.md) item, a [Weapon](Weapons.md) of Uncommon to Common rarity, an [Armor](Equipment.md) piece and a random [Lore](Lore.md) [Book](Books.md).
