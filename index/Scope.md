# Scope

## Description

Scope is a Vesperian NPC in the [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md).

He is collecting explosive boxes found in events all over the Summer Isle. For those who turn an explosive box, he will reward them with a chest and positive Summer Company Rep.

Explosive Box Event
