**Ignition Welder** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfit.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs 300 and requires five (5) [Cloth](Cloth.md), five (5) [Fiber](Fiber.md), five (5) [Iron](Iron.md), and five (5) [Erisore](Erisore.md) to craft.

This Outfit additionally requires the player to be at least [Power](Power.md) 10 and have at least 10 [Fortitude](Fortitude.md) to be crafted.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 3000 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance ResistanceBlunt ResistanceSlash, +25% Elemental resistance [ResistanceElemental](Attunement.md).

It also gives the [Master Craftsman](Talents.md#master-craftsman) Talent, allowing you to craft items that would normally require a workbench from your inventory.
