# Ignition Welder

Template:ArmorInfobox**Ignition Welder** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in Deepwoken.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, five (5) Fiber, five (5) [Iron](Ores.md), and five (5) [Erisore](Ores.md).

This Outfit requires [Power](Power.md) 10 and 10 [Fortitude](Attributes.md#fortitude).

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 3000 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +25% Elemental resistance.

It gives the [Master Craftsman](Talents.md#artisan) Talent, allowing you to craft items that would normally require a workbench from your inventory.

## Location

This Outfit's schematic is obtainable from:

-   [Saramaed Hollow](Saramaed_Hollow.md)

## Trivia

-   This outfit is worn by [Ottilde](Ottilde.md) and [Merryweather](Merryweather.md).
