**Ignition Welder** is an Expert [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs 300 and requires five (5) [Cloth](Cloth.md), five (5) [Fiber](Fiber.md), five (5) [Iron](Ores.md#iron), and five (5) [Erisore](Ores.md#erisore) to craft.

This Outfit additionally requires the player to be at least [Power](Power.md) 10 and have at least 10 [Fortitude](Attributes.md#ftd) to be crafted.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 3000 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +25% Elemental resistance.

It also gives the [Master Craftsman](Talents.md#artisan) Talent, allowing you to craft items that would normally require a workbench from your inventory.
