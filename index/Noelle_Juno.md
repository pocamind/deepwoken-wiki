# Noelle Juno

| Noelle Juno | |
|---|---|

## Description

Noelle Juno is a Citadel professor that researched into the scarcity of [Star Boots](Star_Boots.md). Juno concluded that due to their scarcity and unsustainable replacement rate, the supply of Star Boots was expected to be exhausted within the coming years.
