## Description

Noelle Juno is a Citadel professor of particular renown.[\[1\]](#cite-note-update-1)

At one point, the professor published a theory regarding the scarcity of [Star Boots](Star_Boots.md) that had emerged in recent years. Juno concluded that due to their scarcity and unsustainable replacement rate, the supply of Star Boots was expected to be exhausted within the coming years.[\[1\]](#cite-note-update-1)

Shortly afterwards, the boots did indeed disappear.[\[1\]](#cite-note-update-1)
