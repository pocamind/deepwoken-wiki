# Starfield Grove

Template:Location2

Template:Areainfobox**Starfield Grove** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md) island found in the East Luminant at Aratel Island (or Hive) to the left of the Greathive Gate. Using an Ignition Hook near the entrance would lead you to a static [Stone Knight](Stone_Knight.md) spawn as well as [Iblis, The Fallen Angel](Iblis,_The_Fallen_Angel.md).

By taking an entrance through the Bluster's Rift, you may find two threshers blocking your way. Here, [Linkstriders](Oaths.md) may obtain [Stardust](Stardust.md) alongside any other [Voidzones](Voidzones.md) that contains a Star Tree located in the map. You can also find [Skyvalor Flowers](Ingredients.md) for a [Willpower Attribute Flask](Ingredients.md).
