# Yesterwynde Tavern

LocationL Yesterwynde Tavern LocationR

  
  

On the left once passing the mudskipper knights on the way to Kj's brother for Lost Brother quest.

Tavern Entrance door on island between Lower Erisia and Isle of Vigils.

Entrance at Isle of Vigils. Behind the food stand down to the left.

  

Can be entered through several doors.

One of the records at Minityrsa above the abandoned church and camp.

Upon entering you can walk up to [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md) behind the bar and begin dialogue with him. He says "One lurks over the machinery threaded through the caldera of a volcano, another lies within a nestled spot of nature within a frozen waste, and the final record can be found in a land lost to time, yet familiar all the same." (They're at Miner's Landing, Minityrsa, and Upper Erisia)

At starswept valley to the right of the door

Each record play's their respective songs when nearby.

Unlocking on one slot will unlock it for all other slots.

  
Each of the gilded instruments obtained after completing the quest.
