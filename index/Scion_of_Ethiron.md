# Scion of Ethiron

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

The Scion of Ethiron is a boss encountered in the Eternal Gale. They are tied to [Ethiron](<Ethiron,_The_Maelstrom's_Eye.md>) the [Drowned God](Lore.md) in some way. They are the boss of Layer 2:2 (Layer 2 has 2 floors, :# in this case 2).
