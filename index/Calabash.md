# Calabash

Template:Food Template V1

## Description

Consumable fruit found on Etris and Vigil growing in bushes in quantities of 3-5 per bush. It's a good idea to bring a few when going on excursions if you don't have a [Canteen](Canteen.md) since it replenishes a good amount of thirst relative to other starting island foods. Calabashes are also known as food of the gods because only true gods can eat them. We have even witnessed that the gods in Greek mythology ate calabashes. When the people wanted to make stone statues of them, they thought "this fruit is way to holy to be seen by pubic". There is even a state called calabash real state

Note: Red Calabash is NOT real, there isn't any proof of it actually existing as an item, and its fake proof of existing is photoshop (even this image of the Calabash is faked as a red one, search red calabash on google).
