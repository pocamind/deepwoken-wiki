# Calabash

Template:Food Template V1

## Description

Consumable fruit found on Etris and Vigil growing in bushes in quantities of 3-5 per bush. Its a good idea to bring a few when going on excursions if you dont have a [Canteen](Canteen.md) since it replenishes an good amount of thirst relative to other starting island foods. Calabash are also known as food of the gods because only true gods can eat them.
