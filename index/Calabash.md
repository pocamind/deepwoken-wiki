# Calabash

Template:Food Template V1

## Description

Consumable fruit found on Etris and Vigil growing in bushes in quantities of 3-5 per bush. Its a good idea to bring a few when going on excursions if you dont have a [Canteen](Canteen.md) since it replenishes an good amount of thirst relative to other starting island foods. Calabash are also known as food of the gods because only true gods can eat them. We have even witnessed that the gods in greek mythology ate calabashes.

but when the people wanted to make stone statues of them they thought "this fruit is way to holy to be seen by public". There is even a state called calabash real state
