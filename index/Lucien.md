## Description

**Lucien** is an NPC located in the [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md) who grants the recipe to crafting [Iron Bombs](Iron_Bomb.md) through the [Quest](Talent_Quests.md) **Mineskipper Mayhem**.
