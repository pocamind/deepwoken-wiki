**Valient Valley** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique areas exclusive to the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) gamemode.

## Overview

Valient Valley is a large valley on the outskirts of [Kingmaker's Isle](<Kingmaker's_Isle.md>). It has many [Lifevents](Lifevents.md) and cliffs to run around.
