# Surge

Template:NPC name

## Description

Surge is a Canor man located in the depths, and seems to be extremely knowledgeable. He is interested in the deep owl, and will teach you tricks of the mind in return for Void Feathers.

## Location

In order to get to Surge, you must enter the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) cave in The Depths. Note that the Widow will continually pull you if you do not kill it beforehand, but it is possible to talk to Surge without killing it.

  

## Notes

-   The requirements for the Visionshaper Oath are two: 50 Charisma and at least 1 Void Feather. You must also complete the [Aelita Quest](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Aelita) in the Valley of Heroes, which spawns there (high chance) when it isn't Winter.
-   You can only learn illusion magic and the Visionshaper talents if you bring Surge more void feathers after obtaining the Oath. You need in total 6 Void Feathers.
-   The first time you give him a void feather he will teach you the Oath. You unlock mantras and talents by giving him additional feathers, 5 to max.
-   Surge used to have the visionshaper eye customization that he teaches upon unlocking the oath. After the **official** release of the Oath, this was removed.
-   Surge was described in the update version logs as 'not what he appears to be' and may be a husk of a former visionshaper, destroyed by the Nestmind to lure more prey or hunters to devour.
