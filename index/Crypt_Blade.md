# Crypt Blade

File:Crypt Blade.png

## Description

The Crypt Blade is a Legendary weapon obtainable from Primadon and in the depths (Also from Duke but not sure). It scales with [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) (10) and heavy weapon with 26 base damage. (UNOBTAINABLE)

## Requirements

100 Shadowcast

10 Strength

45 Heavy

## Abilities

\- It procs every [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) passive/talent

\- Highest damaging weapon in the game ( Besides [Crazy Slots](Crazy_Slots.md) )
