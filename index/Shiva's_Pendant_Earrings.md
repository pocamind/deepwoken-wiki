## Description

Shiva's Pendant Earrings are Rare [Equipment](Equipment.md) items under the "Ears" category.

The [Skillful Recovery](Talents.md#skillful-recovery) Talent recovers more posture in all forms of combat.

## Obtainment

This item is exclusively crafted at the [Lost Forge](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md). The recipe is as follows:

-   1x [Amethyst Pendant Earrings](Amethyst_Pendant_Earrings.md) + 1x [Doom of Caeranthil's Scale](<Doom_of_Caeranthil's_Scale.md>) + 1x [Shiva's Codex](<Shiva's_Codex.md>)
