## Description

The **Dread Serpent's Mane** is a rare direct drop from the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md).

## Crafting

The Dread Serpent's Mane can be used to craft an item with the [Lost Forge](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md) found at the [Lost Forge of Leiuna](Lost_Forge_of_Leiuna.md).

[Ingredients](Ingredients.md)

-   [Ether Refined Hide](Ether_Refined_Hide.md): 1x [Primadon Pelt](Primadon_Pelt.md) + 2x [Ferryman's Coat Cloth](<Ferryman's_Coat_Cloth.md>) + 1x Dread Serpent's Mane
