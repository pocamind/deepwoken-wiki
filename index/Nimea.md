“ Light is to Void as Paint is to a Canvas- it draws out substance. ”

― Nimea

  

## Description

Nimea is a NPC inside a house in [The Hidden Village](The_Hidden_Village.md) to shares information about [Lightkeepers](Lightkeep.md). As well as a backstory on the situation of the Hidden Village, on the status of the civilians and what may of happened to them.
