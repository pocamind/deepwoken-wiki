## Description

The Stranger is an NPC that rarely spawns as one of many [Random Encounters](Random_Encounters.md) at [Erisia](Erisia.md). He's a [Canor](Canor.md) from [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md) who sits until someone approaches him.

## Tacet Obtainment

Via having 50 [Charisma](Attributes.md), you will obtain [Tacet](Murmur.md) without having to fulfill any kill requirements.
