## Description

Flareblood Kamas is a Mythical [Light Weapon](Weapons.md) that scales with both Light and Intelligence. Has a unique critical that summons a blood explosion to your cursor. Wielding it requires 60 Light and 45 Intelligence. It does 14.2 base damage and 4.2 bleed damage. It also has a swing speed of 0.98x.

It was made by and thus obtained from Chaser.

## Trivia

-   Despite being a Mythical Weapon, it has a gray border in chests.
    -   This makes it easy to miss due to it being at the bottom of the GUI, so check thoroughly.
-   Has a unique critical that summons a blood explosion to your cursor.
    -   Critical is likely based on Chaser's "Burning Blood Explosion."
-   This weapon is probably a reference to the anime _Demon Slayer_, being very similar to Gyutaro's Blood Sickles.
