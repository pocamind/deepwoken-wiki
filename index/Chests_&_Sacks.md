# Chests & Sacks

i just wanted to create this page. im going to just write random stuff related to chests and maybe someone can clean it up?

Chests are spawned at the end of most events in the game, awarding loot dependent on the location, which can cause it to drop better quality loot. Luck however does not have any influence over what actually drops, and is solely based on your real life luck.

Enchants will appear in chests as a teal/whitish blue color.
