# Dye Packet

Dye Packet's GUI

## Description

A Dye Packet is a [Relic](Relics.md) that can be obtained from [Klaris Llfiend, Mother of Lights](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) by handing in one [Kyrsan Medallion](Kyrsan_Medallions.md) per packet, Buying it from [Sev](Sev.md) by exchanging [Crowns](Crowns.md), getting it from the [Weird Trader](Weird_Trader.md), a chest from the [Carnival of Hearts](Carnival_of_Hearts.md), buying it from [Traan Zakshun](Traan_Zakshun.md) for, or rarely in [Merchant Ships](Travelling_Merchant.md) for around. It cannot be dropped but the dyed equipment can be dropped. Dye Remover no longer comes with the Dye Packet when you purchase one. Dyes can be removed at a [Barber](Barber.md). The maximum Dye Packets that can be held per slot is 99.

## Dye-able Components

-   [Equipment](Equipment.md)
-   Hair (each hair slot can be dyed separately)
-   Eyes (both Pupils and Sclera)
-   [Resonance](Resonance.md) bells
-   [Oath](Oaths.md) visuals (some Oaths, like [Starkindred](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md) come with multiple separately dyable parts)
-   [Outfits](Outfits.md)
-   Aspect deco EX: Gremor Horns

## Trivia

-   [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) masks and [Ganymede](Ganymede.md) hats have separate parts that can be dyed individually. After choosing the dye for the mask/hat, you will be prompted to change the dye on the ink/hat underside. Pressing submit without changing anything will allow you to progress to the secondary dye without spending a Dye Packet.
-   Most equipment and outfits are just "tinted" instead of the actual color changing.
    -   The [Familiar Heretic's Armor](<Familiar_Heretic's_Armor.md>) almost changes color entirely when dyed pure white (255/255/255), changing from darker colors like black to a lighter bluish hue on certain sections of the outfit. For most items, dying them white won't change much or anything at all, but this is an exception.
-   Scaling [rings](Equipment.md#rings) (i.e. [Akira's Ring](<Akira's_Ring.md>) or [Prophet's Ring](<Prophet's_Ring.md>)) cannot be dyed.
-   Originally, they could only be obtained from Klaris. This was eventually changed on the August 15th, 2023 update, making them available for purchase from Merchant Ships.
    -   Previously, Dye Packets would come with a Dye Remover item which was the only way to remove an equipment dye. If purchased from Chili's Merchant Ships, they would not come with the Remover. This item was made unobtainable on the April 5th, 2024 update and later entirely removed in a shadow update of April 2025.
-   Dye Packets have no model, so appear invisible on Chili's ship.
-   Choosing to "Dye All" on hairs will only use 1 dye packet.
