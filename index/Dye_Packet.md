# Dye Packet

Dye Packet's GUI

## Description

Dye Packet is an endgame item that makes your equipment change its color. It can be obtained from Klaris by handing in Kyrsan Medallions or very rarely in Merchant Ships for notes. It also be used to dye hair into a different color. It cannot be dropped but the dyed equipment can be dropped. You can also remove a dyed equipment with the Dye Remover that comes with the Dye Packet when you purchase one. The maximum Dye Packets you can get per slot is 10.

## Trivia

-   Some equipment are just "tinted" instead of the actual color changing.
-   An update in August 15, 2023 made you able to buy Dye Packets in Merchant Ships, instead of killing the scion of a god to change the color of your hair.
