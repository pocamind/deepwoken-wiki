## Description

The Star Duster is an equipment item under the Arms category. Equipping it grants you 5 Health, 1 Posture, 3 Pips, and the [Talent](Talents.md) Firmly Planted, which reduces the effectiveness of all slowness debuffs by 30%. It is rarely found in chests from [The Depths](The_Depths.md) ([First Layer](First_Layer.md)), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), and [Fishing](Fishing.md).

## Trivia

-   The Star Duster is part of the "Star" set; with the only other piece being the [Star Boots](Star_Boots.md).
