## Description

Stone is a buffing enchantment that increases the posture damage of your M1s and increases the damage on light attack guardbreak.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to brown and gives it a stone texture.

## Effect

Stone increases the posture damage of your M1s, scaling on your weapon investment. This grants +0.035 flat posture damage for every stat point allocated to your weapon attributes, granting a maximum of +3.5 posture damage at 100 Weapon.

If your M1s guardbreak, they'll deal additional damage based on your weapon's Weight stat, with Stone included. This increases your guardbreak damage by 25% of your weight stat, with Stone included, before damage modifiers.

For example, a 100 Heavy investment [Sovereign's Punishment](<Sovereign's_Punishment.md>) would look like this: 13 Weight + (0.035 \* 100) Stone = 16.5 Posture damage. (13 + 3.5) \* 0.25 = 4.55 additional guard break damage on M1s.

### Notes

-   Stone does **not** grant your weapon Chip Damage despite what its description states.
-   Because Stone's increased damage on guardbreak is effectively an increase to your weapon's scaled damage, damage modifiers will be based off the posture break damage with Stone's effects included.
-   [Wyrmtooth](Wyrmtooth.md) will prioritize your Medium Weapon investment over your Heavy Weapon investment for Stone's posture damage calculations.
