# Origins

Origins are a feature in _Deepwoken_ which provide the player with certain gear and allegiances on spawn, depending on which origin is chosen.

## Origins

### Castaway

_"Wilson! I'm sorry, Wilson. Wilson, I'm sorry! I'm sorry!"_

**Obtainment:** Entering Deepwoken for the first time.

-   Offers starting locations to be drifted into.
-   Begin with nothing but tattered armor and a starting weapon; along with a training item of your highest attribute.
-   No pros or cons; a blank slate for your character.

### Lone Warrior

**Obtainment:** Successfully reach the halfway point of the Trial. (Threshers)

-   Allows instant access/teleportation to the Trial of One.

### Deepbound

_"Your very soul is in eternal contract with The Depths." - Obsolete Flavour Text_

**Obtainment:** Obtained after speaking Akira in Castle Light about "joining" the divers, after encountering him with an Ignition Union Party in Celtor Wastes.

-   Grants innate Talent "Deepbound Contract"; increasing EXP Gain in the Depths while reducing EXP Gain in the Overworld.
-   As a Deepbound, your starting location is always Castle Light; and you always spawn with a pair of [Diver Apprentice Armor](Diver_Apprentice_Robes.md).
-   Castle Light necessities (Deepbound exclusive vendors such as Antiquarians) are available to you.
-   Depths Trial will always be Enforcers.

## Trivia

-   The Deepbound Origin was originally a Flaw; but was changed into an Origin in 8/22/2022.
-   Deepbound is obtained _only_ after talking to Akira about joining the divers; but not actually saying yes. This misconception led to a handful amount of players leaving/disbanding their guilds.
