## Description

Petris is a [Etrean](Etrean.md) NPC. He is an adventurer who explains the ritual on how to summon [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md).

## Location

Petris is located at the front of [Boatman's Watch](<Boatman's_Watch.md>).
