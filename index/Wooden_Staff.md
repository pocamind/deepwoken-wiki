## Description

The Wooden Staff is a [Staff](Weapons.md) weapon in the Medium category.

## Talent: Both Ends

Equipping the Wooden Staff grants its unique [weapon Talent](Talents.md#weapon-talents)(s). Weapon Talents will be lost upon equipping any other weapon, including ability-bound weapons, like from the [Crazy Slots](Crazy_Slots.md) Resonance.

**Both Ends** allows you to followup an M1 with a swing from the opposite end of the staff by pressing M2. This attack comes out much faster, but only does 45% of your weapon damage.
