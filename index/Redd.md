# Redd

Redd's are fruits that can be found almost anywhere

despite the name Redd's are orange in color and spherical

Redd's restore a small portion of thirst and an acceptable amount of hunger considering how common they are

3 Redd's make "Redd Juice" which restores a sizable amount of thirst
