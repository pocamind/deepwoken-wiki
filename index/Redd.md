## Description

A small consumable fruit found in many places across both Luminants. They grow on trees, with around 5-8 Redds per tree.

## Crafting

### Used in

-   [Candied Fruit](Candied_Fruit.md) - 2 Redd and 1 [Sap](Sap.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
-   [Fruit Salad](Fruit_Salad.md) - 1 Redd and 2 other [Fruits](Ingredients.md#fruits), no Campfire required.
-   [Redd Juice](Redd_Juice.md) - 3 Redds, no Campfire required.

## Trivia

-   Real life counterpart is the plum.
-   If used in [Alchemy](Alchemy.md), the primary affect applied is **Grueling**; Secondary effect is **Strengthening**.
