# Ardour Manifestation

“ A manifestation of dark energy. While holding it, you feel drawn to the Voidsea itself, as if... something were calling to you, waiting for you there. ”

― In game description

Ardour Manifestation is a reward granted upon killing any bosses, even the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md) themselves. The only time this item is not granted is either:

-   You already own the item in your inventory, or
-   If you already possess a [Murmur](Murmur.md) (Ardour or Tacet).

## Trivia

-   The Ardour Manifestation has no model, but is still held out like any other item.
-   When using the item it will release the seem effects when using Ardour Scream.
-   The item will NOT trigger if another Dread Serpent spawned through the Manifestation is already present in the server.
-   You can get the Manifestation on your first try against the Dread Serpent since there's a few moments before you get the Murmur.
-   ~There is an amount of bosses killed that will guarantee a drop of this item~ (There's no pity like the Pluripotent Alloy)
-   Its likely stated its a 1/4 drop from Bosses.
