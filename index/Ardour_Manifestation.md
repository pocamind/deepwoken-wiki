# Ardour Manifestation

“ A manifestation of dark energy. While holding it, you feel drawn to the Voidsea itself, as if... something were calling to you, waiting for you there. ”

― In game descripion

  
Ardour Manifestation is an item dropped from other bosses besides The Dread Serpent. You can use this in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) to summon [Ardour](Dread_Serpent.md).

## Trivia

-   It has no model.
-   It will not drop if you already have Ardour or Ardour Scream.
