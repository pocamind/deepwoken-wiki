## Description

Gourdskippers, also known as Pumpskippers, are a unique enemy exclusive to the Trial of the Gourdskipper during the [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md). They are a variant of the Mudskipper. During the [Trial of the Gourdskipper](<Widow's_Wail_Cemetery.md>), Gourdskippers automatically aggro onto the nearest non-infected, though attacking them while infected will cause them to re-aggro onto you, hitting you until you are either knocked, gripped, or out of range.
