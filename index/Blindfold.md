# Blindfold

“ I am legally blind ”

― New player who joined 3 days ago and just found a blindfold

  
Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Blindfolds are accessories found in [Upper](Upper_Erisia.md) [Lower](Lower_Erisia.md) [Erisia](Erisia.md) and [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). Equipping this item gives you the [Blinded](Talents.md) talent. A Blindfold is required for the [Blindseer](Oaths.md) oath.

## Trivia

The Blindfold, Crimson Blindfold and Black Blindfold are all the same item, just with different appearances.
