# Duke Erisia Plushie

## Description

The **Duke Erisia Plushie** was a plushie released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration, classified under the "Plushie" category. Its campaign ran from July 16th, 2024, to August 14th, 2024, before selling out. The plushie measured 20.0 cm in height, 10.5 cm in width, and 13.5 cm in depth. It was made from polypropylene cotton and plush fabric (ultra soft fleece).

### Official Description

"The infamous Duke Ishamon Erisia is in need of a new manor, and he thinks yours should do nicely!  
  
Golems not included."

## Trivia

-   A total of 2,494 Duke Erisia Plushies were sold, and the campaign ended at 1,247% of its funding goal.
