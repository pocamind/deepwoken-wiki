**Path's Respite** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location found in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).

## Overview

Located in Lower Erisia consisting of 5 buildings, a job board, next to a cave, with another room, an item locker and a way behind the mountain. The place used to be overrun by bandits, but now is only occupied by an [Antiques Smuggler](Antiques_Smuggler.md) who will buy items for half the price of a standard antiquarian. Climbing above, you will find the [Temple of the Forgotten Flame](The_Temple_of_the_Forgotten_Flame.md)
