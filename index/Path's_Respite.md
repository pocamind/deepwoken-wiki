# Path's Respite

LocationL Path's Respite LocationR

  
  

**Path's Respite** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location found in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).

## Overview

Located in Lower Erisia consisting of 5 buildings, a job board, and a cave, with another room, an item locker and a way behind the mountain. The place used to be overun by bandits but now is only occupied by an [Antiques Smuggler](Antiques_Smuggler.md) who will buy items for half the price of a standard antiquarian
