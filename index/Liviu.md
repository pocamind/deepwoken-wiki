# Liviu

| Liviu | |
|---|---|
| name | Liviu |
| location | Greathive Aratel Library |

[Chrysid](Chrysid.md) NPC located in the Library at [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md).

Seems to be involved with world around them as they talk a lot about the "Acolyte" and how they're watched to at least "seem busy."

You will be unable to communicate properly to Liviu if you choose "So, do they really burn people over there?" option. It will be accompanied by Liviu saying, _"I don't want to talk to you anymore."_ Dialogue will then be replaced with, _"\*Even making eye contact with the Chrysid is downright unbearable. Best to think before you speak next time.\*"_
