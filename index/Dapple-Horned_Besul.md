## Description

Dapple-Horned Besul is a [Gremor](Gremor.md) NPC. She is the leader of the Navaen settlement at Meteor Isle.

## Location

Dapple-Horned Besul is found sitting on a rock with a blanket located at [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md).
