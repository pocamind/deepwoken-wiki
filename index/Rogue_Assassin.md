## Description

**Rogue Assassin** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode.

## Unique Feature

Rogue Assasin is high-skill required memento due to precise posture break moment catching for opportunity to land a backstab for ability to use **Swift Strike** critical to proc assasination talents. In exchange damage is highly rewardable allowing for you to oneshot regular mobs and deal **huge** damage to bosses
