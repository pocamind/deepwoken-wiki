## Description

The Justicar Defender Coat is an animated [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. It is rarely found in chests from [Aelita](Aelita.md), [King Threshers](King_Thresher.md), and [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md)'s seasonal chests.

## Trivia

-   The Justicar Defender Coat used to not have an item rarity for a long while upon release, which made it unsellable. This was fixed on August 25th, 2023.
