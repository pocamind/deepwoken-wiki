# Justicar Defender Coat

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

Justicar Defender Coats are ornate coats with an almost unrivaled level of Pips. It can be very rarely dropped from [King Threshers](Thresher.md#king-thresher), [Deep Owls](Deep_Owl.md) or [Aelita](Aelita.md) chests.
