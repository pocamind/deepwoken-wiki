## Description

The Justicar Defender is an equipment item in the arms category. The stats and Pips gained from equipping it have not been documented. It is rarely found in chests from [King Threshers](Thresher.md), [Aelita](Aelita.md), and [Deep Owls](Deep_Owl.md).
