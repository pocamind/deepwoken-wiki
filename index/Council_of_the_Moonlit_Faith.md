## Description

**Council of the Moonlit Faith** / **The Counsell**[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1) is the highest form of government in the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). They ensure all operations within the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md) run smoothly. They work directly under [the Lady Confessor](The_Lady_Confessor.md) and put all her decrees into action.[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1)[\[2\]](#cite-note-missives-2)

The Council consists of a handful of philosophical, theological, and academic members of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-glossary-1) All members of the Council are very religious and are apart of [The Moonlit Faith](The_Moonlit_Faith.md).[\[2\]](#cite-note-missives-2)

## Notable members

[The Lady Confessor](The_Lady_Confessor.md): The mysterious leader of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md), where her word is law. Not much is known externally of her location, or condition, but interactions with scholars visiting the [East](Luminants.md#etrean-and-eastern-luminants) suggest a sense of great reverence and sincerity towards her decrees.[\[3\]](#cite-note-glossary1-3)

The Acolyte / [Thal'assis Linnaes](<Thal'assis_Linnaes.md>): The current Acolyte of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). A lead ambassador, leading groups of scholars to and from the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) in order to exchange knowledge, updates and information.[\[2\]](#cite-note-missives-2)[\[4\]](#cite-note-glossary2-4)

The Acolyte / [Galvika](Galvika.md): One of the previous Acolytes of the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md).[\[2\]](#cite-note-missives-2)
