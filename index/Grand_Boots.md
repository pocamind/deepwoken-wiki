# Grand Boots

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

Grand Boots are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can only be found through chests in [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

Grand Boots are part of the "**Grand**" set; with the only other armor piece being the **Grand Pauldrons**.
