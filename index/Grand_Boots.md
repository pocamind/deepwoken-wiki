# Grand Boots

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

Grand Boots are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can only be found through chests in [The Depths](<https://ludi-1.thewart.repl.co/sw/hvtrs8%2F-lwdk-3.vhgwcrv.perl%2Ccm%2Fqw-https%3A%250F%2Fdeepwoken'2Afandom.com%250Fwiki%2FThe'5FDepths>).

Grand Boots are part of the "**Grand**" set; with the only other armor piece being the **Grand Pauldrons**.
