# Den Master

## The Den Master

The Den Master is a Felinor that sells Tier 1 Mantra Modifiers.

### Location

To get to the Den Master, you have to go to the Etris Island Docks and hug the right wall of the island. Once you make it past the tall stone peninsula, there is a small, sandy cove with a noticeable dent in the wall that can be destroyed with environment-changing mantras (Ice Smash, Thunder Beam, Shadow Meteors, etc.) with enough Elemental Intensity to enter.

-   The Den Masted sells Crystal Lens (Further mantra projectile range), Drift Shards (Faster mantra projectile speed), Cloudstones (Larger mantra size), and Amnesic Driftwood (Removes mantra modifiers).
