## Description

**Squelcap Pilaf** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Plant**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Carnivore](Talents.md#carnivore) Talent.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 45% Stomach and 45% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Full buff, affecting 35% of the Stomach bar. The buff reduces natural Stomach loss by 10%.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   3 [Latchgrain](Latchgrain.md) and 1 [Squelcap](Squelcap.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) [CraftingCampfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
