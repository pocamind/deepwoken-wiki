## Description

Frank F'alzone, is a [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC who was part of [Captain Orlandeau's](<Cap'n_Orlandeau.md>) crew in [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>), apparently he is the crew's cartographer, janitor and sniper according to his outfit.

## Dialogue

Talking to him:

Aye, 'appy to see ye, \[Name\]. I' eard Orlandeau hollerin' at ye from here! Did he teach ye well? Didn't kill yer confidence, aye?

1.  You could say that.
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

'Appy to hear, mate. I've been doin' what I do best 'round 'ere. \*The man proudly holds yp a mop, as if it were a trophy.\* This.. this here, my beauty, my star. With her, Oi'm the destroyer of all mold upon this deck.

1.  You've been swabbing?
2.  \[End Dialogue\]

Ye can bet yer soul I'll swab all there is to swab. They call me **The Swabber**, they does. I swab all things'at need swabbin'.

1.  \[End Dialogue\]
