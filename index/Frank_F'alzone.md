## Description

Frank F'alzone, is a [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC who was part of [Captain Orlandeau's](<Cap'n_Orlandeau.md>) crew in [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>). They were the crew's cartographer and janitor.
