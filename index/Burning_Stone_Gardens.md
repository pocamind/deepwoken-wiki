# Burning Stone Gardens

## Description

Template:Areainfobox A secluded place high up on the island where many golems and sharkos can be seen fighting (purple spiders can be seen as well). Visually, the gardens mountainous with large glowing trees that are unique to the region. They are not minable and change colors depending on the season. The rain and water in the gardens are poisonous and will harm you contact it (adding to the already tough challenge of fighting sharkos, spiders, and golems). There are a few abandoned structures and mines, however it is suggested to traverse along the walls and by gliders to avoid fighting monsters below.
