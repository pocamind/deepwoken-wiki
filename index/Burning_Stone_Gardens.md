# Burning Stone Gardens

## Description

Template:Areainfobox A secluded place high up on the island where many Golems and Sharkos can be seen fighting. Visually, the gardens mountainous, with large glowing trees that are unique to the region. They cannot be cut down, and change colors depending on the season. The rain and water in the gardens is poisonous, and will harm you if you contact it. There are a few abandoned structures and mines, however, it is suggested to traverse along the walls and by gliders to avoid fighting monsters below. Nearby, the entrance to [Lord Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>), and inside the mineshaft, there are frozen Golems that will fire cannons at spotted players. It takes 2-3 seconds to fire the lasers, which have the potential to 2 shot players, and will vaporize them if they health is low. You can prevent this by hiding behind walls to block the path of the laser.

  

  

Note: The mineshaft has many golem turrets and appears to be empty. However, there seems to be an unclickable button on a wall if you turn right at the first junction (2 turrets) and follow the hallway down to a stalactite. To your left should be 1 mori turret, and a path that curves right. After dodging the first mori turret, you will find the button but be directly infront of a second turret. You have several seconds to get out (idk if it is a button or what it does but do not have enough fortitude to check) (there might also be a doorway but didnt have time to check)
