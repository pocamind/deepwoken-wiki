# Burning Stone Gardens

## Description

Template:Areainfobox A secluded place high up on the island where many golems and sharkos can be seen fighting (purple spiders can be seen as well). Visually, the gardens mountainous with large glowing trees that are unique to the region. They are not minable and change colors depending on the season. The rain and water in the gardens are poisonous and will harm you contact it (adding to the already tough challenge of fighting sharkos, spiders, and golems). There are a few abandoned structures and mines, however it is suggested to traverse along the walls and by gliders to avoid fighting monsters below. Nearby the entrance to Lord Erisia's Manor and inside the mineshaft (pictured below) there are frozen golems that will fire cannons at spotted players. It takes 2-3 seconds to fire lazers that have the potential to 2 shot players and will vaporize them if they health is low. You can prevent this by hiding behind walls to block the path of the laser.

The mineshaft has many golem turrets and appears to be empty. However, there seems to be an unclickable button on a wall if you turn right at the first junction (2 turrets) and follow the hallway down to a stalactite. To your left should be 1 mori turret and a path that curves right. After dodging the first mori turret, you will find the button but be directly infront of a second turret. You have several seconds to get out (idk if it is a button or what it does but do not have enough fortitude to check) (there might also be a doorway but didnt have time to check)
