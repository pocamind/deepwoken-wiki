# Fishing

Welcome to FishWoken.

Fishing is an action undertaken in Deepwoken by purchasing a fishing rod or crafting one (2 sticks and 1 fiber at a crafting table). To fish you need to go to any body of water and throw out your fishing line, after awhile your character gets a bite and the camera locks. Your character will start leaning in one of three directions, left, back and right. To reel the fish in you need to hold the corresponding key (A for Left Leaning, S for Back Leaning, D for Right Leaning) While you are holding the correct key, you need to click your mouse. Spam clicking usually works if you're fast enough on the leaning swap.

You can get several items from fishing: Squid, Red Snapper, Sea Bass, Tuna, Seaweed, Urchin and Pufferfish. Tuna, Red Snappers and Sea bass give you "Fish Meat". This can be cooked, made into bait (Two in in crafting table) or it can be eaten raw, which WILL make you puke. Sea bass gives you one meat, Red snappers gives two meat and Tuna gives you three. Seaweed can be cooked aswell.

You can use just about any eatable item as bait, such as any mushroom, raw fish meat, fruit, tree sap, squids, etc, Excluding meals (Example being Cooked fish meat or Mushroom soup.) You do not have to bait your line, however you are very much less likely to get a bite.

In order to use bait you need to hold out your fishing rod then left click on the desired bait. If you catch a fish or get one on your line and do not catch them then you will lose your bait. If you reel in your line, or unequip your fishing pole then you will keep the bait.
