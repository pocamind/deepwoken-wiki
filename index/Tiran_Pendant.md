## Description

The Tiran Pendant is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Torso" category.

Equipping it grants the Talent "Featherfall" which grants Near-complete immunity to fall damage. After a certain amount of damage has been negated, the pendant will go on cooldown for 2 minutes.
