# Echoes of Bloodshed

LocationL Echoes of Bloodshed LocationR

When all is said and done, all that will be remembered of this battle are the steps you each took in the dance of bloodshed.

  

**Echoes of Bloodshed** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the 1v1 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**Echoes of Bloodshed** is a large, gray and white area with a circular arena in the middle. It resembles the [Fragments of Self](Fragments_of_Self.md) and plays the same music.

## Trivia

-   Notoriously the worst Chime of Conflict map due to issues surrounding players falling out of the arena during matches.
