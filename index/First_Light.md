# First Light

The First Light is one of the many heavy weapons you can obtain in the game. obtainable by killing a red sharko at songseeker wilds.

its called first light because of the red megalodaunt special flame ability

Keep in mind it isn't a guaranteed drop, it's speculated to be around the same rate as an Enforcer's Axe (50% chance)
