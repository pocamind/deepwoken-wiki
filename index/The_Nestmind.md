# The Nestmind

File:TheNestMind.png

  

## Description

The Nestmind is an NPC in the Conscious Intersection, the place you are teleported to after giving (Owl)Surge a Void Feather for the Visionshaper Oath. The Conscious Intersection is an area with a small parkour section, covered in white light.

## Dialogue

The **bold** text will be the player. The _italic_ text will be The Nestmind. Choose the **bold** dialogue options to get the Visionshaper Oath.

_Ah, the mouse seeks **covenant** with my kind._

**Where am I?**

_This is what you mice might call a **layer**... though your kind seems to misunderstand that which lies outside of its cage, thinking it ordered, **sequential**... We stand within a place that acts as an interstice between human consciousness. Where my kind make our nest..._

**Your kind?**

_Yes, we **Owls**... Or I suppose, that's just how we appear to your limited perceptive abilities... An insidious predator that **feasts** on the **meek**... And you have come here for **audience** with us..._

**But... I thought I was supposed to be hunting you?**

Things aren't always what they seem, little mouse... In time, you may pretend to be one of us, use abilities inherent to my kind... And you may do with these as you please, but are you prepared to pay the toll**?**

**The toll?**

_Your mind. We **Owls** find man to be capable of... suitably sophisticated thought, enough to **indulge** our palate... You will fill the minds of our prey with grand facades, ripen them for us. When your time comes to pass, you too will become a feast for the **Nest**..._

**I accept.**

_**Wonderful**. When you leave this, you will forget this pact, as have all before you. Though, you may continue to strengthen your bond using our **mark**. Mouse, **please do not disappoint**, as there is a limit to our patience._

_**You vow to see only that which you wish to see. Reality itself is malleable, pliable to your deft hands.**_
