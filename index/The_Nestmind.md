# The Nestmind

File:TheNestMind.png

_"Ah, the mouse seeks **covenant** with my kind._

_This is what you mice might call a **layer**... though your kind seems to misunderstand that which lies outside of its cage, thinking it ordered, **sequential**... We stand within a place that acts as an interstice between human consciousness. **Where my kind make our nest...**_

_Yes, we **Owls**... Or I suppose, that's just how we appear to your limited perceptive abilities... An insidious predator that **feasts** on the **meek**... And you have come here for **audience** with us..._

_Things aren't always what they seem, **little mouse**... In time, you may pretend to be one of us, use abilities inherent to my **kind**... And you may do with these as you please, but are you prepared to **pay the toll?**_

_Your mind._

_We **Owls** find man to be capable of... suitably sophisticated thought, enough to **indulge** our palate... You will fill the minds of our prey with grand facades, ripen them for us. When your time comes to pass, you too will become a feast for the **Nest**..._

**You vow to see only that which you wish to see. Reality itself is malleable, pliable to your deft hands.**

_**Wonderful**. When you leave this, you will forget this pact, as have all before you. Though, you may continue to strengthen your bond using our **mark**. Mouse, **please do not disappoint**, as there is a limit to our patience._

_"_

  

\-Repeated ramblings of a man found within the depths by the Ignition Union, before disappearing with one of the members.
