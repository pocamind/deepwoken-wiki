# Herbalist's Hat

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Herbalist's Hat is an Armor Equipment piece in the Helmet category. Equipping it grants you The Iron Gut and [Herbivore](Talents.md) talents, along with randomized stats based on the Pips you get. The Herbalist's Hat can only be found in Depths Chests.

## Trivia

-   The Herbalist's Hat and the Alchemist's Hat have a very similar design, with the only difference being the color of the string.
-   The Herbalist's Hat has a bigger version of itself, which is called the [Big Herbalist's Hat](<Big_Herbalist's_Hat.md>).
