## Description

The Evanspear War Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category, and is a part of the Evanspear War Set. The Evanspear War Plate is a green and yellow chest plate with matching bracers and an animated dark green cape.

This item is used to craft the [Reinforced War Plate](Reinforced_War_Plate.md) at the [Merit Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md).

## Trivia

-   The cape only has outward facing textures, allowing you to see through it if looking behind yourself.
