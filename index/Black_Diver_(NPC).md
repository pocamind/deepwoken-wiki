# Black Diver (NPC)

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Black Divers are a now removed NPC that acted as guards for [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md).

They used Markor's Inheritor, Pale Morning and the Forgotten Gladius

They were removed during the 1/16/2023 update.
