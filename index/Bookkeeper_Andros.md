## Overview

Bookkeeper Andros is an NPC located at the [Etris](Etris.md) library, near the inn. He offers lore related to the Old World, and provides information about [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md) in the quest to defeat [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).
