## Description

Shaman was an ancient deep-dwelling [Ganymede](Ganymede.md) Nomad, known for wielding [Umbrite](Ores.md#umbrite) through forbidden rites.[\[1\]](#cite-note-bow-1)

Through these taboo rituals, Shaman was able to bend Umbrite to their will, shaping it into tools that appeared almost alive.[\[1\]](#cite-note-bow-1) Among these creations was the [Shadebolt](Shadebolt.md), forged through their mastery of these rites.

While some came to know the figure simply as "Shaman," others never learned a name at all.[\[1\]](#cite-note-bow-1)
