# Duke Erisia's Laboratory

LocationL Duke Erisia's Laboratory LocationR

What was stolen from below lies within.

  

Duke Erisia's Laboratory consists of a small interlude hall where you fight [Prime Golems](Rock_Golem.md#golem-prime), [Brainsuckers](Brainsucker.md) and [Mindless Servants](Mindless_Servant.md) and the arena where the player fights [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md). You can enter this area by going through [Duke Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>) and descending down through the elevator.

When entering the arena section, you will see the Duke himself and several tubes with [Mindless Servants](Mindless_Servant.md) in them.

## Notes

-   The Golems and Brainsuckers respawn after a set amount of time, so it's best to just run past them instead of taking the time to kill them all.
