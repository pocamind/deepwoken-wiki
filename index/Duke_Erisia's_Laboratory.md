# Duke Erisia's Laboratory

Duke Erisia's laboratory is an area where you will fight duke. When entering you will see the duke himself and several tubes with Dukes minions in themin them.

When entering duke will give you a special dialogue afterwards you will start fighting duke. After a while the tubes break and the dukes minions start trying to help the Duke kill you. After killing the duke it will spawn a large chest with decent loot every once in a while.

## Trivia

**.**The Dukes minions are good health packs during the fight
