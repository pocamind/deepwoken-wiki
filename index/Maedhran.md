# Maedhran

Template:NPC name

Maedhran is an NPC found in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>). He will sell you [Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md) for each.

The mantra modifiers he sells are;

1.  Aeon Logstones
2.  Amnesic Driftwood
3.  Drift Shards
4.  Crystal Lens
5.  Cloudstones

## Dialogue

**Maedhran:** So there I was... Surrounded by the most terrifying warriors you've ever seen....

1.  **What?**
2.  \[Listen Intently\]

Hey. Don't interrupt me. _\*His expression momentarily flashes with tremendous coldness, like hell freezing over, but then switches back to his normal expression as if nothing had ever happened.\*_

1.  **Uh, sorry.**

I was poised like a tiger ready to strike... Just waiting for one of those lions to lift a paw.

1.  I'm sorry, what?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

They weren't actually lions. That's a metaphor. We skilled taleweavers use them to make you think things and then they're actually not those things... Cleaver I know.

1.  I'm sorry, what?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

I have my eyes on the leader, by the name of Ironjaw. They called him that because of his jaw... Was made of iron.

1.  What?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

That's not a metaphor. His jaw was actually replaced by Iron. Crazy, right?...

1.  What?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

I reached into my _Bag of Tricks_ to find something to use... And realized I had left them at my secret base...

1.  What are you on about?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

Where is my secret base, I hear you ask? Heh. If I told you, I'd have to kill you...

1.  I'm sorry, what?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

So don't ask. Because you're a good listener. And I'd have to kill you if I told you.

1.  What?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

So... Where was I? Ah, of course. The story that made me, Maedhran, a legend. That's Maedhran pronounced MAY-THRAN, by the way.

1.  Pardon?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

You probably already knew that, though. So anyway... I have my eye trained on their leader, Ironjaw, who we've already been over.

1.  Huh?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

I don't have my _Bag of Tricks_, so I have to use what my mama gave me. My fist. That's right, buddy. This is a martial arts story.

1.  What are you on about?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

I quickly run up the wall, and effortlessly deliver a quadruple backflip spin kick directly into Ironjaw's teeth.

1.  What?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

I hit the guy so hard in his face that old of the screws in his jaw came loose. It was awful. For him, that is. For me it was awesome.

1.  Huh
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

The rest of his warriors, seeing how many I destroyed him with a quadruple backflip spin kick to the jaw, all ran. Probably a good idea, because they didn't have an iron jaw to protect their faces.

1.  What are you on about?
2.  **\[Listen Intently\]**

So there ends just one of the tales of Maedhran the Strong... How'd you like it?

1.  **Uh...**

No, say no more. I know a true appreciator of a good story when I see one. And your one of them. **\*He winks so hard you think he might burst a blood vessel\***

1.  **\[End Dialogue\]**

Heh. Come for more of my tales?

1.  **No. Do you have anything for sale?**

You bet. How else do you think I became so strong? _\*He points his thumb towards his chest.\*_ Reading.

1.  Aeon Logstone
2.  Amnesic Driftwood
3.  Cloudstone
4.  Crystal Lens
5.  Drift Shard
