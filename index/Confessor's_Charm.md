## Description

The Confessor's Charm is a Torso [Equipment](Equipment.md) piece you can commonly find after defeating Chaser. Alternatively, you can farm Hell Mode for it, but it won't be as common. This is without a doubt, currently the best Torso piece. Unless you prefer Elemental Armor over Physical, then [Canticlysm Pendant](Canticlysm_Pendant.md) is for you.

## Trivia

-   Both Confessor's Charm & [Canticlysm Pendant](Canticlysm_Pendant.md) were added together a while after the release of Verse Two, they came out on 2/06/23. They were also added along with [Old Blood Earrings](Old_Blood_Earrings.md) & [Moonlit Earrings](Crescent_Earrings.md), Both Earrings also being one of the best Earring pieces in their own way.
