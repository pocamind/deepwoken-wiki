## Description

The Den Master is a [Canor](Canor.md) [NPC](NPC.md). He sells basic tier one and two [Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md).

## Location

The Den Master is located in the [Etrean Wilds](Etrean_Wilds.md), in a cave near a shore off the side of [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md).
