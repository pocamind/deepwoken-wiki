## Mime

Mime is an NPC found within a cave on [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). He appears around a lot of miner mudskippers disguised as barrels, which upon killing will allow you to speak with him. If you have 21+ points in [Charisma](Attributes.md), he will give you the mantra Disguise, which allows you to disguise yourself as a barrel.

| Name | Image | Card Description | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Disguise |

 | _"Take on an unimaginable disguise."_ | Disguise yourself as an unassuming object for a seemingly indefinite amount of time.

Applying Sparks to the mantra will alter what you diguise as, variations are listed ahead.

**Sparks**:

**Barrel**: Nothing

**Statue**: 1 Reversal

**Crate**: 1 Tornado

**Lantern**: 1 Blast

**Gobletto**: 1 Reversal, 1 Tornado

**Campfire**: 1 Tornado, 1 Blast

**Skull**: 1 Reversal, 1 Blast
