## Mime

Mime is an NPC found within a cave on [Songseeker Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md). He appears around a lot of miner mudskippers disguised as barrels, which upon killing will allow you to speak with him. If you have 25 points in [Charisma](Attributes.md), he will give you the mantra Disguise, which allows you to disguise yourself as a barrel.
