# Varicosa District

LocationL Varicosa District LocationR

TERRITORY OF celtor

It's sometimes mused that the greed, excess and petty politics of those in this district had already killed this city before it drowned.

  

**Varicosa District** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark in [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).

## Overview

Section of The City of the Drowned distinguished by its rocky yellow-topped buildings. Here, you can find the Shadow trainer and common places to barrel-up to enter the Cathedral of Interstice from above.

There is an NPC here that will give you a random item for a deepgem.
