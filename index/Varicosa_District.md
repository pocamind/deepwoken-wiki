# Varicosa District

LocationL Varicosa District LocationR

LAWLESS TERRITORY OF Lost Celtor

It's sometimes mused that the greed, excess, and petty politics of those in this district had already killed the city before it drowned.

  

**Varicosa District** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark in [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).

## Overview

The Varicosa District is a section of The City of the Drowned distinguished by bright paths and buildings with red roofs.

[Jeremiah](Jeremiah.md), an NPC that you can trade Deep Gems to for items, can be found here.

## Trivia

-   Due to the [Varicosan Finery](Varicosan_Finery.md) Outfit and the location flavour text, it can be assumed that this was the home to the upper class of Celtor.
