# Varicosa District

Template:Location2

**Varicosa District** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark in [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md).

## Overview

The Varicosa District is a section of The City of the Drowned distinguished by bright paths and buildings with red roofs.

[Jeremiah](Jeremiah.md), an NPC that you can trade Deep Gems to for items, can be found here.

## Trivia

-   Varicosa is a species of [sea slug](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phyllidia_varicosa).
-   Due to the [Varicosan Finery](Varicosan_Finery.md) Outfit and the location flavour text, it can be assumed that this was the home to the upper class of Celtor.
