## Description

**Flashing Thunder** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. Similarly to its precursor Sightless, it starts with the 'Blinded' talent and a Pathfinder Lantern. It starts with a unique talent, Gathering Storm, which stores damage you deal into orbiting lightning orbs around you, up to a max of 6. Sheathing your weapon will fire all lightning orbs at your opponent.
