# The Recollection

A set of simulations created by the Reclaimer of the Recollection to simulate past Calamities from eyewitnesses within the Circle of Honor. These eyewitnesses are the Ironsworn.

These simulations are possibly being aided by the Central Authority's Inquisition as their max rank being Handler are given control over mind veils, albeit limited to one or two persons.
