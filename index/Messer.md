### **Description**

  
The messer is a medium type weapon sword that can be obtained by buying it at Etris, or from chest in Lower erisia (bandit island) and Songseeker isles (this needs to be confirmed yet), this weapon has a base damage of 20 and has a requirement of 10 Medium weapons to be used, this weapon has no special effects or passives.

### Notes

-   if you are looking forward to buying this as your freshie weapon, keep in mind there are better alternatives depending for what you wanna use this for, for example in PVE i'd reccomend the [spear](Iron_Spear.md), which can be found in the same armory shop where you'd buy the messer. (messer is still better than the starting sword tho)
-   You can switch between stances (1 handed or 2 handed) by pressing the Y button on your keyboard, most medium weapons have this possibility.
