# Trident Spear

The Trident is a Medium Weapon which Requires 50 Medium to use.

You can either get it from [The Depths](The_Depths.md), [Summer isle](Summer_Isle.md), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), and [Nyanna](Nyanna.md)

-   Type: Spear

-   Damage: 19

-   Medium Weapons Scaling: 6

-   Weight: 5

-   Range: 9

-   Swing Speed: 0.68x
