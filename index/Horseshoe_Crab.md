# Horseshoe Crab

The Horseshoe Crab is an unused monster that only appears during the [Charisma](Attributes.md) unbound quest.

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Horseshoe Crabs are small, brown creatures with a spike protruding out of its head. They have a hard shell on top, with a mouth underneath, presumably used for slaughtering their foes.

## Moves

-   **Swarm**\- The Horseshoe Crabs come together and crawl all over their victim's body, tearing apart its flesh.

## Notes

-   Currently, these monsters have no other purpose than to attack Karliah during the [Charisma](Attributes.md) unbound quest.
