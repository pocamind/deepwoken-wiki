# Horseshoe Crab

_Also known as Karliah killers._

The Horseshoe Crab is an unnused monster that only appears during the [Charisma](Attributes.md) unbound quest.

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Horseshoe Crabs are small, brown creatures with a spike protruding out of it's head. They have a hard shell on top, with a mouth underneath, presumably used for slaughtering their foes.

  

## Moves

-   **Swarm** - The Horseshoe Crabs come together and crawl all over their victims body, tearing apart it's flesh.

  

## Notes

-   Currently, these monsters have no other purpose than to attack Karliah during the [Charisma](Attributes.md) unbound quest.
-   They can be located inside the Mudskipper Cave on [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).
