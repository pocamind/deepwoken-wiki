# Drallis Ehr

## Description

Drallis Ehr is the head of state of [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md). He is a man of mystery, even surpassing [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md) of [Etrea](Etrea.md) in that department, as none have even seen his face. The [Authority Helm](Authority_Helm.md) was specifically crafted to evoke the image of faceless warriors, thereby mirroring his unseen status.
