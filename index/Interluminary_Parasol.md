# Interluminary Parasol

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

The Interluminary Parasol is a giant floating creature resembling the gigameds in terms of physical shape/structure but with the exception of more fleshy and stony skin and Long tentacles.

## Attack moves

Using the floating pincers, it will dive towards to pierce its target

Summoning squids and broodlord (unconfirmed)
