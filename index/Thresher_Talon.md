## Description

A Thresher Talon is an item only dropped by [Threshers](Thresher.md) which can be found on [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md) and in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md). It's purpose is to serve as a material to obtain the Burrow mantra from [Carrion](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md).
