# Caeranthil

_This page is for Caeranthil, the island ravaged by the [Doom of Caeranthil](Doom_of_Caeranthil.md) boss._

**Content is not present in-game**

## Description

Caeranthil was an island, destroyed by the [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md), a colossal sea monster.[\[1\]](#cite-note-spine-1)

Not much is known about the island, the only known information that it was from the Southern Luminant.[\[1\]](#cite-note-spine-1)
