# Attunements

In Deepwoken, every player has a certain element they are attuned to.

## Overview

There are four base elements that anyone may select when first creating a character. Picking an element will disallow you from starting with one of the other three elements until you unlock a different one while playing. You can stick to one element or you may have multiple elements, but having multiple elements will be less effective as a cost. As you increase your Power, you will be able to gain Mantras for your element. Mantras are fighting abilities you learn that serve as offensive spells.

While it is possible to learn every or most mantras of a given attunement, you are limited to the amount you can use. By default, players are given 3 combat mantras slots, 1 mobility mantra slot, 1 support mantra slot and 1 wildcard (any type) mantra slot. Extra mantras will be stored in the inventory under their name in the pink item category ([mantras modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md)). Those limits can be increase via [Oaths](Oaths.md).

Other than the base elements there are other unlockable elements that can be taught by their respective trainers such as shadowcast.

At the Character Selection you may choose no Attunement, which will start the player with no element, though elements can be aquried through their respective trainers.

In order to gain every Attunement in the game, after learning three Attunements you must be given the other respective Attunement items by other players (Shadowblood, Glassblood, etc).
