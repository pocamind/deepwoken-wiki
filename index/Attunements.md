# Attunements

In Deepwoken, every player has a certain element they are attuned to.

## Overview

There are 4 base elements. You can stick to one element or you may have multiple elements, but having multiple elements will be less effective as a cost. As you increase your Power, you will be able to gain Mantras for your element. Mantras are fighting abilities you learn that serve as offensive spells.

At the Character Selection, you may choose no Attunement, which could mean you can learn an element that is not one of the four basic elements as you progress your character, possibly an element like Shadow.
