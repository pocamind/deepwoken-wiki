## Description

Cheruba is a [Capra](Capra.md) NPC. They are a vendor who sells starter equipment aimed towards new and early game players.

## Location

Cheruba is located at the Navaen Nomad camp at [Bluster Rapids](Bluster_Rapids.md), near [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md).
