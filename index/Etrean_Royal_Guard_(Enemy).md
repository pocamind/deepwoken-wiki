# Etrean Royal Guard (Enemy)

Template:Creature Info Box v2

## Description

A Palace Guard is a Type of enemie NPC that wields a Katana and can be found inside and outside the Palace at [Etris](Etris.md). Attacking them or going near them without enough reputation brings you the same fate as attacking the Fort Merit guards. Also, if you have enough [negative reputation](Reputation.md) with [Etris](Etris.md) they will personally come out from the Palace and attack you.
