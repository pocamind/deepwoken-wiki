# Serena

File:Screenshot 16.png

Serena, waiting for a Pathfinder to exploit their kindess.

Serena is a sphinx Felinor that can randomly spawn around Starswept Valley. When talked to, she asks for water. If you have a canteen with water, you can give her a sip, from here, two things can happen:

-   She thanks the player and reward them with a chest
-   She attacks the player

When knocked and gripping, she'll also _kindly_ ask you if she "is convincing"
