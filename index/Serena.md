# Serena

File:Screenshot 16.png

Serena, waiting for a Pathfinder to exploit their kindess.

Serena is a sphinx Felinor that can randomly spawn around Starswept Valley. When talked to, she asks for a canteen. If you do give her the canteen, she will consume 1 use of it and start attacking you, exclaiming "GOTCHA!" in all caps.

When knocked and gripping, she'll also _kindly_ ask you if she "is convincing"
