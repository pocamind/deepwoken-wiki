## Description

Apprentice Kj is a student taught under [Evengarde Rest](Evengarde_Rest.md) at [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md).

He gives the quest "Vigil's Savior" that tasks the player to find his brother, [Vigil Isaac](Vigil_Isaac.md), in a ruined house in [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md). The quest rewards the player with a [Curiosity](Quests.md) [Talent](Talents.md) hand and an audience with [Evengarde Rest](Evengarde_Rest.md), allowing the player to uncap their weapon and as a requirement to fight [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md).

Apprentice Kj wears the [Cloak of Winds](Cloak_of_Winds.md) outfit along with a [White Deepwoken Cloak](Deepwoken_Cloak.md).

## Location

Apprentice Kj is located inside the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md), sitting in a corner of a room.
