## Description

Apprentice Kj is a student along with his brother [Vigil Isaac](Vigil_Isaac.md), having [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) as their main teacher. He can be found sitting in a room inside of the [Temple of the Blade](Temple_of_the_Blade.md).

Completing his quest grants you a [Compassion](Quests.md) Talent hand as well as an audience with [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md), allowing you to uncap your weapon and spar with him.
