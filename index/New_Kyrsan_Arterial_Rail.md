# New Kyrsan Arterial Rail

LocationL Kyrsan Railways LocationR

  
  

**Kyrsan Railways** is a 1v1 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) arena that is based off of [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). It consists of two large trains, with a broken down train car in the middle.

Between the two is a large open area for combat, with the high walls of the trains making it difficult to escape and the broken train being a great spot for high ground.

## Trivia

-   Every train is breakable through basic attacks.
