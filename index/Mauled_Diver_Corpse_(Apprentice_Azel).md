## Description

Mauled Diver Corpse is the lifeless body of [Azel Corbet](<Mauled_Diver_Corpse_(Apprentice_Azel).md>). The player is able to pocket the journal left behind by the corpse and turn it in to [Klaris Llfiend](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) for the [Deepshore Fossil](Deepshore_Fossil.md).
