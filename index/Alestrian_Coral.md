## Description

Alestrian Coral is an [Ingredient](Ingredients.md) found in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md), in the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md). It is pink with brown-ish spots. Its most notable use is in the creation of Speed [Potions](Alchemy.md), but it also has the highest negative posture damage effect. It is the [First Layer](First_Layer.md)'s counterpart to [Galewax](Galewax.md), although it can be taken outside of [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

## Trivia

-   Alestrian Coral is basically a useless material, unless you are specifically looking to create speed potions.
-   The shape of the coral resembles trees in [The Depths](The_Depths.md).
