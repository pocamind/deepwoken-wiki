## Description

The Legion Shield is a [side weapon](Weapons.md) in the Shield category, providing +8 Maximum Posture when equipped.

## Trivia

-   The practions of decorating Legion Shields with a face may reference the [aegis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/aegis), a weapon wielded by [Athena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Athena) and is usually depicted as a shield, often with the head of [Gorgons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorgons).
-   Prior to Verse 2, the Legion Shield was the best shield in the game. This title was later replaced by [The Path's Defender](<The_Path's_Defender.md>) following the update.
-   In the update on 2/16/26, the name of this shield was changed from the Khan Shield to the Legion Shield.
