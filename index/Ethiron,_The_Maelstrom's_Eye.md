# Ethiron, The Maelstrom's Eye

_Not to be confused with the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md), the boss of Layer 2, Floor 2._

“ YOU HAVE DONE US A GREAT SERVICE LITTLE GODSEEKER. YOUR SCREAMS WILL ECHO ACROSS EONS. AN ETERNITY OF SUFFERING AWAITS YOU IN THE AGONY OF THE GALE. ”

― Voice of Ethiron

**Ethiron, the Maelstrom's Eye,** also known as the Eye of Calamity, is a [Drowned God](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Drowned_Gods), sleeping deep within the Second Layer.

## Description

Little is known about Ethiron other than that they are the Drowned God of wind, pressure and sky. [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) describes [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) as like a bedchamber to Ethiron, with the [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md) working hard to keep them asleep. Ethiron seemingly emits a fog known as Mist Presence, which eventually brainwashed them and the [Lost Divers](Lost_Diver.md) into worshipping them and devoting themselves to helping the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) grow.

Ethiron is one of the most powerful beings in Deepwoken due to being a Drowned God, the phenomena in Layer 2 showing some of their tremendous powers despite being in a state of "sleep". Their powers appear to be total control of the Eternal Gale and any forms of gale, mind control, specially in Floor 2 showing how the Mist Presence produced by Ethiron controls the Kyrsgarde, the Lost Divers and the ability of time manipulation. This is less so an actual ability so much as a consequence of Ethiron's winds, so strong as to push time itself.

Ethiron has a personal domain of their own in the depths being the Second Layer. Despite having a lot of control in the Second Layer, showing how in the first floor, [The Ethironal Shrine](Second_Layer/The_Ethironal_Shrine.md), is constantly showing its effects, some other powers of Ethiron have less effects in certain parts of Layer 2, for example the time dilatation effects can only be seen in Floor 2 and in the [Cathedral of Ethiron](Cathedral_of_Ethiron.md) in the Second Floor in the weakest way. This can probably mean that some parts of Layer 2 he has less control over, for example the Ignition Union base and the multiple safepoints in Layer 2 are unaffected by Ethiron's powers, at least in their weakened state.
