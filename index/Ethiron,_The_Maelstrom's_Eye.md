# Ethiron, The Maelstrom's Eye

_Not to be confused with the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md), the boss of Layer 2, Floor 2._

“ YOU HAVE DONE US A GREAT SERVICE LITTLE GODSEEKER. YOUR SCREAMS WILL ECHO ACROSS EONS. AN ETERNITY OF SUFFERING AWAITS YOU IN THE AGONY OF THE GALE. ”

― Voice of Ethiron

**Ethiron, the Eye of Calamity,** is a Drowned God, sleeping deep within the Second Layer.

## Description

Little is known about Ethiron other than that they are the Drowned God of wind, pressure and sky. [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) describes [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) as like a bedchamber to Ethiron, with the [Kyrsgarde](Kyrsgarde.md) working hard to keep them asleep. Ethiron seemingly emits a fog known as Mist Presence, which eventually brainwashed them and the [Lost Divers](Lost_Diver.md) into worshipping them and devoting themselves to helping the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) grow.

Ethiron is one of the most powerful beings in Deepwoken due to being a Drowned God, the phenomena in Layer 2 showing some of their tremendous powers despite being in a state of "sleep". Their powers appear to be total control of the Eternal Gale and any forms of gale, mind control, specially in Floor 2 showing how the Mist Presence produced by Ethiron controls the Krysgarde, the Lost Divers and the ability of time manipulation. This is unknown to be intentional or not, but is certain that Ethiron can control how time flows around their domains and near their being.

Ethiron has a personal domain of their own in the depths being the Second Layer. Despite having a lot of control in the Second Layer, showing how in the first floor, the Eternal Gale, is constantly showing its effects, some other powers of Ethiron have less effects in certain parts of Layer 2, for example the time dilatation effects can only be seen in Floor 2 and in the Cathedral of Ethiron in the Second Floor in the weakest way. This can probably mean that some parts of Layer 2 he has less control over, for example the Ignition Union base and the multiple safepoints in Layer 2 are unaffected by Ethiron's powers, at least in their weakened state.
