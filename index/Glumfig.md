# Glumfig

“ What in Alsin's name is a Glumfig.. ”

― A Puzzled Bandit

  
Template:Food Template V1

## Description

A small fruit that originated from the Western Luminant. This fruit is exclusively sold by [Trahne Zakshun](Traan_Zakshun.md) for in his black market or in his regular shop rotation.

## Recipes used in

-   [Candied Fruit](Food.md) (Any fruit + Sap)

## Trivia

-   Glumfigs were first mentioned ingame from an NPC dialogue exchange. Nearly a month after this dialogue exchange was added, the Glumfig was officially added to the game.
-   Upon release, the Glumfig had the most potent instant health effect for potion effects, healing ~20 health per fig. This was so potent in fact that it created its own potion type, the _Rejuvenating **Remedy**_. This of course was a bug and was patched shortly afterwards.
-   Glumfigs were originally sold in [Trahne Zakshun's](Traan_Zakshun.md) normal shop rotation for a piece. They were later transferred to his black market shop rotation, being sold for a piece. After Glumfigs were nerfed, they were then transferred back to his regular rotation, being sold for a piece. Quite the price inflation.
