# Glumfig

“ What in Alsin's name is a Glumfig.. ”

― A Puzzled Bandit

  
Template:Food Template V1

## Description

A small fruit that originated from the Western Luminant. This fruit is exclusively sold by [Traan Zakshun](Traan_Zakshun.md) for in his black market or in his regular shop rotation.

## Recipes used in

-   [Candied Fruit](Food.md) (Any fruit + Sap)

## Trivia

-   Glumfigs were first mentioned ingame from an NPC dialogue exchange. Nearly a month after this dialogue exchange was added, the Glumfig was officially added to the game.
-   Upon release, the Glumfig had the most potent instant health effect for potion effects, healing ~20 health per fig. This was so potent in fact that it created its own potion type, the _Rejuvenating **Remedy**_. This of course was a bug and was patched shortly afterwards.
-   Glumfigs have had an interesting track record for obtainment price. Originally sold by [Traan Zakshun](Traan_Zakshun.md) for in the regular shop rotation, they were later transferred to his black market and were sold for. After some time and a nerf to Glumfigs potion effectiveness, they were re-added to his normal shop rotation, but 500 Notes more expensive, being sold for each. Quite the price inflation.
    -   The above point is subject to change as Traan edits their pricing for Deepwoken's rampant inflation. As the pricing of Glumfigs continue to rise, they will not be mentioned in this trivia note each time. Please keep this in mind.
