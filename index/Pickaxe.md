# Pickaxe

| Pickaxe | |
|---|---|
| usage | To Harvest Ores/Minerals. |
| recipe | (2) Iron, (2) Wood |

## Description

Pickaxes are used for mining [ores/minerals](Ores.md) such as rock, iron, irithine, etc.

## Locations

-   The Nautilodaunt spawn in [The First Layer](First_Layer.md).
    -   Costs **5 notes**.
-   The General Store located in [Etris](Etris.md).
    -   Costs **3 notes**.

-   Next to the blacksmith in [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md).
    -   Costs **3 notes**.

-   Next to the innkeeper on [Isle Of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md).
    -   Costs **3 notes**.

-   Bandit Camp in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md).
    -   The cost is free

-   In a cart just outside the docks of [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md).
    -   Costs **3 notes.**

-   On a crate inside the pub in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).
    -   Costs **3 notes**.

## Trivia

-   A pickaxe can also be crafted at a [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md) using (2) Iron and (2) Wood.
