## Description

**Lavasuckers** are counterparts [**Brainsuckers**](Brainsucker.md), only found in [Saramaed Summit (Vow of Iron)](<Saramaed_Summit_(Vow_of_Iron).md>). They share an appearance with some differences: Lavasuckers appear a bit darker than Brainsuckers and have flames streaming from their heads.

## Tips

-   Their attacks, particularly Bite, have very strange hitboxes, making parrying them difficult. This, combined with its low damage, makes it more worthwhile to simply mash your basic attack until it dies.
