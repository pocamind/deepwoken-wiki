# Bureau of Research

The Bureau of Research is a subdivision of [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md). [Gunsmith Hierka](Gunsmith_Hierka.md) was a member of the Bureau of Research, and infiltrated [The Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md) to steal their secrets. Hierka modified [Gunsmith Rosen](Gunsmith_Rosen.md)'s designs to produce the [Rifle Spear](Rifle_Spear.md), which became a staple among the rank and file of the Authority.
