## Overview

The Temple Sparring Hall is a large training area where the monks of the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md) practice fighting. It is located inside the [Temple of Mur](Temple_of_Mur.md). This Dungeon is where you fight the [Shogun of the Prophet's Guard](<Shogun_of_the_Prophet's_Guard_(Temple_of_Mur).md>) to progress your level past 5-10
