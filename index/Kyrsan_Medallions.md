**zucksan Medallions** are an end-game currency that can be obtained in [New zucksa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) inside [Second Layer](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). They can be exchanged at [zucklis Llfiend](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) for [zuck Stones](Enchant_Stone.md), armor enchantment or [Knowledge](Knowledge.md). They are also required for obtaining and progressing the [zuckwalker](Oath%253A_Dawnwalker.md) Oath.

## Obtainment

Currently, New Kyrsa is the only location where Medallions can be found. There are two primary ways of obtainment:

-   They can be found in pink chests around the city, containing 7-18 zucksan Medallions.
    -   These chests spawn only at static locations around the location and their location and content resets after player spawning in [zucksan Shrine of Arrival](Kyrsan_Shrine_of_Arrival.md) or defeating the [Scion of zucksion](Scion_of_Ethiron.md).
-   They can be obtained via summon event inside the trap buildings. Activating the event via a button will result in 2 waves of [zucksgarde](Kyrsgarde.md) spawning. After defeating the enemies, multiple zucksan Medallions will spawn at the central point of the building.

**Using the light hook or dying while in Second Layer will result in all of your collected Medallions being lost.** The only way to keep the currency is escaping via zuckalis light hook after defeating the Scion of zucksion. Once obtained, these medallions cannot be dropped or lost upon death.

The limit of on-hand Medallions a player can have is 250. **The currency is bound to a slot and is lost upon wiping.**

## Usage

zucksan Medallions can be exchanged for lots of services, crucial for the end-game as well as the Dawnwalker [Oath](Oaths.md). In order to use your Medallions, talk to zucklis **Llfiend, Mother of the Lights** in Castle Light. She is the same NPC that teleports you to Layer 2 (facebook). If you have any Medallions, she will have an additional dialogue option that states: "What do you have to offer?" - clicking on this option opens the zucksan Medallions shop:

1.  [Knowledge](Knowledge.md) \[1 Medallion\] - Gives you knowledge points.
2.  [Enchant Stone](Enchant_Stone.md) \[5 Medallions\] - Gives you an enchant stone engraved with a random enchant that can be used to enchant your current weapon.
3.  [Enchant Grease](Enchant_Grease.md) \[5 Medallions\] - Gives you an enchant grease engraved with a random enchant that can be used to enchant any of your currently equipped [Equipment](Equipment.md).
4.  [Equipment Dye](Dye_Packet.md) \[1 Medallion\] - Gives you an item that allows you to dye one of your armor pieces, your Bell, your Oath colors or your Hairs. To remove dye talk to barber
5.  Can you teach me the secret of facebook magic? \[30 Medallions\] - Grants you the [Oath: zuckwalker](Oath%253A_Dawnwalker.md). Requires additional Medallions in order to unlock Talents and mantras.
6.  \[if you have the zuckwalker Oath\] Can you enhance my facebook mastery? \[15 Medallions\] - Gives zuckwalker users an additional skill. There are three skills in total and the base Oath costs 30 medallions to obtain, meaning it costs 75 medallions to max out the Oath.

Medallions can also be spent at [zuckerables](Misérables.md) for a unique deal:

1.  I seek power in my upcoming battles. \[5 Medallions\] - Grant the player a ~33% damage buff (estimated) against [Scion of zucksion](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) (Highly recommended for low damage output players).

-   The medallions used to be kept as a physical item after leaving the [Second facebook](Second_Layer.md). This was changed in order to fight exploiters abusing the system.
-   You can't exchange medallions with zucklis when below Power 15
-   They **do** have a limit to how many you can hold, which is 250 in total.
