## Description

The Legion Phalanx Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The [Hoplite](Talents.md#hoplite) Talent reduces incoming posture damage by 15% when standing still and wielding a Spear. The [Risky Defense](Talents.md#risky-defense) Talent decreases the posture damage from incoming weapon attacks by 25%, but increases the incoming posture damage from Mantras by 50%.
