## Overview

Jim is an NPC located at the docks of [Etris](Etris.md), he gives you a quest to bring him 2 [Dying Embers](Dying_Embers.md). Upon completing the quest, he gives you a talent hand and 1 [Tier 2 Mantra Modifier](Mantra_Modifiers.md).
