# The Condemned Archives

LocationL The Condemned Archives LocationR

A forbidden store of Old World texts once deemed to be too dangerous for common consumption, now laid bare for profiteers.

  
**The Condemned Archives** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md).

## Description

**The Condemned Archives** is a large library containing texts of the Old World.

## Trivia

-   Many of the bookshelves are breakable with basic attacks.
