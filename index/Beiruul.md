## Description

Beiruul is a Redwood [Gremor](Gremor.md) NPC, a resident of [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md), she wants to take revenge on the Central Authority for the constant harassment they enact and the looming threat they pose to her people; choosing the correct dialogue responses will begin the [Navae's Retribution](Quests.md) sidequest.

During the Navae's Retribution quest, she will fight alongside the player to defeat several [Authority Peacekeepers](Authority_Peacekeeper.md) and [Authority Officers](Authority_Officer.md).

## Location

Beiruul is located at [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md) sitting under a tree above the docks, overlooking the ocean towards [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).

## Quest Details

Completing her sidequest will reward you with EXP, positive [The Children of Navae](The_Children_of_Navae.md) reputation, two [Knowledge](Knowledge.md), Bravery, and the [Beiruul's Vengence](Talents.md#beiruuls-vengence) Talent, granting a 5% damage buff against NPCs from [factions](Reputation.md) that hate you.

1.  Head over to the side of Fort Merit nearest to Beiruul.
2.  Climb up Fort Merit with the elevator or the air vents.
3.  Once having climbed up, Beiruul will spawn alongside several Authority Peacekeepers and Authority Officers.
4.  Kill all of the Authority Peacekeepers and Authority Officers.
5.  Return to Beiruul and interact with her.
