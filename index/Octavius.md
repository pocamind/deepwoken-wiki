## Description

Octavius, alongside [Aladrian](Aladrian.md), is the father of [Warden Jericho](Warden_Jericho.md). Octavius previously held the rank of [Inquisitor Maximus](Inquisitor_Maximus.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-jerich-1)

## Trivia

-   Octavius' name and title was inspired by the heavy usage of Latin by the Imperium of Man in _[Warhammer 40,000](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warhammer_40,000)_.
    -   Ironically, not only is Inquisitor Maximus not a rank held by the Imperium's Inquisitors, the Inquisitors of the Imperium do not use Latin-based rank names.
