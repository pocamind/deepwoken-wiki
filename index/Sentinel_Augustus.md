## Description

Sentinel Augustus is a [Adret](Adret.md) NPC, he is part of the Shock Trooper division for [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md).

He wields the [Duskguard Axe](Duskguard_Axe.md).

## Location

Sentinel Augustus is located on a balcony along-side [Chud Steel](Chud_Steel.md) at the large castle in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).
