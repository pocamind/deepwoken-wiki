# Temple Sparring Hall (Dungeon)

LocationL Temple Sparring Hall LocationR

TERRITORY OF Etrea \[LAWLESS\]

A solemn sanctuary carved in stone, where warriors hone the dancing steps of war.

  

## Overview

Temple Sparring Hall is a dungeon that can be reached from the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). It is an enormous well lit hall, made in a chinese style, surrounded by stone statues and with the lanterns on the roof.

-   This is a place, where a player fights with the [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md). You can reach it by completing certain actions and then talking to the Maestro. Upon entering the dungeon, talk to the Evengarde Rest and the fight will start. Once you do it, you can't leave this place until you defeat him or die. More information see on his boss page.

## Potential enemies

## Trivia/Notes
