# Ethironal Shrine Outskirts

LocationL ethironal shrine outskirts LocationR

You awake to a land covered in frost, the wind piercing your ears like the cries of lost souls.

  

## Contents

Template:AreainfoboxEthironal Shrine Outskirts is a small place in Second Layer of The Depths, where every person wakes up when he gets here through Castle Light or void. It's a cold place, full of Eternal Gale parasites. If you will stay here for long, parasites will instantly kill you. It takes around 7 minutes, before you'll die from them. In order to leave this place and get further, you have to pick up key and open gate, that leads to Sanctuary Gatehouse. From Sanctuary Gatehouse you'll reach Ethironal Shrine Sanctuary.

## Trivia

-   This is a starting Second Layer place, means you will be here everytime you enter Second Layer. This place is also affected by Space-time collapse.
