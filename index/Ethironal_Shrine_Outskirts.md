# Ethironal Shrine Outskirts

LocationL Ethironal Shrine Outskirts LocationR

You awake to a land covered in frost, the wind piercing your ears like the cries of lost souls.

  

## Contents

The Ethironal Shrine Outskirts is a landmark in [The Second Layer of The Depths](Second_Layer/The_Ethironal_Shrine.md).

This is the landing location of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). You can arrive here through speaking to [Klaris](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) and entering the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md) or jumping into the void in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) while above a health threshold. The Ethironal Shrine Outskirts is a cold place, full of Eternal Gale parasites.

If you stay here for too long, parasites in the form of snowballs will latch onto you, it will take a few minutes before said parasites will pop out and kill you instantly.

In order to leave this area, you have to pick up light key and open a gate. That gate leads to the [Sanctuary Gatehouse](Sanctuary_Gatehouse.md). From the Sanctuary Gatehouse you'll reach the [Ethironal Shrine Sanctuary](Ethironal_Shrine_Sanctuary.md).

## Objectives

-   Obtain the Key
-   Open the gate with the key

## Trivia

-   The Ethironal Shrine is named after the [drowned god Ethiron](<Ethiron,_The_Maelstrom's_Eye.md>), who oversees the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md)
