## Description

**Wheat** is an [Ingredient](Ingredients.md) in Deepwoken. It can be found most commonly at the [Etrean Wilds](Etrean_Wilds.md), and various parts of [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). It can be used to to make a large variety of [Foods](Food.md), And used to make various Potions.

For more information, read [Alchemy](Alchemy.md) and [Ingredients](Ingredients.md).

## Recipes

-   Bread - 3 Gathered Wheat
-   Sticky Bun - 1 Sap, 3 Gathered Wheat
-   Noodles - 1 Egg, 2 Gathered Wheat
-   Fruit Cake - 2 different Fruits, 1 Gathered Wheat
-   Plumfruit Muffin - 1 Plumfruit, 1 Gathered Wheat
-   Cake - 1 Egg, 3 Gathered Wheat, 1 Sap
