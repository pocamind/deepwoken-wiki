# Shackled Shank

**Content is not present in-game**  
The subject of this page is mentioned in-game, but is not tangibly implemented into the game.  
E.G. a character mentioned in a book doesn't exist as an interactible NPC.

The Shackled Shank are a smuggling ring that has had frequent encounters with [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md). During [Operation 'Distant Light'](<Operation_'Distant_Light'.md>), the CASS 'Shallow Grave' came across a Shackled Shank contraband outpost near the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). The vessel opened fire on the outpost, obliterating it and killing its defenders.[\[1\]](#cite-note-opdlr-1)
