# Shackled Shank

The Shackled Shank are a smuggling ring that has had frequent encounters with [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md). During [Operation 'Distant Light'](<Operation_'Distant_Light'.md>), the CASS _Shallow Grave_ came across a Shackled Shank contraband outpost near the [Lullaby Isles](Lullaby_Isles.md). The vessel opened fire on the outpost, obliterating it and killing its defenders.
