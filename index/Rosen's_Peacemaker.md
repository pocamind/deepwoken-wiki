## Description

Rosen's Peacemaker is an upcoming Medium Weapon for the Layer/Verse 2 major content update. Little is known about the Rifle weapon type outside of the fact that it's a gun that scales off of the Medium Weapons attribute instead of Light Weapons, boasting a polearm-like stance and higher weight than the Gun type, but slower firing speed.
