## Description

Well-tended Gravestone is an "NPC" that is part of a quest, it's the gravestone of [Iris'](Iris.md) brother.

## Location

Well-tended Gravestone is located in [The Etrean Sea](The_Etrean_Sea.md), on a small island behind [Ishmael's](Ishmael.md) fisher hut.
