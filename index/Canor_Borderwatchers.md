## Description

The Canor Borderwatchers popularized the use of the [Zweihander](Zweihander.md)[\[1\]](#cite-note-1). Among their weapons was the [Bloodbane](Bloodbane.md). A blood-soaked heirloom passed down through their leaders for generations, said to carry a heartbeat within its steel.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2)
