## Description

The Pathfinder is the protagonist of _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)'s_ [Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) gamemode. While the Pathfinder can choose to be non-aligned in their journey, they can also choose the multitude of [origins](Origins.md) which can provide them certain bonuses.

## Trivia

-   Technically, the Pathfinder cant wield certain legendary weapons, as they are already wielded by certain [NPCs](NPCs.md) in-game.
