## Description

The Pathfinder is the protagonist of _[Deepwoken](<Deepwoken_(game).md>)'s_ [Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) gamemode. While The Pathfinder can choose to be non-aligned in their journey, they can also choose the multitude of [origins](Origins.md) which can provide The Pathfinder certain bonuses.

While their seems to be no definitive goal for The Pathfinder, the consensus objective is to obtain [Echoes](Echoes.md) which can be used to provide upgrades for The Pathfinder beyond death on all [character slots](Character_Slots.md).
