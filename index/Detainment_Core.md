# Detainment Core

A dungeon in Fort Merit, you can enter without any requirements.

In the Hundred Legion Camp in Lower Erisia you can talk to [Caitus](Caitus.md) and receive the "[Legion Intelligence](Legion_Intelligence.md)" quest item. When you complete the quest you can receive [Imperium Kata](Imperium_Kata.md#critical)

Override Key is obtained from the Warden NPC in the Room before the laser section. Master Key is found from the NPC at the very end of the prison, between lasers and after the large bridge section\\
