## Description

The **Megalodaunt Coral** is used to gain the [Coral Spear](Monster_Mantras.md#coral-spear) [Monster Mantra](Monster_Mantras.md) from [Carrion, Father of Evolution](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md) in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) after he has been interacted with in [Floor 2](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). It is obtained as rare drop from any [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) variant besides the [Mechalodaunt](Mechalodaunt.md). It cannot be dropped alongside every other rare monster part.
