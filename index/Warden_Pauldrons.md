## Description

The Warden Pauldrons are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping them grants you 10 Health, 2 Posture, and 2 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip). They are rarely found in chests from [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) and the [Overrun Authority Stronghold sea event](Random_Encounters.md)
