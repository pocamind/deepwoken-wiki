# Relics

**Relics** are the either the creations of Drowned Gods, or naturally occurring anomalous objects found within the depths.

| Media | Description |
| --- | --- |

File:Screenshot 2023-05-23 170241.png

The Forge of Sin

 | **The Forge of Sin** - An undying purple flame that can be collected around the Etrean Luminant and given to bankers in [Etris](Etris.md) or [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) for 800 notes and around 10-12 low tier items.

**Lore**: A relic created by the Drowned God [Korilfiend](<Korilfiend,_the_Inferno's_Provenance.md>) and sought after by Etris' temporary king, [Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md). It is currently in the possession of [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) and is theorized to have been used by the Ministry for the Great Drowning. (Read [Lore](Lore.md) 11.4)

 | **Rain Spire** - A relic that can be found from Layer 2 and chime that has the ability to summon rain for 180 seconds in a certain radius. This can only be used once every hour. Prior to the 5th of June, this could only be used once before it was removed from your inventory.

Lore: No real lore right now, but is theorized that it could have been created by the Drowned God of Layer 2, [Ethiron](<Ethiron,_The_Maelstrom's_Eye.md>).

 |
| 

 | **Coral Throne** - A relic found in the First Layer by [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md). Has the ability to buff the creature sitting on it by around 20+ in-game levels. It is currently in the possession of [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md) and is the reason he is able to cast a mental veil around the entirety of Etris. It is theorized to have been created by the Drowned God [Yun'Shul](<Yun'Shul,_Keeper_of_Hearts.md>), due to having coral surrounding it which is only accessible in the First Layer. |
| 

 | **The Queen's Blade** - An sword relic currently in use by [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md). It was used in the assassination of the True King of Etrea, causing his soul to get sent into the depths. As long as the sword stays in his body, his soul will be trapped in the depths. |
