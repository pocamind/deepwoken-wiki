# Deepwoken (manga)

_**Deepwoken**_ is a one-shot manga written by Keiichi Tsuha and illustrated by Akeji Fujimura. It was published as part of a collection of short stories serving as adaptations of _Roblox_ games. The collection was distributed during the Roblox Developers Conference of 2025.

## Plot

An adventuring party are informed by an old man of the return of the Duke, now hiding in the Northern Temple. He notes that if the Duke is not stopped he'll destroy the city, or worse, the entire island. An injured man reveals himself to be the only survivor who escaped from the Duke, and the old man recounts the history of the island: once beset by constant invasions, the island was nearly destroyed, though the Duke's science combined with the will of its people and Marquis helped them hang on. The Duke eventually resorted to human experimentation, claiming many lives in the process and using their souls to create stone golems. The old man urges the party to defeat the Duke.

The part arrive at the Northern Temple and confront the Duke, who greets them, asking if they too seek power. The party dismiss any similarities between them and him, prompting the Duke to transform into a giant creature and unleash his army of golems onto the party in a demonstration of his strength.
