# Deepwoken (manga)

_**Deepwoken**_ is a one-shot manga written by Keiichi Tsuha and illustrated by Akeji Fujimura. It was published as part of a collection of short stories serving as adaptations of _Roblox_ games. The collection was distributed during the Roblox Developers Conference of 2025.

## Beginning-Middle-End

The beginning of the manga starts with the main protagonist, Gordo Massi (credits to \_nyanwinter on discord for the character) going out on Erisia. However, when fate takes a thrilling turn and Gordo is sent to the depths along with a Linkstrider Vendet by the name of Aemilius Vendet (credit to \_bbange on discord for the character), our lovely Protagonist will have to face a series of events such as killing Ethiron and escaping the Trials, though, she was tricked to form a Vow of Thorn. Will Gordo and Aemilius make it out?
