# Prophet's Cloak

Template:ArmorInfoboxProphet's Cloak is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, one (1) Bloodless Gem, five (5) Vibrant Gems, five (5) Umbral Obsidian, one (1) Dark Feather, one (1) Odd Tentacle and one (1) Thresher Spine.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 45.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2600 durability and grants +25% Physical resistance, +25% Elemental resistance, +20% Ice resistance, +20% Fire resistance and +400% Ether Regen.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Crippling Darkness_, which makes all non-basic attacks have +25% Armor Penetration (PEN).

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)
-   [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) chests.
