# Tideknight

## Description

Tideknights can only found within the later waves of [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md). They are quite formidable foes as they have a high amount of HP and deal decent damage without even taking into acount their vast numbers. they also disable mantras on some waves includeing them.
