# Tideknight

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Tideknights can only found within the later waves of [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md).

  
They wear the [Celtorian Tideknight](Celtorian_Tideknight.md) armor and have a wide array of weapons, mantras, and oaths.

## Trivia

-   They share the same armor with the [Celtorian Guards](Celtorian_Guard.md)
