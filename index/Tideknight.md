# Tideknight

## Description

Tideknights can only found within the later waves of [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md). They are quite formidable foes as they have a high amount of HP and deal decent damage without even taking into acount their vast numbers. they also disable mantras on some waves includeing them. Template:EnemyInfobox1.0 They wear the [Celtorian Tideknight](Celtorian_Tideknight.md) armor and have a wide array of weapons, mantras, and oaths.

## Trivia

-   They wear the Celtor Tideknight armor so it is only fitting to call them Tideknights untill their true name is updated to this page
-   They share the same armor with the [Celtorian gaurds](Celtorian_Guard.md)
