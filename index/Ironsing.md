# Ironsing

Your ability to harness metal from your surroundings.

## Description

Ironsing is an attunement based around the ability to harness metal, commonly used by Blacksmiths around Lumen.

## Training Ironsing

To train it you have to do karliahs quest and protect her from the bugs
