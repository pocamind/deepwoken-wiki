# Mushroom Soup

## Description

Template:Food Template V1Mushroom Soup is a [Food Item](Food.md#campfire-recipes) present in the game, which can be bought from [Lance's Food](Etris.md#lances-food) at [Etris](Etris.md), or crafted at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).

## Recipe

Mushroom Soup is crafted with any combo of two different mushrooms at a campfire.

**Example:** _[Dentifilo](Dentifilo.md) + [Gobletto](Gobletto.md) **OR** [Dentifillo](Dentifilo.md) + [Browncap](Ingredients.md) **OR** [Browncap](Ingredients.md) + [Gobletto](Gobletto.md)_
