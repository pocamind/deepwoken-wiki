# Mushroom Soup

## Description

Template:Food Template V1Mushroom Soup is a [Food Item](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Food?so=search#Campfire_Recipes) present in the game, which can be bought from [Lance's Food](<https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Etris?so=search#Lance's_Food>) at [Etris](Etris.md), or crafted at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md). You can only hold 10 Mushroom Soups at a time without any talents that boost your food capacity..

  

File:Mushroom-soup.png

Art by T0LKA

## Recipe

Mushroom Soup is crafted with any combo of two different mushrooms at a campfire.

**Example:** _[Dentifillo](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search) + [Gobletto](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search) **OR** [Dentifillo](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search) + [Browncap](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search) **OR** [Browncap](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search) + [Gobletto](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Ingredients?so=search)_
