# Hive Guard

Template:Creature Info Box v2

## Description

Hive Guard are NPCs that can only be found outisde and around the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md). Killing one will give the player negative reputation with [The Hive](Reputation.md).

They are all either Vesperians or Ganymedes. They wear Pathfinder Cloaks, Woodland Pauldrons and Woodland boots.

## Moveset

A Hive Guard's kit consists of a [Ritual Spear](Ritual_Spear.md) and the [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) attunement:

| Name | Description | Parryable? | Blockable? | Danger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Assault | Firmly thrusts spear at opponent and continuing to combo them until knocked. | ◯ | ◯ | (★☆☆) |
| Critical | Unleash a quick double slash with spear. | ◯ | ◯ | (★☆☆) |
| Flame Assault | Burst forward in flames. | ◯ | ◯ | (★★☆) |
