# Notes

**Notes** () is the standard currency in _Deepwoken_. They are used to purchase various things, such as [Food](Food.md), [Weapons](Weapons.md), and tools.

“ Show me shiny, show me magic! I give you sand dollar. ”

― [Mantra Collector](Mantra_Collector.md)

  

## Functions

the Notes symbol

Notes can be used in various ways, listed below.

### Dropping

Upon clicking your Notes on the bottom right, you can choose to drop any amount to maximum of 1000.

### Obtaining

The max amount of Notes you can carry is 15,000.

-   Asking the helper at Etris will give you, for the helper at Isle of Vigils, but this can only be done once. (Note: you cannot take the notes from one and then travel to the other island for their notes as they will give you the same dialogue as if you already took the notes.)
-   Selling various things to an Antiquarian.
-   Completing various [quests](Quests.md).

## Trivia/Notes

-   Modifier buyers refer to Notes as "Sand Dollars," which is the same.
