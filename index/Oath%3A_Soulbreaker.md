## Description

**[Oath](Oaths.md): Soulbreaker** is an Oath related to murmurs, specifically the ability to wield three at once. There is not much else known about this Oath, besides a possible mantra effect that was shown. In order to obtain the Oath, you must talk to Yun'Shul and choose the "For now I am one, but I wish to be three" dialogue.
