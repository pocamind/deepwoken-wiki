# Cheral

Template:NPC name

## Description

The Eastern Camp Master is an interactable NPC that can only be found in the Navaen Nomad Camp in [Etris](Etris.md). This NPC is used to obtain the [Way of Navae](Fists.md) fighting style.

He wears [Navaen Nomad Robes](Navaen_Nomad_Robes.md) with his race being a [Gremor](Gremor.md).
