# Jarvis

| Jarvis | |
|---|---|
| name | Jarvis |
| location | Summer Isle |

## Description

Jarvis is an NPC located in the basement of [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md)'s castle.

His main use is to smelt two pieces of equipment into one - the first keeping its attributes, the second being its overall appearance for 1000 Notes.

## Trivia

-   While his race may not be known, he appears to be [Human](Human.md), rather than an [Etrean](Etrean.md).
-   Another NPC known as the Artisan can be hired in your [guild base](Guild_Bases.md). They will function the same way as Jarvis does, with a much more cheaper cost.
