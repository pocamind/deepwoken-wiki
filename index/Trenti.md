# Trenti

Template:NPC name

## Description

Trenti is an Etrean NPC you can find at the [Etris](Etris.md) Inn. He is usually the first NPC you talk to when starting a new character. He can help new players figure out where to go when starting out, directly mentioning the island of [Erisia](Erisia.md). He can also give you 25 notes, which is useful starting out.

## Notes

You can find [Aristo](Aristo.md), an NPC in the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) Inn that does the same things that Trenti does.

## Dialogue

Trenti - Well well well, look who's up. Oh please, hold your praise, I know how much you must be wanting to thank your hero!

1 - Err... Thanks? / Relax, I'm kidding. I'm Trenti, I found you washed up on the shore and figured you looked like you needed some help. I brought you back here and you've been sleeping off some bad drowner sickness for about a week. Looks like you fully recovered though! Have a look around town and get your bearings and come back to me if you have any questions. / End

2 - How can I earn some money? / Well... You won't find much in the way of work in the town, but if you find anything of interest the local **Antiquarian** might buy it off you. I suppose you could try to find riches on Erisia like most of the travelers who come here. You'll be eable to find Erisia by sailing straight out from the docks.

\- Erisia? / Yeah, it's a whole island that drifted in from the Voidsea not too long ago. Place is meant to be crawling with opportunists, but maybe there's some things left to find? / End

\- Would you be able to spare me some cash? / \*He sighs.\* Alright, this should be enough to get you started. \[+25 Notes\]. / END

\- End

3 - How do I get stronger? / Through training, I should think. You should be eable to find some shops around this island that will sell you gear to train your various attributes. Though, you'll probably want to put that training into practice in order to reinforce it. / How can I earn some money?/ See above / END

4 - Where can I get some clothes? / Head down to Caragan's forge, he can probably whip you up something. / END
