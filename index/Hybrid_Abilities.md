# Hybrid Abilities

### _**This page is being worked on, and information may not be complete and/or correct.**_

Hybrid mantras are special types of [mantras](Mantras.md) that require 2 different [attunements](Attunements.md) and a certain amount of investment points in those two attunements to be able to discover and use.
