## Description

**Santiago Talo** was a Ranger of [the Divers](The_Divers.md). He was assigned with leading a patrol party, consisting of him, Ranger Bykof, Ranger Emirad and new Diver Recruits. Eventually, he diverted his scouting party away from his assigned path, heading towards the direction of the Landfall.[\[2\]](#cite-note-interrogation-2)

With 2 other Rangers: Bykof and Emirad, they dispatched of a stray [thresher](Thresher.md) and found deceased bodies of 2 [celtorian citizens](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md) of the upper class. After which, they looked at [the City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md) and saw (presumably) members of [the Ministry](The_Ministry.md) leading a procession into the city. Eventually, they decided to stick to the assigned route and finish it.[\[2\]](#cite-note-interrogation-2)

On Sunsdawn, 9th Bloomfall, 904 CE he was interrogated by a [Black Diver](Black_Divers_division.md) [Ulysses Disii](Ulysses_Disii.md) on the case of insubordination. [The High Convocation](The_High_Convocation.md) were responsible for his punishment, although, Disii recommended for his assignment to sanitation duties at [the Citadel](The_Citadel_of_Markor.md) for two weeks.[\[2\]](#cite-note-interrogation-2)
