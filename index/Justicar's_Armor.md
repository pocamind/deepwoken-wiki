# Justicar's Armor

Template:ArmorInfoboxJusticar's Armor is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, five (5) Fiber, one (1) Wayward Gem, five (5) Gale Stone, four (4) Thresher Spine and one (1) Dark Feather.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 45.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 2800 durability and grants +40% Physical resistance and +30% Elemental resistance.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   Etris War Event (Super Rare)
