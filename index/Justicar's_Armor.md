# Justicar's Armor

Template:ArmorInfoboxLightkeeper is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, five (5) Fiber, one (1) Wayward Gem, five (5) Gale Stone, four (4) Thresher Spine and one (1) Dark Feather.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 45.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1500 durability and grants +30% Physical resistance and +30% Elemental resistance.
