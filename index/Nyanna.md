# Nyanna

“ Are you slow? Yes, premium notes. I sell to the lazy and rich. If you don't like my prices, go try finding these schematics yourself. ”

― Nyanna to a neophyte

Template:NPC name

## Description

Nyanna is a seller NPC located in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md), at the ground floor. She as the unique mechanic of selling a few items that change every hour.

## Shop rotation

Nyanna's shop run on an hourly rotation. She posses a list of items and every hour, she sells 4 items chosen at random from the list. She can sell the same item multiple time across multiple rotations. Most of her shop consist of uncommon weapon and schematics that are geared toward mid game.

-   **Weapons:** Trident Spear, Whaling Knife, Adretian Axe
-   **Armor Schematic:** [Mercenary Armor](Hired_Mercenary.md), [Merchant Robe](Merchant_Robes.md), Authority Uniform, [Authority Commander Uniform](Authority_Captain_Uniform.md), [Diver Apprentice](Diver_Apprentice_Robes.md), [Hive Drone](Hive_Drone.md), Ignition Coat, Keeper, Legion Centurion, [Prophet's Cloak](<Prophet's_Cloak.md>), [Vigil Cloak](Cloak_of_Winds.md), [Tactician](Hive_Tactician_Armor.md), [Pathfinder Journeyman](Pathfinder_Cloak.md)

**Note that the list is imcomplete and requires more work done to find every item she sells.**

## Notes

-   You can check her wares, log and wait an hour or more to try to get a specific weapon or armor.
