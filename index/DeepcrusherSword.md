## Description

The DeepcrusherSword was a Greathammer added to the game during the [Ironsing](Ironsing.md) update. It was later removed, all copies voiding.

Given its name, appearance and stats, it was likely the placeholder model for the [Ignition Deepcrusher's](Ignition_Deepcrusher.md) sword variant.

## Trivia

-   Despite being very obviously a sword, it is classed as a Greathammer.
-   It is a reskinned [Forge Greathammer](Forge_Greathammer.md).
