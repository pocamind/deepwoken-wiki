# Suncross

“ THE WANDERING STAR! ITS MOVEMENTS SO CALCULATED! ITS WARMTH - ITS LIGHT! SUCH BEAUTY - SUCH INTENT! ”

― Soothsayer about the Suncross

  

## Description

The Suncross is one of the two celestial objects that orbits between Lumen and the Heavens - standing as Deepwoken's equivalent of the Sun and the counterpart to the [Moonseye](Moonseye.md). Orbiting around Lumen in an elliptical path, the Suncross is partly responsible for Deepwoken's day/night cycle.

The Suncross is always seen as a white star with six distinct points. The Suncross can only be seen during the daytime, and cannot be witnessed during nighttime, retreating into the Voidfog - during which the Moonseye becomes more visible in the night sky.

## Cultural aspects

Although it is an infrequently-used symbol in Deepwoken compared to the Moonseye, it is generally viewed as the embodiment & bringer of life by both Man & Celestial.

### Religion

The Kyrsans have a unique depiction of the Suncross, which is referred to as Ameda. Within Old Kyrsa is the Amedan Temple which still stands amongst the ruins of the city, but none make their pilgrimage to to the temple.

The Starkindred Faith shows followers who view the Suncross as a Celestial, while others do not. As a whole, it remains as a sacred object to the Faith.
