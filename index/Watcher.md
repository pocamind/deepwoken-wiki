# Watcher

> "OH GOD, THE PIXAR LAMPS ARE-" -Unfortunate Diver

## Appearance

File:Depth Seeker.jpg

Seeker located in [The Depths](The_Depths.md)

" BIG BLACK MAN!!!" - DazGames

Watchers, also known as Seekers, are lamp-like monsters featuring a closely-humanoid body with similarities to the [Demogorgon](<https://strangerthings.fandom.com/wiki/Demogorgon_(species)>) from Stranger Things.

These monsters have been documented to be **UNKILLABLE** as they simply teleport away upon taking external damage.

However, being spotted with their beam that acts like a spotlight will result in a combat tag as well as the beam turning from white to a reddish hue.

Shortly after, [Angels](Hollow_Angel.md) will be summoned to kill the person spotted.

## Attacks

-   Summoning of Angels - Summons 2 Angels to attack whoever it spots with its Spotlight
-   Leap - Leaps to another area to spot players.

## Trivia

-   The Watcher located in the Temple Of The Forgotten Flame only summons one Angel.
-   The Watcher by the Temple of the Forgotten Flame is also known as the "God on the Peak" from [Alastir's](Alastir.md) dialog upon bringing him the surviving villagers.
