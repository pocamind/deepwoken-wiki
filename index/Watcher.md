# Watcher

## Appearance

Huge and mysterious creatures that lurk in The Depths. Grey in color, they have a flower-like head with a spotlight inside it.

File:Depth Seeker.jpg

Seeker located in [The Depths](The_Depths.md)

(Looks close to the [Demogorgon](<https://strangerthings.fandom.com/wiki/Demogorgon_(species)>) from Stranger Things)

## Attacks

-   Summoning of Angels - Summons 2 Angels to attack whoever it spots with its Spotlight
-   Leap - Leaps to another area to spot players.

-   [The Depths (most common here)](The_Depths.md)
