# Sanguine Candle

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

The Sanguine Candle is an item obtainable from [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) chests. It's used to spawn [Ministry](The_Ministry.md) NPCS with specific weapons tied to them, Specific ones can be spawned via interacting with Strange Sigils around the entire map, or alternatively just be placed down and lit. The NPCS drop the weapon they are holding upon killing them
