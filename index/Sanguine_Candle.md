# Sanguine Candle

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

  
The Sanguine Candle is an item obtainable from [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) chests. It's used to spawn in a [Ministry](The_Ministry.md) NPC with a red [Light's Final Toll](<Light's_Final_Toll.md>) and [Jungle Jim](Jungle_Jim.md), although the exact methods to do this are unknown. Based on interactions with the black markings found throughout the map, the use of the candle seems to be tied to them. Currently, the NPCs drops nothing on death, so the purpose of this candle is a mystery.
