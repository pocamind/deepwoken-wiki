# Lord Regent Plush

## Description

The **Lord Regent Plush** is a plush that will release as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration. Classified under the "Plushie" category, it features [The Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md). Its campaign will start on June 12th, 2026.

"The Lord Regent has graciously humoured you for an audience within your home. All he requires is the most prominent spot upon your shelf, and respect befitting of his station.  
  
No strings attached!"
