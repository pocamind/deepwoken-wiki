# Stonespark

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Stonesparks are a mob usually found on [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md) a light infantry version of the [Rock golem](Rock_Golem.md), they appear to be deactivated but once a player enters its detection range it activates, attacking it's current target until it loses sight. interestingly it does have the capacity of parrying,

## Tips

-   Your weapon swings stun the monster allowing for multiple hits

-   You should try to parry him as much as you can to land a guard break for free hits

## Trivia

-   You can find a high amount of stonesparks close to the dukes and duchess of erisia statues.
-   When not in combat Stonesparks will deactivate and kneel down.
-   [Marcus](Marcus.md), located in [The Hidden Village](The_Hidden_Village.md), is the only known passive Stonespark.
-   Stonesparks can block and get guard broken.
