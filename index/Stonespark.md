# Stonespark

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

Stonesparks are a mob usually found on Lower Erisia a light infantry version of the Stonelife golem, they appear to be deactivated but once a player enters its detection range it activates, attacking it's current target until it loses sight. This mob, like the enforcer has the ability to block but interestingly it does have the capacity of parrying, a other thing that should be acknowledged is the fact these light infantry golems have a single posture breaking attack swung by their right arm that has a small highlighting indicator appearing at the center of their fist.
