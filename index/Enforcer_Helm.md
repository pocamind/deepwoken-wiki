## Description

The **Enforcer Helm** is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) Item found under the "Head" category. This helmet resembles the head of an [Enforcer](Enforcer.md).

The [Blend In](Talents.md#blend-in) Talent makes most Depths Monsters neutral towards you, removing their aggro unless you hit them first.
