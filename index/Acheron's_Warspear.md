## Description

Acheron's Warspear is a MED weapon that can be obtained by defeating a [Corrupted King Thresher](Thresher.md) in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). Despite subpar stats, it deals bleed damage, which means that the spear does more than what's shown.

## Trivia

-   The highest damage spear in the game due to having bleed. Including the 1.3x damage multiplier from bleed damage, it does 37.44 damage at 100 medium.
-   This weapon is in the same “family” of spears, with the others being [Serrated Warspear](Serrated_Warspear.md) and True Seraph’s Spear.
