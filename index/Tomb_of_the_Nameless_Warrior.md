# Tomb of the Nameless Warrior

LocationL Tomb of the Nameless warrior LocationR

  
  

**Tomb of the Nameless Warrior** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark below the [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md) found in [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md).

## Overview

Tomb of the Nameless Warrior is a cavern that is home to a [Rotskipper](Rotskipper.md). A hidden door behind the [Rotskipper](Rotskipper.md) can be broken open with any destructible Mantra. It is an Authority Stronghold disguised as a place of rest, however it appears the Etrean military have become aware of this and have destroyed sections of it, though are yet to find the segment hidden behind the door.

This location is important to obtaining [Strength Unbound](Attributes_Unbound.md).

The entrance can be found on a shore around the rear of [Erisia](Erisia.md).

This location has an abundance for [Erisore](Ores.md#erisore). You can make a Navean Nomad Robe easily here.
