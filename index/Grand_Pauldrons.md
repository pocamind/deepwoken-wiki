## Description

The Grand Pauldrons are an equipment item in the arms category. The stats and Pips gained from equipping them have not been documented. They are commonly found in chests from [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) and [The Depths](The_Depths.md) ([First Layer](First_Layer.md)).

## Trivia

-   The Grand Pauldrons are part of the “Grand” set; with the only other piece being the [Grand Boots](Grand_Boots.md).
-   Used by [The Meat Lord](The_Meat_Lord.md) together with the rest of the set.
