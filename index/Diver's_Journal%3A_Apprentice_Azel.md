# Diver's Journal: Apprentice Azel

_This is the book availabe in the current game version. For its older counterpart, see [Diver Journal, I](Diver_Journal,_I.md)._

## Description

_**Diver's Journal: Apprentice Azel**_ is a [Book](Books.md) found in the first floor of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md), written by and collected from the corpse of [Apprentice Azel](<Mauled_Diver_Corpse_(Apprentice_Azel).md>).

## Text

### 

Diver's Journal: Apprentice Azel

> \- Eternal Gale Expeditionary Log This was never an expedition. This was a setup.
> 
> Chaser has gone mad. The [Kyrsgarde](Glossary.md#kyrsgarde) have gone mad.
> 
> No matter what direction I run, I'm met with the same adversity from those I'm supposed to trust.
> 
> I don't even know if Chaser took us here to find anything. More likely, he took us here to feed on us.
> 
> Even the [Kyrsgarde](Glossary.md#kyrsgarde). They act kind. All the while praising the nightmare beneath us. It's all a façade.
> 
> It's always been a façade. I can't trust anyone anymore. Even the damn snow is a lie.
> 
> And even now, I hear them. My death is already assured. Soon, I'm sure, the creatures resting nearby will wake.
> 
> To them, I'll just be another meal. Another [Diver's](Glossary.md#the-divers) name hastily carved into another memorial stone.
> 
> As if my name was ever worth carving into anything. As if anyone will even be there to read that name out.
> 
> What was all this even for? We're all doomed by the greed of fools like Chaser.
> 
> So, Ma, there won't be any more money sent home. I couldn't even stick around long enough for you to get treated.
> 
> I'm sorry I was such a useless son.
> 
> You can curse my name, if it helps, since I'm already in hell.
