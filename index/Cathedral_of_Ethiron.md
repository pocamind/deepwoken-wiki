# Cathedral of Ethiron

LocationL Cathedral of Ethiron LocationR

The once sacred hall reeks of decaying corpses and profane heresy.

  

## Overview

The Cathedral of Ethiron is a landmark in [The Maw of Ethiron](The_Maw_of_Ethiron.md). It is the final destination point of layer 2's first floor, and serves as the arena for Chaser's fight.

## Contents

[Chaser, Scholar of the Burning Blood](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) can be found here. Defeating him will reward you with a large chest as well as an entrance to the second floor of layer 2 and an exit to the first layer.

## Trivia

There are numerous corpses belonging to apprentice divers hanging on hooks, presumably killed by Chaser. There is also one ignition Deepdelver corpse here.
