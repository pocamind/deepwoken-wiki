## Description:

Eylis is the leader of the [Knives of Eylis](The_Knives_of_Eylis.md). Not much is known about her besides the fact that she has some sort of contract with the [Voidmother](The_Voidmother.md). In the Voidwalker description it says she has plans in mind for you and that she says your different from the other whalers, what this could mean is beyond anybody, but some think it's hinting at a quest line.
