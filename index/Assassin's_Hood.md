# Assassin's Hood

## Description

Assassin Hoods are accessories under the "Head" category. They can be found through chests in Hive Chests, Gold and Mist rarely from Aelita.

## Trivia

This is meant to be paired with the Pale Assassin's Cloak/Mist Assassin's Cloak, although they are rarely paired due to the hood being ugly.Template:EquipmentStats
