# The Sunken Railways

Template:Location2

**The Sunken Railways** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location of [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md).

## Overview

The Sunken Railways is a gloomy location of Layer Two. It is where one of the five lanterns required to access the [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) boss fight is located. It's most notable feature is its bottom floor, containing the Kyrsan lantern, two railways with trains on them, and a [Kyrsan medallion](Kyrsan_Medallions.md) chest spawn.

The top floor mostly does not have anything important, but does contain one more Kyrsan medallion chest and many spawns for [Kyrsgarde Crawlers](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Kyrsgarde?so=search#Kyrsgarde_Crawler_).

Not much else is known about this location, since it does not have much content.
