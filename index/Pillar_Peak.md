**Pillar Peak** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique areas exclusive to the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) gamemode.

## Overview

Pillar Peak is a giant temple found high above near [King's Colosseum](<King's_Colosseum.md>), watching over the rest of [Kingmaker's Isle](<Kingmaker's_Isle.md>).
