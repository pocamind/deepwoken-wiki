## Description

The Bloodforged Crown is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category. It is found as an exceedingly rare (<2%) drop from [Chaser, Scholar of the Burning Blood](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md). It has an innate 5 Health, as well as 1 rare pip and 1 legendary pip. It has the innate talent [Cauterized Wounds](Flamecharm.md#flame-warden).

When equipped, even while its visibility is toggled off, it will play an animation of a red swirling particle around the wearer's head, currently unknown if this transfers to smelted equipment? (PLEASE FIND OUT, THIS METHOD WORKS WITH ENCHANT PARTICLES)

## Trivia

-   It is the only "Head" equipment to have a LVL requirement, as every other head equipment that has a requirement to wear only requires FTD.
