# Fragments of Self

## Description

Fragment of Self acts as the purgatory of _Deepwoken_. Players are sent to it whenever they die in _[The Depths](The_Depths.md)_.

## Proposed Service

-   **Campfire:** a permantly lit campfire located at the back of the area, near a torn tent. Good to reread your characters stats or remove pieces of equipment to be used at the Hippocampal Pool.
-   **[Hippocampal Pool](Hippocampal_Pool.md):** Located to the left, this pool let you input up to 2 items that will be transferred to your next character.
-   **Shrine of the Drowned:** Located before Self, the shrine let you make an offering (1000 robux) to return to The Etrean Luminant. This option is only available once per character.

## Dialogue

The only NPC you can find here is yourself, or fragments of yourself. Talking to yourself will result in the following:

-   **Self:** You. Can be found at the end of the Fragment Of Self, and will proceed with the following dialogue:
    -   Self: So... this is it for us, huh?
    -   Player: What do you mean?
    -   S: You know what I mean. You're me, after all. This is where we as a person end.
    -   P: But I don't want to go.
    -   S (looking down): _\*Sigh.\*_ There was so much left to do, wasn't there?
    -   P: Isn't there something we can do?
    -   S: _\*You see your face, racked with a pained expression.\*_ No. You know there isn't.
    -   P: What is all this?
    -   S: This... All of this around us... Is all our mind is able to make sense of right now. It's just holding on to all it can still remember.
    -   P: So, this is really the end?
    -   S: Yeah, I suppose it is. Come speak to me again when you want to... Well... You know.

-   After which, talking to Self again prompt the following dialogue:
    -   S: _\*You see yourself smile weakly.\*_ Don't want to go out without a smile, do we?

Taking the \[The End\] option officially marks the end of your character as your are sent back to the main menu and to the character creation screen.
