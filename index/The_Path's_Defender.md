## Description

The Path's Defender is a [side weapon](Weapons.md) in the Shield category. It can be obtained from the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). The requirement to wield it is 75 FTD and harbors the highest posture buff out of any shield in the game.

## Notes

-   This offhand weapon changes its appearance based on what enchant your main weapon has.
