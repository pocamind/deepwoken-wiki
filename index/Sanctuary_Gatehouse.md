# Sanctuary Gatehouse

LocationL Sanctuary Gatehouse LocationR

A Gatehouse left alone. For what fool would dare tread upon sacred ground?

  

## Contents

Template:AreainfoboxA small temple guarded by a gatehouse with a corridor leading to the [Ethironal Shrine Sanctuary](Ethironal_Shrine_Sanctuary.md). To be able to open the big ancient door you need to find your key.

Only the NPC diver [Falei](Falei.md) resides at this location, likely the sole survivor of Chaser's expedition team. If you happen to talk to the NPC, he will tell you a little about [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) and give you a little tip about the gales when you leave the place.
