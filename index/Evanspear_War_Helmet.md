## Description

The Evanspear War Helmet is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category, and is a part of the Evanspear War Set. The Evanspear War Helmet is a green and yellow corinthian helmet, similar to Roman headgear.

This item is used to craft the [Reinforced War Helmet](Reinforced_War_Helmet.md) at the [Merit Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md).
