# Version History/2025

## LEGEND

Something is considered a new update if it happens on a different day as the past update and/or if the update is significant.

Something is considered a patch if it's just bug fixes and/or minor changes.

-   \+ means the addition of anything.

-   ^ means the edit of a feature.

-   \* means the patch of a problem.

-   \- means the removal of something.

-   \> may be used for something that doesn't match one of the previous categories.
