## **July 7th, 2026; pv\_JUL\_08\_10:30a**

**Content & Systems**  
📰 This week's patch is a bit smaller as we've been hard at work on the Across the Boundary Update, but comes with a lot of improvements to the game's performance, especially frame lag  
💡 Following an increase in volcanic activity, Saramaed Summit has partially collapsed in on itself, prompting the Union to reconfigure their facility there, but improving access to the caldera and throughout the island  
💡 Added 2 additional hotbar slots, by default bound to '-' and '='. Though we don't want to encourage hotbar spam, having to dive into your backpack to use talents and mantras was needless friction and something that often just got macro'ed anyway  
💡 Added a setting to turn off guild/squad invites  
🔧 Chests will now stick to moving objects like ships and will now fall if the object they're sitting on is removed  
🔧 Chests no longer collide with ships while in the water  
  
**Mob Behavior & Combat Tag Tweaks**  
⚖️ Mob aggro now factors in the damage dealt by an enemy recently into its scoring algorithm. This should make mobs less prone to flipflopping between targets and make them prioritise the biggest threats  
⚖️ DoT ticks after the first tick of damage no longer count as additional 'hits' on combat tags, making it so bleed weapons don't immediately aggro allied mobs if you hit them accidentally  
⚖️ Mute (aka Animal King) no longer makes monsters see you as an ally, and instead greatly reduces their aggro range against you, letting you still slip past monsters undetected. Mute has been enabled in instanced content again. The former behavior led to a lot of broken interactions and didn't represent the design of the regalia being 'monsters can't sense you as easily'  
🩹 Fixed mobs not receiving updates to whether they're in danger or not. Was making it so mobs that don't aggro on sight wouldn't ever retaliate  
🩹 Fixed a bug where if a mob uses a move with a rushdown it'd delay the rest of the move until the rushdown was over, unlike if a player used the same move  
  
**Renaming & Merges**  
🔀 Threadwork Summer Scarf has been merged into the Summer Scarf as a style  
🔧 Browline Citadel Frames and Round Citadel Frames are now merged into the Citadel Frames as styles (the items were previously identical stat-wise)

**Major Optimizations**  
⚙️ Introduced a system to batch vfx packets into a single packet payload. This means that network traffic and packet frequency can be greatly reduced and better scheduled, and also allows us to introduce a range of further optimisations  
⚙️ Batched vfx utilises spatial chunks to not send vfx to clients that are far away. This was handled on the client before to reduce frame lag, but now it's handled on the server to reduce server lag too  
⚙️ A lot of combat vfx is now batched  
⚙️ Sound effect networking is now batched alongside vfx, reducing packet traffic greatly  
⚙️ Greatly optimized blockbreak vfx. Hard to understate how much blockbreaks would tank frames  
⚙️ Greatly optimized on-hit vfx and reduced the amount of overlapping vfx  
⚙️ Greatly optimized Sapped status vfx  
⚙️ Greatly optimized surge pylon vfx  
⚙️ Greatly optimized metal rod vfx  
⚙️ Greatly optimized crystal status vfx

⚙️ Optimized armor damage/bloodstain attachments on characters  
⚙️ Optimized Lightning Strike effects, should be most notable on Emotion Wave  
⚙️ Optimized Shadow Eruption to stop its FPS drops
