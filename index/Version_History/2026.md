# Version History/2026

## Legend

Something is considered a new update if it happens on a different week as the past update.  
Something is considered a patch if it's added after an update but doesn't contain enough content to be considered one.  
The update highest on the webpage (the one right below the legend) is the most recent game update that has been put into the wiki.

  

## **February 16th, 2026 | pv\_FEB\_18\_00:46a**

**World Events Rework**  
💡 World Events have been reworked with a new system. Much like how Carnival of Hearts worked before, a UI will pop up on the side of the screen advertising that a World Event has begun. Unlike how Carnival worked previously, clicking join will matchmake you and your squad with players from across the game and transport you to the World Event's location. Will you cooperate with strangers or squabble over the loot?  
💡 Battle Royale has now been converted into a World Event. The game mode can no longer be accessed through Chime of Conflict (on weekends), but instead can be joined when it occurs as a World Event. Your build does not carry over to the gamemode, so the gameplay is unaffected; you duke it out in a free-for-all brawl from scratch. Winning a match that started with 25 or more participants will grant you a unique reward. This reward is automatically granted to the slot you joined the world event on  
💡 New World Event Island: Leiuna  
💡 Parasol World Event has been reworked. Parasol has received multiple new moves and mechanics  
💡 New Carnival Gamemode "Ingredient Hunt"  
🔧 Beam Blitz Gamemode remade  
🔧 Circus Bombs are no longer plantable and are now thrown with Left Click along with "E"  
🔧 Caeranthil's Wake and Etris Raid have been rebranded as 'Disasters'. Disasters work the same way that World Events formerly did, being large-scale events that occur on a set rotation within a specific region
