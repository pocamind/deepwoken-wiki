## Description

A stats changed [Rifle Spear](Rifle_Spear.md) made for [Carrion, Father of Evolution](Carrion,_Father_of_Evolution.md).

## Critical

The user aims down sights and shoots the spear, unleashing a blast in front. If the blast doesn't hit anyone, a bullet projectile is fired off.

## Trivia

-   This weapon is a direct reference to the famed Rifle Spear in the game _Bloodborne_, the original model being a direct copy.
-   Accidentally made obtainable on release but was reverted back to a normal Rifle Spear in Version PV\_JAN\_19\_22:13.
