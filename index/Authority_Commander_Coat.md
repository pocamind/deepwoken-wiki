## Description

The Authority Commander Coat is an animated equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 10 Health as well as 2 Pips by default. They can only be found in chests from [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md)

## Trivia

Despite being called the Authority Commander Coat, [Authority Commanders](<Authority_Commander_(Enemy).md>) don't wear it.
