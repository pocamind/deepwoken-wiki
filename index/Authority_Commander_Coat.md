# Authority Commander Coat

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Authority Commander Coat is an equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 10 HP and a few more randomized stats based on the Pips you get. The Authority Commander Coat can only be found in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) chests.

## Trivia

Despite being called the Authority Commander Coat, [Authority Commanders](<Authority_Commander_(Enemy).md>) don't wear it.
