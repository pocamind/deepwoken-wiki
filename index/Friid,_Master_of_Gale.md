# Friid, Master of Gale

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Friid is an NPC who acts as both progression for the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) Oath and the [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md) trainer.

In order to obtain Galebreathe, you must bring five [Gale Stones](Gale_Stone.md) to Friid. Alternatively, if you have obtained the Silentheart Oath, you must kill them in order to get one of five oath talents. They are found at a camp near the [Temple Approach](Temple_Approach.md).
