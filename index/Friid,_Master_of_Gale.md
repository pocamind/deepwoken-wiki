# Friid, Master of Gale

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0In order to obtain [Galebreath](Galebreathe.md), you must bring 5 [Gale Stones](Gale_Stone.md) to Friid. Alternatively, if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, you must kill them in order to get one of five oath talents. They are found at a camp near the [Temple Approach](Temple_Approach.md).
