## Description

Insignia Gem is a light blue diamond-shaped [Deep Gem](Deep_Gems.md).

## Effect

Upon landing a Mantra with an Insignia Gem equipped, your next Mantra cast within the next 3 seconds will have its windup reduced by 15%. This effect has a 5 second cooldown.

### Notes

-   This Gem is best used on easy to hit Mantras that can be used to combo into other Mantras.
-   Some Mantras cannot be buffed by Insignia Gem's effect.
    -   [Eclipse Kick](Shadowcast.md#eclipse-kick)
    -   [Sightless Beam](Oath%253A_Blindseer.md#sightless-beam)
    -   TBA
