## Description

Stench is an equipment enchantment that creates a damaging area on proc.

This enchantment applies green-yellow stench clouds to the equipment piece.

## Effect

Upon taking damage, Stench has a 20% chance to proc. On proc, create a stench cloud that deals 1% of your opponent's maximum health per tick. This ticks 3 times, totaling to 3% damage dealt.

### Notes

-   Stench cannot deal more than 10 damage per tick.
-   Stench's gas is 360 blockable, dealing 0 posture damage when blocked.
