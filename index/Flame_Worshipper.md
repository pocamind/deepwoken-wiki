## Description

**Flame Worshipper** is a playable [Memento](Mementos.md) in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) gamemode. You also spawn with the [Simmerbloom Diadem](Simmerbloom_Diadem.md) and can equip the [Pleeksty's Inferno](<Pleeksty's_Inferno.md>) which you can find as a static spawn at [Miners Landing](<Miner's_Landing_(Vow_of_Iron).md>).

## Available Mantras

### Starting Mantras

-   [Fire Palm](Flamecharm.md#fire-palm-)
-   [Burning Servants](Flamecharm.md#burning-servants-) (250 Notes)

### Power 5 Mantras

-   [Flame Leap](Flamecharm.md#flame-leap-) (250 Notes)
-   [Fire Blade](Flamecharm.md#fire-blade-) (250 Notes)

### Power 10 Mantras

-   [Relentless Flames](Flamecharm.md#relentless-flames-) (750 Notes)
-   [Flame Assault](Flamecharm.md#flame-assault-) (750 Notes)

## Boss Unlocks

### Shogun of the Prophet's Guard

-   Pleeksty Inheritance - Enhances all Flamecharm mantras
-   Ascended Phoenix Down - When downed once per fight, be restored to 30% HP

### Dravik, The Rat King

-   Pleeksty's Wrath - After dealing 500 damage with Flame Mantras, call forth a hail of firey swords

### True Heart of Enmity

-   True Mirage Clone - Successfully dodging leaves behind a heat mirage clone that sets enemies that swung at you on fire.

-   Flames Of The Past - Your flamecharm mantras now call forth firey homing spirits.
