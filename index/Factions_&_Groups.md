# Factions & Groups

## Description

The Factions of Lumen feature their own diverse narratives, characters, objectives, and territories. They serve as the key driving forces of the game.

The player's [reputation](Reputation.md) shapes how both them and the faction interacts with each other, positively or negatively.

The page and list below reflects the current status of the Factions in _Deepwoken_.

## List of Factions

The primary and reputable factions in _Deepwoken_ include:
