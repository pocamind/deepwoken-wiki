# Factions & Groups

## Description

The Factions of Lumen feature their own diverse narratives, characters, objectives, and territories. They serve as the key driving forces of the game.

The player's [reputation](Reputation.md) shapes how both them and the faction interacts with each other, positively or negatively.

The page and list below reflects the current status of the Factions in _Deepwoken_.

## List of Factions

The primary and reputable factions in _Deepwoken_ include:

| Faction | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [Etrea](Etrea.md) [Etreaiconfaction](Etrea.md) | Known as the Kingdom of Etrea, it occupies one half of the Eastern Luminant with the Isle of Vigils. It is temporarily ruled by [the Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md). |
| [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) [Authorityiconfaction](The_Central_Authority.md) | A predominant military and political force run by Sovereign [Drallis Ehr.](Drallis_Ehr.md) The Authority is focused on uniting all of Lumen together in order to stand against [the Depths](The_Depths.md). In the Eastern Luminant, they are at war with Etrea and the Hundred Legions. |
| [The Divers](The_Divers.md) [Diversiconfaction](The_Divers.md) | A division of the Markor Citadel that conducts expeditions into the Depths to study its nature and gather materials. |
| [The Hive](The_Hive.md) [Hiveiconfaction](The_Hive.md) | Operating on four Greathives of Lumen, they seek to rise above the Tides through their use of Lifeweave. |
| [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md) | A freelancer company that is primarily focused on mining expeditions, trades, and Depths exploration. They often come into conflict with the Divers and the Summer Company. |
| [The Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md) [Summericonfaction](The_Summer_Company.md) | Founded by [Gunsmith Rosen](Gunsmith_Rosen.md), the Summer Company is the primary manufacturer of firearms that were brought back from The Canticlysm. They are often found sabotaging the Ignition Union for the sake of aggressive competition. |
| [The Children of Navae](The_Children_of_Navae.md) [Navaeiconfaction](The_Children_of_Navae.md) | Primarily a [Gremor](Gremor.md)\-focused group that are usually nomadic and worship the celestial Navae. |
| [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md) | A predominant power in the Western Luminant, the Hundred Legions are an empire consisting of [Khans](Khan.md). After a recent pact breaking between them and the Authority, |
| [The Knives of Eylis](The_Knives_of_Eylis.md) [Knives of Eylis](The_Knives_of_Eylis.md) | Serving under [Eylis, the Punished Dreamer](Eylis,_the_Punished_Dreamer.md), the Knives of Eylis are a ruthless mercenary organization that seeks to get the job done, no matter what banner they serve under. |
| [Lost Celtor](Lost_Celtor.md) | Fallen to the Submergence of its own nation, the remnants of Celtor are no more than degraded survivors that slowly succumb to the effects of the Depths. |
| [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md) [Ministryiconfaction](The_Ministry.md) | Led by the Nine Prophets, the Ministry seeks to achieve great power and plunge the entirety of Lumen into the Depths. |
