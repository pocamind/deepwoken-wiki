## Description

The **Assassin's Cloak** is an animated [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category.

The [Lowstride](Talents.md#lowstride) Talent grants increased [stealth](Stealth.md) and allows you to move faster while crouched. Additionally, it removes the sound effect that plays when you sheathe or unsheathe your weapon.

## Trivia

-   The Assassin's Cloak is part of the “Assassin's” set; with the only other piece being the [Assassin's Hood](<Assassin's_Hood.md>).
