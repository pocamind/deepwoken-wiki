# Assassin's Cloak

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

**Assassin's Cloaks** are animated equipment under the "Arms" category. They can be obtained through chests in [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) (**Rogue Construct**), [Aelita](Aelita.md), [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) and the [First Layer](First_Layer.md).
