## Description

The Assassin's Cloak is an animated equipment item under the Arms category. Equipping it grants you 6 Health, 3% Stealth, 3 Pips (One guaranteed legendary Pip), and the [Talent](Talents.md#assassin) Now You See Me, which increases stealth while crouching and silences the unsheathing animation. It is rarely found in chests from [Aelita](Aelita.md), [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), [Rogue Constructs](Rogue_Construct.md), and [Scyphozia](First_Layer.md).

## Trivia

-   The Assassin's Cloak is part of the “Assassin's” set; with the only other piece being the [Assassin's Hood](<Assassin's_Hood.md>).
