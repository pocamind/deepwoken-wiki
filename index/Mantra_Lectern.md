# Mantra Lectern

| Mantra Lectern | |
|---|---|

## Description

Mantra Tables are a utility item that are used to improve your existing [Mantras](Mantras.md). They are typically found in larger settlements like the Isle of Vigils or Etris.

Mantra Tables resemble typical tables with segments of what looks like sand inside. The five blue discs will start to illuminate if a specific requirement is needed. Most of these requirements are still unknown, but it is suspected [Intelligence](Attributes.md) and [Attunement](Attunements.md) levels are likely. The purpose of the two darker segments on the left are unknown.

## Usage

Mantra Tables can be used to modify most [Mantras](Mantras.md) in _Deepwoken_.

To use the Mantra Table, you must first recall a Mantra while resting. Next you must add the Mantra to the table along with modifiers. To see all combinations of effects you can improve your Mantra with, see [Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md).

You can also rename Mantras by typing out your desired name into the chat box and pressing enter.

## Trivia

-   When enough mantra modifiers are used (minimum of 1 enhanced and 3 regular in my testing), the five blue circles on top of the mantra table, as shown in the image, will begin to glow.
