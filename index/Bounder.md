# Bounder

Skrillbo

## Description

Bounders, also known as Sand Megalodaunt, El Chupacabra, Primal Megalodaunt, Ass Eater, Frog Megalodaunt, Juan, Dull Lingal Berg, Trevor, Frank, and Skrillbo, Golden Megalodaunt, are an unreleased monster in Layer 2 of The Depths.

Bounders have a very similar body type to a [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md), with a difference of having much more elongated limbs, a hunched over body, a wider mouth, a huge set of sharpened teeth which wrap around the entire front of the head, and six black ringed eyes, similar to that of a spider.

A very interesting note is that its legs have a high resemblance to that of a frog, shown even more with its ability to leap far and high. It also makes use of its elongated limbs to swing at enemies.

It grips by grabbing ahold of its victim eating them.

One final interesting note is its environment appears to be almost identical to the one [Bone Keepers](Bone_Keeper.md) appear in, being a very narrow, dark, foggy, rocky tunnel, possibly implying a dungeon or something similar.
