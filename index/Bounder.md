# Bounder

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Overview

Bounders, also known as El Chupacabra or The Boner, are a monster in Layer 2 of The Depths.

Bounders have a very similar body type to a [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md), with a difference of having much more elongated limbs, a hunched over body, a wider mouth, a huge set of sharpened teeth which wrap around the entire front of the head, and six black ringed eyes, similar to that of a spider.
