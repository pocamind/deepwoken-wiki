# Bounder

Skrillbo

## Description

Bounders, also known as Sand Megalodaunt, El Chupacabra, Primal Megalodaunt, Ass Eater, Frog Megalodaunt, Juan, Frank, and Skrillbo, are a new monster in Layer 2 of The Depths.

Bounders have a very similar body type to a [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md), with a difference of having much more elongated limbs, a hunched over body, a wider mouth, and a HUGE set of teeth sharpened teeth that wraps around the entire front of the head, and finally six black ringed eyes, reminisce of a spider.

A very interesting note is it's legs have a high resemblance to that of a frog, shown even more with its ability to leap far and high. It also makes use of it's elongated limbs to swing at enemies.

It grips by grabbing ahold of its victim, and straight up eating them in its maw.

One final interesting note is its environment appears to be almost identical to the one [Bone Keepers](Bone_Keeper.md) appear in, being a very narrow, dark, foggy, rocky tunnel, possibly implying a dungeon or something similar.
