## Description

The **Sinner's Ash** [Relic](Relics.md) are remnants of the Forge of Sin that stain one's very soul and corrupting their [Resonance](Resonance.md) in the process.  
_(one-time use, re-obtainable)_

Using **Sinner's Ash** whilst having a bell will **not** reroll your bell's current allocated [Resonance](Resonance.md) stats. Alternatively, with an already corrupted [Resonance](Resonance.md), It will only reroll its downside. Using the Sinner's Ash without having obtained a [Resonance](Resonance.md) will do nothing, and dialogue will appear saying "The ashes don't seem to have any effect."

Using **Sinner's Ash** in [Duke Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>) after defeating the Duke while having a [Murmur](Murmur.md) and meeting the stat requirements to obtain [Soulbreaker](Oath%253A_Soulbreaker.md) will grant you the Oath without corrupting your Resonance. More info on the [Soulbreaker](Oath%253A_Soulbreaker.md) page.

The known downsides of a Corrupted [Resonance](Resonance.md) are as followed:

-   **WITHER:** On use player will receive Wither for 10% of their max HP.
-   **MANIA:** On use, player will lose 40 sanity.
-   **POISON:** On use, player will lose 7% HP. If used below 6% health, you will be left at 1 health.
-   **BLOOD LOSS:** On use player will lose 15% of their max blood.
