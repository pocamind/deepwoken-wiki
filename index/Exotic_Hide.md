## Description

The **Exotic Hide** is an [Ingredient](Ingredients.md) that drops guaranteed from the extremely rare [Exotic Megalodaunt](Exotic_Megalodaunt.md) or the [Weird Trader](Weird_Trader.md). It can be traded with the Gaunt man to receive a [Curse of Captain Greene](Curse_of_Captain_Greene.md) enchant stone.
