# Megalodaunt Hide

Megalodaunt hide can be obtained by killing [Sharkos/Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md), with a pretty high drop rate.

## Uses

\- Crafting the "[Hive Drone"](Outfits.md) outfit.

\- Crafting the ["Black Diver](Outfits.md)" outfit.

\- Crafting the "[Sharkscale Glider](Gliders.md)" Item.

\- Using 5 of them to fight the ["Primadon"](Monsters.md) boss.

Picture of a player holding the Megalodaunt Hide.

\-Sold for 15 Notes at the Antiquarian.
