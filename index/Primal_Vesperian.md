# Primal Vesperian

Template:Races-Main

"Ancient Vesperians lost to time."

**Currently Unobtainable**

## Description

An alternate version of Vesperian with dark chitin and white markings, currently unobtainable by normal means.  

## Lore

Primal Vesperians do not play a significant role in the lore, thought they did exist, and have some brief lore behind them.

Primal Vesperians were a small group of deviant [Vesperians](Vesperian.md) who left The Convicts Hand and went on to further their more animalistic traits in their newly formed group known as the Primal Vesperians, they eventually died off as a group leaving one sole surviving member, this being Stag Acrossus.
