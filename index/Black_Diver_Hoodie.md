# Black Diver Hoodie

## Description

The **Black Diver Hoodie** was a hoodie released as part of a Deepwoken and Makeship collaboration. Classified under the "Hoodies" category, it was based on the [Black Diver](Black_Diver.md) [Outfit](Outfits.md). Its campaign ran from July 18th, 2025, to August 16th, 2025, before selling out. The hoodie was designed to be unisex and was made from 41% cotton, 57% polyester, and 2% other fibers, excluding decorative details.

  

### Official Description

"Eclectic, strange, but a vital part of the Divers' mission, the Black Divers are idiosyncratic pioneers.  
  
Equip the uniform of a Black Diver, pull your hood up, and steel yourself against the horrors of your ears getting cold.  
  
Prove to the outside world that your sanity is very much intact."

## Trivia

-   A total of 304 Black Diver Hoodies were sold, and the campaign ended at 101% of its funding goal.
