**Spark Glands** are an item in _Deepwoken_.

## Description

Spark Glands are used to get [Thundercall](Thundercall.md), one of the five Attunements by giving them to the Thundercall [Trainer](Attunement_Trainers.md). They can also be consumed, though they will barely give any hunger or thirst.

## Obtainment

Spark Glands drop from any NPC that uses the [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) attunement.

## Trivia/Notes

-   Prior to the Verse 2 Update, Spark Glands used to give [Reservoir](Reservoir.md) upon consumption.
