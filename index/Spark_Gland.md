**Spark Glands** are an item in _Deepwoken_.

## Description

Spark Glands are used to get [Thundercall](Thundercall.md), one of the five attunements (elements) by giving them to the thundercall [trainer](Attunement_Trainers.md). They can also be consumed, though they will barely give any hunger or thirst.

## Obtainment

Spark Glands drop from any NPC that uses the [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) attunement.
