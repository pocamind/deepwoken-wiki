# Spark Gland

File:Eat .png

Spark Gland being An consumed

_"An organ responsible for the production of electricity."_

**Item Obtainability:**

-   Drops from enemies using thundercall.
-   Drops from jellyfish from The Depths.

**Item Description:**

A consumable item that fills up the ether reservoir for Thundercall users (may work for other elements).

**What It Does:**

Sells for 15 notes. (prices vary depending on island and merchant)
