## _"Used by a wandering demon..."_

Held OniBeads \* The OniBeads are a bloodshed version of the [Monastery Beads](Monastery_Beads.md). They're said to be worn by the [Wandering Demon](Wandering_Demon.md). Oni means Demon in Japanese. \* The set consists of the OniBeads and the [Demon Mask](Demon_Mask.md). \* Obtainable from [Etris: Siege](Cinders_of_Etris.md).
