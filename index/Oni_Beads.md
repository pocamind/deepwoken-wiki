## Description

The OniBeads are a bloodshed version of the [Monastery Beads](Monastery_Beads.md).

## Talent

  The [True Demon Step](Talents.md#true-demon-step) Talent grants a Talent tool that when activated makes the user charge forward in a straight line, gaining i-frames. This consumes 10 Ether on cast with a 0.5 second cooldown.

## Trivia

-   This item's talent is an extremely buffed version of the [Familiar Demon's Armor](<Familiar_Demon's_Armor.md>)
-   It is related to the upcoming [Wandering Demon](Wandering_Demon.md) [Memento](Mementos.md).
