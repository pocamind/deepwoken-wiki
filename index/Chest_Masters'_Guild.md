## Description

Primarily [Felinors](Felinor.md), the [Chest Masters](Chest_Master.md) are a guild of skilled lockpickers, capable of opening [Chests](Chests_&_Sacks.md) with great precision. Their techniques are closely guarded, making them one of the few reliable ways to access secured chests.

It remains unclear why Chest Masters no longer exist in the [Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) gamemode, though their skills may have become unnecessary.
