## Description

Primarily [Felinors](Felinor.md), they are aficionados of lockpicking, capable of opening [Chests](Chests_&_Sacks.md) with precision. They keep their methods a secret, they are the one of the ways to access treasure inside of chests inside of the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) game mode aside from [antiquaries](Antiquarian.md)

Unless you have a [Skeleton Key (Item)](<Skeleton_Key_(Item).md>) obtained from either [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md)'s Mystery Box or [Strange Merchant](Strange_Merchant.md)'s Magic Coffin. it is intended to instantly open any chest without the need for [antiquaries](Antiquarian.md) or [chest masters](Chest_Master.md). Currently bugged and does absolutely nothing.
