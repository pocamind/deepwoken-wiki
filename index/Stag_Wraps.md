## Trivia

-   Stag Wraps stats are known, but Nuttoons prefers to keep it confidential.
-   Stag Wraps are one of the two Arms [Equipment](Equipment.md) that emit particles by default. The other one being [Eternal Restraints](Eternal_Restraints.md)
-   Nuttoons is the only one wearing Stag Wraps, and it'll be staying that way.
-   Nuttoons wears Stag Wraps along with another item exclusive to him, [Toon's Goggles](<Toon's_Goggles.md>).
-   Lore: Stag Wraps are made of song, to voluntarily suppress Stag’s curse. Stag is under a curse where he can't die, he exchanged physical pain for power, and the pain is suppressed by the wraps (which also heavily nerfs him).
