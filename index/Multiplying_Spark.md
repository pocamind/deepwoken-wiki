# Multiplying Spark

Redirect to:

-   [Mantra Modifiers](Mantra_Modifiers.md)

**Item Obtainability:**

Bandits, Chests, Mudskippers, (could be more ways)

**Item Description:**

"you swear you see double or triple holding it."

a green circular shape with spikes coming out.

**What It Does:**

Appears to multiply (hit) effects of mantras to a certain amount (either twice or thrice as of current testing on Fire Blade which when hit creats 3 slice hit SFX.) REQUIRES EXTRA TESTING FOR OTHER SPELLS. UNFINISHED

Incompatible with: Glacial Arc
