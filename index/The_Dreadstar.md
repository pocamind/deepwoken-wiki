## Description

The Dreadstar is a Shrine NPC. It is a he [Silentheart](Oath%253A_Silentheart.md) [Oath](Oaths.md)**,** being the main quest giver for it.

The Dreadstar displays hatred and disgust for the Song and its usage, being a pivotal part of obtaining Silentheart and inviting Pathfinders on its path for revenge against users of the Song.

The origin of The Dreadstar and how it came to be in the Beloved Zofia are unknown. It may be a congregation of spirits given its use of the term "we," or it may be a similar entity to Yun'Shul.
