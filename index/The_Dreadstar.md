# The Dreadstar

“ "Your Very Being Lacks Conviction. There's No Purpose To The Path You Walk. Leave This Place" ”

― - Dreadstar, The mysterious entity

File:DreadstarAEhfa.png

**The Dreadstar** is a new NPC that appears in Beloved Zofia, He appears to serve no purpose outside of maybe telling lore, from what's seen in footages and my own experience, his dialogue seems to change, based on one's Oath, based on a theory, this shrine could be related to the new **Silentheart Oath,** as it mentions lack of "Conviction" and purpose of "One's path"

**Theories:**

This shrine mentions lack of "Desire for Revenge" if you speak to him while having an oath, as he is the one that grants the Silentheart Oath, Could have the requirement of "Conviction". Also seeing how his design is similar to that of Yun'Shul's Main Shrine in the depths, he must be one of the entities that the humanity has angered during the Canticlism, and would need a strong mind and no link to attunements to do said work. hence the progression method involving killing all elemental trainers
