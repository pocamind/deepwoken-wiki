# Jade Vigil's Weave

Template:ArmorInfoboxJade Vigil's Weave is a Master [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Requirements

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth, five (5) Fiber, one (1) Dark feather, five (5) Gale stones, one (1) Thresher spine and one (1) Wind gem,

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Talents.md) 15 and 10 Agility.

## Armor Stats

This Outfit has 2800 durability and grants +30% Physical Armor, +25% Elemental Armor, +15% Wind Armor, 25% Blood Armor, and +25% Ether Regen.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _**Serpent's Dance**_, which gives the [Fang and Coil](Fang_and_Coil.md) fist style a new Running Critical Attack.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found as a reward from:

-   [The Doom of Caeranthil](The_Doom_of_Caeranthil.md)
-   [Carnival of hearts](Carnival_of_Hearts.md)
