# Diver's Camp

LocationL Diver's Camp LocationR

  
  

## Description

**Diver's Camp** is an area in [The Depths](The_Depths.md). It is a landmark in the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md), and is a campsite set up by the Divers for their [Apprentice Divers](Apprentice_Diver.md).

The Camp is found straight and to the left of the District of Commerce Ramp Gate. It is protected by [The Divers](The_Divers.md), so if any hostile monster wanders in, several of the Divers will jump to attack it. However, do be warned that the low-level [Apprentice Divers](Apprentice_Diver.md) are relatively weak and therefore cannot hold against the monsters on their own.

This is an area intended to be used for players who have drowned and cannot reach [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md) because of it.
