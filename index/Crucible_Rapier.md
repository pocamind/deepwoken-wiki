## Description

The Crucible Rapier is around the same as the [Apprentice Rapier](Apprentice_Rapier.md) and [Inquisitor's Thorn](<Inquisitor's_Thorn.md>), not having too much of a difference of the both except for higher damage, and honing a 20% penetration stat. It can so far be obtained in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md).

## Trivia

-   It is the highest tier of damage amongst all the other Rapiers.
-   It keeps the same crit as any other rapier, dealing 4 hits at a time.
