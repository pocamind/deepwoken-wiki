## Description

The Crucible Rapier is around the same as the [Apprentice Rapier](Apprentice_Rapier.md) and [Inquisitor's Thorn](<Inquisitor's_Thorn.md>), not having too much of a difference of the both except for higher damage and a 20% penetration stat. It can be obtained in [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) as well as the [Rogue Hive Constructs](Hive_Construct.md) on [Minitrysa](Minityrsa.md). The Crucible Rapier does 28.5 damage at 100 LHT, and 25.125 at 75 LHT (the minimum requirement to equip this weapon.)

## Trivia

-   It is the highest tier of damage amongst all the other Rapiers.
-   It keeps the same crit as any other rapier, dealing 4 hits at a time.
-   The Crucible Rapier bears a strong resemblance to Velka's Rapier from the _Dark Souls_ series.
