# Temple of the Blade

LocationL Temple of the Blade LocationR

TERRITORY OF Etrea

A solemn sanctuary carved in the stone, where warriors hone the dancing steps of war.

  

**Temple of the Blade** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location in the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md).

## Description

The Temple of the Blade is a large temple found in the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). It is the core of the Isle and contains most of the Isle's necessities.

## Contents

#### Courtyard

-   Players can find [Stratos](Stratos.md), the second-hand to [Evengarde Rest](Evengarde_Rest.md) who sits in front of the fountain.
-   A [Jetstriker](Oath%253A_Jetstriker.md) orb can spawn at this location, in front of Stratos for those who are progressing the Oath.

#### Bookstore

-   [Kelsius](Kelsius.md) whom will give you a quest to find missing books.
-   [Suyuan](Suyuan.md), son of Vigil [Kiriban](Kiriban.md), who asks you to encounter [Ghosts](Ghost.md).
-   [Malte](Malte.md), an NPC who studies the [Sky Statue](Sky_Statue.md).
-   [Valikor](Valikor.md),an NPC who can teach the Talent [Spell Shout](Spell_Shout.md).
-   Players can find a [Mantra Lectern](Mantra_Lectern.md) in here, along with a [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md).

#### Blacksmith

-   [Baragan](Baragan.md), a [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) NPC.
-   [Crafting Station](Crafting_Station.md).

#### Training Hall

-   [Das](Das.md), a sparring instructor who will allow you to enter [Training Mode](Training_Mode.md).
-   [Avian](Avian.md), a student who believe Evengarde is a fraud.
-   [Evengarde Rest](Evengarde_Rest.md), the head of the Vigils and master of weapons.
-   A shortcut to the Training hall and Blacksmith Area can be found on the stairway leading to the Temple of the Blade.

## Quests

### Lance's Brother Quest

-   To start this quest, talk to [Lance's Brother](<Lance's_Brother.md>), the food vendor near the docks.
-   If asked to be helped, a red food crate appears on the ground, and Lance's brother asks you to deliver it to Stratos.
-   Stratos is located near the water fountain in the Temple of the Blade.
-   Upon delivery, Stratos gives you 25, some EXP, and some positive reputation with Etris.

### Etrean Swordsman Quest

-   To start this quest, talk to Avian in the Sparring Arena, located next to Das.
-   If asked about his attitude, he wants to be better, so he may show up a rival, but lacks the proper knowledge in the sword. He then asks you to give him a [Weapon Manual](Weapon_Manual.md) - doing so consumes the manual and awards a handful of EXP.

### Lost Sibling Quest

-   Talk to [Apprentice Kj](Apprentice_Kj.md) in The Temple of the Blade located at the left corridor room; sitting down sobbing.
-   He asks you to rescue, or find his sibling in [Erisia](Erisia.md).
-   His sibling, [Vigil Isaac](Vigil_Isaac.md) is found in an Abandoned Wood Lodge in the back of [Burning Stone Gardens](Burning_Stone_Gardens.md), near the shore to the North of [Duke Erisia's Manor](<Duke_Erisia's_Manor.md>).
-   Obtain a [Gale Stone](Gale_Stone.md) and give it to the sibling. He'll heal his wounds and ask you to head back to Isle of Vigils and talk to Kj about his status.
-   Talking to Apprentice Kj after giving the Gale Stone to Vigil Isaac finishes the quest. This awards you some EXP, a Compassion Talent hand, a unique Talent, and an audience with Evengarde Rest.
-   This quest will allow you to obtain [Attributes Unbound](Attributes_Unbound.md) a weapon of your choice.
-   Vigil Isaac can be found next to the Mantra Modification table at Vigils after completing the quest.

### The Librarian Quest

-   Speak to Kelsius, the Adret Librarian in the Library; located inside The Temple of the Blade.
-   Kelsius will mention of an Etrean who borrowed the books, and pinpoints his location in an Abandoned Lodge under Etris.
    -   If the quest has already been completed on your account once, the books will be located on a rock behind the building the [Banker](Banker.md) and [Antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) are in.
-   Upon arriving, a NPC by the name of [Sentinel](Sentinel.md) guards the front door. He is not hostile, and can grant you passage inside the house if you talk to him.
-   Confronting [Khan](<Khan_(NPC).md>), the supposed Etrean, your character questions him on the whereabouts of Kelsius' Books. He will ask if you know that you are working for a murderer, Further dialogue triggers a bone-chilling atmosphere.
-   Return to Kelsius. Rewards you a hand of cards as if you gained a Power Level, and the option to increase 1 of your Attributes at the cost of Notes.
    -   You can question Kelsius what Khan told you, either bluntly or politely ("Are you a Murderer?" or "Who are you, really?")
    -   Talking to Kelsius after completing [Tutorial](Tutorial.md) will give you unique dialogue, but you will not receive a mantra hand due to already getting one

### The Wind Woken Quest

-   You need to have [Galebreathe](Galebreathe.md) for this quest.
-   Speak to Stratos with positive reputation with Etrea.
-   He will tell you that he is willing to teach you a Talent, but you must learn to be one with the wind first.
-   Go to the docks and get on a boat, and once you begin sailing, turn your sails to match the directions of the wind trails on the sea for ~10 minutes.
-   Come back to Stratos and he will teach you the _Windwaker_ Talent, which allows you to obtain a temporary massive speed boost while sailing akin to that of a sea gate.
    -   To use the Windwaker Talent, just click it in your inventory while you are sailing.
