# Widow's Hollow

LocationL Widow's Hollow LocationR

  

  

The **Widow's Hollow** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark of the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) found in the [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md).

## Overview

The Widow's hollow is a large cave with its entrance found near the top of the giant mountain in the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). It consists of two "floors" eventually opening into a large chamber where the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) resides as well as an exit which leads to the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md).

## Contents

### Deep Widow

-   The [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md) can be fought here. For more information on its attacks, see [here.](Deep_Widow.md)

### Shelter

-   At the upper "floor" of the cave, there is a boarded-up door that can be destroyed or opened, leading to a small library of sorts containing the [Shrine Of Mastery](Deep_Shrines.md) as well as [Ciea](Ciea.md). For some reason, this place does not count as its own area.
    -   A button in the room with Ciea can be interacted with, opening a door that directly leads into the cave with the Deep Widow.

## Trivia/Notes

-   Legion Snipers have a small chance to spawn here.
-   While not being present through normal gameplay, clipping outside of the Lightkeeper Temple brings up the location marker for Widow's Hollow, and is established as a [voidzone](Voidzones.md).
