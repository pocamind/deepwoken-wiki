# Widow's Hollow

LocationL Widow's Hollow LocationR

  
  

**Widow's Hollow** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is a landmark of the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) found in the [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md).

## Overview

Widow's Hollow

The Widow's hollow is a large cave with its entrance found near the top of the giant mountain in the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). It consists of two "floors" eventually opening into a large chamber where the Deep Widow resides as well as an exit which leads to the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md).

## Contents:

At the lower "floor" of the widow cave, there is a boarded up door that can be destroyed or opened, behind it there is a small library of sorts containing the [Shrine Of Mastery](Deep_Shrines.md) as well as [Ciea](Ciea.md). For some reason, this place does not count as its own area.
