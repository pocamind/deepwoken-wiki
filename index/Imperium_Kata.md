## Description

**Imperium Kata** is a martial arts-based fighting style obtained by helping [Caitus](Caitus.md) break [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md) out of containment at the [Merit Detainment Core](Merit_Detainment_Core.md) within the Eastern Luminant's [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md), with Ally reputation or higher with [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md), and after defeating [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md).

### Critical

Its attacks consist of a chain of punches before a kick, and a side kick into a rear-leg side kick.

## Obtainment

To obtain it, you must obtain the [Legion Intelligence](Legion_Intelligence.md), defeat [Titus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md), and then return to [Caitus](Caitus.md).
