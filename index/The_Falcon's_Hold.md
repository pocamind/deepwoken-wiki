# The Falcon's Hold

LocationL The Falcon's Hold LocationR

A mountain fortress at the edge of the Luminants, contested not only by the machinations of Man, but by the encroaching Void.

  

**The Falcon's Hold** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique and randomly selected arenas exclusive to the 2v2 [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) gamemode.

## Description

**The Falcon's Hold** takes place within the front of the mountain fortress, containing a few stalls and small scaffolding.
