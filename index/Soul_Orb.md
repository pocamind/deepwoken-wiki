# Soul Orb

## Description

Soul Orbs are small, white glowing orbs with smoke emanating from them. They grant a free investment point.

## **Obtainment**

A Soul Orb has a low chance to drop from any [Enemies](Enemies.md) or [Monsters](Monsters.md). They will not drop from players and seem to have the same chance of obtainment on all humanoids.

## Trivia

-   Prior to the release of Verse 2, they used to grant [Luck](Luck.md).
