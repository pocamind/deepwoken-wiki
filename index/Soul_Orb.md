# Soul Orb

## Description

Soul Orbs are small, white glowing orbs with smoke emanating from them. They grant a free investment point.

## **Obtainment**

A Soul Orb has a low chance to drop from any [enemies](Enemies.md), including the guards from [Etris](Etris.md) or [Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md). They will not drop from players and seem to have the same chance of obtainment on all humanoids.

## Trivia

-   Prior to the release of Verse 2, they used to grant [luck.](Luck.md)
