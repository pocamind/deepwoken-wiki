# Soul Orb

## Description

Soul Orbs are small, white glowing orbs with smoke emanating from them. They grant a free investment point.

## **Obtainment**

A Soul Orb has a low chance to drop from any NPC or Monster, including Guards from Etris or Vigils, they will not, however, drop from players. They seem to have the same chance of obtainment on all humanoids, including mudskippers.

## Trivia

-   Prior to the release of Verse 2, they used to grant luck.
