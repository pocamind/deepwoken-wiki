## Description

**Candied Fruit** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item.

It is a type of **Plant**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Carnivore](Talents.md#carnivore) Talent.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 20% Stomach and 15% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Enchant buff, affecting 10% of the Stomach bar. The buff increases Mantra damage by 5%.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   2 [Fruit](Ingredients.md#fruits) and 1 [Sap](Sap.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) [CraftingCampfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
