# Eastern Nomad Leader

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

An Eastern Nomad Leader is an enemy NPC that can be found fighting [Ministry Scouts](Ministry_Scout.md) on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) in groups of two. Unlike the [Eastern Tribe Nomad](Eastern_Nomad.md), an Eastern Nomad Leader has the ability to use the [Frostdraw](Frostdraw.md) attunement.

They wear a [Rebel's Bandana](Outfits.md), [Novice's Brace](Outfits.md), [Navaen Nomad Robes](Navaen_Nomad_Robes.md) and can either appear younger or above 40 years old.
