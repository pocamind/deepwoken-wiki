## Description

The Pale Morning is an endgame Greathammer that can be obtained from Chests in the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md) (Very Rare), Merchant Ships (Extremely Rare) [The Duke](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), or [The Depths](The_Depths.md). This weapon has a base damage of 24 and a strength requirement of 10 to equip. This weapon has no special effects or passives. The description mentions a [Canor](Canor.md) warlord whose name is currently unknown.
