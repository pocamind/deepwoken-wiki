# Umbral Obsidian

Umbral Obsidian is a matterial used to obtain the [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) Mantra. It can be obtained by killing creatures other than jellyfish in the depths such as [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md) and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md). 5 Umbral Obsidian are required to obtain Shadowcast. (This may have other uses but for now this is the only one that I know of.)
