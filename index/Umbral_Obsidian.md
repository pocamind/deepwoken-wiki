Umbral Obsidian is a material used to obtain the [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) [attunement](Attunements.md) and to spawn [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md). It can be obtained by killing creatures such as[Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md) with a roughly 10% chance, [Threshers](Thresher.md) and [King Threshers](Monsters.md) have a chance aswell, however it is significantly smaller. There are a few exceptions so don't think you can simply farm [Jellyfish](Gigamed.md) for them. By giving (5) Umbral Obsidian to the trainer located in the Celtor Wastes, you will obtain [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md). Umbral Obsidian can also be consumed just as other Attunement Items ([Gale Stones](Gale_Stone.md), [Dying Embers](Dying_Embers.md), etc.) to restore your Reservoir. Umbral Obsidian seems to drop much more frequently from [Threshers](Thresher.md) in the Starswept Valley.

## Description

The description of Umbral Obsidian states that, "While holding it, you can feel the shadows darkening your soul," referencing how Umbral Obsidian is used to obtain [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md).
