# Umbral Obsidian

Umbral Obsidian is a material used to obtain the [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) [attunement](Attunements.md) and to spawn [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md). It can be obtained by killing creatures such as [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md) and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md) with a roughly 10% chance, [Threshers](Thresher.md) and [King Threshers](Monsters.md) have a chance aswell, however it is significantly smaller. There are a few exceptions so don't think you can simply farm [Jellyfish](Gigamed.md) for them. By giving the trainer located in the Celtor Wastes 5 Umbral Obsidian you will obtain [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md). Umbral Obsidian can also be consumed just as other Attunement items (Gale Stones, Dying Embers, etc.) to restore your Reservoir. Umbral Obsidian seems to drop much more frequently from [Threshers](Thresher.md) in the Starswept Valley.

Umbral Obsidian can be obtained fighting other enemies such as the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md), [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md), [Lionfish](Lionfish.md), and [Thresher](Thresher.md).
