# Umbral Obsidian

Umbral Obsidian is a material used to obtain the [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) Mantra and to spawn [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md). It can be obtained by killing creatures such as [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md) and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md) with a roughly 10% chance, Threshers and King Threshers have a chance aswell, however it is significantly smaller. There are a few exceptions so don't think you can simply farm Jellyfish for them. By giving the trainer located in the Celtor Wastes 5 Umbral Obsidian you will obtain Shadowcast. Umbral Obsidian can also be consumed just as other Attunement items (Gale Stones, Dying Embers, etc.) to restore your Reservoir. Umbral Obsidian seems to drop much more frequently from Threshers in the Starswept Valley.

Umbral Obsidian doesn't drop strictly from Depths monsters. Umbral Obsidian can be obtained through other enemies such as the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md), [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md), [Lionfish](Lionfish.md), and [Thresher](Thresher.md).
