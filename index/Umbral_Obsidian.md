# Umbral Obsidian

Umbral Obsidian is a material used to obtain the [Shadowcast](Shadowcast.md) Mantra. It can be obtained by killing creatures such as [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md) and [Lionfish](Lionfish.md) with a roughly 10% chance. There are a few exceptions so don't think you can simply farm Jellyfish for them. It is only used to obtain the hidden attunement, Shadowcast, by giving the trainer located in the Celtor Wastes 5 Umbral Obsidian. (It may have other uses, but for now, this is the only one that we currently know of.) Umbral Obsidian can also be consumed just as other Attunement items (Gale Stones, Dying Embers, etc.) to restore your Reservoir. Umbral Obsidian seems to drop much more frequently from Threshers in the Starswept Valley.

Umbral Obsidian doesn't drop strictly from Depths monsters. Umbral Obsidian can be obtained through other enemies such as the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md), [Sharkos](Megalodaunt.md), [Lionfish](Lionfish.md), and [Thresher](Thresher.md).
