## Description

**Plumfruit Muffin** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item. It is also purchased from [Traan Zakshun's](Traan_Zakshun.md) black market for 1.

It is a type of **Generic** food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 18% Stomach and 10% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   1 [Gathered Wheat](Gathered_Wheat.md) and 1 [Plumfruit](Plumfruit.md) at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) [CraftingCampfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
