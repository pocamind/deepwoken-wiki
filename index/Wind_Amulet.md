## Description

The Wind Amulet is a Mythic Torso [Equipment](Equipment.md) that is currently unreleased to the public. However, in March of 2024, developer Ragoozer, accidentally or intentionally, dropped it as loot in a [Chime of Conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md) match.[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) The only ever Wind Amulet in public existence had 2 Stars, meaning it had 2 extra Pips. Based off of this, we can infer that the 3% Wind Armor was actually the innate stats, and the extra stats were just extra pips from the stars.

It also comes with the [Talent](Talents.md): _Cauterized Wounds:_ Blood loss from all sources is lowered. Do note that this talent is [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md)\-only, and up until this point could only be equipped if you were a [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) user or wearing the [Bloodforged Crown](Bloodforged_Crown.md), but this item would then be the second item ever to give this talent.
