# Shipping Schedule

| Shipping Schedule | |
|---|---|
| bankable | Yes |
| droppable | Yes |
| pass down | No |
| desc | A categorical list of every incoming Etrean and Gremor prisoner scheduled to be imprisoned at the fort, including the dates they will arrive. |

## Description

A Shipping Schedule is an item only obtainable from [Authority Commanders](<Authority_Commander_(Enemy).md>). It can be turned in to the Etrean Guardmaster to obtain a medium-sized chest containing decent Fort Merit loot.

## Trivia

-   The Schedule doesn't count towards the Etrean Hostage count.
