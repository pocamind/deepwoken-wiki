## Description

A Shipping Schedule is an item only obtainable from [Authority Commanders](<Authority_Commander_(NPC).md>). It can be turned in to the [Etrean Guardmaster](Etrean_Guardmaster.md) to obtain a medium-sized chest containing decent [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) loot.

## Trivia

-   The Schedule doesn't count towards the Etrean Hostage count.
