## Description

**[Oath](Oaths.md): Bladeharper** is one of the 14 obtainable Oaths in _Deepwoken_.

## Obtainment

The Oath is obtained by inspecting one of the many Sky Statues located around the overworld. After inspecting the statue, you will be teleported to a new server and a location called "The Blade Within" where you will fight a replica of your slot after pledging yourself to the sword statue located in the middle. Defeat the clone to obtain the oath.

## Progression

After obtaining the Oath, interact with the statue again and click "Seize your Future," where you will fight the replica npc 2 more times. Grip the npc to progress.

## Effects

_Upon obtaining this oath, two snake breacelets will wrap around your arms._

## Abilities

-   **Lithe Step - Passive**
    -   When Sprinting, roll-cancel and instantly vanish and surge with unmatched speed
        -   Makes the player invisible, similar to ghost.

-   **Palm Strike - Combat Mantra**
-   **Decimate - Combat Mantra**
-   **Reveal - Utility/tool**
