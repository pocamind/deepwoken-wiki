# Khan (NPC)

picture of him

Khan is part of the [Lost Books](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Quests#Lost_Books) quest and is required to talk to him to finish the quest.

  
"Ah, hello. I see my friend let you in."

Yeah, Kelsius sent me to grab a few books.

"Oh, so Kelsius sent you to grab some books? ... But may I ask why you're here?"

For the books...

"Of course. That's why you're here. But have you ever stopped to consider _why_ you're doing this?"

Doing what?

"Working for a _murderer_, you swine."

  
After this, you can choose between 3 choices.

1.  Kelsius isn't a murderer.
2.  Kelsius is a good man.
3.  Kelsius is not who he appears to be.

None of the choices affect what happens though, as the same dialogue still happens.

"You act as though your actions are of your own volition, but even now you show yourself to be a mere servant. Did you really think that any of your choices were of any consequence? Worry not, little swine, your true masters will soon wake.

1-30. Obey

After you choose "Obey," a shadow figure will snap your neck and Khan will disappear.
