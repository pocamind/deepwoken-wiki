## Description

The Novice Brace is an equipment item in the arms category. Equipping it grants you 2 Health, 1 Posture, and an unknown number of Pips. It is commonly found in chests from [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) and [Erisia](Erisia.md).

## Trivia

-   The Novice Brace is part of the "Novice" set; with the only other piece being the [Novice Boots](Novice_Boots.md).
