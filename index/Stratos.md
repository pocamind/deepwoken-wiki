# Stratos

File:Vigil.png

An albino Felinor, dressed up as a Temple of Blades Guard.

**Stratos, an NPC in the 'Temple of Blades'.**

As of now little is know about this very NPC.

All I know about him, is that when approaching him with Bad - Neutral Reputation you'll just be ignored.

When talking to him with Good Reputation he'll firstly say something along "Speak, what do you need?" when then replying with something like this "Could you teach me anything?" He'll just send you off to find Wind or whatever...

Then he'll just keep on saying: "You do not know of the Wind yet." and just sending you straight away again.

**Possible theories**

During the Lost Sibling Quest, Isaac will mention "The Stream". From Dialogue from Stratos and Post-Quest Isaac, the player is told to "walk with the wind" and "you do not yet know of the essence of the wind". Combined with the knowledge of the Wind Pylons (the green glowing monuments found on Songseeker), I think that upon interacting with the Pylons, one can experience "the essence of the wind". The question is if it's possible to interact with them, and how to do so.

**Possible Things to try:**

Get 75 - 100 Gale Breath > Get good Reputation with the Vigils > Accept the Stratos Quest > Do the Isaac's Sibling quest > Do the Weapon Manual Quest > Do the Librarian Book Quest > Go uncap your Agility at the Windrunner > Go to all Sky Statues > Try talking to Stratos again.

**do not pick an oath.**
