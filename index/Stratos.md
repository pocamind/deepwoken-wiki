# Stratos

File:Vigil.png

An albino Felinor, dressed up as a Temple of Blades Guard.

**Stratos, an NPC in the 'Temple of Blades'.**

As of now little is know about this very NPC.

All I know about him, is that when approaching him with Bad - Neutral Reputation you'll just be ignored.

When talking to him with Good Reputation he'll firstly say something along "Speak, what do you need?" when then replying with something like this "Could you teach me anything?" He'll just send you off to find Wind or whatever...

Then he'll just keep on saying: "You do not know of the Wind yet." and just sending you straight away again.

  
**What I've tried:**

\- 0 STR | 100 FTD| 15 AGI | 0 INT | 2 CHR

\- 100 MED & 75 Gale

\- Most of the Gale breathing Talents
