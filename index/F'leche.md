## Description

F'leche is a Tiran woman found in [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). She is wearing a [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md) with [Eyeglasses](Eyeglasses.md). Similar with other residents of the city, she is unable to recall her past. Unable to remember the names of Kyrsgarde, but notes that they weren't always this frenzied. Gives insight to summon [Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md).

## Trivia

-   Unlike some NPCs in New Kyrsa, she is safe to talk to and cannot harm you.
-   F'leche's racial variant is a Cardinal.
