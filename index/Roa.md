## Description

Roa is a [Celtor](Celtor.md) NPC. They have a grudge against [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).

## Location

Roa is located at shores of [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md), next to a broken [Stonespark](Stonespark.md).
