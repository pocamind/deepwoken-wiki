# The Knives of Eylis

## Description

The Knives of Eylis are a organization of sea pirates that raid merchant and Etrean ships. A group of 3 pirates can be seen in [The Aratel Sea](The_Aratel_Sea.md) fighting the Etrean navy with both ships on fire and wearing an assortment of stolen accessories from ships they raided. Out of the 3, 2 are the raiders while one (Presumably the leader) casts an orb-shaped field that slows the player in it. They are also rarely found in [The Viper's Jaw](<The_Viper's_Jaw.md>) and [Pathfinder's Respite](<Pathfinder's_Respite.md>). Killing the 3 pirates will reward the player with a chest in one of the lower decks of 1 of the 2 ships (Sometimes it doesn't drop a chest for some reason).

## Notes/Trivia

-   The Knives of Eylis are stronger than the bandits in [Erisia](Erisia.md); and are not related to the bandits.
