## Description

The Knives of Eylis are an organization of ruthless mercenaries. They serve Eylis, the Punished Dreamer. They are hired by [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) to cause carnage in the Eastern Luminant, raiding Etrean naval vessels. Their base is found in the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md), a mysterious location said to exist within the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md).
