## Description

The **Firfire Cliffs** are a small sub-section of [Firfire](Firfire.md) in [The Eternal Gale](Second_Layer.md). They are off to the sides of the village, allowing the view of [The Maw of Ethiron](The_Maw_of_Ethiron.md) to fully take place.
