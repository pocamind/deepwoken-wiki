# Bosses

"**Boss**" is a very loose term in deepwoken. It ranges from the strongest [Enemies](Enemies.md) and [Monsters](Monsters.md), to those with unique spawns/drops, and even those that simply reward [Luck](Luck.md)/[Progress](Resonance.md). While nothing is REALLY a boss, the developers have given SOME recognization to a few foes.
