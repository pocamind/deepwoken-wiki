# Suri

Suri is an NPC belonging to the Hive faction, and can be found in the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md).

### Flower Quest

Once the player approaches Suri, they will be prompted to deliver a [flower](Delicate_Flower.md) to a Vesperian grave on the Isle of Vigils.

Completing the quest will grant the player 1 [Vibrant Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md#regular-modifiers) (may have a different drop, will test later).
