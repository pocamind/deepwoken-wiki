# Suri

Suri is an NPC belonging to the Hive faction, and can be found in the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md).

### Flower Quest

Once the player approaches Suri, they will be prompted to deliver a Delicate Flower to a Vesperian grave on the Isle of Vigils. Taking any damage will ruin the flower and require talking to Suri again.

Completing the quest will grant the player 1 [Vibrant Gem](Mantra_Modifiers.md) and a free [Talent](Talents.md) hand.

## Trivia

-   Likely a reference to Hollow Knight, which has a similar quest in which you deliver a flower to a grave and lose it if you take damage.
