## Description

The Starkindred Church is a powerful and malevolent cult, presumably originating from the [Western Luminant](Luminants.md#western-luminant). They are practitioners of the Starkindred Faith and their power stems from drinking the holy ichor of the [Celestials](Celestial.md) and feasting on their flesh. Those with high enough ranking in the cult to do so are called [Starkindreds](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md).

As powerful as the [Oath](Oath%253A_Starkindred.md) may seem, if the Oathtaker's faith falters - the ichor rejects their body and they turn into a [Maljuror](Maljuror.md), a wretched beast, wandering the world, seeking salvation.

[The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md) and [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) once went on an excursion against the Starkindred Church, defeating a Cardinal, and coming back victorious with the [Pale Briar](Pale_Briar.md) as a trophy, which afterwards fell into the hands of [Maestro](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) and can be obtained by the player by defeating him.

In the [Eastern Luminant](Luminants.md#eastern-luminant), their only base is the [Derelict Highchurch](Derelict_Highchurch.md) above the [Ancient Rotlands](Ancient_Rotlands.md).

## Notable Members
