## Description

the Kyrsgarde Champion is a boss located in [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) after the enhanced [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) has been defeated, you wish for power from [Misérables](Misérables.md), and interacted with the [Purge Shrine](Purge_Shrine.md) in front of [Kaide](Kaide,_the_Drowned_Light.md) has been activated. Summoning this boss increases the time you have to escape from 3 minutes to 7 minutes.
