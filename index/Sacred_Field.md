# Sacred Field

| Sacred Field | |
|---|---|
| effect | Grants yourself and allies incredible defense. |
| appearance | Orange field that spreads around the user. |

“ Conjure a field of sacred light, protecting you and your allies from harm. ”

― Sacred Field description

  

## Description

**Sacred Field** is a [Resonance](Resonance.md) or "bell" that players can obtain, and is uncommon to roll.

### _Appearance_

When used, an orange, red, blue, green or purple field will appear around the user for around fifteen to twenty seconds. There is also a chance for a blue field, but it's very rare.

## Usage

Pressing C will activate the **Sacred Field** Resonance.

**Sacred Field** will spawn an area that grants you and your allies defense against physical and elemental damage. **Sacred Field** has a cooldown of ~20-30 seconds.

### _Variants_

**Sacred Field** has multiple combination of variants and a unique Corrupted effect.

#### _Versions_

Sacred field is unique in its variation system in that there are many parameters that are randomly decided when obtaining the bell, these include:

-   % of Physical Resist (ranging from 1(?)% to 99(?)%)
-   % of Elemental Resist (ranging from 1(?)% to 99(?)%)
-   Size (ranging from 20(?) studs to 100+ studs)
-   Duration (ranging from 5(?) seconds to 30(?) seconds)
-   Color (known colors include yellow, purple, red, orange, green)

#### _Corrupted_

If **Sacred Field** is found to be Corrupted, the Resonance's range becomes larger and lasts longer.

## Trivia/Notes

-   **Sacred Field's** usage can allow players to focus less on their health and more on damage output.
-   When in a fight, using this next to a healing station will allow you to out heal damage. This most to only applies to Physical resist fields though.
-   This resonance work with any build just like [Portal](Portals.md), [Blood Scourge](Blood_Scourge.md), and etc. It is a good support bell for you and your team party.
-   **Sacred Field** is very useful for fighting large groups of people or bosses such as Duke.
