# Sacred Field

| Sacred Field | |
|---|---|
| effect | Grants yourself and allies incredible defense. |
| appearance | Orange field that spreads around the user. |

## Description

**Sacred Field** is a [Resonance](Resonance.md) or "bell" that players can obtain, and is uncommon to roll.

### _Appearance_

When used, an orange or red field will appear around the user for around fifteen to twenty seconds.

## Usage

Pressing C will activate the **Sacred Field** Resonance.

**Sacred Field** will spawn an area that grants you and your allies defense against physical and elemental damage. **Sacred Field** has a cooldown of ~20-30 seconds.

### _Variants_

**Sacred Field** has two versions and a unique Corrupted effect.

#### _Versions_

-   **Version 1** will grant players inside 3 to 4x defense against _physical_ damage and halved elemental damage.
    -   You can obtain 2 versions with Version 1. Most common variant of this is the longer lasting but shorter range/circle. The rarer version of this is the big range but lasts slightly less.
-   **Version 2** will grant players inside 3 to 4x defense against _elemental_ damage and halved damage from physical. This version also lasts longer.

#### _Corrupted_

If **Sacred Field** is found to be Corrupted, the Resonance's range becomes larger and lasts longer.

## Trivia/Notes

-   **Sacred Field's** usage can allow players to focus less on their health and more on damage output.
-   Be warned, if someone figures out which variant of **Sacred Field** you have, they may choose to do what the field only half resists.
