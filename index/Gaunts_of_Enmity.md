## Description

The **Gaunts of Enmity** are a unique fist weapon exclusive to [Heart of Enmity](Heart_of_Enmity.md). The Gaunts of Enmity retain the critical attack of your Fist Style.

  

## Trivia

-   The Gaunts of Enmity **are** enchantable despite their unique rarity.
-   Meeting the requirement of LHT, the damage is (24.3)
-   At max LHT, the damage is (27.4)
-   At max LHT, 6 Proficiency and with bleed, the damage is (36.8) (32.2 + **4.6**)
-   The Gaunts of Enmity are undroppable and unbankable, meaning the only way to use them is either to beat the [Heart of Enmity](Heart_of_Enmity.md) on a light weapon build or to wipe and pass down the weapon after obtaining it.
-   A sidegrade to the [Flamekeeper Cestus](Flamekeeper_Cestus.md), boasting similar stats with a slight upper edge when it comes to chip and posture damage.
