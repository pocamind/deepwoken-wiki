## Description

The **Gaunts of Enmity** are a unique fist weapon exclusive to [Heart of Enmity](Heart_of_Enmity.md). They feature high Light weapon scaling and high chip damage, which is fairly rare among fist weapons.

It is undroppable and unbankable.

It deals 26.1 DMG at 75 LHT and 29.8 DMG at 100 LHT with 3 Proficiency, at 6 Proficiency 27.9 at 75 LHT and 32.2 at 100 LHT.
