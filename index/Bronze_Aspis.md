## Description

Bronze Aspis is a [side weapon](Weapons.md) in the [Shields](Weapons.md#offhand) category. The requirement to wield it is 15 FTD.

## Notes

The second worst shield in the game, having more weight than [Targe](Targe.md), but less than [Kite Shield](Kite_Shield.md).
