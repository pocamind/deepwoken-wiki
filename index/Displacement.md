## Description

Displacement is an equipment enchantment that grants a speed boost on proc.

This enchantment applies horizontal white particle effects to the equipment piece.

## Effect

Upon taking damage, Displacement has a 30% chance to proc. On proc, you will gain a speed boost for 4 seconds and brief hyperarmor.

### Notes

-   This enchantment does not proc against non-humanoid NPCs.
