# Temple of Hearts

_"An esoteric place even the most pious of men are known to seek out, though all take the secret to their graves."_

## Description

A secret temple hidden inside a cliff in The Depths. An ancient mechanical door leads inside and cannot be opened unless a person has a Resonance. Opening the door will lead you into a hall that is overgrown with underwater foliage and these strange statues(?) on the sides. Going through will lead into an altar with Yun'shul, a mysterious being chained to one place.

Yun'Shul can be talked to, but it only results in it killing you by exploding your head.

## Monsters/Enemies

Yun'Shul

## Notes/Trivia

-   Only accesible by having a bell.
-   Talking to Yun'Shul will instant kill(?).
