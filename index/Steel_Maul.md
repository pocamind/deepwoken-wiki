## Description

The Steel Maul is a Heavy Greathammer that deals a Base Damage of 22 with 7 Scaling and 9 Range. The Steel Maul can be purchased in the weapons shop of [Etris](Etris.md) or found through chests in [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md). The Steel Maul is also [Alloyable](Pluripotent_Alloy.md), turning it into an endgame weapon by increasing its stats and giving it a damascus steel-type texture.

## Trivia

-   Typically used as a starter Greathammer when unalloyed.
-   Generally regarded as a bad weapon when not alloyed.
-   Meeting the non-alloyed requirement of HVY, the damage is (24.3)
    -   Meeting the alloyed requirement, the damage is (34.8)
-   At max HVY, the non-alloyed damage is (33.6)
    -   At max HVY, the alloyed damage is (38.4)
