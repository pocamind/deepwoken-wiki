# Bari

Template:NPC name

## Description

Bari is an [Etrean](Etrean.md) that gives new players a rundown on some of the mechanics of the game. He can be found at the docs of [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) and they inform the player on how campfires and gliders work. They are very similar to [Seraphina](Seraphina.md) at [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md) in the sense that they both teach the new players about the game, however, they are slightly worse than Seraphina since they do not give you the [Repair Kit](Repair_Kit.md) recipe.
