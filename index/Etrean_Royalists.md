## Description

The Etrean Royalists, also known as the Old Guard, are a group of [Etreans](Etrean.md) belonging to the various ancient clans that make up the Old Guard of Etrea who were respected before the Lord Regent's coup, having been now treated as 'a gaggle of rebellious lords, - or worse, scheming usurpers' thanks to the Regent's mind veil over the people of [Etris](Etris.md). They appear exclusively in the [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) mode and are working alongside the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md) to take down [The Lord Regent](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>).

The Royalists are made up of four distinct clans: Clan Anako, Clan Mofisu, Clan Kobura and Clan Ganba. They are all led by their respective 'Sachans' or Clan Leaders: Yi Anako, Chofi Mofisu, Shisui Kobura and Zaru Ganba.

They are the primary enemy of [The Lord Regent](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>) and seek to free [Etrea](Etrea.md) from the mind veil he has imposed over its citizens which makes them piercive the Royalists as bandits instead of the people trying to set them free. The Royalists during the Etrean Civil War used the [Temple of Mur](Temple_of_Mur.md) as a clandistine base of operations to plan their fight, however, it and the nearby Viridian Sanctuary were attacked by [Shoguns](<Shogun_of_the_Prophet's_Guard.md>) sent by the Lord Regent effectively causing the Mofisu clan to go in exile.

Following a heated arguement between each of the Sachans at [The Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md), the Old Guard was effectively disbanded leaving each clan alone to their own plans. Clan Ganba wished to make contact with [The Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) and use it as a base of operations against the Lord Regent, and if not possible, make their way westward and attempt to join with [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md), they also wished to retake the Monastary of Mur and the Viridian Sanctuary to which Clan Mofisu and Clan Anako were 'adamant' about.

On the other hand, Clan Kobura wanted to join forces with [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) claiming they needed 'powerful allies against the Regent' or even join the Regent's forces if no other solution presented themselves.

## Reputation

Every Memento regardless of aspect starts with Neutral Reputiation with the Royalists. Their reputation is shared across every clan meaning doing the jobs of a single clan will raise it for the entire faction.

There are only a few ways to gain reputation with the Royalists:

-   Talking to [Seon](Seon.md) or [Kazue](Kazue.md) and chosing the 'I seek the Lord Regent's defeat.' dialogue which will set your reputation to Neutral. It can only be done once per character.
-   Completing Jobs from the various estates. Jobs on red boards require Power 5 to be accepted, jobs on blue boards require Power 10.
-   Help Clan Ganba soldiers defeat Ignition Union members near the entrance of the Sable Estate.

The Royalists have a Reputation reset NPC, [Yen](Yen.md), near the front of the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md). They also are one of the few factions to have NPCs that freely reset your reputation to Neutral with no cost, those being [Seon](Seon.md) and [Kazue](Kazue.md), near [The Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md) and The Sable Estate respectively.
