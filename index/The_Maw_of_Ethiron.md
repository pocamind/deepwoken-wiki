# The Maw of Ethiron

Template:Location2

## Overview

The Maw of Ethiron is a large network of stone towers within the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md), connected by small bridges that quickly crumble upon being touched, the [Cathedral of Ethiron](Cathedral_of_Ethiron.md) can be found at its center, a large building where [Chaser, Scholar of the Burning Blood](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) resides.
