## Overview

The Maw of Ethiron is a large network of stone towers within the [Second Layer](Second_Layer/The_Ethironal_Shrine.md), connected by small bridges that quickly crumble upon being touched. The [Cathedral of Ethiron](Cathedral_of_Ethiron.md) can be found at its center, a large building where [Chaser, Scholar of the Crimson Contract](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) resides.
