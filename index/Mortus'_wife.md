# Mortus' wife

| Mortus' wife | |
|---|---|
| family | Mortus (husband)[1] |
| death | Caught by an octopus[1] |

“ **The Pathfinder**: What happened to your wife?  
**Mortus**: Fish got 'er. Were like one of them octopuses or summin'. We was tryin' to lure it to land to catch it. Caught us instead. We don't fish for them octopuses no more. ”

― The Pathfinder and Mortus

## Description

She was a beloved wife of [Mortus](Mortus.md) before being killed or dragged into [the Depths](The_Depths.md) by what is presumed to be a [Nautilodaunt](Nautilodaunt.md). [\[1\]](#cite-note-mortus-1)

## Trivia

-   [Mortus](Mortus.md) misses his wife very much and says "too soon it were" about her. [\[1\]](#cite-note-mortus-1)
