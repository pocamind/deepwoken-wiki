## Description

[Mortus](Mortus.md)' late wife was known to fish alongside her husband. One day, the pair attempted to fish up an "octopus", likely a [Nautilodaunt](Nautilodaunt.md), by luring it to land. Unfortunately for her, the creature proved more dangerous than expected and killed her. Mortus swore off attempting to catch more of these creatures.[\[1\]](#cite-note-mortus-1)

Mortus later recounted this tale to [the Pathfinder](The_Pathfinder.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-mortus-1)
