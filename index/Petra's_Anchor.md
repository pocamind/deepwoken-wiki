## Description

**Petra's Anchor** is an endgame Greathammer that can be obtained from the [Dread Serpent](Dread_Serpent.md) or merchant ships, albeit at a rather high price. This weapon has a base damage of 26 with a swing speed of 0.8x, and requires 75 HVY and 10 STR to equip.

## Critical

The user briefly winds up before rushing forward, briefly ragdolling the target when hit. If landed, the user can follow up using an M1 to initiate an overhead strike that ragdolls and sends the target flying.

The critical has a cooldown of **9 seconds**.
