# Stardust

An [Adret](Adret.md) [Linkstrider](Oaths.md) holding Stardust.

## Description

_"A piece of the very stars, allowing one to cross the world's webs"._

## Uses

Stardust is used by the Linkstrider oath to gate to various places. These gate locations are unlocked via interacting with meteors found around the map.

## Obtainment

Stardust is obtained by finding it at Star Trees located at:

-   [Starfield Tundra](Starfield_Tundra.md)
-   [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md)
-   [Starfield Veldt](Starfield_Veldt.md)
