## Description

The Divers is a faction implanted in The Central Luminant at the Island of Markor. They are more notably seen in [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

They are an organization focused on the traversal and research of The Depths, discovering and reporting valuable data to the surface to their father faction, The Authority.
