## Description

**Warden's Armor Piece** is a direct drop from [Titus Fulminus Destellus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md)'s chest.

## Uses

-   Used as a crafting material for the following items:
    -   [Titus's Raiment](<Titus's_Raiment.md>) Master Armor
    -   [Specialist Helmet](Specialist_Helmet.md)
    -   [Reinforced War Helmet](Reinforced_War_Helmet.md)
    -   [Authority Voltspark Mask](Authority_Voltspark_Mask.md)

## Trivia

-   This item and the [Titus' Armor Piece](<Titus'_Armor_Piece.md>) have the second highest sell value out of all other items in the game, being 6,000.
    -   With [Snake Oil](Talents.md#silvertongue), you can sell this for 8,400.
