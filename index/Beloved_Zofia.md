# Beloved Zofia

LocationL Beloved Zofia LocationR

An abandoned ship lost to time, now corrupted by the spirits of the Voidsea.

  

## Overview

**Beloved Zofia** is a sunken ship added to **Deepwoken** on March 6th, 2023, it has a very similar appearance to [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>), the ship which the [tutorial](Tutorial.md) takes place in. It is located in the **[Voidsea](Voidsea.md)** of the Etrean Luminant. One can find it by following the Minityrsa wall and taking a hard left on the corner. After turning, keep going straight until you spot the ship.
