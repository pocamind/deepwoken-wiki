# Beloved Zofia

### An abandoned ship lost to time, now corrupted by the spirits of the Voidsea.

**Beloved Zofia** is a sunken ship added to **Deepwoken** on March 6th, 2023, located in the **Voidsea.**

The island contains a shrine named **"Dreadstar"**, required for the **Ghost quest**, located at the **Isle of Vigils**, and the **[Silentheart](Oaths.md)** oath obtainment quest.

This is likely the same ship as the one in the [Castaway](Origins.md) tutorial, as the inside and top seem to be the same.

Notably, There are no real major damages to the ship, spite it being mostly sunk that is.

The area in which an antiquarian resided in the tutorial is replaced by the "Dreadstar"

Another thing to be noted, Is that the ghosts inside the ship seem to be friendly..? To the player after the small scuffle
