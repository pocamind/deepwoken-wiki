# Beloved Zofia

Template:Location2

File:Beloved Zofia Closer Print -D.png

## Overview

The **Beloved Zofia** is a wrecked ship found in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) behind [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md). The ship is similar in appearance to [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>), where the [Tutorial](Tutorial.md) takes place.

## Contents

### [The Dreadstar](The_Dreadstar.md)

-   Required to speak to for the obtainment of [Silentheart](Oath%253A_Silentheart.md).

### [Ghosts](Ghost.md)

A ghost can appear at the ship's helm. If the player has killed no one, the ghost says, "Remember the dead." However, if the player has killed anyone, the ghost says, "...Did you think we'd forget?" and four weak, short-lived apparitions attack.

-   Speaking to a ghost and fighting them is required for the completion of [Suyuan](Suyuan.md)'s [Quest](Quests.md).

## Location

The Beloved Zofia can be found by hugging the Minityrsa wall and making a left turn at its corner. After turning, keep going straight until the ship is spotted.

## Trivia

-   While the _Beloved Zofia_ is not the ship in the tutorial, they share similar - if not the exact same model. The Beloved Zofia is just a slanted version of the [The Scoundrel's Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>). This is likely just due to them both being the same make of ship.
-   The ship is named after the [Beloved Zofia](https://fireemblem.fandom.com/wiki/Beloved_Zofia), the signature weapon of Celica in _Fire Emblem Echoes: Shadows of Valentia._
