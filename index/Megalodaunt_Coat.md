# Megalodaunt Coat

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Megalodaunt Coat is an _arms_ item. Equipping this item will give you random placeholder stats. While it might have low stats, the recent update has made it a bit better with requiring only 15 levels instead of 35, also known for many players to use it for its appearance. It specializes in Monster-related stats.
