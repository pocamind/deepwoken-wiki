# Megalodaunt Coat

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

The Megalodaunt Coat is a arms item. Equipping this item will give you random placeholder stats. It has relatively low stats, but many players use it for its appearance.

## Trivia

You can smelt a stronger armor's stats onto this armor, keeping its appearance but getting better stats
