## Description

The Megalodaunt Coat is an animated [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. It can only be obtained as a rare drop from [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md).

## Trivia

-   The Megalodaunt Coat is one of two equipment items with the Damage Vs Monsters stat, the other being [Pathfinder Elite](Pathfinder_Elite.md).
    -   Due to Pathfinder Elite having better pips, it is seen as the better of the two.
-   The power requirement for the coat used to be much higher, but was later reduced as anyone who could meet the power requirement would already have far better options.
    -   The player is able to obtain this item from [Mechalodaunts](Mechalodaunt.md), who do not even possess hides, nor any visible biological matter whatsoever.
