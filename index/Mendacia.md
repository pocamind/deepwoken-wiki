# Mendacia

| Mendacia | |
|---|---|

## Description

Mendacia is a Tiran with black diver outfit NPC that gives you the quest of saving here in Layer 2, she proclaims to be a Arch-Diver in charge of the Interstice Skies, upon completing her quest your awarded with 2 knowledge and some lore information. You can find her in the Ethironial Outskirts once you spawn in layer either by lighthook or jumping into the void, she will be in some rocks remaining invisible until the player interacts with her. When the player interacts with Mendacia she will manifest in a "Ghost" form saying that you need to free her from New Kyrsa in Floor 2 saying that she is being imprisioned by the Kyrsgarde, Once you complete here quest and "free her" she will appear before the Scion of Ethiron boss-fight revealing herself as the Voice of Ethiron, mocking you and revealing she tricked you into awakening the Scion of Ethiron saying that she was holding a form of a "Scum meat bag of your foolishness". At the end of her dialogue she awards you with two knowledge.

## Quest

After interacting for the first time with her in the 1st floor, near the spawn, you'll need to interact with her in the Firfire caves, between the entrance and the fake floor hole between some spikes of stone. Shell tell you to go to the Cathedral of Ethiron to enter the city of New Kyrsa.

\-Upon arriving to New Kyrsa she will be in front of the first obelisk near the spawn of Floor 2, she will tell you to activate the 5 Obelisk in New Kyrsa to "free" her from her prison. Upon activating the 5 Obelisk she will be near the entrance to the Scion of Ethiron boss fight (Near the long tunnel) revealing herself and her true intentions calling you "LITTLE GODSEEKER" revealing that she tricked you into awakening the Scion Of Ethiron.

  
The rewards of the quest is 2 Knowledge.

## Trivia

Once you encounter her in the 1 floor and go back to Klaris you can ask her about the Arch-divers and the Interstice Skies, Klaris warns you about the Arch-Divers, but doesnt know about the Interstice skies.
