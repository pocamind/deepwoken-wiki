# Songwright

The Songwright next to the Mystic

They are an NPC in the Song room([Guild Base](Guild_Bases.md)). They can be hired after building 9+ rooms for 20,000 Notes.

They can upgrade your mantras of any attunement including attunementless. Unlike the other mantra trainers, they cost 50% more Notes per upgrade, totaling 1500 notes per level 5 mantra.
