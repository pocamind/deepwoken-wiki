# Songwright

The Songwright next to the Mystic

They are an NPC in the Song room of a [Guild Base](Guild_Bases.md). They can be hired after building 9+ rooms for 20,000 Notes.

They can upgrade your mantras of any [attunement](Attunements.md) including [attunementless](Attunement-less.md). Unlike the other mantra trainers, they cost 50% more Notes per upgrade, totaling 1500 notes per level 5 mantra.

Costs for each level up:

-   LVL 2: 150 notes
-   LVL 3: 300 notes
-   LVL 4: 450 notes
-   LVL 5: 600 notes

Additionally, there is a requirement for each upgrade level that correlates to the attribute or element associated with each mantra.

Requirements to level up:

-   LVL 2: 20 Stat
-   LVL 3: 40 Stat
-   LVL 4: 60 Stat
-   LVL 5: 80 Stat
