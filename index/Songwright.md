# Songwright

| Songwright | |
|---|---|

## Description

The Songwright was a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) NPC. This NPC functioned as a Mantra trainer for all [Attunements](Attunements.md), including [Attunement-less](Attunement-less.md), allowing you to comfortably level any Mantra on the fly.

The Songwright's appearance varies from guild to guild, but they are always a [Chrysid](Chrysid.md) wearing the [Pathfinder Arch-Sorcerer](Pathfinder_Arch-Sorcerer.md) outfit.

## Trivia

-   The Songwright can be hired for 20,000 in the Song Room after obtaining at least 9 rooms total.
-   The Songwright charges 50% more Notes to level your Mantras than any other Attunement trainer.
    -   Leveling a Mantra from level 1 to level 2 costs 150 and 20 stat investment.
    -   Leveling a Mantra from level 2 to level 3 costs 300 and 40 stat investment.
    -   Leveling a Mantra from level 3 to level 4 costs 450 and 60 stat investment.
    -   Leveling a Mantra from level 4 to level 5 costs 600 and 80 stat investment.
    -   Fully upgrading from level 1 to 5 will cost you 1,500 total Notes.
