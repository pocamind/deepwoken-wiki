# Duelist's Attire

Template:ArmorInfobox**Duelist's Attire** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit has no cost and requires five (5) Glass Stones.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 5.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1200 durability and grants +20% Physical resistance and +10% Slash resistance.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)
-   [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md)

## Trivia/Notes

-   Identical or similar to the armor worn by Celtor Soldiers in the City of the Drowned.
