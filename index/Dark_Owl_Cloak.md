## Description

The Dark Owl Cloak is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping this item will give you the talent Disbelief, as well as extra randomized stats based on the amount of pips. It can be obtained from Layer 2 chests, or as a rare drop from [Deep Owls](Deep_Owl.md), or as a drop from [Deep widows](Deep_Widow.md) and [Chime of conflict](Chime_of_Conflict.md).

## Trivia

-   This item is part of the Dark Owl series consisting of the [Dark Owl Chapeau](Dark_Owl_Chapeau.md) and the Dark Owl Cape.
