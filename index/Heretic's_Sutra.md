# Heretic's Sutra

[Heretic's Sutra](Talents.md) is a [Talent](Talents.md) that can be obtained in Layer 2 of The Depths.

File:Heretic's sutra card.png

**_Card for Heretic's Sutra_**

**Obtainment:**

It's obtainment involves 80 Willpower and Tier 1 [Insanity](Insanity.md) by which you would then head to Layer 2 by either void or by Klaris, and you would then locate [Kasen](Kasen.md). You can watch this video here for a tutorial on how to get it:

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVUnkoyqOHo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVUnkoyqOHo)

**Abilities:**

The use of Heretic's Sutra causes you to gain 35% insanity instantly, which can be useful if you are using talents such as Lose your Mind, in which Heretic's Sutra can be crucial for that eventual +15% damage buff. If you are under 80% sanity you will only gain 15% insanity upon usage. It is advised that you also get Unfazed with this, as it would negate many of the the damaging side effects of insanity.
