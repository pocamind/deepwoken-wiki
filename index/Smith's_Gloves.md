## Description

Smith's Gloves are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping them grants you +5 Health, and 2 Pips. They are uncommonly found in chests at [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>), [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md), [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md), [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md), [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md), [Maestro Evengarde Rest](Maestro_Evengarde_Rest.md) and both the [first](First_Layer.md) and [second](Second_Layer.md) layers of The Depths.
