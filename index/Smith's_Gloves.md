## Description

Smith's Gloves are an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category, and is a part of the Smith Set. When worn, this grants +5 Health and +2 Rare Pips.

## Trivia

-   Worn by all [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) NPCs, alongside the rest of the Smith set, though the Blacksmith's variant of this item has leather hand protection on their left hand.
