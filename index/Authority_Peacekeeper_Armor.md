# Authority Peacekeeper Armor

Template:ArmorInfobox**Authority Peacekeeper Armor** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber and one (1) piece of Iron.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 5 and [Fortitude](Attributes.md) 5.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1600 durability and grants +15% Physical resistance, +10% Elemental resistance, +10% Slash resistance and +15% Fire resistance.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Now You See Me_, which increases your [Stealth](Stealth.md) while crouching.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   Sold by [Nyanna](Nyanna.md) in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)
-   [Primadon](Primadon,_Titan_of_the_East.md) Chests

## Trivia

-   This is the standard uniform of The Authority.
    -   Notably, the [Authority Captain Uniform](Authority_Captain_Uniform.md) is just this but with a red coat over it.
-   One of the best Adept armors in game because of the low cost, excellent durability, and the material requirements.
-   Sadly, despite being an Adept armor, the only way of obtaining it is in the first layer of [The Depths](The_Depths.md), [Scyphozia](First_Layer.md).
