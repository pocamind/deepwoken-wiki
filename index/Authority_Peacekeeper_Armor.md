# Authority Peacekeeper Armor

Template:ArmorInfobox**Authority Peacekeeper Armour** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires five (5) Cloth and Fiber as well as one (1) Iron.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 5 and 5 [Fortitude](Attributes.md).

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1600 durability, grants +15% Physical resistance, +10% Elemental resistance, +10% Slash resistance, and +15% Fire resistance. Additionally, it provides the [Lowstride](Talents.md#assassin) Talent.

-   Due to resistances being combined multiplicatively instead of additively, the total resistances are: 23.5% Slash, 15% Blunt, 23.5% Flamecharm, and 10% Elemental.

The [Lowstride](Talents.md#assassin) Talent increases your [Stealth](Stealth.md) & Speed while crouching, as well as removing the noise equipping a weapon makes.

## Trivia

-   This is the standard uniform of The Authority.
    -   Notably, the [Authority Captain Uniform](Authority_Captain_Uniform.md) is just this but with a red coat over it.
-   One of the best Adept armors in game because of the low cost, excellent durability, and the material requirements.
-   Despite being an Adept armor, the only chests having its schematics are green chests from [The Depths](The_Depths.md), [Scyphozia](First_Layer.md).
