# Kyrsan Shrine of Arrival

LocationL Kyrsan Shrine of Arrival LocationR

A brooding darkness welcomes those adventuring far below the Gale.

  

File:Kyrsan shrine o arrival.png

_The Kyrsan Shrine of Arrival_

The Kyrsan Shrine of Arrival is the first thing adventurers will find upon entering the second floor of [Layer 2.](Second_Layer.md)

It is a carved-out dark cave with statues on each wall.

When the space-time collapse takes place within New Kyrsa for the first time, you will be sent back here and forced to reenter the city. This event taking place twice will resort in you being torn away from reality and wiping.

There is not a lot of use for this place other than acting as a spawn point and being the gateway to the rest of New Kyrsa.
