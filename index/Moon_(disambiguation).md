## Trivia

-   The [Moonseye](Moonseye.md) is the counterpart to the [Suncross](Suncross.md).
-   The [Astral](Astral.md) [Enchant](Enchantments.md) was originally an exclusive drop from the [Star Knight](Star_Knight.md), until multiple miscellaneous sources including the [Moon Knights](Moon_Knight.md) was added.
-   The [Moon Brow Rings](Moon_Brow_Rings.md), [Moon Septum Ring](Moon_Septum_Ring.md), [Moon Lip Ring](Moon_Lip_Ring.md), [Moon Dermal Rings](Moon_Dermal_Rings.md) was released alongside [Brow Stud](Brow_Stud.md), [Dermal Stud](Dermal_Stud.md), [Nose Stud](Nose_Stud.md), and [Snakebite Studs](Snakebite_Studs.md) on the [October 18th, 2025](Version_History/2025.md#october-18th-2025) update.
-   [Carrion's Moonseye Scalpel](<Carrion's_Moonseye_Scalpel.md>) used to be a spec exclusive weapon that later released to the public as [Moonseye Scalpel](Moonseye_Scalpel.md).
    -   Moonseye Scalpel then got removed from the game, and later, an identical looking weapon called [Rifle Spear](Rifle_Spear.md) was added.
-   There are 2 [Outfits](Outfits.md) named "Moonseye": [Moonseye (Outfit)](<Moonseye_(Outfit).md>) and [Moonseye (Spec Outfit)](<Moonseye_(Spec_Outfit).md>). The former is exclusive to [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md), and the latter is a [Developer Spec](Developer_Specs.md)
