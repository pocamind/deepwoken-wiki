# Etris Palace

File:EtrisPalace.png

Etris Palace is a palace in Etris. It is surrounded by [palace guards](<Etrean_Royal_Guard_(Enemy).md>) on the inside and outside. If you manage to make it past the palace guards and reach the second floor you will be surrounded by what looks like black spikes and killed for trespassing. There is what looks like a king on the second floor, sitting on a throne.
