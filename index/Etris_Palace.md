# Etris Palace

LocationL Etris Palace LocationR

TERRITORY OF Etrea

An imposing aura of pride, tradition, and dignity courses through the halls of the estate.

  

**Etris Palace** is a palace in [Etris](Etris.md) located in [The King's Garden](<The_King's_Garden.md>). It is surrounded by palace [Etrean Royal Guard](<Etrean_Royal_Guard_(Enemy).md>) on the inside and outside.

The palace has two floors, the first has some unremarkable rooms, and the second [Lord Regent](The_Lord_Regent.md), needed to obtain the key for [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md) or the [Contractor Oath](Oath%253A_Contractor.md). If you do not have an active quest gain on the aforementioned Duke, the Lord Regent will simply kill you when you approach him. Above the palace, there's an orb necessary for progressing the [Jetstriker](Oath%253A_Jetstriker.md) oath
