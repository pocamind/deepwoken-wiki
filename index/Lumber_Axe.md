# Lumber Axe

## **Description**

File:Axe.png

Axe in general store

Axe used to cut down trees.

There are two ways to obtain the axe.

-   By talking to [an NPC who is either in Etris and Isle of Vigils.](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Quests#Coexisting_Quests%7C)
-   Buying it for 3 Notes in the General Store or at the Dock in Etris
