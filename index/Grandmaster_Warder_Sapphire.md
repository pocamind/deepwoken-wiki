# Grandmaster Warder Sapphire

Grandmaster Warder Sapphire is a Chrysid NPC that join the Warder group of the Justicars.

Justicar players upon reaching the rank of 'Elder', may talk to her to join the Warder group of the Justicars.

Just like Grandmaster Vanguard Legidae, she also encourages the idea of being around with 'like-minded individuals', which leads to the formation of groups. We can assume that the Vanguard and Warder groups are the major groups of the Justicar.
