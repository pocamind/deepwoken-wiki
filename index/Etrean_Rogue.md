# Etrean Rogue

Template:ArmorInfobox**Etrean Rogue** is a Placeholder [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires three (3) Crystal Lenses and three (3) Spark Glands.

This Outfit requires 30 [Agility](Attributes.md).

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 500 durability and grants +20% Slash resistance.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) "_Test"_, which does absolutely nothing.

## Location

This Outfit can only be obtained by having once owned the [Wyvern](Wyvern.md), [Wyvern Officer](Wyvern_Officer.md) or [WyvernLeader](WyvernLeader.md) Outfits and having worn them at the time of their deletion.

## Trivia

-   This Outfit is a placeholder; its existence is not indicative of an actual Outfit being added to the game.
