**Etrean Rogue** is a Placeholder [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit requires three (3) [Crystal Lenses](Mantra_Modifiers.md#regular-modifiers) and three (3) [Spark Glands](Spark_Gland.md) to craft.

This Outfit additionally requires the player to have 30 [Agility](Attributes.md#agl) to be crafted.

Though, crafting this Outfit is not actually possible; pressing craft will do absolutely nothing even if you meet the requirements to do so.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 500 durability and grants +20% Slash resistance ResistanceSlash.

This Outfit also grants the **Test** Talent, which does absolutely nothing.

## Trivia

-   Etrean Rogue shares an appearance with [Stranded](Stranded.md).
-   This Outfit is a placeholder; its existence is not indicative of an actual Outfit being added to the game.
-   Etrean Rogue can be temporarily obtained by equipping a newly added Outfit and then joining a server in which said Outfit does not exist. This is due to Etrean Rogue being a placeholder item.
-   Etrean Rogue can only be permanently obtained by having once owned the [Wyvern](Wyvern.md), [Wyvern Officer](Wyvern_Officer.md) or [WyvernLeader](WyvernLeader.md) Outfits and having worn them at the time of their deletion.
-   Even if you do manage to have this item equipped, you technically won't have it crafted. Crafting this item is not possible.
-   If you change Outfits while having Etrean Rogue equipped, it will be permanently lost.
