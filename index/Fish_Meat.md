## Description

**Fish Meat** is a craftable [Food](Food.md) item and crafting ingredient. It can be crafted from any type of [Fish](Fishing.md) and is used in recipes to cook different kinds of Food.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 5% Stomach and 2% Water.

## Crafting

### Recipe

-   Any [Fish](Fishing.md). Different types of fish will craft into different amounts of Fish Meat:
    -   Sea Bass crafts into 1.
    -   Red Snapper crafts into 2.
    -   Tuna crafts into 3.
    -   Swordfish crafts into 4.

### Used in

-   [Cooked Fish](Cooked_Fish.md) - 1 **Fish Meat**, at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
-   [Fish Omelette](Fish_Omelette.md) - 2 [Eggs](Egg.md), 1 **Fish Meat** at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md).
-   [Fish Stew](Fish_Stew.md) - 1 [Squid](Squid.md), 1 **Fish Meat** OR 1 [Seaweed Bundle](Seaweed_Bundle.md), 2 **Fish Meat** at a [Campfire](Campfire_Pit.md) (for both recipes).
