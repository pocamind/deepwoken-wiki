## Description

Guiding Star is a bow obtained by completing "Lodestar Bounty" Job which spawns a single npc with gale/frostdraw mantras and the blindseer oath. This Job has been proven to spawn at:

-   Summer Isle

-   Starswept Valley

-   Songseeker

It is the first "Bow" category weapon. The Guiding Star uses your ether to attack, however you can still attack with 0 ether, it just does reduced damage. The attacks for the Guiding Star have range fall-off, meaning that they do decreased damage at longer ranges.

## Critical

Fires an explosive long ranged arrow three times in a row
