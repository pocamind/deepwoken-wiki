## Description

Neophyte Jefferson is a [Canor](Canor.md) NPC. He is a new and inexperienced recruit of [the Justicar](The_Justicar.md).

## Location

Neophyte Jefferson is located on [Alsin's Landing](<Alsin's_Landing.md>), standing under a tent beside [Elder Sasha](Elder_Sasha.md) and [Disciple Gobletto](Disciple_Gobletto.md).
