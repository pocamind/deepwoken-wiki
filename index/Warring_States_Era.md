# Warring States Era

The Warring States Era is an April fools event similar to [Mudwoken](Mudwoken.md), where 3 teams compete for points by killing enemies from the other faction (10 points in the overworld, 2 in chime), Solo'ing Bosses, And Fishing. The list of teams includes:

-   The Blue skippers
-   The Red daunts
-   The Green threshers
