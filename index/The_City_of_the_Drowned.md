# The City of the Drowned

## The City of the Drowned

The main city at the center of [The Depths](The_Depths.md) housing two main districts, the District of Commerce and Varicosa District. The city also houses the only way to leave the depths, through the elevator in the Cathedral of Interstice at the center.

The City of the Drowned is composed of large Attack on Titan-like walls over a Central Authority-controlled area. There are references to there being a similar city in the upcoming Central Luminant coming in Update 1.
