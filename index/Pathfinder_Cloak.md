# Pathfinder Cloak

Template:ArmorInfobox**Pathfinder Cloak** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

## Statistics

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires four (4) Cloth and Fiber and six (6) pieces of Iron.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 15.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1200 durability and grants +20% Physical resistance and +5% Slash resistance.

This Outfit also grants the [Talent Card](Talents.md) _Environmentalist_, which reduces the damage from certain weather effects.

## Location

This Outfit's Schematic can be found in:

-   [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md)
-   Near the campfire at Isle of Vigils.
-   Sold by Nyanna in [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)
