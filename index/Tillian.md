## Description

Tillian is a unique NPC that found during the [Carnival of Hearts](Carnival_of_Hearts.md) World Event. Upon finding and interacting with him in the surface, he will take you to the Carnival of Hearts.

He is a young albino Canor sporting long, messy, and spiky white hair. Tillian's pupils are unique in the way they resemble an upward purple crescent. Furthermore, he has orange eyeliner, unique markings, and a 4-pointed star on one eye (with a basic gradient on the other). Tillian also has a piercing sitting below his lips, which is only shown in his [carnival attire](<Trickster's_Habit.md>).

Outside of the Carnival of Hearts, Tillian wears [Seamstress's Attire](<Seamstress's_Attire.md>).

He is the brother of [Aelita](Aelita.md), and like his sister, both take the [Visionshaper Oath](Oath%253A_Visionshaper.md).
