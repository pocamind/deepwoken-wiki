## Description

Tillian is a unique NPC that found during the [Carnival of Hearts](Carnival_of_Hearts.md) World Event. Upon finding interacting with him in the surface, he will take you to the Carnival of Hearts.

He is a young albino Canor sporting long, messy, and spiky white hair. Tillian's pupils are unique in a way that they resemble an upward purple crescent. Furthermore, he has orange eyeliner dons unique markings; a 4-pointed star on one eye, whereas the other is a basic gradient. Tillian has a piercing sitting below his lips, which is only shown in his [carnival attire](<Trickster's_Habit.md>).

Outside of the Carnival of Hearts, Tillian wears [Seamstress's Attire](<Seamstress's_Attire.md>).

He is the brother of [Aelita](Aelita.md), and like his sister, both take the [Visionshaper Oath](Oath%253A_Visionshaper.md).
