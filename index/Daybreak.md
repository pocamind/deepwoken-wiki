# Daybreak

**Content is not present in-game**  
The subject of this page is mentioned in-game, but is not tangibly implemented into the game.  
E.G. a character mentioned in a book doesn't exist as an interactible NPC.

## Description

The Daybreak was the radiant counterpart to the [Night Axe](Night_Axe.md), forged by the ancient folk of [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) as its luminous twin.[\[1\]](#cite-note-na-1)

Together, the two weapons were wielded by [Ekrim](Ekrim.md), the Third's Eclipse of the Rouhlx Dyad, their combined might becoming the foundation of countless tales throughout the old settlements.[\[1\]](#cite-note-na-1) The history of the duo was said to be whispered in tales of unstoppable might and haunting interplay of night and day.[\[1\]](#cite-note-na-1)

According to now fragmented accounts, both weapons ultimately met their end at the hands of the very power their wielders once worshipped, leaving the Daybreak to pass into legend alongside the Night Axe.[\[1\]](#cite-note-na-1)
