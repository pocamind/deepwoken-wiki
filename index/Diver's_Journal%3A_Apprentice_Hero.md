# Diver's Journal: Apprentice Hero

## Description

_**Diver's Journal: Apprentice Hero**_ is a [Book](Books.md) found in the first floor of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md).

## Text

### 

Diver's Journal: Apprentice Hero

> \- Eternal Gale Expeditionary Log This is the third day without food. Chaser refuses to let us near the Ignition Union's settlement here.
> 
> I understand we're at odds with the Union, but... I'm hungry. I'm cold. They have heat there, so I've been told. Their lamps, they can burn away the parasites. A few members of the expedition got fed up with Chaser, and tried to sneak away, but they weren't in their bunks the following morning.
> 
> Maybe they did get away, and are staying with the Union now? Skeli told me the Union's settlement was along the ground floor beneath the bridges, marked by steam towers, but.. I heard it's dangerous. Bounders reside there. I don't know what to do.. I may just seek out the settlement myself. I need warmth.
> 
> * * *
> 
> The fourth day has come. I broke off from the group. Chaser's determined to let us all starve. I won't die for his ego.
> 
> I made good progress, but I'm growing weaker by the hour. It's getting hard to even pick my feet up off the ground. The snow's bound to be filled with protein, right? Maybe there's a way to cook it.
> 
> I'm running out of options. It's better to take a gamble than starve, right?
> 
> If my luck runs out, I'm dead anyway.
> 
> * * *
> 
> All things said, I feel a bit better now. It was hard to stomach the things, but I can feel my fingers again, so I'll take that minor victory.
> 
> Even if the parasites survived, if I have the energy to make it to the Union base I'm sure they can treat me before it gets too bad.
> 
> I just have to put one foot in front of the other.
> 
> * * *
> 
> They survived.
> 
> It burns something fierce, but I can feel the adrenaline in my veins propelling me forwards.
> 
> Not far now.
> 
> I'll write again once I make it to the base, I can't waste the energy right now.
