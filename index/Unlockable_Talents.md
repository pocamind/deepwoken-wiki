# Unlockable Talents

“ A discovery to pass down... ”

― Talent unlock quote

**Unlockable Talents** are Talents that the player can obtain upon wiping when certain conditions are met for future usage. This is not to be confused with [Regalias](Regalias.md).

Whenever the player meets a requirement for an unlockable Talent, upon finishing the dialogue with 'You' in Fragments of Self and taking the \[The End\] option, the Talent will appear on the left of the screen with the quote "A discovery to pass down..."

When this is done, these cards are added to the card pool of the player and can be drawn normally like normal cards.

## Talents

-   Termite \[Common Talent, Fortitude\] - You can eat things that most would consider inedible.
    -   Gain the ability to consume non-food items. (+3 health)
    -   Obtainment: Wipe with Iron Gut.

-   Survival Kit \[Common Talent, Mind (20 Willpower, Intelligence, or Charisma)\] - You keep a well-stocked set of tools on your person at all times. Your flint never runs out of uses.
    -   Flint have infinite durability.
    -   Obtainment: Wipe with Iron Gut and Termite.
