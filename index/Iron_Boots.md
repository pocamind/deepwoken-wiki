# Iron Boots

Template:EquipmentStats

## Description

Iron Boots are accessories under the "Boots" category. They can be found through chests in [The Depths](The_Depths.md).

Iron Boots are part of the "Iron" set, with the only other being the [Iron Pauldrons](Iron_Pauldrons.md).
