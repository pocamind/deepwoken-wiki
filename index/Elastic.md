## Description

Elastic is a debuffing enchantment that tethers your opponent on hit.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to a light pink and covers it with a pink haze.

## Effect

Elastic applies the [Tethered](Status_Effects.md#tethered) status effect on M1, pulling your opponent towards yourself.

### Notes

-   Elastic cannot proc on [Bullets](Bullets.md).
-   Elastic's effects can be procced on weapon criticals if they have the M1 tag.
-   Elastic has a range limit of 92.5 studs.
