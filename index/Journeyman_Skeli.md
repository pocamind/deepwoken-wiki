## Description

**Journeyman Skeli** was a [Diver](The_Divers.md), participating in the second expedition to the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md). His team was sent by [Klaris](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) to retrieve [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md), a [Black Diver](Black_Divers_division.md), and the first expedition team.[\[1\]](#cite-note-journal-ii-1)

He suspects [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) of being their enemy long before he openly kills everyone. In his [journal](Diver_Journal,_II.md) he talks about his suspicion on [Chaser](Chaser,_Scholar_of_the_Crimson_Contract.md) and how he cannot be trusted and granted access to [the Forbidden City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md). It is unknown how Skeli met his demise, as he scribbled out the last pages of his journal.[\[1\]](#cite-note-journal-ii-1)

He is also mentioned in the [third Diver Journal](Diver_Journal,_III.md), in which he told [Apprentice Hero](<Mauled_Diver_Corpse_(Apprentice_Hero).md>) about [the Frontier Furnace](The_Frontier_Furnace.md)'s location.[\[4\]](#cite-note-journal-iii-4)
