## Description

The True Heart of Enmity is a Boss found in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) of the Depths, located in the [Cathedral of the Interstice](Cathedral_of_the_Interstice.md). The same creature he is in the Pathfinder gamemode, makes it a foe wrought of iron and animosity, incarnated as the [Voice of Enmity](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Voices_of_the_Depths#Voice_of_Enmity). This boss is not optional, as they are mandatory to uncap Power 15 and break the barrier that keeps [Zi'eer](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>) safe.
