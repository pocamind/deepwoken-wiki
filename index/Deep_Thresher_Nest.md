# Deep Thresher Nest

Template:Location2

**The Deep Thresher Nest** is a location in _Deepwoken._ It is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md) found in [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md).

## Overview

The Deep Thresher Nest is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md). Found in a part of the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md) that has been overrun by the [Threshers](Thresher.md), connecting to the cavern on the other side. There are a few [Threshers](Thresher.md) and a [King Thresher](King_Thresher.md) that reside in the nest.

## Points of Interest

-   After entering from the entrance, there is a [King Thresher](King_Thresher.md) in the hall on the right.
-   Deep there's a prison-like hall with several environmental chests with the loot of [the Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md)
-   At the end, there is a cave leading to the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), where there are two [Threshers](Thresher.md), one [King Thresher](King_Thresher.md), and several [Empyrean Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#empyrean-lotus) and [Brilliant Lotus](Primordial_Lotuses.md#brilliant-lotus).
