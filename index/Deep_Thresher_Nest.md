# Deep Thresher Nest

Template:Location2

The **Deep Thresher Nest** is a location in _Deepwoken._ It is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md) found in [Aratel Island](Aratel_Island.md).

## Overview

The Deep Thresher Nest is a [Voidzone](Voidzones.md). Found in a part of the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md) that has been overrun by the Threshers, connecting to the cavern on the other side. There are many [Threshers](Thresher.md) and [King Threshers](King_Thresher.md) living in it.

## Points of Interest

-   After entering from the entrance, there is a king Thresher in the hall on the right.
-   Deep there's a prison-like hall with several environmental chests with the loot of Starswept Valley
-   At the end, there is a cave leading to the [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md), where there are two regular Threshers and one King Thresher. as well as [Empyrean Lotus](Ingredients.md) and [Brilliant Lotus](Ingredients.md).
