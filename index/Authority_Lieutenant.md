# Authority Lieutenant

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

The Authority Lieutenant is a guard NPC found outside the shipwright hut in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).

Their appearance consists of a Authority Captain Uniform, a Shrouded Cape and, unusually for an Authority member, Polarised Sunglasses. They are similar in appearance to the [Authority Captain](Authority_Captain.md) and [Authority Commander](Authority_Commander.md).

## Trivia

-   Along with the [Authority Captain](Authority_Captain.md), they are the only Authority soldiers not to wear helmets.
-   The Authority Lieutenant's dialogue when they are being executed is usually "you're pretty strong...", "how is this even possible?" or "what is someone of your strength doing here...?" the same as the Authority Captain.
-   Authority Lieutenants do not spawn with a weapon, due to a bug, forcing it to parry and block most attacks. They are, however, still capable of using mantras.
