# Merchant Ships

Merchant Ships spawn in the Etrean and Eastern Luminant, most merchant ships have 4 items on sale with one [merchant](Travelling_Merchant.md) sitting in center of bottom deck of the ship.

File:Screenshot 2023-06-20 at 13.45.50.png

Example of expensive item

Items that are sold can range from 10 - 4000 [Notes](Notes.md) (estimated)

There is one thing you should be aware of while searching for merchant ships in the Eastern Luminant, is that if you happen to find a merchant ship that appears to be on fire, the ship will have a [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) abord it if you aren't able to kill a [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) the best option will be to turn your boat away. Even if you were unlucky and the [Megalodaunt](Megalodaunt.md) caught sight of you, you will most likely be able to get away from it, if you find yourself in a server with slow boating speeds try main-menuing to escape it.

Fun facts about merchant ships,

1 - Although merchant ships are only supposed to be in the overworld a merchant ship was once found in the Ethrion Wastes, there aren't that many records of this happening and was most likely not intended by the developers.

File:Screenshot 2023-06-20 at 14.08.59.png

A screen shot of a Merchant ship

2 - In one instance someone was able to find [Petra's Anchor](<Petra's_Anchor.md>) being sold in a merchant ship, this is extremely rare because [Petra's Anchor](<Petra's_Anchor.md>) is a item that can only be dropped from the dread serpent.
