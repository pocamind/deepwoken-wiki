# Merchant Ships

## Overview

File:Screenshot 2023-06-20 at 14.08.59.png

Merchant Ships are random events that spawn in both Luminants. They appear as a Schooner with blue sails, within the lower deck of which is [Chili](Travelling_Merchant.md) and 4 random items on sail; 1 weapon, 1 equipment piece, 1 high-tier food item and 1 book. The armor and weapon, notably, have a chance to be a rare mythical/legendary tier item, selling at a vastly increased price.

The weapons and armors are sold for an estimated 50-200 notes (3500-4500 if high-tier), whereas the food and books sell for about 15-30 notes[\[1\]](#cite-note-1).
