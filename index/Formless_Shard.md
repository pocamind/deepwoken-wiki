## Formless Shard

A Formless Shard can be obtained via using a [Smith's Alloy](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) on a valid weapon and selecting the Motif option, granting you it's Motif and turning the weapon into a Formless Shard.

Can be put on weapons that share the same type (_testing required on compatibility_).

Banking the weapon with a motif will void the Motif.

Despite there being no point in doing so, you **can** enchant it.

Motif Stones **ARE NOT** bankable.
