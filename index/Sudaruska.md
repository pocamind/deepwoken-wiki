## Description

Sudaruska, known as the Unbroken, was an inhabitant of [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) prior to it's colonization by [Etrea](Etrea.md)'s [Clan Kobura](Clan_Kobura.md)[\[2\]](#cite-note-2) and the [The Ministry](The_Ministry.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-gran-1)

When the island was invaded by the Ministry, Sudaruska fought to protect her homeland from them and eventually found herself as the island's last defender. However, she was mortally wounded in the process. Sudaruska made the choice to imbue her being into her greataxe, giving her the necessary strength to cut down hundreds of the invaders, but ultimately died in the process. In her place, her self and the axe became one, turning it into the [Gran Sudaruska](Gran_Sudaruska.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-gran-1)

Tragically, Sudaruska's death proved to be in vain when the Ministry eventually settled into Minityrsa 38 years after the [Etrean Civil War](Etrean_Civil_War.md).[\[3\]](#cite-note-3)[\[4\]](#cite-note-4)

The tale of Sudaruska's fate spread beyond the shores of Minityrsa. Deirdre looked up to Sudaruska and hoped to possibly replicate the process, but her untimely death made such a task impossible.[\[5\]](#cite-note-5)

## Trivia

-   Give the similarity between its name and her epithet, the [Crypt of the Unbroken](Crypt_of_the_Unbroken.md) may have been made for Sudaruska after her death. This adds an extra layer of sad irony due to its later perversion by the Ministry.
