## Description

Kana was a [Felinor](Felinor.md) NPC located in [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md). They stood by a balcony and using the [Thundercall](Thundercall.md) attunement to make an iron ball float.

They wore the [Keeper](Keeper.md) [Outfit](Outfits.md) with the gold coloring being dark blue instead.
