**Hall of Heroes** is a location in _Deepwoken_. It is one of the unique areas exclusive to the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) gamemode.

## Overview

Hall of Heroes is a temple with large statues of figures presumably from [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md) within the [Kingmaker's Isle](<Kingmaker's_Isle.md>).
