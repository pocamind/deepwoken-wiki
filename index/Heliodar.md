# Heliodar

Template:Races-Main

Rise from the ashes and be born again.

Chance of obtaining race: **0%**

## Description

"The Phoenix Race" - An entirely new race which slightly resembles Tiran however no lore connection is currently made, they have orange eyes that emit a faint orange fire similar to Visionshaper eyes. They also have a unique glowing orange pendant of a phoenix around their neck.

Eclipse (Racial Variant likely) - "For what if the Ash were to never return to flames?"
