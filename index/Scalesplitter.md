## Description

The **Scalesplitter** is a medium twinblade weapon that is currently only known to be obtainable from fishing or the Etris Raid event (confirmed).

## M1s and Aerial/Running

The running M1 on this weapon is slightly delayed, making it a good mix up tool.

When 2 handed, the weapon has no flourish, which can be used for even more mix up potential.

The weapon has custom M1 (for both one and two handed) and Aerial animations, although unlike the running M1 and 2 handing the weapon, there doesn't seem to be anything too special about these.

## Critical

This weapon has 1 critical with a 3~ second cooldown. The weapon moves you forwards slightly and jabs in front of you, pushing enemies away a decent amount (does not ragdoll). Posture damage will have to be tested.
