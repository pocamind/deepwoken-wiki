# Age

Age in Deepwoken is viewable by going to your stats menu. This number increases when it gets to the birth month of your character. (Ex. if my character was born in Autumn, every Autumn my character survives for my character increases their age by 1.)

  

## Notes

-   Some NPCs have dialogue depending on your age, such as the old Etrean in Etrea calling you a Youngster if you're 18 or above
