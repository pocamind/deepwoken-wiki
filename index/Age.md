# Age

Age in Deepwoken is viewable by going to your stats menu. Your age increases every year, it changes when the game season gets to the birth season of your character.(Every 4 days starting from character creation)

## Notes

-   Some NPCs have dialogue depending on your age.
-   It is said that characters suffer from appearance changes the older they get depending on their race, such as getting wrinkles, ~[Canors](Canor.md) growing a mane(?) (This is however not tested)~(I have a 50 year old canor that doesn’t have a mane)
    
-   Your character can age in the character creation screen aswell
    

  

## Effects of aging

The section below will be continually updated after any visual changes, the pictured character below is currently "AGE 65" (been alive since Deepwoken's release). Moon Rings stop your character from changing visually, but they still age.

-   Age 40 - Crow's Feet (wrinkles next to eyes)
-   If you are significantly older than another player of the same race then they get the "Juvenile" title over their name like it would for a family member and you get "Elder" above yours for them.
-   Stage 4 is the last stage of age
