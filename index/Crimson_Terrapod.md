# Crimson Terrapod

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

The [Terrapod](Terrapod.md) is a [monster](Monsters.md) notorious for having weird parry timings and attack animations, having very high movement during attacks and shredding health quickly if you're not careful.

The bloodsoaked crimson variant has higher health, and causes the enemy to face devastating bleed damage on top of their strange timings, making each missed defensive opportunity cost not only more health but also blood, which may prove lethal if you have no way to regain said blood.

## Tips and Notes

_(transcluded from [Terrapod](Terrapod.md))_

-   It's recommended to dodge or just move out of a Terrapod's way instead of parrying due to the confusing parry window and the carelessness of its attacks (they don't auto-track).
    -   By landing a few hits and running to avoid its attacks, you can kill one of these without blocking, parrying or dodging.
-   This monster is hostile towards every other monster in [Upper Erisia](Upper_Erisia.md).
-   Terrapods are one of the only monsters that _cannot_ be snuck up on from any direction.
-   There are three guaranteed spawns for a Terrapod:
    -   One near the entrance of [The Garden Reservoir](The_Garden_Reservoir.md)
    -   One in front of the ruins near the [Legendary Boot](Legendary_Boot.md)
    -   One sitting under a tree behind [Path's Respite](<Path's_Respite.md>) in Lower Erisia
