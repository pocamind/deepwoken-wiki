# Mementos

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

## Mementos

Mementos are an exclusive feature to [Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md). Mementos are progressed with predetermined talents and mantras, and each Memento has unique restrictions. The player unlocks 6 mementos by default: Berserker, Flame Worshipper, Saint Jay, Sightless, Spear Dancer, and Rogue Assassin. Other Mementos can be unlocked through various challenges within Vow of Iron.

Mementos can use any weapon in their listed category regardless of stats but can only equip weapons of their categories and if their final stats meet the requirement. For example, Saint Jay can only use Shields, Greatswords, Greathammers and Greataxes, but cannot equip weapons such as the Kanabo.

Beating the [Shogun of the Prophet's Guard (Temple of Mur)](<Shogun_of_the_Prophet's_Guard_(Temple_of_Mur).md>), [Dravik, The Rat King](Dravik,_The_Rat_King.md), and [Zi'eer, the Fourth Prophet](<Zi'eer,_the_Fourth_Prophet.md>) adds completion marks to your Memento tag in the character creation menu.

In the menu, the Mementos are represented by an Iron Memento, a metal tag worn by certain members of the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md).
