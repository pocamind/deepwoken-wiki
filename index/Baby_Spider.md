# Baby Spider

## Description

The Baby Spider is a now-removed enemy that previously used to spawn in the [Widow's Hollow](<Widow's_Hollow.md>) by being spawned in by the [Deep Widow](Deep_Widow.md). Much like [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md)'s [Mindless Servant](Mindless_Servant.md), they were somewhat weak enemies summoned by a stronger foe. It is part of a group of old enemies that got removed either before the game's release or right after it's release. For the Baby Spider, it was removed shortly after the release, so there is footage of it available.

We are unsure why the Baby Spider got removed. Many speculate it's due to the lag that they caused, seeing as the [Widow's Hollow](<Widow's_Hollow.md>) is not in a Dungeon, meaning it would cause a lot more lag to the server as a whole.
