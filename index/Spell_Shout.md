# Spell Shout

“ You will now shout the name of your spells upon cast. ”

― Talent description

  

## Description

This talent allows your character to shout the name of your mantra when casted.

A bit impractical to use in combat because you are literally telling your opponent your next move. (Unless you have given your mantra a different nickname.)

You can get this talent by passing a Charisma check, or paying 50 Notes to [Valikor](Valikor.md).
