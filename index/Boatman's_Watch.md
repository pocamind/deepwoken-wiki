## Description

The Boatman's Watch is an island surrounded by whirlpools, located within the void sea.

The "Ferryman" can be fought here, more information needed.

## Notes

Boatman's Watch can be found by going straight along the wall from Ministryia into the Void Sea, visible after about 20 seconds of sailing.

By lighting 4 campires on the copper stands around the ritual circle you summon the ferryman, an extremely difficult boss who promises to give you an artifact of sorts if you beat him. (If you are on two lifes when you fight the ferryman you do not immediatly go to depths if you die and instead respawn with your last life intact.)
