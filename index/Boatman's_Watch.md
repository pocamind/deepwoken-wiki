## Description

The Boatman's Watch is an island surrounded by whirlpools, located within the void sea.

The "Ferryman" can be fought here, more information needed.

## Notes

~Currently it is unclear where exactly Boatman's Watch resides within the void sea however it is possible that the island itself is a random encounter that can appear anywhere within the void sea.~

Can be found by going straight along the wall from Ministryia into the Void Sea, visible after about 20 seconds of sailing.

By lighting 4 campires on the copper stands around the ritual circle you summon the ferryman, an extremely difficult boss who promises to give you an artifact of sorts if you beat him.
