## Description

The Kanabo is a Legendary [Heavy Weapon](Weapons.md) that scales with and requires [Strength](Attributes.md) in addition to Heavy. It is a long mace-like hammer that displays pure damage.

## Criticals

The Kanabo possess two different criticals.

<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
  <td>The regular critical is a heavy strike that sends the enemy flying.</td>
