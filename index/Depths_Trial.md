# Depths Trial

\*note, I made this page because I couldn't find any pages that talked about the depths trial, this page in VERY poorly made and I would appreciate if others could help me improve on this page for my main goal in to give knowledge to others who are having trouble with the depths trial. If you notice anything wrong or you just want to add something to this page feel free to edit this page.

The depths trial is located at the tower in the middle of Lost Celtor, beating the trial will send you back to the surface,

Enemies in the Trial,

**POWER 1 - 3 :** The Voices of The Depths will send you to the surface without having to fight anything. If you do this trial with another player who is a higher level than you, the monster that would have spawned for that player would be spawned.

**POWER 4 - 6 :** You will have to fight 1 Mudskipper knight which is a stronger version of a normal Mudskipper.

**POWER 7 - 9 :** You will have to fight 1 Lesser Angel (same Angels that the watchers spawn

**POWER 10 - 14 :** You will have to fight 1 Megladaunt

**POWER 15 - 20 :** You will have to fight 1 Enforcer. If the player is being accompanied by 1 other player of a lower power, a Naughtlidaunt will be spawned.
