# Power

Power, or prestiege, is the leveling mechanic of Deep Woken that enables players to increase their stats as well as unlock new talents, mantras, and select an oath. (Increaseable stats can be found in a list at the bottom of the page)

An example of a player's Power and stat allotment gained through prestieging.

Each level of power alots 5 investment points to a player, these investment points can be put into any of the skills present within the game which are redirected into their actual stats. Once all 5 points have been invested a player will increase their power. Once powered up, players will be allowed to select a Power Card from a hand of four cards which can be a Talent, Mantra, Oath, or rarely a fifth card which is fold. Fold cards will postpone the player's reception of a Card until their next power up. Once a player has powered up again they will be alotted two different hands, one hand for the current power and one for their previously folded power hand. This second hand will be randomized and differ from the previously folded one. Do note however that if you are a shit player with bad lack (and just need to get good at the game) you can actually receive the same exact cards you originally folded because of sheer misfortune.

As players power up further the EXP required to invest a point will increase as well. While there is no known power cap it is theorized that a soft cap exists based on the scaling nature of EXP requirements and unless you are a loser with absolutely no life you will not be able to progress a character further.
