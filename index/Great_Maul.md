## Description

The Great Maul is a greathammer that can be dropped from the [Rock Golem](Rock_Golem.md) and [Merchant Ship](Travelling_Merchant.md) and it requires 65 Heavy Weapons and 30 Strength to wield. The weapon has a Base Damage of 25 and a scaling of 8.3. Notably, the Great Maul has the highest swingspeed of any greathammer at 0.85x.
