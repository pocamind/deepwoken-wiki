## Description

**Titus' Armor Piece** is found from [Titus Fulminus Destellus](Titus_Fulminus_Destellus.md) in his exclusive chest.

## Uses

-   Used as a crafting material for the following items:
    -   [Titus's Raiment](<Titus's_Raiment.md>) Master Armor
    -   [Reinforced War Plate](Reinforced_War_Plate.md)
    -   [Specialist Plate](Specialist_Plate.md)

## Trivia

-   This item and the [Warden's Armor Piece](<Warden's_Armor_Piece.md>) have the second highest sell value out of all other items in the game, being 6,000.
    -   With [Snake Oil](Talents.md#silvertongue), you can sell this for 8,400.
