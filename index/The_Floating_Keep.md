# [The Floating Keep](https://www.roblox.com/library/9840060449/Audio-spellhardt1)

-   **Using exploits, you are able to reach The Floating Keep, however, it is either completely invisible or removed, as it is underneath the floor of the ocean and appears to be absolutely nothing. Attempting to approach the exact location results in being teleported back up to the ocean. Nothing else can be seen besides the green skybox.**
-   It is unknown what this area contains or is supposed to be.
-   Exploits show The Floating Keep is far underneath the ocean, deep in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md).
-   Roughly 19,000 studs out from [Minityrsa.](Minityrsa.md)
-   To the left of the docks of [Etris](Etris.md), The Floating Keep is roughly 12,000 studs out.
