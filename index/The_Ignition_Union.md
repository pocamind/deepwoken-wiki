## Description

The Ignition Union is a faction found throughout the Eastern Luminant and [The Depths](The_Depths.md). They are very steampunk oriented, working with industrial era equipment in Miner's Landing, even having their own reactor, though whether this a nuclear reactor isn't clear. They are a group of miners who specialize in Depths exploration. They are also producers of certain greatswords, such as the [Darksteel Greatsword](Darksteel_Greatsword.md) and [Railblade](Railblade.md),

They are one of the three factions hired by [The Authority](The_Central_Authority.md).

They frequently come to blows with the [Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md), in order to steal their gunpowder and explosives for themselves. Their forward operating base is currently [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).

They became far more prominent in Verse 2, getting an island for themselves called [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) along with helping the player in [Layer 2](Second_Layer.md) of the Depths.

## Reputation

You can increase reputation with them by helping them against encounters on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) and helping them in the upper floors of [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>).
