# Jungle Jim

**[Vow of Iron](Vow_of_Iron.md) Exclusive**  
This page describes content that is exclusive to the **Vow of Iron** gamemode.  
This content is not accessible outside of this gamemode.

Jungle Jim is a Mini Boss That has the Oath Contractor. He only spawns in the VOW OF IRON gamemode, with a Small Chance upon spawning when you Kill any enemy. Can be summoned manually by using [Sanguine Candle](Sanguine_Candle.md) on strange sigil in the Summer Isle ruined castles basement
