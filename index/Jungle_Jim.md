# Jungle Jim

He Bald

Jungle Jim is a Mini Boss That has the Oath Contractor. He only spawns in the VOW OF IRON gamemode, with a Small Chance upon spawning when you Kill any enemy. Can be summoned manually by using [Sanguine candle](Sanguine_Candle.md) on strange sigil in Summer Isle Basement
