# Amashi, Master of Flame

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0In order to obtain [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md), you must bring 5 [Dying Embers](Dying_Embers.md) to Amashi. Alternatively, if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, you must kill them in order to get one of five oath talents. They are found at the [Songseekers Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md) inside the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md).
