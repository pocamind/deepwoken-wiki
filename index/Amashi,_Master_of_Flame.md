# Amashi, Master of Flame

Template:EnemyInfobox1.0

## Description

Amashi, the **Flame Worshipper** is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) who appears to have mastered the [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) Attunement. He seems to be in fine relationship with Songseekers as he is residing and teaching Flamecharm in their temple.

In order to obtain [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md), you must bring 5 [Dying Embers](Dying_Embers.md) to Amashi. Alternatively, if you have obtained the [Silentheart](Oaths.md) oath, you must kill him in order to get one of five oath talents. He is found in the [Songseekers Wilds](Songseeker_Wilds.md) inside the [Songseeker Temple](Songseeker_Temple.md).
