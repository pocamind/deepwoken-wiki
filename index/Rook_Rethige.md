## Description

Root Rethige is an NPC in the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md). He seemingly gives lore about his character, which is based on a tester.

After you say "I see. Can you help me with affording equipment?" to [Raen](Raen.md) you will unlock a new dialogue with this npc allowing you to buy [Whaling Knife](Whaling_Knife.md) for 300 notes
