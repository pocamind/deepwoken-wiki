# Rotten Lot

| Rotten Lot | |
|---|---|
| leader | Cap'n Orlandeau |
| goals | Improve their crew members’ lives |
| dissolved | 1271 CE[1] |

The Rotten Lot is a pirate crew. Not much is known about them, but they are said to travel with crew members who want to improve their lives.[\[2\]](#cite-note-2) Their boat is [The Scoundrel’s Bounty](<The_Scoundrel's_Bounty.md>) and their captain is [Captain Orlandeau](<Cap'n_Orlandeau.md>). However this changed when, on a voyage to Etrea, their boat was attacked by [The Central Authority](The_Central_Authority.md) and capsized[\[3\]](#cite-note-3), leading to the deaths and disappearances of most of the crew — including the Captain.

Some other crew members include:

-   [Flabbernaster Filigree](Flabbernaster_Filingree,_the_First_Mate.md)
    -   A Gremor. She is the First Mate (Second in-command) of the ship. She is also seemingly proficient in combat, as she is said to have ‘devastated’ the player character in their previous spar.[\[4\]](#cite-note-4)
-   [Frank F'alzone](<Frank_F'alzone.md>)
    -   A Celtor, who is potentially the cartographer/janitor of the ship. He is nicknamed ‘The Swabber’ for his abilities in cleaning.
-   [Pete](Pete.md)
    -   A Vesperian, who is found sitting down against the wall on the ship. He seems to be nauseous from a meal that was given to him, along with his seasickness. His role on the ship is unknown.
-   [Mr. Quarters](Mr._Quarters,_Halfmaster.md)
    -   An Adret who serves as the Halfmaster of the ship.
-   [Serendi](Serendi.md)
    -   An apparently 'short' Etrean found standing up on two crates, keeping watch of the sea. She mentions a ship that she sees on the horizon — it is implied that this is the Authority boat which attacked the Bounty.
-   [The player character](The_Pathfinder.md) (Assuming they are a Castaway)

## Trivia

-   Their boat is where the game’s tutorial takes place.
    -   This means that if you are a [Castaway](Castaway.md), you were on the boat as part of the crew, and the reason you’re a Castaway is due to the Authority’s attack, being the sole survivor.
-   Despite being hinted at that she is short, Serendi is physically the same height as the rest of the crew.
-   Captain Orlandeau is supposedly quite tall, as said in Serendi’s dialogue.[\[5\]](#cite-note-5)
-   A ‘Halfmaster’ is not a real role on a ship. It is likely that Mr. Quarters’ role is equivalent to a quartermaster, given his name.
