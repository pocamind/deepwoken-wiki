# Parliament

**Parliament** is a Boss summoned via [The Vat](The_Vat.md), which is a dungeon accessible by [The Gaunt Man](The_Gaunt_Man.md). **Parliament** heavily resembles the head of [The Nestmind](The_Nestmind.md), as its a [Deep Owl](Deep_Owl.md) with multiple heads, not much is currently known about their health, nor their drops, though it can be guarenteed that it drops good items.
