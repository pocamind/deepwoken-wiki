# Parliament

Template:Creature Infobox V3

## Description

**Parliament** is a Boss summoned via [The Vat](The_Vat.md), which is a dungeon accessible by [The Gaunt Man](The_Gaunt_Man.md). It heavily resembles the head of [The Nestmind](The_Nestmind.md), as its a [Deep Owl](Deep_Owl.md) with multiple heads. Unlike most bosses, **Parliament** has a high chance of dropping its special drop, the Nightbringer Enchant Stone.
