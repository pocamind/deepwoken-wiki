# Lionfish

## Appearance

File:Menos Fish.jpg

A lionfish in [The Depths](The_Depths.md)

Brown, Looks to be half a fish, Teeth Visible.

It drops an item called "Lionfish Scale"

  
  

## Attacks

**Cero**: Lionfish raises its head upwards, and fires a giant laser beam that takes around 3-4 seconds to charge up and does an immense amount of damage (depths only). Can be perfect dodged by rolling when it puts its head down.

**Bite**: Bites multiple times, can be parried.

**Dive**: Although non-offensive, I am putting this here. Lionfish descends into the water for a short amount of time and then resurfaces to try and get the jump on you.

## Located

-   [The Depths](The_Depths.md)
-   Aratel Sea (Spawns naturally but can also be fished up)

## Notes

-   It is recommended to be roughly power 20 (preferably higher) to take on a lionfish.
-   In order to assert your dominance and become the bigger fish you must jump in the water and start slashing.
