# Lionfish

## Appearance

File:Menos Fish.jpg

A dumbass looking fish in [The Depths](The_Depths.md)

Brown, Looks to be half a fish, Teeth Visible.

It drops an item called "Lionfish Scale"

  
  

## Attacks

**Cero**: Lionfish raises it's head upwards, and fires a giant lazer beam that takes around 3-4 seconds to charge up and does an immense amount of damage (depths only). Can be perfect dodged by rolling when it puts its head down.

**Bite**: Bites multiple times, can be parried.
