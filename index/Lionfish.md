# Lionfish

## Appearance

File:Menos Fish.jpg

A lionfish in [The Depths](The_Depths.md)

Half-fish, half-lion. A large brown fish with a black mane of a Lion, thus it's name. It has white blank eyes, with it's teeth visible.

Seams to be based off the Underwater panther or ''_**[Mishipeshu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Underwater_panther)''**_ Of indigenous/Native/Indian Americans (Or Arch got it off from ark's Shadowmane which is what I know the myth from). Considering that Deep's landscape seams to take inspiration from early American settlers (especially seen in the Summer Company's teritories) and Chinese/Japanese architecture in other areas this is possible. -Biscuit

It drops a Lionfish Scale, which is used for crafting armor sets.

  
  

## Attacks

**Cero**: Lionfish raises its head upwards, and fires a giant laser beam that takes around 3-4 seconds to charge up and does an immense amount of damage (depths only). Can be perfect dodged by rolling when it puts its head down.

**Bite**: Bites multiple times, can be parried.

**Dive**: Although non-offensive, I am putting this here. Lionfish descends into the water for a short amount of time and then resurfaces to try and get the jump on you.

## Notes

-   It is recommended to be roughly power 20 (preferably higher) to take on a lionfish. **(not really you can solo it at power 1, just dash the lazer beam and stand under it so it doesnt bite you)**
-   Lionfishs do not go directly for the ship but the players on it. To protect a ship, players must slow it down/stop it then jump in the water to move the lionfish away from the boat.
