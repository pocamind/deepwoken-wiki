## Description

The Covenant of Flame are worshippers, Flamekeepers[\[1\]](#cite-note-1) and Priests[\[2\]](#cite-note-2) that don the [Flame Worshipper Armor](Flame_Worshipper_Armor.md). They follow the teachings of [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md), the pioneer of [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md). They do not have much power around the world, as they are mostly found in the [Tiran](Tiran.md) homelands in the [Western Luminant](Luminants.md) and potentially Layer 3 of the Depths.

The worship of [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md) is centered on a dichotomy between the Flame and [The Depths](The_Depths.md)[\[3\]](#cite-note-3), with the _Flame Within_[\[4\]](#cite-note-4) representing the soul and the Depths viewed as the ultimate threat to mankind.

The leader is [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md), a [Tiran](Tiran.md) man, who is a figure known as a [Deepwoken](Deepwoken.md). He would travel to the 3rd Layer of the Depths, and come across [Korilfiend's](<Korilfiend,_the_Inferno's_Provenance.md>) garden, where he consumed a flower imbued with a fragment of the Drowned God. He was gifted the [Pleeksty's Inferno](<Pleeksty's_Inferno.md>) before being passed on to [Aska](Aska.md) for safekeeping.
