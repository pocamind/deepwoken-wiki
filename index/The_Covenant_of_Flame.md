# The Covenant of Flame

| The Covenant of Flame | |
|---|---|

## Description

The Covenant of Flame are a group of people, whos primary goals are worshipping [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md), the [Deepwoken](Lore.md#deepwoken) of Flamecharm, and learning his way of using [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md).

There's not much currently known about this [faction](Factions_&_Groups.md), as they don't appear in-game very much and do not hold any global power.

They all supposedly wear the [Flame Worshipper Armor](Flame_Worshipper_Armor.md), as it is featuring their emblem and their name.

## Notable Members

[Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md): The [Deepwoken](Lore.md#deepwoken) of Flamecharm, who ate a Flower of [Korilfiend](<Korilfiend,_the_Inferno's_Provenance.md>), and pioneered [Flamecharm](Flamecharm.md). The main worshipping figure of the Covenant of Flames.

[Ploom](Ploom.md): Grants the [Pleeksty's Will](Talents.md#inferno) [talent](Talents.md) and reveals [lore](Lore.md#amorus-pleeksty) on [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md).

[Venn Kaelhand](Venn_Kaelhand.md): A researcher and a disciple of [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md). Wielded the [First Light](First_Light.md). Was studying monsters, exposed to [Korilfiend](<Korilfiend,_the_Inferno's_Provenance.md>)'s influence which would result in their own death as they were eaten by a [Crimson Megalodaunt](Crimson_Megalodaunt.md).
