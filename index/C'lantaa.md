# C'lantaa

| C'lantaa | |
|---|---|
| name | C'lantaa |
| location | New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City |

## Description

A [Tiran](Tiran.md) man found in [New Kyrsa, The Sleeping City](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) in a building with the teleporter to the tunnel near the vent into [The Sunken Railways](The_Sunken_Railways.md). He is wearing a [Hive Adjudicator](Hive_Adjudicator.md) outfit. Similar with other residents of the city, he is unable to recall his past. Gives general information and insight to the denizens of the city.
