# Bullets

Bullets are ammunition for [guns](Guns.md) that can apply special effects when fired. Effects can be added by adding [bullet casings](Bullet_Casings.md).

## Obtainment

There are 2 ways to obtain bullets:

-   Purchase them for 5 from the [Etris](Etris.md) weapon store or [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md)
-   Craft them using 1 [iron](Ores.md#iron) per 50 bullets
