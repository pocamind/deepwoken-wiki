## Description

An Ignition Union Fighter is a type of enemy [NPC](NPCs.md) that can spawn in the following events:

-   Ignition Union Fighters vs [Akira](Akira.md) ([The Depths](The_Depths.md))
-   Ignition Union Fighters vs Summer Company Guards ([Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md))
    -   **Interactable only after event finishes.**

They only wear an [Ignition Union Uniform](Ignition_Union_Uniform.md) outfit with [aspect](Aspects.md) varying.

### Talents

-   Brutal Momentum - Successfully dodging will give your next swing hyperarmor.
-   Destructive Recovery - Causes your enemies to regenerate significantly less posture when they parry you.

## Finisher

An Ignition Union Fighter executes the player after knocking them:

| Name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Execute | \[[Falchion](Falchion.md)\] Pins down opponent before slicing their throat.  
\[[Darksteel Greatsword](Darksteel_Greatsword.md)\] Pins down opponent while raising weapon before firmly bashing the opponent's face.

 |

## Tips

-   Apply standard humanoid fighting tactics.
-   Try pinning them against a wall and then feinting your next attack before performing a critical, that should do some serious damage to them.
-   Make sure to roll when you see them use their **Critical** as hitting them during the **Critical** won't cancel it due to the [Darksteel Greatsword's](Darksteel_Greatsword.md) crit having hyperarmor for the majority of it's duration.
