# Ships

_"Soon may the Wellerman come, To bring us Redds and Pomars and Calabash" - A Celtor, hanging on with a sea shanty_

## Description

Ships are a form of transportation available in the Etrean Luminant. It is the main form of transportation between the various regions and islands dotted around the sea.

Players can acquire new ships and spawn them by talking to the Shipwright NPC, located in most ports. In more hostiles areas that aren't civilized, a Shipyard, represented by a wooden pole near the shore in gravel, allows the spawning of ships, but not the purchase or repair of them.

The Dockmaster at Greathive Aratel says he can Lifeweave modifications into your ship (you need max reputations at the Greathive) but I yet have to understand what this means.

## Trivia/Notes

-   Being a [Celtor](Celtor.md) reduces the prices of ships by 20%.
-   It's much cheaper to repair a ship when it almost broken than to salvage a ship that broke (e.g. an ironclad at 250/10000 health would cost a few hundred notes to repair, while a broken one would cost 3000).
-   Ships with a resting table can be spawned on by members of your guild through roblox resetting or dying.
