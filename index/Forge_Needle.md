|---|---|

Crafted using 1 [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) and 5 [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) (Can craft without a crafting bench)

This Item is an Advanced version of the Armorer's Needle. Instead of rolling a pip on an armor, the Forge Needle is a [Relic](Relics.md) that upgrades one of your equipment by one star.
