# Forge Needle

The Forge Needle is a [Relic](Relics.md) that upgrades one of you equipment by one star, it is crafted with 1 [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) and 5 [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>)
