## Description

Crafted using 1 [Armorer's Needle](<Armorer's_Needle.md>) and 5 [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>) (Can be crafted without a crafting bench)

This Item is an Advanced version of the Armorer's Needle. Instead of rolling a pip on an armor, the Forge Needle is a [Relic](Relics.md) that upgrades one of your equipment by one star. It basically does the same thing as the [Smith's Alloy](<Smith's_Alloy.md>), except for [Equipment](Equipment.md).

This is considered one of the most expensive items in the game because it requires five [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>). For most equipment, this is not necessary, and is much more expensive and time-consuming to get than just simply getting the equipment once again. For rare equipment like the [Simmerbloom Diadem](Simmerbloom_Diadem.md) or [Bloodforged Crown](Bloodforged_Crown.md), though, it can prove useful.

## Trivia

-   This item was added on August 2, 2024, along with the [Grand Fisher Set](Grand_Fisher_Plate.md).
-   Considered somewhat useless, as grinding out the armor set usually takes much less time then getting five [Smith's Alloys](<Smith's_Alloy.md>).
