## Description

An Inquisitor Maximus is the highest-ranking position within [the Inquisition](The_Inquisition.md), overseeing all High Inquisitors. [\[1\]](#cite-note-warden-jericho-1) Individuals holding this rank are responsible for directing the Inquisition’s operations and ensuring that High Inquisitors carry out their duties. It is unknown who currently holds the title.

[Octavius](Octavius.md) was the former Inquisitor Maximus and father of [Warden Jericho](Warden_Jericho.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-warden-jericho-1)
