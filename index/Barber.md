# Barber

| Barber | |
|---|---|
| name | Barber |
| location | Near Nerin in Etris, Inside the castle on Summer Isle. |

“ They call me butcher because i chop off- well, that's not a very good introduction, now is it. ”

― Barber

  

The Barber allows hair customization to your character; no notes needed!

To use its features, a hair ID from the catalog is needed.

## Dialogue

"Hey Friend, need a new haircut?"

1.  Sure. (Allows Hair Customization.)
2.  Restore me to my natural hair. (Restore original hair style.)
3.  Change my gender. (Change...Gender?)
4.  Change my face. (Changes face decal.)

## Trivia

-   Unlike other NPCs, you can walk while talking to him.
