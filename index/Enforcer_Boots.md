## Description

Enforcer Boots are accessories under the "Legs" category. They can only be found through chests in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md) and from killing [Duke Erisia](Duke_Ishamon_Erisia.md).

  
Equipping both items grants the "Enforcer's Pull" Talent, which increases the strength of the [Enforcer Pull](Monster_Mantras.md#enforcer-pull) Mantra after landing a flourish.
