## Description

The Enforcer Boots is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Legs" category and is a part of the Enforcer Set.

The [Conquer your Fears](Talents.md#conquer-your-fears) Talent restores the Player's Sanity if they or one of their allies kills a Monster in [the Depths](The_Depths.md).

This item is used to craft the [Hardened Enforcer Boots](Hardened_Enforcer_Boots.md).
