# Elykris

| Elykris | |
|---|---|

## Description

Elykris is an NPC at [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) that gives you a quest to find a report. This report is located right by the ladder to [Trial of One](Trial_of_One.md). The quest reward is 3 Aces.

He is located leaning on a tree, which is on a ledge to the left of a Ministry Camp above Outpost Antumbra.
