## Description

Elykris is an NPC at [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md) that gives you a quest to find a report. This report is located right by the ladder to [Trial of One](Trial_of_One.md). The quest reward is 3 Aces and a Talent next power up (assuming you haven't yet claimed any other Curiosity quests).

He is located leaning on a tree, which is on a ledge to the left of a Ministry Camp above [Outpost Antumbra](Outpost_Antumbra.md).

## Field Report Quest

### First encounter

> Elykris: They're lurking here. I know they are. The Trial has lured them to this land, in search of power.
> 
> 1.  What are you talking about?
> 
> Elykris: The Ministry. A shadow organization that rarely shows their face, yet here a great nest of them lies. They discovered something powerful beneath this land. A trial able to grant unfathomable power.

### Second encounter (without Ministry Field Report)

> Elykris: You're still walking; that's a good sign. How goes the mission?
> 
> 1.  I haven't found the report yet.
> 
> Elykris: Very well. Their ruins are on a cliffside here on Minityrsa, surrounded by many patrols of their members. They wield a powerful attunement, so be on guard.

### Interacting with the field report

> \*A field report lies here, alone\*
> 
> 1.  \[Inspect\]
> 
> -   The text details an account of the Ministry's actions on Minityrsa.\*
> 
> 1.  \[Take\]
> 
> -   You take the book, holding on to it closely.\*

### Second encounter (with Ministry Field Report)

> Elykris: You're still walking; that's a good sign. How goes the mission?
> 
> 1.  Here's the report.
> 
> Elykris: I see. So it seems my intuition was correct. I can only wonder what they'll use this power for, but all that's left to do is stop them. Thank you for your assistance, traveler. Allow me to lend you a.. gift of sorts, the ability to alter your path. When fate presents its hand to you, you can eliminate the paths you no longer desire now.

### Third encounter

> Elykris: Thank you again, traveler. The Ministry must be stopped, lest we fall victim to their power.

## Notes & Trivia

-   You can turn in the Ministry Field Report to the [Guild Base](Guild_Bases.md) Librarian, locking you out of the quest entirely.
-   Wears the old version of the "[Darksteel Plate](Darksteel_Plate.md)" schematic, a Black Parka, a [Black Blindfold](Blindfold.md), and a [Brigand's Bicorn](<Brigand's_Bicorn.md>).
