# Guild Bases

## Nodes

-   Messhall (XL) - Requires 2 Nodes worth of Rooms; a large cafeteria with 2 long tables. (Can you tell us if you can get food here?)
-   Trophy Room - A room mostly of decor; consisting a stationary globe in the center alongside display cases, tables and bookshelves.
    -   Artisan - An upgrade to the Trophy Room. For 800 Notes, allows the Player to merge attributes and appearance of an Item.
