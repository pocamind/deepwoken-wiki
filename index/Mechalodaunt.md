## Description

The Mechalodaunt is a weapon produced by [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md) by enhancing [Megalodaunts](Megalodaunt.md) with iron shells, machinery and a powerful shoulder-mounted gatling gun. They are found in [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md), later stages of the [Saramaed Hollow](Saramaed_Hollow.md) and [The Diluvian Mechanism](The_Diluvian_Mechanism.md). They are unfinished and were activated prematurely by [The Summer Company](The_Summer_Company.md), thus defaulting to their natural behavior, resulting in them attacking both "friend" and foe.[\[1\]](#cite-note-1)

However, it seems that some Mechalodaunts have been brought back under control, as some can be seen communicating through some sort of radio speaker system when being [ambushed](Faction_Ambushes.md) by one.

## Tips

The Mechalodaunt's unique moves do a lot of damage, but are relatively easy to parry and dodge. The recommended Power for these is 15-20 due to the sheer damage output they can have, easily ending lower leveled players in a few attacks.

It is important to know the distinction between **Kick** and **Blazing Kick**, as missing either your dodge or your block can result in heavy unwanted damage.

Mechalodaunts in the [Saramaed Summit](Saramaed_Summit.md) often spawn in groups of two, only spawning a chest after both have been defeated. Its recommended that you are careful when exploring the area as alerting both Mechalodaunts can make them significantly more difficult to kill off. They are relatively easy to deal with by going past doorways, which they are too big to pass through, and by setting up campfires to heal with when retreating. This can also be done in Saramaed Summit, however due to the fact that [Voidzones](Voidzones.md) cause all Campfires to last significantly less and reduce all healing, this strategy is much less effective.
