## Points of interest

A Lamp in Pilgrim's respite, interact with it to remove all snow clumps on you, if you accumulate too much clumps on you, a parasite will instantly kill you.

  

  

  

A radio in Pilgrim's respite, interacting with it will lead into a dialogue with someone who seems to be lost and alone, he will talk about generators.
