# The 72 Seasons

_This is about The **72 Seasons** inn, which was created as a part of the event. For more general information on the event, see [Metallica x Deepwoken '72 Seasons' Listening Party](<Metallica_x_Deepwoken_'72_Seasons'_Listening_Party.md>)._

LocationL The 72 Seasons LocationR

Music permeates the halls of this inn between space and time.

  

## Description

The 72 Seasons is a large inn floating through space and time.

There are a total of seven tavern doors scattered across Lumen that can lead to this place. The different doors in the Etrean Luminant are located at:

1.  In front of [Erisia](Erisia.md), sailing from [Etris](Etris.md), where the tree bandits would be placed around a campfire, on the right.
2.  Near the food stand in the [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), walk behind stand that [Lance's Brother](<Lance's_Brother.md>) owns and to the left, continuing further until you're met with a gateway on the side of the rock.
3.  Also in [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md), near the [Barber](Barber.md) or [Mantra training gear](Training_Gear.md#attunement-training-gears) shop. On the right of it would be the gateway.
4.  In [Minityrsa](Minityrsa.md), found by going to the shore and hugging the left edge of the island until reaching the area with the downwards zipline to the shore next to another zipline. The door is embedded in a wall next to free snow-capped trees.

With the other doors being placed in the Eastern Luminant are:

1.  In the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md), near the [Fountain Gates](Fountain_Gates.md), walk forward until you meet the stairs outside of Greathive. On your left and besides the stairs would be the gateway.
2.  In front of the door to [The Lordsgrove](The_Lordsgrove.md) from [Starswept Valley](The_Starswept_Valley.md). On the right of the door, near the jump pad is the tavern door.
3.  To the left of the log that leads to [Cerulean](Cerulean.md)'s cave.

## Points of Interest

### The Metal Lord

-   The [Metal Lord](The_Meat_Lord.md) can be found at a bar inside the Inn He sells meat alongside a potion that grants immense speed.

### The Pit

-   "Only one can stay at the center of this place."
-   The Pit is a large area where players can knock each others socks off while earning points for themselves.
    -   Once they accumulate 100 points, they will be given the Metalhead badge alongside the Headbang emote.
-   [The Ferryman](The_Ferryman.md) can be found outside The Pit, who sees the appeal as spectacular.

### UGC Tables

-   Various UGC items related to Metallica can be purchased here.
-   [Yamaketzal](Yamaketzal.md) is seen standing in front of the tables.

### The Stage

-   The Metallica band members can be seen performing on the stage.
