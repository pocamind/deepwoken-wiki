# Oath Shards

## Description

Oath shards are an item that is exclusive to the [Battle Royale](Battle_Royale.md) gamemode. Consuming 3 of them grants you a random [Oath](Oaths.md) and a mantra that is part of that Oath. Consuming Oath Shards after you are granted the oath grants you more of the corresponding mantras of that oath. It is unknown whether [Oathless](Oath%253A_Oathless.md), [Silentheart](Oath%253A_Silentheart.md), and [Linkstrider](Oath%253A_Linkstrider.md) will be obtainable in the future.

Picture of an Oath Shard

## Obtainment

1.  Opening a wooden chest in Battle Royale
2.  Eliminating another player

## Trivia

-   It resembles a translucent version of the [Alloy](Pluripotent_Alloy.md)/[Enchant Stone](Enchant_Stone.md) model with a slight iridescent tint.
