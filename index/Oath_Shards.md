# Oath Shards

Oath shards are an item that is exclusive to the Battle Royale gamemode, and consuming 3 of them grants you a random oath and a mantra that is part of that oath. Consuming Oath Shards after you are granted the oath grants you more of the corresponding mantras of that oath. (excluding maybe silentheart, but i have not tested it yet.)

Appearance: It resembles a translucent version of the alloy/enchant stone model with a slight iridescent tint
