## Description

The Gourdguard is a pumpkin shaped [Shield](Weapons.md). It has a requirement of 50 FTD to use and has 1.9 Weight. You can obtain this by buying this from [Lysander Spellhardt](Lysander_Spellhardt.md) (during Hallowtide only), or by going to the [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md) if you have brought the "Hallowtide 23 Medal" (from [Lysander Spellhardt](Lysander_Spellhardt.md) during Hallowtide) for 5 knowledge anytime.

## Trivia

-   It is identical to the [Ethiron Curseshield](Ethiron_Curseshield.md) in both stats and requirements, with the only difference being looks.
