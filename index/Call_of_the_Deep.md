# Call of the Deep

| Call of the Deep | |
|---|---|
| usage | Sinking into the Depths without the need to go into the whirlpool., Instantly teleporting to Castle Light(In the Depths). |

## Description

Call of the Deep is a [Deepbound](Origins.md#deepbound)\-only ability that lets you instantly delve into [the Depths](The_Depths.md) without [drowning](Death.md) or having to go to a [whirlpool](Whirlpools.md). Although, this ability is Deepbound-only, you can obtain it with the [Voidwalker](Origins.md#voidwalker) origin by having the Two Faced [Regalia](Regalia.md) and buying the ability from [Rook Rethige](Rook_Rethige.md).

If used in the Depths, you will be teleported to [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md). If used in the [Depths trial](Cathedral_of_the_Interstice.md) you will be teleported to the middle of the arena. Cannot be used in combat or dungeons.

When using the ability the character will kneel with the ground turning into a black whirlpool, black particles appearing around him and black rain pouring down. After ~5 seconds the player will be dragged down into the whirlpool.

The ability has a 20 minute cooldown.
