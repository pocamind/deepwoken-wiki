# Kaab

Kaab is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) Shipwright NPC found in the Shipyard of the [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md).

He will not talk to you or function as a Shipwright until you have reached maximum reputation with [The Hive](The_Hive.md).

Once you do reach maximum reputation, he will let you use him as a normal Shipwright NPC, while also mentioning being able to modify your ship with Lifeweave, though this is not actually possible in-game.

### Trivia

From his dialogue, he can presumed to be a Lifeweaver, making him the only Lifeweaver seen ingame.
