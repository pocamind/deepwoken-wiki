## Description

Kaab is a [Vesperian](Vesperian.md) Shipwright NPC who will only allow you to speak to him until you have reached Ally reputation with [The Hive](The_Hive.md).

## Location

Kaab is located at the Shipyard of [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md), which can be accessed by using the [Fountain Gate](Fountain_Gates.md) down the hall labeled "Shipyard."
