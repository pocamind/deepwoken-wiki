## Description

Curse of the Bloodthirsty is a buffing curse enchantment that drains your opponent's blood on hit.

## Effect

Curse of the Bloodthirsty grants a +10% damage modifier to your M1s. Additionally, your M1s attacks gain a blood drain effect.

### Curse

Upon whiffing an M1, lose 2% of your maximum blood.

### Notes

-   The blood drain from this enchantment is unaffected by damage modifiers, blood damage resistances such as [Alloyblood](Talents.md#alloyblood) or [Cauterized Wounds](Talents.md#cauterized-wounds), and the [Haemophilia](Boons_and_Flaws.md#haemophilia) flaw. However, it is affected by your opponent's damage resistances.
-   Bloodthirsty's blood drain formula is as follows:
    -   Base: 0.1 \* S \* M + (0.1 \* S + 3) × (0.9 + 0.1 \* M)
    -   [Dagger](Daggers.md): (0.1 \* S + 1) \* (0.9 + 0.1 \* M)
    -   [Alloyblood](Talents.md#alloyblood): 0.1 \* S \* M \* 0.25 + (0.1 \* S + 3) × (0.9 + 0.25(2 - M) + 0.1 \* M)
    -   Key: S = weapon's scaled damage dealt, after resistances. M = blood damage multiplier; use 1 for Slash damage, 0.65 for Blunt damage, and 3 for Daggers.
-   The blood drain from this Enchantment can be used to proc [Hemorrhaging](Bloodrend.md), dealing 15% of any PvE enemy's health once a blood drain threshold is met. Because of how high the proc threshold is, this may not proc on non-Boss enemies. Additionally, it is recommended to pair this enchantment with the Bloodrend attunement to more consistently proc this effect.
