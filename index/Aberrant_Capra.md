# Aberrant Capra

Aberrant Capra is sub race of Capra like [Primal Vesperian](Primal_Vesperian.md) that is exclusive to SudoMesh a winner of the deepwoken art contest.

Lore:

A Aberrant Capra is a Capra that was mutated by the depth when it's still in their fetal form. The case of a stillborn baby surviving in depth is incredibly rare this explains why Sir Phaidros (SudoMesh) is the only confirmed existing Aberrant Capra
