## Overview

Simforea is an island in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md). It is a [Driftland](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Glossary#Driftlands) that has recently come back from the Voidsea into the far edges of the [Eastern Luminant](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Luminants#Etrean_and_Eastern_Luminants). It's a (largely) abandoned island with abundant grows of grey vegetation and huge bells scattered around.
