## Overview

Simforea is an island in the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) It is a [Driftland](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Glossary#Driftlands) that has recently come back from the Voidsea into the far edges of the [Eastern Luminant.](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Luminants#Etrean_and_Eastern_Luminants) It's a (largely) abandoned island with abundant growths of grey vegetation and huge bells scattered around.

In the [Old World](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Glossary#The_Old_World), Simforea was inhabited by the [Anansi](Anansi.md) people, an elusive and reclusive civilization which ultimately met their end during [The Tides.](https://deepwoken.fandom.com/wiki/Glossary#The_Tides) Simforea eventually drifted in to the [Voidsea](Voidsea.md) with [Knell](Knell,_The_Lunatic_Resonator.md) being left alone with his ever deteriorating sanity...
