# Simforea

**[Pathfinder](Pathfinder.md) Exclusive**

LocationL Simforea LocationR

At the outermost extremities of a vast and ancient mechanism, whose true purpose has long since been swallowed by the passage of ages.

  

"THEY HAVEN'T SEEN ME FOR A WHILE NOW. I FEAR MY TIME IS UP"

The small black text in the bottom right reads "THE CRADLE"

"MY FUMES ARE FLICKERING/ THE WEIGHT OF MY SOULS"

"MY ORGAN. IT WAS SUCCESSFUL, BUT THE BOND WAS UNBEARABLE."
