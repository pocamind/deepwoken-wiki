## Description

Moderator Cap is an unobtainable [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Head" category. It's inspired by one of humanity's all-time favorite hats; The Propeller Cap. Equipping it grants you +5 Health & +1 Uncommon Pip

## Trivia

-   Moderator Cap is one of the five [Equipment](Equipment.md) pieces obtained through the Mod Shop. The others being [A Lot of Hats](A_Lot_of_Hats.md), [MICROWAVETM](MICROWAVETM.md), (idk), and [THE MACHINE](THE_MACHINE.md).
