# Lower Erisia

Lower Erisia is the first part of Erisia's territory. Lower Erisia consists hills, beaches, caves and cliffs.. This area is a lot more dangerous then Lower Erisia due to a heavy population of monsters and other creatures scattered in Upper Erisia. It is recommended to grind Lower Erisia's Monsters until htiting a decent level, then heading to Upper Erisia.
