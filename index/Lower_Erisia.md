# Lower Erisia

Lower Erisia is the first part of Erisia's territory. Lower Erisia consists hills, beaches, caves and cliffs.. This area is a lot more dangerous then Lower Erisia due to a heavy population of monsters and other creatures scattered in Upper Erisia. It is recommended to grind Lower Erisia's Monsters until htiting a decent level, then heading to Upper Erisia.

## Parts of Lower Erisia

Vipers Jaw

Pathfinders Sanctuary

## Events

###### Bandits resting

A group of 3 bandits(2 [Bandits](Bandit.md), 1 [Bandit Leader](Bandit_Leader.md) or 3 [Bandit Assasins](Thief.md)) sit near a campfire. This is event like many others, completes when the bandits die and a chest spawns. This can be done very simply by crouching behind each bandit and assasinating them before they see you.

###### Bandits ambush

Similar to the resting event but 3 bandits will spawn on top of you. Killing these npcs will spawn a chest where they initially appeared.

###### Etreans Vs Authority

3 Etrean knights and 3 Adretian footsoldiers will start duking it out using basic galebreath and thundercall mantras. Authority members will always be hostile to you unless you have unlocked the charisma talent that makes people think more highly of you. Each side has a small chance to drop a galestone or a spark gland respectively and Officer notes from the adrets.

###### Mudskipper meeting

On the way to pathfinders sanctuary there is a guaranteed event that spawns 9 mudskippers on the ledges of the cave.

###### Magical Mudskippers

Random event that sees 9 mudskipper pop out of the ground in batches of 3. These conniving coral creatures can be given the galebreath ability Air force or detonate once gripped.

###### Sharko😃

One [sharko](Megalodaunt.md) will spawn and chase a freshie to the ends of the earth.

###### Fire Hobo

Some half naked dude will spawn with 3 chests around him. Looting one of the chests will cause him to aggro and upon gripping them a fourth chest will appear.
