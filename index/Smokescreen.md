# Smokescreen

File:Imageeeee.png

Smokescreen bell in action

Smokescreen creates smoke around you making it difficult to see for you and your opponent. While in the smoke you become completely invisible unless you leave or use a mantra or m1 which completely removes the smoke.

_While corrupted press c again to relese 3 smoke clouds and tp to a random one._
