# Diver Apprentice Robes

## Description

Template:ArmorInfobox**Diver Apprentice** is an Adept [Outfit](Outfits.md) in _Deepwoken_.

### _Requirements_

This Outfit costs and requires four (4) Cloth and Fiber and three (3) Crystal Lens.

This Outfit requires [Power Level](Power.md) 15 and [Intelligence](Attributes.md) 10.

### _Armor Stats_

This Outfit has 1000 durability and grants +5% Physical resistance, +20% Elemental resistance and +100% Ether Regen.
