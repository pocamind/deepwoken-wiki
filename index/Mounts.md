# Mounts

File:A Mudskipper riding a Thresher.png

A wild mudskipper riding a Thresher

## Description

Mounts are tamed creatures that can be used to traverse terrain quicker. Mounts can be purchased at [Sordino](Sordino.md) after completing his quest. Doing so will unlock his shop permanently across all slots. Obtaining a mount will give you the [Beast Whistle](Beast_Whistle.md), which is used to summon your mount. However, equipping a weapon, using a Mantra, or getting combat tagged will instantly dismount you, and you cannot summon your mount while in combat.

  

## Thresher Usage Notes

-   While mounted on a Thresher, you can completely resist the slow effect of the wind in Layer 2, but still can be pushed away.
-   The Thresher walks with speed of 22 stud/sec and runs with speed of 34.5 stud/s.
    -   The Thresher mount's movement speed is affected by any movement speed modifiers you currently have, though the effectiveness of your movement speed modifiers will be greatly reduced.
-   Having a mount doesn't negate fall damage but slightly reduces it by around 2%. This value needs further testing.
-   The Thresher mount can walk on lava without dismounting the rider.
-   If you are carrying something before using the Whistle, you will continue to carry said item while mounted. This greatly increases your movement speed while carrying objects.
