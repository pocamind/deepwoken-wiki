# Unidentified clam species

| Unidentified clam species | |
|---|---|
| habitat | Southern Luminant[1] |

## Description

A species of giant clam were known to be native to the [Southern Luminant](Luminants.md#southern-luminant) in the years prior to the fall of Celtor. Their pearls were seen as valuable, but harvesting them was very difficult due to the ferocity of the clams.[\[1\]](#cite-note-pearl-1)

With the destruction of much of the Southern Luminant by the Submergence, the clams have gone extinct due to habitat-loss[\[1\]](#cite-note-pearl-1)

Their pearls were used to create jewelry, such as the [Dark Pearls](Dark_Pearls.md), once worn by the upper-class and/or nobility of the Southern Luminant[\[1\]](#cite-note-pearl-1)
