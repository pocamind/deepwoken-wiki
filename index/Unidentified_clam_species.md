# Unidentified clam species

| Unidentified clam species | |
|---|---|
| habitat | Southern Luminant[1] |

## Description

A species of giant clam were known to be native to the Southern Luminant in the years prior to the fall of Celtor. Their pearls were seen as valuable, but harvesting them was very difficult due to the ferocity of the clams.[\[1\]](#cite-note-pearl-1)

With the destruction of much of the Southern Luminant, the clams are on the verge of extinction.[\[1\]](#cite-note-pearl-1)
