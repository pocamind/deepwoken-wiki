## Description

**Megurger** is a [Food](Food.md) item. It is exclusively purchased from [Lance Leshi](Lance.md) for 15.

It is a type of **Meat**, meaning it cannot be eaten with the [Vegetarian](Boons_and_Flaws.md#vegetarian) Flaw.

### Effect

When consumed, it replenishes 25% Stomach and 10% Water.  
  
Additionally, it grants the Power buff, affecting 12% of the Stomach bar. The buff increases M1 damage by 5%.
