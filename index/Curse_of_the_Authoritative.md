## Description

Curse of the Authoritative is a debuffing curse enchantment that applies negative posture resistance to your opponent.

This enchantment changes your weapon's color to a glowing yellow and gives it an arcing yellow lightning effect.

## Effect

On uppercut, flourish, or critical attack, Curse of the Authoritative applies the [Authoritative](Status_Effects.md#authoritative) status effect, increasing the posture damage they take by 50% for 30 seconds.

### Curse

Upon being guardbroken, **you** take 50% more damage.

### Notes

-   The curse effect of this enchantment applies a +50% damage modifier to the attack that guardbroke you. This is affected by the soft and hard damage modifier caps.
