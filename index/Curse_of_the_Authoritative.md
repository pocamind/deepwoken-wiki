# Curse of the Authoritative

| Curse of the Authoritative | |
|---|---|
| bankable | Yes |
| droppable | No |
| pass down | No |
| desc | On critical, uppercut, or flourish, apply Authoritative on your opponent making them take 50% more posture damage. When you get guardbroken, take 50% more damage. |
| obtainment | Curse of the Authoritative Enchant Stone:, Titus Fulminus Destellus |

## Description

Curse of the Authoritative is a debuffing curse [enchantment](Enchantments.md) that applies negative posture resistance to your opponent.

## Effect

On uppercut, flourish, or critical attack, Curse of the Authoritative applies the [Authoritative](Status_Effects.md#authoritative) status effect to the user, increasing the posture damage they deal by 50% for 30 seconds.

### Curse

Upon being guardbroken, **you** take 50% more damage.

### Notes

-   The curse effect of this enchantment applies a +50% damage modifier to the attack that guardbroke you. This is affected by the soft and hard damage modifier caps.
-   The visual effect on proc and the description implies that the opponent gains a status effect that increases incoming posture damage by 50%, but instead the user gains a status effect that increases their posture damage by 50%. This means you can proc this enchantment by hitting person A and then still gain bonus posture damage against person B.
