# Mute

_This page is redundant. Please go to the [Regalias](Regalia.md) page._

Mute, or less formally known as 'Animal King' completely neutralizes the hostility of all monster-type mobs towards you.

File:Image 2022-03-16 204525.png

A King of Animals standing in front of a King Thresher

This means you have to hit the mob first if you want them to aggro on you.
