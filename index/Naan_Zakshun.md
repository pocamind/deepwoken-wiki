## Description

Naan Zakshun is an NPC merchant who sells various equipment and weapons labelled as "Naan's Salvaged Stock".

## Location

Naan Zakshun is located in the [Celtor Wastes](Celtor_Wastes.md), inside of a ruined building with many signs pointing to her shop.

## Black Market

Once every 3-4 hours, instead of a regular item rotation, Naan Zakshun will sell goods from her black market stock. These items are a variety of [Relics](Relics.md), [Equipment](Equipment.md), and [Weapons](Weapons.md).
