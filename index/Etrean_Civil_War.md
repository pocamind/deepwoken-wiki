The **Etrean Civil War** was a civil conflict that took place between the [Ministry](The_Ministry.md)\-backed forces of [Etrea](Etrea.md) and the combined forces of the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md), [The Vigils](The_Vigils.md), and the [Circle of Honour](Circle_of_Honour.md), who opposed the ascension of [Zi'eer](The_Lord_Regent.md) as Etrea's current ruler.

## Prelude

During the coup against, Lord Guardian Ji'ang, Zi'eer and his most loyal guards, whom he had transformed into Shoguns, stormed [Etris Palace](Etris_Palace.md) and rained bloodshed within. Ultimately, this led to Ji'ang's and the Etrean resistance's murders—allowing Zi'eer to usurp the throne in the palace.

From there, he would shield the palace with an impenetrable barrier to ensure that he could not be taken down.

Zi'eer's capabilities led to placing a Mind Veil over the people in [Etris](<Etris_(Vow_of_Iron).md>), erasing memories of the civil war's cause and altering the perception of the [Etrean Royalists](Etrean_Royalists.md)—ancient, well-respected families in its society, now being seen as bandits, or even scheming usurpers.

## Conflict

Learning about this illegal seize of power, the Royalists sprung up in rebellion. The Royalists would also be susceptible to attacks by Zi'eer, notably being the destruction of Clan Mofisu's [Viridian Sanctuary](Viridian_Sanctuary_Ruins.md) and the neighboring [Temple of Mur](Temple_of_Mur.md) through his Shoguns in 1233 CE.

In the same year, the Sachans of the Royalists gathered in [The Golden Rat](The_Golden_Rat.md) to discuss strategies pertaining to fighting [Dravik, The Rat King](Dravik,_The_Rat_King.md), who holds the secret to destroying the barrier placed around the palace. Unable to decide a strategy, an argument broke out between the four Sachans, causing the Royalists to separate and go on their own ways.

Sachan Mofisu insisted he would take back his destroyed territory and the temple, which was backed by Sachan Anako, while Sachan Ganba decided to seek contact with The Vigils and Sachan Kobura recognizing the need for powerful allies like [The Authority](The_Central_Authority.md), much to their dismay. In the end, Clan Ganba decided to head to the west and speak with [The Hundred Legions](The_Hundred_Legions.md), and Clan Kobura withdrew from the war.
