# The Military District

LocationL The Military District LocationR

LAWLESS TERRITORY OF Lost Celtor

The top brass of the military are said to have opted not to raise the alarm in time, saving their own skins by slipping out before the chaos of a mass evacuation.

  

**The Military District** is a [location](Locations.md) in _Deepwoken_. It is a sub-location of [The Depths](The_Depths.md) found in the [First Layer](First_Layer.md).

## Overview

The Military District is a small area of [The City of the Drowned](The_City_of_the_Drowned.md) which can be entered via a walkway.
