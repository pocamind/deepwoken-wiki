# Water Well

## Description

Water Wells, are a source of Clean water for players, usually located in major Cities or towns.

Pressing E on them will give you a nice amount of Thirst back, also [Canteen](Canteen.md) can collect Clean water here,

## Location

Water wells can be found all over the map

-   Etrean Locations
    -   [Etris](Etris.md)
        -   2 locations here, probably more
    -   [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md)
        -   3-4 locations from the top of my head
    -   Bandit camp in [Erisia](Erisia.md)

-   Eastern Locations
    -   fort merit
    -   greathive
    -   summer isle
    -   miners landing

-   Both
    -   voidheart

-   Depths Locations
    -   i think none

## Trivia

-   Useful for [Old Stranger](Old_Stranger.md), since you only need clean water and one drink left in the [Canteen](Canteen.md).
