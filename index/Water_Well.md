# Water Well

| Water Well | |
|---|---|
| usage | A source of drinkable water. |

## Description

Water Wells are a source of drinkable water for players, usually located in major cities or towns.

Interacting (E) with a Well restores ~50% thirst. Clicking on a Well with a [Canteen](Canteen.md) can collect up to 5 uses of clean water.

## Locations

**Etrean Luminant:**

**Etris:**

The water well locations in [Etris](Etris.md) are:

1.  Next to Guild house. (might be removed)
2.  Vetween [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md) and entrance to [Etris](Etris.md) near the slope and stairway up.
3.  On the right entrance to [the King's Garden](<The_King's_Garden.md>).

**Isle of Vigils:**

The water well locations in [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) are:

1.  At entrance of [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md) near [Lance's Brother](<Lance's_Brother.md>).
2.  Between [Temple Approach](Temple_Approach.md) and [Friid, Master of Gale](Friid,_Master_of_Gale.md).
3.  Behind the [Inn](Inn.md).

**Lower Erisia:**

The water well in the [Lower Erisia](Lower_Erisia.md) is located in middle of Bandit camp.

**Eastern Luminant:**

**Summer Isle:**

The water well on [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md) is located behind the antiquarian.

**Miner's Landing:**

The water well at [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>) is located nearby Smith next to a wooden building.

**Meteor Isle:**

The water well on [Meteor Isle](Meteor_Isle.md) can be found in the middle of Isle.

**Fort Merit:**

The water well at [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md) can be found at highest point of Fort Merit between guild hall and job board.

**Greathive Aratel:**

The water well at [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md) is located in front of [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md).

-   You need a canteen to use this Well, as there is no interact prompt to drink with your hands.

**Voidheart:**

The water well in the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md) is located next to the [Blacksmith](Blacksmith.md).

-   The Voidheart can be accessed from both the Etrean and Eastern Luminants by [Voidwalkers](Voidwalker.md).

## Trivia

-   Useful for [Old Stranger](Old_Stranger.md), since you only need clean water and one drink left in the [Canteen](Canteen.md).
-   Previously, breaking a water-well with a mantra would splatter hundreds of parts, causing the server to lag. This was patched in a late 2024 update.
