# Water Well

## Description

Water Wells are a source of Drinkable Water for Players, usually located in major Cities or towns.

Pressing E on them will give you ~50% of Thirst back, also [Canteens](Canteen.md) can collect Clean water here,

## Location

Water Wells can be found all over the map

-   Etrean Luminant
    -   [Etris](Etris.md)
        -   3 locations: [Etris Docks](Etris_Docks.md), [Etris Palace](Etris_Palace.md), behind [Nerin](Nerin.md)'s house.
    -   [Isle of Vigils](Isle_of_Vigils.md)
        -   3-4 locations
    -   [Erisia](Erisia.md)
        -   Bandit camp in [Erisia](Erisia.md)

-   Eastern Luminant
    -   [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md)
    -   [Greathive Aratel](Greathive_Aratel.md)
    -   [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md)
    -   [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>)

-   Both
    -   [Voidheart](Voidheart.md)
    -   [The Floating Keep](The_Floating_Keep.md)

## Trivia

-   Useful for [Old Stranger](Old_Stranger.md), since you only need clean water and one drink left in the [Canteen](Canteen.md).
-   Previously, breaking a water-well with a mantra would splatter hundreds of parts, causing the server to lag. This was patched in a late 2024 update.
