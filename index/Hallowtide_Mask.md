# Hallowtide Mask

| Hallowtide Mask | |
|---|---|
| usage | N/A |
| obtainment | Lysander Spellhardt |

Hallowtide mask is a Tool obtained by Lysander (in the [Floating keep](The_Floating_Keep.md) during [Hallowtide](Hallowtide.md) event). There are multiple variants each representing Primadon, Owl and Mudskipper. It can be used to spook (not all) NPCs (Each spook grants you 1 [Joy](Joy.md)).

When spooked, NPCs can say:

"I HATE HALLOWTIDE- AHHHHHHH!"

"MONSTER MONSTER MONSTER!"

"MONSTER- Wait. You look.. oh, you!"

"JEEPERS!"

"THAT'S RUDE, BAH!"

"JIMINY!"

"Ah- ah. Love a good scare, Cheers.

"NOOOOOOOOOO!"

"BAH! You're cruel!"

"You-AH! I'll get you for that.. Watch your back."

"Help, help!"

"GET AWAY FROM ME!"

"PLEASE NO!"

"Bah! Shoo, shoo! I have a bad heart.."

"Gods below! Awful, awful display! Go away!"

"AHHHHHHHHHH!!!"

"ACK!"

Some NPCs like, [Lord regent](The_Lord_Regent.md), [Maestro](Evengarde_Rest.md), [Polis](Polis.md) are not able to be spooked.

Guards also just absolutely ignore you when your wearing the Hallowtide mask and just give the same dialouge when you press E.
