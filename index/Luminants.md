# Luminants

## Description

Luminants are described to be certain regions of the world in Deepwoken. As of 1/20/22, there is only one Luminant in the game, which is the Etrean Luminant, implied to be ruled by people of Etrea. In the future, you will be able to travel to other Luminants through the Lighthouse.

you will probably be able to spawn in a different luminant when creating a character

## Etrean Luminant

A Luminant that is mostly under the rule of Etrea. It is the eastern-most luminant. The islands of the Eastern Luminant seemingly drift in and out of the void sea, bringing new life and species, and even bringing up monsters from the Depths below. Currently in a never-ending state of war, the Central Authority has been fighting against Etrea for a while.

The Eastern Luminant is split up into two seas. The Etrean Sea is ruled over the esteemed Etreans. The waters are calm, which is off-putting to most sailors, but it doesn't excuse the danger that looms on it. The Aratel Sea is always in a state of conflicts, that being minor or major. It's significantly less-developed, with less highways, and is home to numerous dangerous creatures and hostile factions.

## Central Luminant

A Luminant that is under the rule by the mighty Central Authority. This Luminant also inhabit the Knives of Eylis, also known as the Whalers, which use a Murmur called Adaigo that slows down time in a bubble.

## Western Luminant

Mostly nothing is known about the Western Luminant, but it is home to the Legion and might be ruled over by the Khans.
