## Description

The Abyss Wanderer's Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping it grants you 5 Health, 3 Legendary Pips, and the Kick Off and Braced Collapse [Talents](Talents.md). It is rarely found in chests from the [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>), specifically the Saramaed Dungeon, and other unknown locations.
