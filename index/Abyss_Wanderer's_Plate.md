## Description

The Abyss Wanderer's Plate is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping it grants you 5 Health, 3 Legendary Pips, and the Kick Off and Braced Collapse [Talents](Talents.md). It is rarely found in chests from the [Saramaed Hollow](Saramaed_Hollow.md) in [Miner's Landing](<Miner's_Landing.md>)

## Trivia

-   One of the obtainable mythical [Equipment](Equipment.md) items, the others being [Bloodforged Crown](Bloodforged_Crown.md), [Diver's Light Plate](<Diver's_Light_Plate.md>) and [Delver's Duster.](<Delver's_Duster.md>)
-   It is part of the Abyss Wanderer set, going with the [Abyss Wanderer's Boots](<Abyss_Wanderer's_Boots.md>).
    -   The Abyss Wanderer set was designed by IronKronoXD, being the second place entry of the "Black Divers Art Concept Contest", although not being planned to be added originally, the set was later added.
