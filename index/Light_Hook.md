# Light Hook

## Description

The **Light Hook** is a utility item that's used as a lifeline and escape rope in the second layer. It is temporarily given to players entering the [second layer](Second_Layer.md) of The Depths through [Klaris Llfiend, Mother of Lights](Klaris_Llfiend,_Mother_of_Lights.md) at [Castle Light](Castle_Light.md)

## Usage

The Light Hook can be activated manually or automatically by player death. Upon activation, it will send the player back to Castle Light with their health restored.

-   The Light Hook lasts for a finite amount of time, and will disappear once that time is up — leaving the player stranded unless they can escape through - much more difficult - alternative means.
-   The Light Hook will last for 20 minutes at full charge, and will recharge over time when not in use (including when you are not in-game). Note that the Light Hook does not need to be fully charged to be obtained again, and will only begin recharging after 10 minutes have passed since its last use
-   You can see how much time you have left by hovering over the item in your inventory.
-   Heading to [New Kyrsa](Second_Layer/New_Kyrsa.md) sets the timer to 10 minutes, and beginning the fight with [Scion of Ethiron](Scion_of_Ethiron.md) will obliterate it.
-   Leaving via the Light Hook tool will **not** save any items gained.
