# Frosviernr Joraelnaero

| Frosviernr Jorealnaero | |
|---|---|
| name | Frosviernr Jorealnaero |
| location | New Kyrsa |

“ ...My brother could fashion the finest spears of frost infinitely more elegant than the conjured weaponry of our contemporarys. ”

― Naerotiv

An NPC found in **Floor 2** of the [Second Layer](Second_Layer.md), behind a wall on the right side of the escape path. He made and wields the [Kyrswynter](Kyrswynter.md).

He is the brother of Kyrsan NPC [Naerotiv](Naerotiv.md).
