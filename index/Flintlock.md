# Flintlock

File:Flintlocks.png

_A vesperian holding two flintlocks_

**The Flintlock Pistol** is a [gun](Guns.md) that can be purchased for 100 [notes](Notes.md) in [Summer Isle](Summer_Isle.md).

The requirements for a **Flintlock** is 20 l. weapon and 100 notes.

The **Flintlock** deals 10 base damage and has 12 range. It attacks extremely fast (1.3x) and has a unique attack cycle. After you fire one shot you can fire another shot from the [gun](Guns.md) in your off hand (If you have one). You can also fire the [gun](Guns.md) in your off hand 2 times in quick succesion. When you attack with any [gun](Guns.md) it will use Ether Bullets unless you have bullets. Bullets can be bought in the general and weapon store in etrea for 5 notes. Bullets can also be crafted with 1 iron (creates 5).
