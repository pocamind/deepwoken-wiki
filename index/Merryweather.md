## Description

Merryweather is a Canor NPC that appears to be working for [The Ignition Union](The_Ignition_Union.md). She seems to be studying the [worms](Worm.md) next to her.

## Location

Merryweather is located at [The Chariot's Spire](<The_Chariot's_Spire.md>) standing at her research desk, that's across the bridge to the Deep Driller.
