## Description

Located near a large cluster of ruins in the Starswept Valley, Sordino sells the players mounts- docile, ridable creatures that let players get around faster, at the cost of [Crowns](Crowns.md). However, before you can get access to his services, he will give you an introductory quest in which you have to complete.

The quest in question requires you to go to the [Lightkeeper Temple](Lightkeeper_Temple.md)/[Deep Thresher Nest](Deep_Thresher_Nest.md) and collect three Thresher Eggs before returning them to him, after which Mounts will become purchasable for 3 [Crowns](Crowns.md). You only need to do this quest once to unlock the shop across all slots.
