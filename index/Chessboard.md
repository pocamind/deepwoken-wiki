# Chessboard

Template:Areainfobox The **Chessboard** is a small location in the [Voidheart](Voidheart.md). The pieces are movable and the game functions.

There is no reward or anything related to this chessboard. Playing it is just a matter of having a good time.

To play, grab the piece you want to move, then place it where you want it to move.

## Pieces

-   Mudskipper = Pawn
-   Tower = Rook
-   Gigamed = Knight
-   Sun = Bishop
-   Face down statue = Queen
-   Front facing statue = King
