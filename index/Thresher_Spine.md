# Thresher Spine

(Different from King Thresher Spines)

## Description

Image of a Thresher Spine

Thresher Spines are common drops from [threshers](Thresher.md). Despite being called spines, they look more like teeth. They can be sold to an [antiquarian](Antiquarian.md) for 15 notes. If you have 10 of them in your inventory, you cannot pick up any more.
