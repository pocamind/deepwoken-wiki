# Venn Kaelhand

| Venn Kaelhand | |
|---|---|
| name | Venn Kaelhand[1] |
| death | Eaten by a Crimson Megalodaunt[1] |

## Description

Venn Kaelhand was a researcher and disciple of [Amorus Pleeksty](Amorus_Pleeksty.md) who wielded the [First Light](First_Light.md). They studied monsters that had been affected by exposure to Korilfiend's influence, which would result in their death when they were eaten by a [Crimson Megalodaunt](Crimson_Megalodaunt.md).[\[1\]](#cite-note-fl-1)
