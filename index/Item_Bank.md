## Overview

The Item Bank is a way of securely storing items across slots and wipes. They are accessible at any banker (npcs that you turn in artifacts to), or a hired banker inside the Trophy room of the guild base. You start off with 6 item slots but you can additional for 10,000 [Notes](Notes.md) each, capping at a maximum slot count of 24. To store an item, drag an item from your inventory to the slots in the bank.

## Restrictions

Along with this ability comes several limitations, being that certain items of significance are treated differently or have requirements to be stored in the bank

-   To store any item that can be soulbound, including legendary weapons and enchants, you will be prompted to spend 1 [Knowledge](Knowledge.md) to perform a "Vow of Safekeeping"
-   When storing any eligible [relics](Relics.md), each individual relic will take up their own slot and cannot stack like regular items do.
-   When storing weapons or armor, they will likewise take up their own slot and cannot stack like regular items do.
-   [Monster Mantra](Monster_Mantras.md) ingredients cannot be stored.
-   Certain relics cannot be stored. This includes the Moonseye Tome, Void Spire, and Rain Spire.
-   Divers Light Plate cannot be stored.

## Miscellaneous

-   Despite not being an item that is soulbound or of the legendary designation, Divers Light Plate cannot be stored.
-   The item bank follows the rules of how items are displayed in chests; if an item in a chest stacks, then it can stack in the bank.
-   Hallowtide weapons and equipment can be stored for free unless they are enchanted, then they will follow the soulbind rule.
