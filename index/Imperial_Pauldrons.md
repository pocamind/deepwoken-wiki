## Description

The Imperial Pauldrons is an [Equipment](Equipment.md) item under the "Arms" category. Equipping them grants you 3 Health, 1 Posture, and 2 Pips. They are commonly found in chests from [The Depths](The_Depths.md) ([Second Layer](Second_Layer.md)) and [Fort Merit](Fort_Merit.md).

## Trivia

-   The Imperial Pauldrons are part of the "Imperial" set; with the only other piece being the [Imperial Boots](Imperial_Boots.md).
